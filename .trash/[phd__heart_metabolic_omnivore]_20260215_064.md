# Zettel Heart Metabolic Omnivore

## Core Idea
Myocardium is metabolic omnivore: uses acetate, glucose, fatty acids. Prefers fatty acids for energy but requires more O2.

(Lewandowski & Ingwall, 1994, in Hurst's The Heart)
