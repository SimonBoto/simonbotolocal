**Definition**  
An estimate of the likely course of a disease over time based on population-level data.

**Clinical meaning**  
Prognosis describes *what tends to happen on average*, not what must happen to an individual patient.

**Common confusion**
- Prognosis is often interpreted as fate.
- Patients assume statistical outcomes apply personally.

**Key clarification**
Prognosis is conditional on:
- Baseline biology
- Modifiable risk burden
- Intervention quality and timing

**Clinical note**
Prognosis should be communicated as a starting frame, not an endpoint.
