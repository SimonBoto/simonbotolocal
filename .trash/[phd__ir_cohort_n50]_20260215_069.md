# IR Cohort N=50 (Adipic/Suberic Validation)

**Objective:** Validate Adipic acid (>5μmol/L early IR) & Suberic acid (>10μmol/L IR) as urinary/plasma biomarkers via GC-MS in N=50 clinic patients.

## Protocol Outline
1. **Recruitment:** Clinic patients (18-65y, fasting, suspected IR: HOMA-IR>2.5 or clinical signs).
2. **Preanalytics:** [[44-Clinic/Protocol_Clinical_IR_Preanalytics] (plasma/urine, fasting standards).
3. **Analysis:** Targeted GC-MS (OA/FFA panel); quantify Adipic/Suberic.
4. **Endpoints:** 
   - Correlation (Adipic/Suberic vs HOMA-IR).
   - Cutoffs: Adipic >5μmol/L, Suberic >10μmol/L.
   - Sensitivity/specificity.
5. **Stats:** ROC, Pearson corr. (n=50 power for r>0.4).
6. **Timeline:** Q1 2026 recruit/analyze; Q2 lit/stats.
