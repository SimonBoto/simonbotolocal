Purpose: a shared language layer.
- Definitions we agree on
- Units / ranges / naming conventions
- Short “what it is / why it matters” for clinical use
- No long essays, no protocols

This folder is reference infrastructure, not Zettelkasten.

---

## What belongs here
- Terms: metabolomics, phenotype, methylation, dysbiosis, resolvins
- Biomarkers: PTH, 25(OH)D, ApoB, homocysteine, HbA1c
- Forms: D3 vs calcitriol, methylfolate vs folic acid, MK7 vs MK4
- Ratios: TG/HDL, LDL/ApoB
- Methods: GC-MS, organic acids, fatty acid panels

---

## What does NOT belong here
- Decision logic (goes to #type/rule)
- Step-by-step treatment (goes to #type/protocol)
- Patient narratives (goes to #type/case)
- Long teaching articles (goes to library or zettels)

---

## Entry format (keep short)
Each entry should be 6–12 lines.

Template:
- **Term:**
- **Definition (1–2 lines):**
- **Clinical relevance (1–2 lines):**
- **Operational notes:** units, typical targets, pitfalls
- **Links:** Defines / Explains / Implements targets
