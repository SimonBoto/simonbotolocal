# Dictionary NAFLD

## Core Idea
**Non-Alcoholic Fatty Liver Disease is characterized by the presence of fat in the liver (>5-10%), not associated with other causes of steatosis such as alcohol, viruses, drugs, toxins, autoimmune disease or iron overload, commonly accompanied by insulin resistance.**

Refs:
- Chalasani et al. Hepatology 2012
- Yki-Järvinen, Lancet Diabetes Endocrinol. 2014
