Definition:
Health is a continuum (many grades), not a binary state.

Clinical meaning:
- Patients can move toward health in small increments even if a diagnosis remains.
- Tracking trajectory (energy, sleep, function, flares) matters alongside labels.

Common confusion:
- “If I have a diagnosis, I can’t be healthier.” (False.)
- “If tests are normal, I’m healthy.” (Not necessarily; function matters.)
- “Health is just absence of disease.” (Too narrow for chronic care.)
