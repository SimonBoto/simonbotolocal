# Zettel MC Intro Update

## Core Idea
30 trillion human cells interact with 300 trillion microbes via thousands of chemical reactions to maintain homeostasis. Metabolism produces metabolites studied by metabolomics. Survival via adaptation to deficiencies/stresses leads to dysfunction/disease as part of adaptation.

Metabolism responds to food signals. Macros: fats/proteins for energy/structure, carbs for energy/storage leading to insulin resistance if excess.

Gut is key: microbes, enzymes affected by diet (alcohol, seed oils, fibers, oxalates, lactose, casein).
