# Ref: Marchand et al. (2018) — Metabolomic Biomarker Framework

## TL;DR
Practical framework for translating metabolomic discoveries into clinically useful biomarkers, addressing the "valley of death" between research findings and clinical implementation.

## Citation
Marchand CR et al (2018) 'A Framework for Development of Useful Metabolomic Biomarkers and Their Effective Knowledge Translation', Metabolites. 8(4):59. doi: 10.3390/metabo8040059

## Key Findings

| Finding | Evidence | Relevance to METHAP |
|---------|----------|---------------------|
| Biomarker pipeline stages defined | Discovery → Validation → Clinical | METHAP positions in validation phase |
| Knowledge translation barriers | Regulatory, economic, educational | Anticipate implementation challenges |
| Clinical utility requirements | Must improve outcomes | Adipic acid must predict/prognosticate IR |
| Stakeholder engagement essential | Clinicians, patients, industry | Endocrinology clinic partnership |

## Relevance to PhD

**METHAP Translation Strategy:**
- **Discovery:** Adipic acid as IR marker (literature/pilot data)
