# Ref: Kohler et al. (2017) — Integrating Metabolomics & Pharmacology

## TL;DR
Framework for connecting metabolomics-based biomarker discovery with clinical pharmacology to create precision medicine workflows, emphasizing the bidirectional relationship between drug response and metabolic phenotype.

## Citation
Kohler, I. et al. (2017) 'Integrating clinical metabolomics-based biomarker discovery and clinical pharmacology to enable precision medicine', European Journal of Pharmaceutical Sciences. Elsevier, 109(May), pp. S15–S21. doi: 10.1016/j.ejps.2017.05.018.

## Key Findings

| Finding | Evidence | Relevance to METHAP |
|---------|----------|---------------------|
| Biomarker-pharmacology integration | Drug development pipeline | VD as "drug" with metabolic effects |
| Bidirectional relationships | Drug affects metabolome, metabolome predicts response | Adipic acid as both marker and target? |
| Clinical implementation barriers | Regulatory, technical, educational | Anticipate IRB and clinic challenges |
| Multi-omics integration | Genomics + metabolomics | METHAP could expand to genetic stratification |
