# Ref: Beebe et al. (2017) — Metabolic Individuality in Precision Medicine

## TL;DR
Comprehensive review of metabolic individuality as a cornerstone of precision medicine, emphasizing computational approaches to understanding person-to-person variation in metabolism and its implications for individualized therapeutic strategies.

## Citation
Beebe K, et al. (2017) 'Sharpening Precision Medicine by a Thorough Interrogation of Metabolic Individuality', Computational and Structural Biotechnology Journal, Volume 14, 2016, Pages 97-105. doi: 10.1016/j.csbj.2016.11.002

## Key Findings

| Finding | Evidence | Relevance to METHAP |
|---------|----------|---------------------|
| Metabolic individuality is the norm | Population metabolomics studies | Justifies personalized VD dosing vs one-size-fits-all |
| Computational modeling enables prediction | Machine learning on metabolic data | Potential for IR prediction models |
| Integration of multi-omics necessary | Genomics + metabolomics + proteomics | METHAP could expand to multi-omics |
| Temporal metabolic variation significant | Longitudinal metabolomics | Trial design: multiple timepoints essential |

## Relevance to PhD

**METHAP Trial:**
- **Individual variation:** Explains why patients respond differently to same VD dose
- **Baseline stratification:** Metabolic profiling before intervention
- **Personalized endpoints:** Individual metabolite trajectories, not just group means
