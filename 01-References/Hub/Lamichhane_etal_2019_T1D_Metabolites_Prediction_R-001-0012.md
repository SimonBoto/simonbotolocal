## Source Context

## Relevance to METHAP Trial

### The Prediction Model
This study demonstrates that **circulating metabolites predict T1D before clinical onset** — the exact framework for adipic acid as an IR predictor.

### Parallel Logic
| Feature | T1D (Lamichhane) | IR (METHAP hypothesis) |
|---------|------------------|----------------------|
| **Pre-symptomatic detection** | Metabolites before hyperglycemia | Adipic acid before glucose abnormalities |
| **Mechanism** | Metabolic dysfunction precedes β-cell failure | Mitochondrial dysfunction precedes IR |
| **Intervention window** | Earlier immunomodulation | Earlier metabolic correction (VitD) |
| **Biomarker type** | Metabolite panel | Adipic acid + panel |
