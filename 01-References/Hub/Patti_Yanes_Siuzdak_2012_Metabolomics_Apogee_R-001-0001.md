# Ref: Patti, Yanes & Siuzdak (2012) — Metabolomics: Apogee of Omics Trilogy

## TL;DR
Metabolomics represents the culmination of the omics trilogy (genomics, transcriptomics, proteomics → metabolomics), providing the most direct readout of phenotype due to its proximity to cellular function and environmental interactions.

## Citation
Patti, G. J., Yanes, O. and Siuzdak, G. (2012) 'Innovation: Metabolomics: the apogee of the omics trilogy', Nature Reviews Molecular Cell Biology, 13(4), pp. 263–269. doi: 10.1038/nrm3314.

## Key Findings

| Finding | Evidence | Relevance to METHAP |
|---------|----------|---------------------|
| Metabolomics as "apogee" of omics | Position at end of information flow | Justifies metabolomics over genomics for phenotype mapping |
| Proximity to phenotype | Metabolites reflect actual cellular activity | Core rationale for METHAP trial design |
| Environmental sensitivity | Metabolome responds to diet, drugs, lifestyle | Explains Vitamin D as modifiable factor |
| Technical advances | Mass spec, NMR improvements | Methods applicable to OA/TFA analysis |
