# Kurotani et al. (2012) — Fatty Acid Risk Patterns

## Citation
Kurotani K, Sato M, Ejima Y, Nanri A, Yi S, Pham NM, et al. High levels of stearic acid, palmitoleic acid, and dihomo-γ-linolenic acid and low levels of linoleic acid in serum cholesterol ester are associated with high metabolic risk. 2012.

## Relevance to METHAP Trial

### Pro-IR Fatty Acid Signature
- ↑ Stearic acid (18:0) — saturated fat marker
- ↑ Palmitoleic acid (16:1n-7) — lipogenesis marker  
- ↑ Dihomo-γ-linolenic acid (20:3n-6) — pro-inflammatory
- ↓ Linoleic acid (18:2n-6) — essential FA deficiency

### Pattern Recognition Value
Multiple fatty acids combined → metabolic risk score (like UOA pattern for adipic acid)
