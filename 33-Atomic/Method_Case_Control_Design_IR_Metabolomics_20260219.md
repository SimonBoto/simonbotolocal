# Case-Control Study Design: Comparing IR Cases to Healthy Controls

**Design Type:** Case-control comparative study

**Population:**

| Group | Definition | n (Target) |
|-------|------------|------------|
| **Cases** | Insulin resistant (by HOMA-IR or clinical criteria) | 25-50? |
| **Controls** | Healthy, normal metabolic parameters | 25-50? |

**Matching:**
- Age
- Sex
- (Ideally BMI, ethnicity)

**Inclusion Criteria (Cases):**
- Established insulin resistance
- HOMA-IR > 2.5 (or local threshold)
- No diabetes mellitus (focus on pre-diabetic IR)

**Exclusion Criteria:**
- Diabetes mellitus
- Severe comorbidities
- Medications affecting metabolism

**Comparison Variables:**
1. Urine organic acid profiles
2. Plasma free fatty acid composition
3. Conventional markers (fasting glucose, insulin, HbA1c)
4. Lipid profiles

**Expected Outcome:**
Distinct metabolomic signatures differentiating IR from controls

Not specified in protocol — needs clarification
