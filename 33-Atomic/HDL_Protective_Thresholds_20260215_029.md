# HDL Thresholds for CVD Protection (Framingham)

**Claim**: HDL &lt;40-45 mg/dL ↑ CVD risk; &gt;60 mg/dL protective. Not just "good" blanket.

**Evidence**: Framingham Offspring: HDL&lt;45 risk RR↑; &gt;60 inverse. Tsoukalas: Quality matters, inflammation modulates.
