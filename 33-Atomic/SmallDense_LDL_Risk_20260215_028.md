# Small Dense Oxidized LDL Increases CVD Risk

**Claim**: Small, dense LDL particles (oxidized) are pro-atherogenic &amp; causal in CVD; large buoyant protective.

**Evidence**: Tsoukalas: Not total LDL, but pattern—small dense risky due to oxidation/inflammation. Framingham correlates density w/ CAD.
