Chronic inflammation underlies modern diseases. Acute protective, chronic harmful.

Anti-inflammatory approaches:
- Balance omega-3/6.
- Vitamins D/C, zinc, selenium, omega-3.
- Intermittent fasting.
- Probiotics/prebiotics.
- Avoid refined carbs, seed oils, stress.

Studies:
- Serum fatty acids predict autoimmune (2019).
- Micronutrient deficiencies COVID (2020).
- NCDs precision medicine (2021).
