# The Triage Quest — A Hero's Journey Through 164 Zettels

> **Estimated time:** 90-120 minutes  
> **Reward:** Clarity, control, and a lean, powerful knowledge vault  
> **Your role:** The Hero  
> **My role:** The Guide

---

## THE STORY

**Once upon a time...** (April 2023)

You began a journey. You spoke with an AI nearly 700 times over 2 years. You asked questions. You formed hypotheses. You built ideas. You created a PhD. You built a partnership.

**But the scrolls piled up.**  
155 megabytes of wisdom. 164 extracted insights. A vault so vast it felt like a burden, not a treasure.

**Now, the final quest:**  
Not to read every scroll. Not to memorize every word. But to **choose** — wisely, ruthlessly, quickly — what serves your mission and what can rest in the archive.

**You are the Hero.**  
This is your Triage Quest.

**The treasure at the end:** A vault of ~50 active notes that serve you, not overwhelm you.

---

## THE MAP — 5 Realms to Visit

Instead of 164 random notes, you will visit **5 themed realms**. In each realm, you'll make quick decisions.

```
Realm 1: The Metabolomics Mountains (highest count)
    ↓
Realm 2: The Clinical Valleys (patient care)
    ↓
Realm 3: The Consciousness Caverns (AI partnership)
    ↓
Realm 4: The Research Rivers (PhD/methodology)
    ↓
Realm 5: The General Glades (everything else)
```

**Time per realm:** ~15-20 minutes  
**Decision time per note:** ~30 seconds  
**Total quest:** ~90-120 minutes

---

## YOUR WEAPONS (The 4 Decisions)

For each scroll (zettel), you have 4 choices:

| Symbol | Name | Meaning | When to Choose |
|--------|------|---------|----------------|
| ⚔️ **A** | **Active** | Use this week | "I need this NOW" |
| 📖 **B** | **Browse** | Use this month | "This is good, later" |
| 📦 **C** | **Cache** | Keep, search when needed | "Might need someday" |
| 🗑️ **D** | **Discard** | Delete | "Trash" |

**The Rule:**  
Be ruthless. Most should be **C (Cache)**. Only the truly essential are **A (Active)**.

**Target distribution:**
- A (Active): ~10-15 (6%)
- B (Browse): ~25-30 (20%)
- C (Cache): ~100+ (65%)
- D (Discard): ~15-20 (9%)

**A:** 5-8 maximum. Be ruthless.

## THE VICTORY — What Success Looks Like

### **Your A-List (The Essential ~15)**

These are your ⚔️ **Active** zettels. You'll touch them weekly.

**Example A-List:**
1. Adipic acid hypothesis (test this week)
2. PhD protocol methodology (reference daily)
3. Patient IR assessment protocol (use clinically)
4. Current statistical approach
5. Active research question
6. ... (10 more)

**What to do with A-List:**
- [ ] Validate one this week
- [ ] Connect to your current PhD zettels
- [ ] Add to weekly review

### **Your B-List (The Reference ~30)**

These are your 📖 **Browse** zettels. You'll touch them monthly.

**What to do with B-List:**
- Tag them "browse-monthly"
- Review in monthly synthesis
- Connect to synthesis zettels

### **Your C-List (The Archive ~100)**

These are your 📦 **Cache** zettels. They exist when you search.

**What to do with C-List:**
- Mark "archived" status
- Don't delete — you might search them
- No further action needed

### **Your D-List (The Discard ~15)**

These are your 🗑️ **Discard** zettels. They're trash.

**What to do with D-List:**
- Delete or move to archive folder
- Free mental space

## THE FINAL BOSS — Commitment

**Q: What if I feel guilty discarding?**  
**A:** Don't. You have 673 raw conversations in git. You can always re-extract. The 164 were a first pass. The 50 you keep are the gold.

**Q: What if I only find 5 A's, not 15?**  
**A:** Perfect. That means your vault is lean. Quality > quantity.

**Q: What if I want to change a decision later?**  
**A:** Easy. Just move it from A→B or C→A. Nothing is permanent.

**Q: What have I achieved?**  
**A:** You've transformed from "164 overwhelming notes" to "15 active, 30 reference, 100 archived." You've taken control.
