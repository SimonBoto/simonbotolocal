## 🎯 Core Concepts Extracted

### 1. Inherited Metabolic Diseases (IMD) & Newborn Screening Paradigm

> "Τα ΚΜΝ περιγράφηκαν για πρώτη φορά το 1908 από τον Sir Archibald E. Garrod... θέτοντας τις βάσεις για τον προσυμπτωματικό έλεγχο νεογνών (newborn screening, NBS)"

**Translation:** IMD described in 1908 by Sir Archibald E. Garrod, establishing foundations for newborn screening (NBS).

**Key insight:** 49 metabolic disorders in Recommended Uniform Screening Panel; metabolite-based diagnosis established for >100 years.

**METHAP Parallel:** If metabolomics diagnoses 49 IMDs in newborns, it can diagnose IR in adults — same principle, different metabolites.

## 🧠 My Notes

### Strategic Value: HIGH
DE4 bridges **diagnostic methodology** (inherited diseases) to **complex disease application** (IR, metabolic syndrome). The newborn screening paradigm is crucial precedent for adipic acid validation.

### Key Insight
**Newborn Screening (49 IMDs, 100+ years) = Precedent for Adult Metabolic Screening**

If metabolomics can diagnose PKU in 1-day-old infants, it can diagnose IR in adults — the principle is identical: enzyme dysfunction → metabolite accumulation → pattern recognition → diagnosis.
