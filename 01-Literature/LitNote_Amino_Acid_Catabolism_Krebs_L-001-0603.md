## The Amino Acid-Krebs Connection

```
Amino acids (dietary or tissue protein)
    ↓ Deamination (removes amino group)
Alpha-keto acid carbon skeleton
    ↓ Conversion to Krebs intermediates
Krebs cycle entry points
    ↓ Complete oxidation or gluconeogenesis
```
