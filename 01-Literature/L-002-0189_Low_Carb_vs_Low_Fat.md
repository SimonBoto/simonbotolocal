# Low Carb or Low Fat?

## Source
- **Original:** Χαμηλοί Υδατάνθρακες ή Χαμηλά Λιπαρά;
- **English:** "Low Carb or Low Fat?"

## Diet Comparison

### Metabolic Outcomes
| Outcome | Low-Carb | Low-Fat |
|---------|----------|---------|
| **Weight loss** | Greater | Less |
| **Triglycerides** | Lower | Higher |
| **HDL cholesterol** | Higher | Lower |
| **Insulin sensitivity** | Improved | Minimal change |
| **Satiety** | Better | Worse |
| **Adherence** | Better | Worse |

### RCT Evidence
| Finding | Conclusion |
|---------|------------|
| **Low-carb superior** | For metabolic syndrome |
| **Low-carb better** | For insulin resistance |
| **Individual variation** | Response varies |

## Clinical Context

### Who Benefits from Low-Carb
| Population | Rationale |
|------------|-----------|
| **Metabolic syndrome** | Insulin resistance |
| **Type 2 diabetes** | Glycemic control |
| **Obesity** | Satiety, adherence |
