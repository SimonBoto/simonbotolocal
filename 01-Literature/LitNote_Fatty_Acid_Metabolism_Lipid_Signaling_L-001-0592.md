## Fatty Acid Categories

| Category | Examples | Clinical Role |
|----------|----------|---------------|
| **Saturated (SFA)** | Palmitic (16:0), Stearic (18:0) | Structural, stable |
| **Monounsaturated (MUFA)** | Oleic (18:1), Palmitoleic (16:1) | Protective, flexible |
| **Polyunsaturated n-3** | ALA, EPA, DHA | Anti-inflammatory |
| **Polyunsaturated n-6** | LA, GLA, AA | Pro-inflammatory (in excess) |
| **Odd-chain** | C15:0 (pentadecanoic) | Dairy marker, metabolic health |

## Key Clinical Ratios

| Ratio | Optimal | Interpretation |
|-------|---------|----------------|
| **AA/EPA** | < 3 | Inflammatory index |
| **Omega-6:3** | 2-4:1 | Dietary balance |
| **Omega-3 Index** | > 7% | Cardioprotection |
