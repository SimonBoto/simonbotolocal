| Stage | Focus | Goal |
|-------|-------|------|
| 1. Assessment | Intake ritual | Understand patient as metabolic story |
| 2. Sample Logistics | Pre-analytical discipline | Ensure diagnostic accuracy |
| 3. Analysis | OAT + fatty acid interpretation | Create coherent metabolic narrative |
| 4. Reporting | Metabolic storytelling | Explain findings in human language |
| 5. Treatment | Therapeutic algorithm | Mechanism-based prescribing |
