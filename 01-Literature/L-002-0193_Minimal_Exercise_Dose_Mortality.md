# Minimal Exercise Dose for Mortality Reduction

## Source
- **Original:** Ακόμη και 15–20 λεπτά έντονης άσκησης την εβδομάδα συνδέονται με 16–40% χαμηλότερο κίνδυνο θανάτου
- **English:** "Even 15-20 Minutes of Vigorous Exercise Weekly Linked to 16-40% Lower Death Risk"
- **Primary:** Ahmadi MN, et al. European Heart Journal 2022

## Dose-Response

| Exercise | Mortality Reduction |
|----------|---------------------|
| 15-20 min/week vigorous | 16-40% |
| ~25 min/week | 46% all-cause |
| ~25 min/week | 58% CVD |
| ~45 min/week | 37% cancer |

## Key Insight
- **Diminishing returns:** Biggest benefits at low doses
- Small amounts provide most protection
