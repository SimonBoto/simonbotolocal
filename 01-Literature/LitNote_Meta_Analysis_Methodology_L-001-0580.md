## Core Methodology Components

### PRISMA Guidelines

**Essential for Publication:**
- PRISMA 2020 statement
- 27-item checklist
- Flow diagram
- Protocol registration (PROSPERO)

### Key Databases

| Database | Strengths | Best For |
|----------|-----------|----------|
| **PubMed/MEDLINE** | Broad biomedical coverage, MeSH | General medical topics |
| **Embase** | European journals, drug coverage | Pharmacology, toxicology |
| **Cochrane Library** | High-quality trials, reviews | Intervention effectiveness |
| **Web of Science** | Citation tracking, interdisciplinary | Comprehensive searching |
| **Scopus** | Broad coverage, citation analysis | Multi-disciplinary topics |

### Quality Assessment Tools

**Randomized Trials:**
- Cochrane Risk of Bias 2.0 (RoB 2)
- Jadad scale

**Non-Randomized Studies:**
- ROBINS-I
- Newcastle-Ottawa Scale

**Observational Studies:**
- STROBE guidelines
- GRADE approach

---

## Statistical Analysis

### Effect Size Calculation

**Continuous Outcomes:**
- Mean difference (same scale)
- Standardized mean difference (different scales)

**Dichotomous Outcomes:**
- Risk ratio
- Odds ratio
- Risk difference

### Heterogeneity Assessment

**Tests:**
- Cochran's Q
- I² statistic (0-25%: low; 25-50%: moderate; 50-75%: substantial; >75%: considerable)

**Exploration:**
- Subgroup analyses
- Meta-regression
- Sensitivity analyses

### Publication Bias

**Assessment:**
- Funnel plot visualization
- Egger's test
- Trim-and-fill method
