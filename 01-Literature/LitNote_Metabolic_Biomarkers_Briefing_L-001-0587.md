## Core Biomarkers

### 1. Vitamin D (25-OH)

**Optimal Range:** 40-60 ng/mL

**Functions:**
- Bone health
- Immune modulation
- Cardiovascular protection
- Cancer prevention (associative)

**Deficiency:**
- <20 ng/mL: Deficient
- 20-30 ng/mL: Insufficient
- >100 ng/mL: Potential toxicity

**Clinical Actions:**
- Supplement if <30 ng/mL
- Retest in 3 months
- D3 preferred over D2
- Consider K2 cofactor

### 2. Omega-3 Index

**Measurement:**
- RBC EPA+DHA as % of total fatty acids

**Optimal:** >8%

**Cardiovascular Protection:**
- Anti-arrhythmic
- Anti-thrombotic
- Triglyceride lowering
- Endothelial function

**Deficiency:**
- <4%: High risk
- 4-8%: Intermediate

**Intervention:**
- EPA+DHA 2-4g daily
- Recheck in 4 months (RBC turnover)
- Fatty fish or supplements

### 3. hsCRP (High-Sensitivity CRP)

**Inflammation Marker:**

| Level | Risk |
|-------|------|
| <1.0 mg/L | Low |
| 1.0-3.0 mg/L | Moderate |
| >3.0 mg/L | High |

**Interpretation:**
- Systemic inflammation
- Cardiovascular risk
- Metabolic syndrome
- Response to interventions

**Cautions:**
- Acute illness elevates
- Check when healthy
- Trend more important than single value

### 4. Homocysteine

**Optimal:** <10 μmol/L

**Functions:**
- Methylation status marker
- Cardiovascular risk
- Cognitive health
- Pregnancy outcomes

**Elevated Causes:**
- MTHFR polymorphism
- B12 deficiency
- Folate deficiency
- B6 deficiency
- Hypothyroidism
- Kidney disease

**Intervention:**
- Methylfolate (if MTHFR)
- B12 (methylcobalamin)
- B6 (P5P)
- Betaine (TMG)

### 5. HbA1c

**Optimal:** <5.5%

**Glycation Marker:**
- 2-3 month glucose average
- Red blood cell lifespan

**Targets:**
- <5.5%: Optimal
- 5.5-5.7%: Good
- 5.7-6.4%: Prediabetes
- ≥6.5%: Diabetes

**Limitations:**
- Affected by RBC lifespan
- Anemia confounds
- Not fasting dependent

## Summary Panel

**Metabolic Health Assessment:**
1. Vitamin D (25-OH)
2. Omega-3 Index
3. hsCRP
4. Homocysteine
5. HbA1c

**Plus comprehensive metabolic panel:**
- Lipids
- Fasting insulin/glucose
- Liver enzymes
- Kidney function
- Complete blood count
