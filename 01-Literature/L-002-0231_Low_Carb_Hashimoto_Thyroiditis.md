# Low-Carb Diet Reduces Hashimoto's Thyroiditis Inflammation

## Source
- **Original:** Διατροφή Χαμηλών Υδατανθράκων Μειώνει Φλεγμονή Θυρεοειδίτιδας Χασιμότο
- **English:** "Low-Carbohydrate Diet Reduces Hashimoto's Thyroiditis Inflammation"
- **Study:** Huang XS et al. Endocrine Connections 2024

## Key Findings

### MRI Assessment Results
| Marker | Change |
|--------|--------|
| **Thyroid inflammation** | ↓ Significant reduction |
| **TPOAb antibodies** | ↓ Marked decrease |
| **TgAb antibodies** | ↓ Marked decrease |

### Antibody-Quality of Life Connection

Elevated autoantibodies associated with:
- Chronic fatigue
- Difficulty managing body weight
- Dry hair
- Throat tightness sensation
- Irritability
- Nervousness
- Diffuse muscle pain

## Clinical Experience

> Through clinical experience, reduction of thyroid antibodies is consistently associated with **improved health and quality of life** in patients.

## Comprehensive Approach

Beyond low-carb diet, additional improvements from:

| Intervention | Target |
|--------------|--------|
| **Micronutrient repletion** | Vitamin D, magnesium, selenium, zinc |
| **Metabolic correction** | Insulin resistance, gut dysbiosis |
| **Adequate protein** | Thyroid hormone synthesis support |

## Mechanism

| Pathway | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Reduced insulin resistance** | Lower autoimmune drive |
| **Decreased inflammation** | Less thyroid tissue damage |
| **Improved gut barrier** | Reduced antigen exposure |
| **Metabolic optimization** | Better hormone conversion |

## Evidence

> "Low-carbohydrate diet can help improve inflammation in patients with Hashimoto's thyroiditis."
> — Huang XS et al., Endocr Connect 2024
