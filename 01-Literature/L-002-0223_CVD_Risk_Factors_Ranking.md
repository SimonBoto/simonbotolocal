# CVD Risk Factors — Priority Ranking

## Source
- **Original:** Οι κύριοι παράγοντες κινδύνου για την ανάπτυξη καρδιαγγειακής νόσου
- **English:** "Main Risk Factors for Cardiovascular Disease Development"

## Risk Factor Hierarchy (By Importance)

| Rank | Risk Factor | Notes |
|------|-------------|-------|
| **1** | **Diabetes** | Highest impact |
| **2** | **Insulin resistance** | Core metabolic driver |
| **3** | **Hypertension** | Major vascular stress |
| **4** | **Smoking** | Direct endothelial damage |
| **5** | **Low HDL** | "Good" cholesterol |
| **6** | **Elevated triglycerides** | Metabolic marker |
| **7** | **Small dense LDL (sdLDL)** | Glucose-exposed, oxidized |
| **8** | **Increased body weight** | Insulin resistance marker |
| **9** | **Elevated Lp(a)** | Genetic thrombotic risk |
| **10** | **Elevated LDL** | "Bad" cholesterol |
| **11** | **High total cholesterol** | Least predictive |

## Critical Insight: The Metabolic Cluster

Hypertension + high triglycerides + low HDL + sdLDL + obesity = **manifestations of insulin resistance**

These are **not separate risk factors** but **expressions of underlying metabolic dysfunction**.

## The Problem with LDL-Only Focus

### Misguided Approach
| LDL-Centric Focus | Result |
|-------------------|--------|
| Restrict nutritious foods | Meat, eggs, dairy avoided |
| Increase processed foods | Cereals, juices, crackers, low-fat products |
| Worsen real risk factors | Insulin resistance, diabetes, obesity |

### The 80% Coverage
| Factor Category | Risk Coverage |
|-----------------|---------------|
| **Metabolic/lifestyle factors** | ~80% of CVD risk |
| **LDL alone** | Small fraction |

## Guideline Imbalance

| Approach | Detail Level |
|----------|--------------|
| **Pharmaceutical treatment** | Detailed: doses, targets, protocols |
| **Lifestyle interventions** | Vague: "healthy lifestyle," "balanced diet" |

This creates the impression that **LDL is the only/most important factor** — when it ranks #10.

## Small Dense LDL (sdLDL)

| Characteristic | Origin |
|----------------|--------|
| **More atherogenic** | Glucose exposure |
| **Oxidized** | Smoking, alcohol, processed foods |
| **Quality matters** | More than LDL quantity |

## Evidence

> "The exclusive focus on cholesterol and LDL does not fully capture total cardiovascular risk. This focus often leads to wrong choices, such as restricting nutritious foods and increasing processed food intake."
> — Risk factor analysis
