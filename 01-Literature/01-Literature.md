# 📝 01-Literature — Source Processing

**Purpose:** Reformulated source content. Not raw quotes — your understanding of the source, ready for zettel extraction.

> *"Read, reformulate, extract. The middle step is where learning happens."*

---

## 📄 Contents

Literature notes are processed sources:
- Reformulated arguments (not copy-paste)
- Quotes with context
- Your insights and questions
- Extraction candidates flagged

---

## 🔄 Workflow

```
Hub Card (02-References/)
    ↓
Read Source
    ↓
Create LitNote (this folder)
    ↓
Reformulate main arguments
    ↓
Flag extraction candidates
    ↓
Extract Zettels (03-Zettels/Conscious/)
```

---

## 📝 Naming Convention

```
Author_Year_Topic_[L-{DDD}-{SSSS}].md
```

**Examples:**
- `GutierrezRodelo_2017_InsulinSignaling_L-001-0001.md`
- `Reaven_1988_BantingLecture_L-001-0002.md`
