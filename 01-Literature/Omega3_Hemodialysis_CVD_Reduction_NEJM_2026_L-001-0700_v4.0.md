# Lit: Omega-3 Hemodialysis CVD Reduction — NEJM 2026

## Source
- **Full citation:** Lok CE, et al. Fish-Oil Supplementation and Cardiovascular Events in Patients Receiving Hemodialysis. *N Engl J Med*. 2026;394(2):128-137. doi:10.1056/NEJMoa2513032. PMID: 41201837.
- **Online:** 2025-11-07 | **Print:** 2026-01-08
- **Study type:** RCT, multicenter (26 centers, Canada/Australia)
- **Population:** 1,228 hemodialysis patients
- **Duration:** 3.5 years
- **Intervention:** 4g fish oil daily (1600mg EPA + 800mg DHA)
- **Control:** Corn oil placebo

---

## Core Claim
**High-dose omega-3 supplementation (4g/day) reduces major cardiovascular events by 43% in hemodialysis patients** — resolving the dose-response controversy and establishing efficacy in extreme metabolic dysfunction.

---

## Core Concepts Extracted

### 1. Dose-Response Pattern in Omega-3 Efficacy
> "Low-dose studies failed; high-dose studies succeed."

**Low-dose failures (<1g/day):**
- ASCEND — no benefit
- VITAL — no benefit  
- ORIGIN — no benefit
- OMEGA — no benefit
- R&P — no benefit

**High-dose successes (1.8-4g/day):**
- REDUCE-IT — benefit
- JELIS — benefit
- PISCES — benefit
- **This study (4g) — 43% reduction**

**Zettel:** [[Omega3_Dose_Response_CVD_Efficacy_Z-001-0701]]

### 2. Hemodialysis as Metabolic Dysfunction Model
> "Extreme metabolic dysfunction — chronic inflammation, insulin resistance, dyslipidemia, lowest omega-3 levels recorded."

**Population characteristics:**
- 40-50% die from CVD
- Annual mortality ~18%
- Higher than heart failure or many cancers
- Lowest omega-3 blood levels recorded

**Relevance:** If omega-3 works here, it works in extreme metabolic stress.

**Zettel:** [[Hemodialysis_Metabolic_Dysfunction_Model_Z-001-0702]]

---

### 3. Omega-3 Mechanisms in CVD Protection
> "Multi-level action: anti-inflammatory, endothelial function, triglyceride reduction, antithrombotic."

**Mechanisms:**
1. **Anti-inflammatory** — reduces vascular inflammation
2. **Endothelial function** — improves vessel lining
3. **Triglyceride reduction** — ↓TG
4. **Antithrombotic** — limits clot formation

**Zettel:** [[Omega3_CVD_Protection_Mechanisms_Z-001-0703]]

---

### 4. Meta-Analysis Support
> "Two recent meta-analyses confirm cardiovascular benefit."

**Dong et al., Nutrition Journal 2024:**
- 12 trials, ~100,000 participants
- Reduced MI, cardiac death, revascularization need

**Dinu et al., European Journal of Preventive Cardiology 2024:**
- 18 trials, 134,000+ participants
- Benefit even with statin use

**Zettel:** [[Omega3_Meta_Analysis_CVD_Benefit_Z-001-0704]]

## Clinical Application

### Dosing
- **Effective dose:** 4g/day (1600mg EPA + 800mg DHA)
- **Minimum threshold:** Likely >1.8g/day based on prior studies

### Population
- **Primary:** Hemodialysis patients (highest evidence)
- **Secondary:** High CVD risk with metabolic dysfunction

### Combination
- Works with statins (Dinu meta-analysis)
- Synergistic with lifestyle intervention
