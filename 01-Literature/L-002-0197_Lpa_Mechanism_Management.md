# Lipoprotein(a) — Mechanism and Management

## Source
- **Original:** Λιποπρωτεΐνη Άλφα ή Lp(a), τι Είναι και τι Μπορούμε να Κάνουμε
- **English:** "Lipoprotein(a) or Lp(a): What It Is and What We Can Do"

## What is Lp(a)?
- LDL-like particle + apolipoprotein(a)
- Promotes clot formation
- Evolutionary: helped bleeding cessation

## Risk Levels

| Level | Risk |
|-------|------|
| <30 mg/dL | Not increased |
| 30-50 mg/dL | Moderate increase |
| >50 mg/dL | Significant increase (50%+) |

## Current Treatment
- No drugs currently available
- Trials underway (up to 95% reduction)
