## The Ketone Body Hierarchy

```
Fatty acids → Acetyl-CoA → Acetoacetate (primary ketone)
                    ↓
            BHB (reduced form, major circulating)
                    ↓
            Acetone (spontaneous decarboxylation)
```

## Clinical States of Ketogenesis

| State | Trigger | Ketone Level | Purpose |
|-------|---------|--------------|---------|
| **Fasting** | No glucose intake | Moderate | Brain fuel preservation |
| **Ketogenic diet** | Low carbohydrate | Moderate-High | Therapeutic, weight loss |
| **Prolonged exercise** | Glycogen depletion | Moderate | Muscle fuel alternative |
| **Diabetic ketoacidosis** | Insulin deficiency | Very high | Pathological, dangerous |
