# Multiple Myeloma Comprehensive

> **Source:** ChatGPT CGPT-0264 (October 27, 2024)  
> **Key Themes:** Diagnosis, CRAB features, staging, modern treatment approaches

---

## Core Concepts Extracted

### 1. Definition and CRAB Features
> "Multiple myeloma is a malignant plasma cell disorder characterized by clonal proliferation and CRAB features."

**CRAB Features:**
- **C**alcium elevation (hypercalcemia)
- **R**enal dysfunction
- **A**nemia
- **B**one lesions

### 2. Diagnostic Criteria
> "Diagnosis requires clonal plasma cells ≥10% plus CRAB features or biomarkers of malignancy."

**SLiM-CRAB Criteria (2014):**
- **S**ixty percent clonal plasma cells
- **Li**ght chain ratio ≥100
- **M**RI with >1 focal lesion
- Or CRAB features

### 3. Staging Systems
> "ISS and R-ISS staging based on beta-2 microglobulin, albumin, LDH, and cytogenetics."

| Stage | ISS Criteria | R-ISS Additions |
|-------|--------------|-----------------|
| I | β2M <3.5, albumin ≥3.5 | No high-risk cytogenetics, normal LDH |
| II | Neither I nor III | — |
| III | β2M ≥5.5 | High-risk cytogenetics or elevated LDH |

### 4. Treatment Overview
> "Modern therapy includes immunomodulatory drugs, proteasome inhibitors, monoclonal antibodies, and stem cell transplantation."

**Drug Classes:**
- **IMiDs:** Lenalidomide, pomalidomide
- **Proteasome inhibitors:** Bortezomib, carfilzomib
- **Monoclonal antibodies:** Daratumumab, elotuzumab
- **Steroids:** Dexamethasone
