# Uric Acid: Reduce Fructose, Alcohol & Processed Foods

## Source
- **Original:** Αυξημένο Ουρικό Οξύ: Μειώστε την Πρόσληψη Φρουκτόζης, Αλκοόλ & Επεξεργασμένων Τροφών
- **English:** "Elevated Uric Acid: Reduce Fructose, Alcohol & Processed Food Intake"

## Key Findings

### Uric Acid Elevation Causes
| Factor | Mechanism |
|--------|-----------|
| **Fructose metabolism** | Produces uric acid |
| **Alcohol** | Increases uric acid production |
| **Processed foods** | Metabolic dysfunction |

### Health Consequences
- Gout (painful joint inflammation)
- Kidney stones
- Metabolic syndrome
- Cardiovascular disease risk

### Dietary Solutions
- Eliminate high-fructose corn syrup
- Reduce alcohol consumption
- Avoid processed foods
- Focus on whole, unprocessed foods
