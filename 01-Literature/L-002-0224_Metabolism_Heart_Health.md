# Metabolism & Heart Health: Beyond Cholesterol

## Source
- **Original:** Μεταβολισμός & Υγεία της Καρδιάς
- **English:** "Metabolism & Heart Health"
- **References:** Nature Reviews Cardiology

## Key Finding: Cholesterol Is Only One Part

| Common Belief | Reality |
|---------------|---------|
| Cholesterol is primary factor | Only one part, not main determinant |
| Lower cholesterol = heart health | Metabolic factors dominate |

## The 14× Risk Multiplier

| Factor | Risk Increase |
|--------|---------------|
| **Insulin resistance** | **14× greater** coronary artery disease risk |
| **Isolated cholesterol elevation** | Baseline |

> Insulin resistance carries **14 times greater risk** for coronary artery disease compared to cholesterol alone.

## Metabolic Processes Central to Heart Health

| Process | Role |
|---------|------|
| **Energy production** | Cardiac muscle function |
| **Nutrient adequacy** | Micronutrient cofactors |
| **Insulin action** | Vascular health |
| **Inflammation management** | Endothelial protection |

## Silent Progression

| Condition | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Type 2 diabetes | Acts years before symptoms |
| Hypertension | Gradual vascular damage |
| Obesity | Chronic metabolic stress |
| Kidney dysfunction | Multi-system impact |

These factors **create ground for CVD before symptoms appear**.

## Key Principles for Heart Health

1. **Individual metabolism** — Each person's unique metabolic profile affects cardiovascular risk
2. **Insulin resistance, diabetes, obesity** — Metabolic disorders that increase risk
3. **Correcting deficiencies** — Addressing micronutrient gaps improves heart health
4. **Resolving chronic inflammation** — Reducing inflammatory burden protects vasculature
