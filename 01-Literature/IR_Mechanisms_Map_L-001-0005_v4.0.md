## Key Figures & Data

**Central Paradox Diagram:**
- Acute IR (infection, trauma, pregnancy) = protective
- Chronic IR (MetS, T2D, CVD, cancer, dementia) = pathological

**Level Structure:**
- Level 0: Triggers (metabolic, inflammatory, stress, endocrine)
- Level 1: Pathophysiology (cellular mechanisms)
- Level 2: Clinical Manifestation (staging)

---

## My Notes

**Synthesis:** This document represents a comprehensive causal framework connecting triggers through cellular mechanisms to clinical outcomes. The five pathways operate synergistically — rarely does IR develop through a single mechanism.

**Connection to METHAP/PhD:** 
- All five pathways justify the multi-biomarker approach
- Stage 1 focus validates our early detection emphasis
- Vicious cycles explain why IR is self-perpetuating without intervention
- Provides mechanistic rationale for adipic acid as marker (mitochondrial pathway)

**Questions:**
- Which pathway predominates in different populations?
- Can we stratify patients by dominant mechanism?
- Do interventions work better when matched to mechanism?
