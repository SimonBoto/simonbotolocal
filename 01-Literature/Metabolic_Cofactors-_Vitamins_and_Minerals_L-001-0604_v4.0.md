## The Cofactor Hierarchy

| Cofactor | Primary Role | Deficiency Consequence |
|----------|--------------|------------------------|
| **B1 (Thiamine)** | Decarboxylation reactions | Beriberi, Wernicke-Korsakoff |
| **B2 (Riboflavin)** | FAD synthesis | Mucocutaneous lesions, anemia |
| **B3 (Niacin)** | NAD synthesis | Pellagra |
| **B5 (Pantothenate)** | CoA synthesis | Fatigue, paresthesia |
| **B6 (Pyridoxine)** | Transamination | Neurological symptoms |
| **B12 (Cobalamin)** | Methylmalonyl-CoA mutase | Pernicious anemia, neuropathy |
| **Iron** | Heme, Fe-S clusters | Anemia, fatigue |
| **Magnesium** | ATP, kinase reactions | Cramps, arrhythmia |
