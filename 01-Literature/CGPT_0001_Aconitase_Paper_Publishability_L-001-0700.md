### 2. Clinical Relevance Map

**Aconitase connects to:**
- **Cancer metabolism** — TCA cycle node, iron handling
- **Neurodegeneration** — Mitochondrial dysfunction (PD, AD)
- **Ischemia-reperfusion** — ROS sensitivity, tissue damage
- **Inflammation/Immune** — Macrophage metabolism
- **Iron dysregulation** — Fe-S cluster biology

### 3. Key Mechanisms

**Fe-S Cluster Dynamics:**
- Aconitase requires [4Fe-4S] cluster for activity
- Cluster loss → citrate accumulation → metabolic shift
- Redox-sensitive: ROS destroys cluster

**Compartmentalization:**
- ACO1: Cytosolic (iron regulatory protein)
- ACO2: Mitochondrial (TCA cycle enzyme)
- Dual function: metabolic + regulatory
