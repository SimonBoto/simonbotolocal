# CVD Risk Factors — Priority Ranking

## Source

| Rank | Factor |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Diabetes |
| 2 | Insulin resistance |

| 4 | Smoking |
| 5 | Low HDL |
| 6 | Elevated triglycerides |
| 7 | Small dense LDL |

## Key Insight
- LDL is #10 yet receives disproportionate focus
- Metabolic cluster = insulin resistance manifestations
