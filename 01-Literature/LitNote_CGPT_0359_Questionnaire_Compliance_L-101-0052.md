## Context
Seeking ethics approval for multiple questionnaires (SF-36, FFQ, Numex, 24-hour recall, exercise, alcohol) with strategies for broad compliance.

## Eight Strategies for Ethics Approval

### 1. Informed Consent Integration
**Application location:** Ethical considerations or data management section

**Key elements:**
- Study scope explanation
- Risk and benefit disclosure
- Voluntary participation emphasis
- Right to withdraw without penalty
- **Per-questionnaire consent** for sensitive or distinct instruments

### 2. Pilot Testing
**Application location:** Methodology or preliminary studies section

**Content:**
- Preliminary testing plan
- Refinement process based on feedback
- Reliability and validation steps
- Identification of issues before full deployment

### 3. Data Collector Training
**Application location:** Project management or team roles section

**Training components:**
- Ethical research conduct
- Data collection techniques
- Participant interaction protocols
- Quality assurance methods

### 4. Participant Engagement
**Application location:** Recruitment strategy section

**Approaches:**
- Informational sessions
- Community center engagement
- Clear communication of study importance
- Addressing participant concerns proactively

### 5. Accessibility and Convenience
**Application location:** Data collection methods section

**Implementation:**
- Accessible language in all materials
- Online submission options
- Flexible scheduling for interviews
- Multi-language support if needed

### 6. Feedback and Incentives
**Application location:** Participant compensation section

**Considerations:**
- Study findings summary to participants
- Small tokens of appreciation (non-coercive)
- Transparency about incentives
- No undue influence on participation

### 7. Monitoring and Evaluation
**Application location:** Quality control section

**Processes:**
- Ongoing data quality checks
- Regular audits
- Deviation identification and correction
- Protocol adherence monitoring

### 8. Transparent Communication
**Application location:** Project management or ethical considerations

**Stakeholders:**
- Participants — regular updates
- Research team — clear protocols
- Ethics committee — progress reports

## Questionnaire-Specific Considerations

### Per-Questionnaire Consent
**When appropriate:**
- Different sensitivity levels
- Distinct data types
- Longitudinal collection points
- Optional additional instruments

**Phrasing:**
> "Separate informed consents secured before each specific questionnaire, ensuring participants understand the nature and purpose of each distinct set of questions."

### Standardized Instruments
| Questionnaire | Purpose | Considerations |
|---------------|---------|----------------|
| **SF-36** | Quality of life | Widely validated; minimal risk |

| **24-hour recall** | Dietary intake | Training for accuracy |
| **Exercise** | Physical activity | Self-report limitations |
| **Alcohol** | Substance use | Sensitivity; confidentiality |
