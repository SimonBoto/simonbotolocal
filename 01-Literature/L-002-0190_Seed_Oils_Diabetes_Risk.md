# French Fries in Seed Oils Increase Diabetes Risk by 20%

## Source
- **Original:** Τηγανητές Πατάτες σε Σπορέλαιο Αυξάνουν τον Κίνδυνο Διαβήτη κατά 20%
- **English:** "French Fries in Seed Oils Increase Diabetes Risk by 20%"

## Key Finding
| Exposure | Risk |
|----------|------|
| **Fried potatoes in seed oils** | +20% diabetes risk |
| **Mechanism** | Oxidized omega-6 fatty acids |
| **Independent of calories** | Yes |

## Seed Oil Problems

### Metabolic Disruption
| Mechanism | Effect |
|-----------|--------|
| **High omega-6:omega-3 ratio** | Pro-inflammatory |
| **Oxidation during frying** | Toxic byproducts |
| **Insulin signaling disruption** | Glucose intolerance |
| **Mitochondrial damage** | Metabolic dysfunction |

### Foods to Avoid
| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Fried foods** | French fries, fried chicken |
| **Restaurant fried items** | Unknown oil quality |
| **Processed snacks** | Chips, crackers with seed oils |

## Better Alternatives
| Option | Rationale |
|--------|-----------|
| **Olive oil** | Monounsaturated, stable |
| **Animal fats** | Tallow, lard, heat-stable |
| **Coconut oil** | Saturated, stable |
| **Air frying/baking** | No added oils |
