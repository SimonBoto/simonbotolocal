| **Complexity** | "Too complicated," "I don't understand" |
| **Compliance** | "Too many supplements," "Can't change diet" |
| **Previous failures** | "Tried everything, nothing works" |
| **Science challenges** | "Where's the evidence?" |

## Core Communication Principles

1. **Lead with mechanism** — Explain *why*, not just *what*
2. **Use analogies** — Metabolic storytelling
3. **Validate concerns** — Acknowledge before reframing
4. **Offer choices** — Collaborative, not authoritarian
5. **Set expectations** — Timeline, process, outcomes

## My Notes

**Synthesis:** Communication is a clinical skill—patients who understand commit; those who don't drop out.

**Training Application:** Role-play these scripts until responses become automatic.
