# Lit: 8-Week Metabolomics Bootcamp Curriculum

## Source
- **Full citation:** ChatGPT Analysis (2025). *8-Week Metabolomics Bootcamp Curriculum*. ChatGPT Archive CGPT-0022.
- **Inbox origin:** [[CGPT_0022_Metabolomics_training_role_CGPT-0022]]

## Core Claim
An 8-week, time-bound training curriculum designed to scaffold cognitive transformation from biochemical literacy to clinical integration.

---

## Zettels Extracted

### 1. Bootcamp Four Phase Structure
- **Claim:** Training requires four progressive phases: Foundation → OAT Mastery → Lipidomics → Integration
- **Evidence:** "Time-bound, progressive, modular, clinically relevant, designed to scaffold cognitive transformation."
- **Zettel:** [[Bootcamp_Four_Phase_Structure_Z-001-1035]]

### 2. Phase 1 Biochemical Literacy Foundation
- **Claim:** Weeks 1-2 reformat the brain's operating system through biochemical literacy and pathway megastructure
- **Evidence:** "Reformat their brain's operating system. Metabolism as information flow. Mitochondria as decision-makers, not furnaces."
- **Zettel:** [[Phase_1_Biochemical_Literacy_Foundation_Z-001-1036]]

### 3. Phase 2 OAT Pattern Mastery
- **Claim:** Weeks 3-4 develop pattern recognition in organic acids by category, not isolated markers
- **Evidence:** "Pattern recognition by category. Markers come in flocks, never alone. Feel metabolomics as a language."
- **Zettel:** [[Phase_2_OAT_Pattern_Mastery_Z-001-1037]]

### 4. Phase 3 Lipidomics Interpretation
- **Claim:** Weeks 5-6 teach ratio-based fatty acid interpretation and lipid signaling
- **Evidence:** "Ratios matter, not absolutes. Seed oil collapse as clinical insult."
- **Zettel:** [[Phase_3_Lipidomics_Interpretation_Z-001-1038]]

### 5. Phase 4 Clinical Integration Drills
- **Claim:** Weeks 7-8 form the "metabolomics brain" through energy-first reasoning and practice drills
- **Evidence:** "The 'metabolomics brain' being formed. Energy-first reasoning, cellular detox, membrane reconstruction."
- **Zettel:** [[Phase_4_Clinical_Integration_Drills_Z-001-1039]]

### 6. Training Teaching Style Framework
- **Claim:** Teaching style must match trainee personality: Military Instructor vs Socratic Mentor vs Hybrid Precision
- **Evidence:** "Military Instructor vs Socratic Mentor vs Hybrid Precision. Choose based on trainee personality and clinic needs."
- **Zettel:** [[Training_Teaching_Style_Framework_Z-001-1040]]

## Week-by-Week Syllabus

| Week | Phase | Focus | Deliverable |
|------|-------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Foundation | Biochemical literacy reboot | Interactive diagrams, micro-quizzes, thought puzzles |
| 2 | Foundation | Pathway megastructure | Pathway Atlas, 5 failure patterns, oral exam |
| 3 | OAT Mastery | Pattern recognition | Category-based marker interpretation |
| 4 | OAT Mastery | Case-based immersion | 10 curated cases, biochemical inference |
| 5 | Lipidomics | Physiology + interpretation | Ratio gospel, membrane repair |
| 6 | Lipidomics | Clinical decision-making | Fatty acid patterns reveal diet truths |
| 7 | Integration | Three integration algorithms | Energy-first, detox/methylation, membrane |
| 8 | Integration | Clinical practice drills | Daily cases, treatment plans, communication |

## Competency Targets (Week 8)
By graduation, trainees must:
- [ ] Interpret OAT without drowning
- [ ] Read fatty acids like an x-ray
- [ ] Build personalized, mechanistic regimens
- [ ] Understand patient's metabolic story
- [ ] Speak patient-friendly language
- [ ] Fit into clinic workflow without chaos

---

## Synthesis
This curriculum transforms knowledge acquisition into cognitive restructuring—trainees don't just learn metabolomics, they learn to *think* in metabolomics. The progression from pattern recognition → case-based learning → integration mirrors research methodology training.
