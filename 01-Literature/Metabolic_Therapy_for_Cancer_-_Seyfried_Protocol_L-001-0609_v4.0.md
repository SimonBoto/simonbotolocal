# Metabolic Therapy for Cancer — Seyfried Protocol

> **Source:** ChatGPT CGPT-0264 (October 27, 2024)  
> **Key Themes:** Cancer as metabolic disease, ketogenic diet, Press-Pulse strategy, caloric restriction

---

## Core Concepts Extracted

### 1. Cancer as Metabolic Disease
> "Thomas Seyfried proposes that cancer is primarily a metabolic disease rather than a genetic one—mitochondrial dysfunction drives tumorigenesis."

**Paradigm Shift:**
- Traditional view: Cancer = genetic disease (mutations)
- Seyfried view: Cancer = metabolic disease (mitochondrial dysfunction)
- Genetic mutations = downstream effect, not cause

### 2. Ketogenic Diet in Cancer Therapy
> "Ketogenic diets force metabolic inflexibility in cancer cells—normal cells adapt to ketones, cancer cells cannot."

**Mechanism:**
- Cancer cells have defective mitochondria
- Cannot oxidize ketones effectively
- Dependent on glucose
- Ketogenic diet starves cancer while feeding normal cells

### 3. Press-Pulse Therapeutic Strategy
> "Combining chronic metabolic stress (Press) with acute therapeutic interventions (Pulse) for enhanced efficacy."

**Press (Chronic):**
- Ketogenic diet
- Caloric restriction
- Metabolic stress continuously applied

**Pulse (Acute):**
- Chemotherapy
- Radiation
- Hyperbaric oxygen
- Intermittent high-intensity interventions

### 4. Caloric Restriction and Fasting
> "Reducing caloric intake lowers glucose and growth factors that promote cancer growth."

**Benefits:**
- Lowers blood glucose
- Reduces insulin and IGF-1
- Enhances ketone production
- Autophagy activation

## The Seyfried Framework

| Cancer Cell | Normal Cell |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Mitochondrial defect** | Healthy mitochondria |
| **Cannot use ketones** | Can use ketones |
| **Dependent on glucose** | Metabolically flexible |
| **Target for starvation** | Protected by metabolic flexibility |

---

## Clinical Applications

### GBM (Glioblastoma) Protocol
- **Ketogenic diet:** <20g carbs/day
- **Glucose-ketone index (GKI):** Target 1-4
- **Hyperbaric oxygen:** Combined with ketosis
- **Results:** Case reports of prolonged survival

### Implementation Strategy

| Phase | Intervention | Duration |
|-------|--------------|----------|
| **Induction** | Strict ketogenic diet | 2-4 weeks |
| **Maintenance** | Moderate ketosis | Ongoing |
| **Press** | Chronic metabolic stress | Continuous |
| **Pulse** | Drug/radiation bursts | Intermittent |
