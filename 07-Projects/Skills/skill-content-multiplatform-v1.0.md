# Skill: Multi-Platform Content System v1.0 (Vision)

> **From Zettelkasten to omnichannel content machine**

---

## Phase 1: X Mastery (Now — 4 weeks)

**Goal:** 1,000 followers, consistent daily posting

| Week | Focus | Deliverable |
|------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Thread quality | 7 threads, refine voice |
| 2 | Engagement | Reply strategy, community building |
| 3 | Analytics | What works, double down |
| 4 | Automation | Scheduling, batch creation |

**Success:** 1K followers, 5% engagement rate

---

## Phase 2: LinkedIn Expansion (Weeks 5-8)

**Goal:** Professional authority, B2B reach

| Adaptation | X → LinkedIn |
|------------|--------------|
| Threads | → Long-form articles (500-800w) |
| Voice | → More formal, career-focused |
| Hooks | → "In my clinical practice..." |
| CTAs | → Newsletter signup, consultation |

**Content:** Same synthesis, expanded narrative

---

## Phase 3: Newsletter (Weeks 9-12)

**Goal:** Owned audience, deeper relationships

| Element | Specification |
|---------|---------------|
| Frequency | Weekly |
| Length | 1,000-1,500 words |
| Structure | Hook → Evidence → Case → Action → CTA |
| Source | 2-3 syntheses integrated |
| Platform | Substack or Beehiiv |

**Monetization:** Premium tier ($5-10/month) for clinicians

---

## Phase 4: Short Video (Months 4-6)

**Goal:** YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Instagram Reels

| Format | Specs | Production |
|--------|-------|------------|
| Talking head | 60s, phone + ring light | Batch 10 in 2h |
| Voiceover + B-roll | 30-60s, stock footage | Descript |
| Carousel | 5-7 slides, static | Canva template |

**Scripts:** Derived from X threads (already tested)

---

## Phase 5: Long Video + Podcast (Months 7-12)

| Live stream | 60 min | Q&A, case discussions |
