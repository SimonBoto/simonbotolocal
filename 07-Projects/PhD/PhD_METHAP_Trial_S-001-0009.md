## Trial Overview

### Research Question
Does high-dose Vitamin D (with K2) improve insulin sensitivity in metabolically dysregulated patients, and what metabolomic signatures predict response?

### Design
| Element | Specification |
|---------|---------------|
| **Type** | Randomized controlled trial |
| **Arms** | High-dose VitD + K2 vs. Placebo |
| **Duration** | 6 months |
| **N** | 60-80 participants |
| **Primary outcome** | HOMA-IR improvement |
| **Secondary** | Metabolomic panel, inflammatory markers |

- **PI:** Dr. Aristidis Tsatsakis (University of Crete)
- **Co-PI:** Dr. Nikolaos Drakoulis (Athens Pharmacometabolomics)
