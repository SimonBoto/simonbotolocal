1. **-Glutamylcysteine Synthesis**: This step is catalyzed by the enzyme -glutamylcysteine synthetase (also known as glutamate-cysteine ligase). It combines glutamate and cysteine to form -glutamylcysteine.

The major pathway required for its production is the **-glutamyl cycle**, which involves the above two enzymatic reactions. Glutathione plays a crucial role in cellular redox homeostasis, detoxification, and the maintenance of the thiol status of proteins.
