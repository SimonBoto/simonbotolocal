## Trade-off Analysis

**Taxi vs Reality:**
- ❌ Expensive
- ❌ No movement  
- ❌ Doesn't save meaningful time (vs walking: only 10-20 min "saved")
- ❌ **Kills the "extra day" feeling**

**Walking ROI:**
- Free
- Built-in exercise (40-50 min)
- Mental clarity before clinic
- Mood boost
- **Time quality > time quantity**
