# 🏥 Clinic Appointment Types

**System:** Desknet  
**Workflow:** Templates manually adapted  
**Last updated:** April 2, 2026

---

## Appointment Types

| Type | Description | Avg Duration | Prep Needed |
|------|-------------|--------------|-------------|
| **Initial assessment** | First patient visit | 60 min | Full history template |
| **Follow up assessment** | Return patient visit | 30 min | Previous results review |
| **Result email preparation** | Drafting metabolomics reports | 20 min | Lab data, template |
| **Result phone call** | Post-email discussion | 15 min | Notes from email |
| **Explanation of Metabolomics** | Educational session | 45 min | Visual aids, handouts |
| **Metabolomics treatment & guidance** | Protocol planning | 60 min | Personalized plan template |
| **Arranged phone call** | Scheduled callbacks | 10 min | Callback notes |

---

## Phone Batch System

**Daily slot:** 1 hour within protected 2-hour block  
**Process:**
1. All requested calls collected throughout day
2. Batched into 1-hour window
3. Tracked: duration + outcome

**Purpose:** Minimize context switching, consolidate admin
