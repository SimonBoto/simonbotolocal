- Metabolomics (fatty/org acids) biomarkers RA/OA (BCAA/histidine ratio OA knee; steroids RA).

## Full Extract
**Core Thesis**: RA (autoimmune, neutrophils/systemic inflam, 1.5% pop) vs OA (degenerative joints, obesity-linked, 10% >60yo). RA: hidden hunger (micronut defs) + dysbiosis → mimicry/inflam → joint destruction. Metabolomics detects biomarkers (BCAA/histidine OA; steroids RA). Rx: Targeted supps (VitD/Zn resolve inflam), diet (PUFA/polyphenols), microbiome.

**Key Findings**:
- RA: Neutrophils trap/kill bacteria → autoantigens; chronic inflam cytokines.
- OA: Cartilage wear, no systemic.
- Biomarkers: Metab profiling RA/OA (Zhai BCAA knee OA; Yousri steroids RA).
- Hidden Hunger: Defs ↑ RA trajectory (Margină chronic inflam).
- VitD/Microbiome: Tolerance breach (Yamamoto); Zn/VitD therapy (Wessels).
