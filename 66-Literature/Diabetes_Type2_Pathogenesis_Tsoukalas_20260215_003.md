## Full Extract
**Core Thesis**: How T2D declared: IR cascade from hidden hunger (micronut defs), stress/cortisol, microbiome dysbiosis, empty calories → beta-cell failure. Reversible early (Taylor DIADEM n=50 RCT p<0.01); metabolomics Rx.

**Pathogenesis**: T2D type 2 (90%) IR → hyperinsulinemia → beta exhaustion. Causes: hidden hunger, cortisol↑, dysbiosis, sugar overload.

**Reversibility**: Calorie restriction (800kcal) reverses T2D (Lancet Taylor 2019).

**Metabolomics**: 174 markers detect T2D drivers (Tsoukalas).
