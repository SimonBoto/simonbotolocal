## Key Findings
- **7,000 participants** followed >10 years
- Each additional UPF serving/day → **+5% CVD risk**
- Highest vs. lowest consumers: **67% increased risk**
- Risk persists **after controlling for** cholesterol, BP, diabetes
- UPF is an **independent risk factor** for cardiovascular disease
