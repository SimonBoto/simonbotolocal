## Key Findings
- **Mitochondrial dysfunction** identified as a primary hallmark of aging
- Complex I dysfunction impairs cellular energy production
- Metabolic flexibility (glucose vs. ketones) becomes critical when mitochondria are compromised
- Mitochondrial damage linked to chronic diseases: cardiovascular, diabetes, cancer, autoimmune
- Ketone bodies provide alternative fuel source that bypasses defective Complex I
