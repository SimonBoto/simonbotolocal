# Autoimmune Disease — Diet and Lifestyle Root Causes

## Key Studies

### Fasano — Leaky Gut and Autoimmunity (2012)
- **Finding:** Intestinal permeability precedes autoimmune disease
- **Mechanism:** Tight junction dysfunction allows antigen entry
- **Implication:** Gut barrier critical for prevention

### AIP (Autoimmune Protocol) Trials (2017-2019)
- **IBD patients:** Significant symptom improvement
- **Hashimoto's:** Reduced thyroid antibodies
- **Mechanism:** Elimination of triggers + nutrient density

### Gut Microbiome and Autoimmunity (2015-2024)
- **Finding:** Dysbiosis precedes autoimmune onset
- **Mechanism:** Molecular mimicry, immune training
- **Therapeutic:** Probiotics, fecal transplant showing promise

---

## Mechanisms

### Leaky Gut → Autoimmunity
| Step | Process |
|------|---------|
| **1. Barrier dysfunction** | Tight junctions open |
| **2. Antigen entry** | Undigested proteins enter blood |
| **3. Immune activation** | T-cells respond |
| **4. Molecular mimicry** | Cross-reaction with self-tissue |
| **5. Autoimmunity** | Tissue damage, chronic inflammation |

### Dietary Triggers
| Food | Mechanism | Diseases |
|------|-----------|----------|
| **Gluten** | Zonulin release, leaky gut | Celiac, thyroid, MS |
| **Dairy** | Casein cross-reactivity | T1D, thyroid |
| **Nightshades** | Glycoalkaloids | Arthritis, IBD |
| **Lectins** | Gut irritation | Multiple |
| **Processed foods** | Inflammation, dysbiosis | All |
