# UNICEF Report: Obese Children Now Outnumber Underweight Children Globally

## Citation
UNICEF. "A Historical Reversal: For the First Time Globally, Obese Children Outnumber Underweight Children." 2025.

## Source
- **Primary source:** UNICEF Global Report
- **Citation extracted from:** Facebook Posts Collection
- **Type:** Global health report
- **Status:** Citation from source

## Key Findings
- First time in history: obese children > underweight children globally
- Childhood obesity increasing at alarming rate
- One of the most serious threats to children's health
- Driving factors: ultra-processed foods, sugar-sweetened beverages, sedentary lifestyle, screen time, sleep disruption, environmental toxins
- Future implications: chronic disease epidemic, healthcare system strain, economic costs

## Clinical Relevance
Historical reversal from undernutrition to overnutrition as primary childhood health threat. Urgent need for prevention at individual, school, policy, and healthcare levels.
