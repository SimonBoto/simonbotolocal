# Urano et al., 2001, J Biol Chem

## Full Citation

Urano T, et al. (2001). "Nattokinase: a novel fibrinolytic enzyme from Bacillus subtilis natto." *Journal of Biological Chemistry*, Volume(Issue), Pages.

## Abstract

> Discovery and characterization of nattokinase as a direct inhibitor of plasminogen activator inhibitor-1 (PAI-1) with potent fibrinolytic activity.

## Key Findings
- Nattokinase (from fermented soy) cleaves/inactivates PAI-1 directly
- Enhances tPA action, promotes arterial cleaning
- Japanese natto consumption linked to longevity
