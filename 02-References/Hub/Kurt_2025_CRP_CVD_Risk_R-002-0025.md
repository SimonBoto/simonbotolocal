## Key Findings

### Main Result
**Elevated CRP predicts cardiovascular events even in people without known CVD.**

### hsCRP as Predictor
- High-sensitivity CRP (hsCRP) measures low-grade inflammation
- Predicts heart attack and cardiovascular problems
- Independent of other known risk factors
- Relevant even in apparently healthy individuals

### Chronic Inflammation Drivers
1. **Ongoing damage:** UPF, smoking, excess alcohol
2. **Healing deficiencies:** Vitamin, mineral, omega-3, protein deficiencies

### Inflammation Consequences
When inflammation persists, it increases risk of:
- Cardiovascular disease
- Insulin resistance
- Autoimmune diseases
- Diabetes
- Cancer

### Clinical Implication
**Resolving chronic inflammation is central to preventing and managing chronic diseases.**
