## Key Findings

### Fructose and Uric Acid
- **High fructose intake significantly increases hyperuricemia risk**
- Mechanism: Fructose metabolism bypasses phosphofructokinase regulation
- Rapid ATP depletion → AMP → uric acid production

### Other Dietary Factors
| Factor | Effect on Uric Acid |
|--------|---------------------|
| **Fructose** | ↑↑ Strong increase |
| **Alcohol** | ↑ Increase |
| **Meat/Seafood** | ↑ Moderate increase |
| **Coffee** | ↓ Decrease |
| **Dairy** | ↓ Decrease |

### Clinical Implications
- **Reduce fructose:** Avoid high-fructose corn syrup, sugary drinks
- **Limit alcohol:** Especially beer and spirits
- **Moderate protein:** Meat and seafood in moderation
- **Protective foods:** Coffee and dairy products
