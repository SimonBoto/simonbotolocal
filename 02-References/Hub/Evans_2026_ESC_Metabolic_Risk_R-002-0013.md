## Key Findings

### Metabolic Risk Factors (Beyond Cholesterol)
The ESC acknowledges metabolic factors as central to cardiovascular disease:
- **Obesity** — Direct vascular and cardiac effects
- **Insulin resistance** — Atherosclerosis driver
- **Type 2 diabetes** — Independent CVD risk
- **Elevated triglycerides** — Metabolic dyslipidemia
- **Low HDL** — Reverse cholesterol transport impairment
- **Chronic inflammation** — Endothelial dysfunction
- **Oxidative stress** — Vascular damage

### Critical Context
- ESC statement published March 6, 2026
- Represents shift from "cholesterol-centric" to "metabolic health" narrative
- Coincides with rise of GLP-1 agonists (Ozempic, Mounjaro, Wegovy)
- Questions raised about research following market vs. data

### Clinical Implications
- Cardiovascular disease is multifactorial (always was)
- Pharmaceutical approaches without lifestyle intervention insufficient
- Root causes (diet, micronutrient deficiencies, sedentary behavior) must be addressed
