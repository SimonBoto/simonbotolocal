## Key Findings
- **Independent confirmation of higher vitamin D requirements**
- **6,201 IU/day** required to achieve serum 25(OH)D >30 ng/mL
- Validates Veugelers & Ekwaru findings
- Supports physiological vitamin D production of 15,000-20,000 IU/day from sun exposure
- Current recommendations insufficient for optimal health outcomes
