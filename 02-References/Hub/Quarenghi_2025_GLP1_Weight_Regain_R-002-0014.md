## Key Findings

### Primary Finding
Weight regain occurs rapidly after discontinuation of GLP-1 receptor agonists (liraglutide, semaglutide) or tirzepatide, regardless of treatment duration.

### Clinical Implications
- **Permanent use required** for weight maintenance
- **Rapid rebound** — Lost weight returns quickly after stopping medication
- **No lasting metabolic reset** — Drugs manage symptoms, don't cure underlying dysfunction
- **Contrast with lifestyle interventions** — Metabolic improvements from diet/exercise are sustainable

### Context
- Review of randomized studies
- Published May 2025
- Critical for informed consent in obesity treatment
