## Study Design

### Intervention Groups
| Group | Carbohydrate Intake |
|-------|---------------------|
| Moderate restriction | ~100g/day |
| Very low-carb (ketogenic) | ~20-50g/day |
| Control | Standard diet |

### Duration
- **Acute phase:** First 2 weeks (withdrawal symptoms peak)
- **Adaptation phase:** Weeks 3-4 (metabolic shift)

## Key Findings

### Carbohydrate Withdrawal Symptoms (Week 1-2)
- **Fatigue and lethargy**
- **Headaches**
- **Irritability and mood changes**
- **Cravings for sugar/carbs**
- **Difficulty concentrating** ("brain fog")
- **Sleep disturbances**

### Timeline of Adaptation
| Phase | Duration | Experience |
|-------|----------|------------|
| **Withdrawal** | Days 1-7 | Symptoms peak |
| **Transition** | Days 7-14 | Symptoms decrease |
| **Keto-adaptation** | Week 3+ | Energy stabilizes, ketones rise |
| **Full adaptation** | Week 4+ | Mental clarity, stable energy |

### Moderate vs. Severe Restriction
- **Moderate restriction:** Fewer withdrawal symptoms
- **Severe restriction:** More symptoms but faster ketosis
- **Both groups:** Symptoms resolve by week 3

## Clinical Implications
- **Expect withdrawal:** Patients need preparation
- **Timeline matters:** Symptoms are temporary (1-2 weeks)
- **Support strategies:** Hydration, electrolytes, gradual reduction
- **Keto-flu is real:** But transient and manageable
