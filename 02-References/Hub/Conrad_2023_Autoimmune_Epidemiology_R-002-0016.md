## Key Findings

### Genetic vs. Environmental
- **Genetic predisposition:** ~30% of autoimmune risk (twin studies)
- **Environmental factors:** ~70% of risk — modifiable

### Key Modifiable Factors
- Diet quality
- Micronutrient deficiencies (Vitamin D, selenium, zinc, magnesium)
- Gut microbiome dysbiosis
- Lifestyle factors

### Vitamin D Evidence
- Mendelian randomization: Vitamin D deficiency causally linked to MS and SLE risk
- VITAL trial: Vitamin D3 supplementation reduced autoimmune disease incidence

### Clinical Improvement Data
- Tsoukalas et al. BMJ Nutrition 2021: Targeted metabolic intervention improved 95.7% of autoimmune cases
