# Khan et al., 2017, Sci Adv

## Full Citation

Khan MN, et al. (2017). "A loss-of-function mutation in SERPINE1 extends human healthspan and lifespan." *Science Advances*, Volume(Issue), Pages.

## Abstract

> Identification of a SERPINE1 (PAI-1) mutation in the Amish community associated with reduced PAI-1 activity, longer telomeres, lower fasting insulin, diabetes protection, and extended lifespan.

## Key Findings
- Amish mutation (50% PAI-1 function) → longer telomeres
- Amish mutation → lower fasting insulin, diabetes protection
- Amish mutation → +10 years lifespan
