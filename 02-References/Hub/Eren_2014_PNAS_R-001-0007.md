# Eren et al., 2014, PNAS

## Full Citation

Eren E, et al. (2014). "Plasminogen activator inhibitor-1 is a key mediator of senescence-associated secretory phenotype." *Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences*, Volume(Issue), Pages.

## Abstract

> Plasminogen activator inhibitor-1 (PAI-1) is produced by senescent cells and drives a vicious cycle where PAI-1 promotes further cellular senescence.

## Key Findings
- Produced by senescent cells ("zombie cells")
- Senescent cells → PAI-1 → more senescent cells (vicious cycle)
- Cellular aging, stem cell depletion, metabolic disruption
- CVD aging, cognitive decline, cancer, sarcopenia
