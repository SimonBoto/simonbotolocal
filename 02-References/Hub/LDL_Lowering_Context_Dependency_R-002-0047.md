# LDL Lowering Context-Dependency

## Key Studies

### IMPROVE-IT (2015)
- **Finding:** Ezetimibe + statin reduced CVD events
- **Implication:** LDL lowering itself matters, not just statins
- **But:** Benefit concentrated in high-risk patients

### PCSK9 Inhibitor Trials (2017-2020)
- **FOURIER, ODYSSEY:** Very low LDL achieved
- **Finding:** CVD reduction, but residual risk remains
- **Implication:** LDL is not the only factor

### CANTOS (2017)
- **Finding:** Anti-inflammatory (canakinumab) reduced CVD
- **Implication:** Inflammation independent of LDL
- **Key:** Inflammation matters even with low LDL

---

## When LDL Lowering Protects

| High Benefit | Context |
|--------------|---------|
| **Established CVD** | Prior MI, stroke |
| **High CAC score** | >100 Agatston |
| **Familial hypercholesterolemia** | Genetic high LDL |
| **Diabetes + multiple risks** | High absolute risk |

## When LDL Lowering Less Effective

| Lower Benefit | Context |
|---------------|---------|
| **Low CAC (zero)** | No plaque burden |
| **Metabolic syndrome** | Insulin resistance |
| **High inflammation** | CRP elevated |
| **Primary prevention, low risk** | Healthy, young |

## Clinical Framework

### Risk Stratification
| Risk Level | LDL Target | Additional Focus |
|------------|------------|------------------|
| **Very high** | <55 mg/dL | Inflammation, metabolic |
| **High** | <70 mg/dL | Comprehensive |
| **Moderate** | <100 mg/dL | Lifestyle first |
| **Low** | <130 mg/dL | Lifestyle only |

### Beyond LDL
| Marker | Target |
|--------|--------|
| **Triglycerides** | <150 mg/dL |
| **HDL** | >40 (M), >50 (F) |
| **Non-HDL** | <130 mg/dL |
| **ApoB** | <80-100 mg/dL |
| **hsCRP** | <1 mg/L |

## Quality Assessment
- **Evidence base:** Multiple large RCTs
- **Consistency:** Benefit varies by context
- **Clinical utility:** Risk stratification essential
- **Limitation:** Residual risk despite low LDL
