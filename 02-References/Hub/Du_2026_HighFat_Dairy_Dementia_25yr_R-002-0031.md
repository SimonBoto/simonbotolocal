## Study Design

### Cohort
- **Malmö Diet and Cancer Study**
- **Follow-up:** 25 years
- **Participants:** Swedish adults
- **Cases:** Incident dementia (all-cause, Alzheimer's, vascular)

## Key Findings

### High-Fat Dairy and Dementia
| Dairy Type | Association |
|------------|-------------|
| **High-fat cheese** | **Reduced all-cause dementia risk** |
| **High-fat dairy overall** | Protective association |
| **Low-fat dairy** | Neutral or slightly increased |

### Mechanism (Hypothesized)
- **Saturated fat + cholesterol** may support brain structure
- **Ketone bodies** from fat metabolism as brain fuel
- **Fat-soluble vitamins** (A, D, E, K) in high-fat dairy
- Contrast with: low-fat often has added sugars

### APOE4 Interaction
- Effects may vary by genetic risk (APOE4 status)
- High-fat dairy protection stronger in certain genetic subgroups

## Clinical Implications
- **Don't fear high-fat cheese** for cognitive health
- **Whole-fat dairy** may be neuroprotective
- **Quality matters:** Fermented, aged cheeses
- **Context:** Part of overall dietary pattern
