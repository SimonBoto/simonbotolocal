## Key Findings
- **Five product categories responsible for 31% of all global deaths annually:**
  - Fossil fuels: 8.1 million deaths
  - Tobacco: 7.2 million deaths
  - Ultra-processed foods: 2.3 million deaths
  - Industrial chemicals: 1.8 million deaths
  - Alcohol: 1.8 million deaths
- Chronic diseases now cause 74% of deaths globally (90% in developed countries)
- Same corporate practices repeat across industries (tobacco, sugar, food, chemical, pharmaceutical, fossil fuel)
- Evidence hidden for decades while regulations delayed
- 80% of premature deaths preventable through lifestyle interventions
