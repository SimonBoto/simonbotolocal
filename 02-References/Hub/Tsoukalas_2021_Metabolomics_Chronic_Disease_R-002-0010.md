## Key Findings
- **Clinical study of autoimmune and chronic disease patients**
- Metabolomics-based identification of deficiencies and metabolic disturbances
- Targeted interventions: diet, lifestyle, micronutrient correction
- **Results:**
  - 95.7% showed improvement (vs. ~40% with medication alone)
  - Energy levels: 31.2% → 89.6% with adequate energy
  - GI function: 27.8% → 87.6% with good function
  - Pain/chronic inflammation: 40% → 79.9% pain-free
  - Stress management: 69.1% difficulty → 23.9%
  - Physical activity: 27.4% → 81.9% satisfactory levels
