## Key Findings

### Main Result
Daily multivitamin supplementation for 2 years **slowed biological aging by ~4 months** compared to placebo in older adults.

### Method: Epigenetic Clocks
- Used **5 epigenetic clocks** (DNA methylation-based aging biomarkers)
- DNA methylation = "bookmarks" on DNA that change predictably with age
- Measures biological age vs. chronological age

### Enhanced Effect
- **Stronger in those with accelerated aging** (biological age > chronological age)
- Suggests multivitamins most beneficial when nutrient gaps exist

### Clinical Context
- First large-scale RCT examining dietary supplements on epigenetic clocks
- Supports micronutrient repletion for healthy aging
- Quote from Sesso: "Not just to live longer, but to live better"
