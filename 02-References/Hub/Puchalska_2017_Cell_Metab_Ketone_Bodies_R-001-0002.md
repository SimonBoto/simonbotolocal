## Key Findings
- Ketone bodies serve as **alternative fuel** when glucose metabolism is impaired
- β-hydroxybutyrate (BHB) is not just a fuel but also a **signaling molecule**
- Ketones bypass Complex I dysfunction — critical for mitochondrial disease
- Ketone metabolism generates more ATP per oxygen molecule than glucose
- Anti-inflammatory and epigenetic effects of BHB
