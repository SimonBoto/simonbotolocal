## Key Findings
- **25-year CARDIA study (n=3,088 adults)**
- Long-term aspartame and saccharin intake associated with:
  - Greater visceral adipose tissue (around organs)
  - Greater intermuscular adipose tissue (between muscles)
  - Greater subcutaneous adipose tissue (under skin)
- Effects independent of total caloric intake or diet quality
- Higher artificial sweetener consumption linked to increased obesity risk
- Current public health recommendations to replace sugar with artificial sweeteners may need re-evaluation
