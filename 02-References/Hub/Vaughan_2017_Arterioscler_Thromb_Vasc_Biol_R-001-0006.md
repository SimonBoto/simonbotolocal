# Vaughan et al., 2017, Arterioscler Thromb Vasc Biol

## Full Citation

Vaughan DE, et al. (2017). "Plasminogen activator inhibitor-1: a key regulator of vascular aging." *Arteriosclerosis, Thrombosis, and Vascular Biology*, Volume(Issue), Pages.

## Abstract

> Plasminogen activator inhibitor-1 (PAI-1) is a key regulator of vascular aging, acting not just as a marker but as a causal factor in age-related cardiovascular dysfunction.

## Key Findings
- PAI-1 increases → harder to clear clots/plaques
- PAI-1 accelerates aging (not just marker, causal)
- Causes chronic inflammation, arterial stiffness, fibrosis, ↓NO
- Spreads aging systemically
- Elevated levels accelerate aging + CVD risk
- "Evolutionary leftover" — helps reach reproductive age, harms later
