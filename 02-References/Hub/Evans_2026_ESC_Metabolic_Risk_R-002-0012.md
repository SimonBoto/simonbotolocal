## Key Findings
- **European Society of Cardiology position statement (March 6, 2026)**
- **Paradigm shift:** From cholesterol-centric to metabolic health focus

### New ESC Position
- Cardiovascular disease results from **deeper metabolic dysfunction**
- Primary causes identified:
  - Obesity
  - Insulin resistance
  - Type 2 diabetes
  - Chronic inflammation
  - Elevated triglycerides
  - Low HDL
  - Oxidative stress
- **Even with "ideal" cholesterol, CVD risk remains** if metabolic dysfunction present

### Critical Context
- Statement coincides with rise of GLP-1 drugs (Ozempic, Mounjaro, Wegovy)
- GLP-1s became top-selling drug class Q4 2025
- Raises questions: Science following data or market?
- Previous decades: Statins were most profitable drugs

### The Unaddressed Question
- If problem is metabolic, why focus on drugs vs. root causes?
- GLP-1s require lifelong use; weight regains after stopping
- Do not address: diet, lifestyle, micronutrient deficiencies
