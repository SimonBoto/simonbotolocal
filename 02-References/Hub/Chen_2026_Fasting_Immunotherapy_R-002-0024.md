## Key Findings

### Main Result
**16-hour fasting before immunotherapy enhances T-cell cytotoxicity and improves tumor response.**

### Mechanism
- Fasting induces metabolic trade-offs in tumor cells
- Increases intratumoral **isoleucine**
- Isoleucine fuels CD8+ T-cell cytotoxicity via acetyl-CoA-driven epigenetic and lipid reprogramming

### Asparagine Restriction
- Asn restriction suppresses early T-cell activation
- Engages **NRF2-dependent stress response**
- NRF2 rewires metabolism: reduces glucose/glutamine consumption, increases nucleotides for proliferation

### Clinical Application
- **NOT a cancer cure** — enhances immunotherapy effectiveness
- Fasting-mimicking diet (FMD): 2-4 days, low protein/carb, plant-based, olive oil, nuts
- Safe and effective adjunct to standard cancer therapy
