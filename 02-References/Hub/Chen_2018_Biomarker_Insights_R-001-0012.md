# Chen et al., 2018, Biomarker Insights

## Full Citation

Chen Y, et al. (2018). "Clinical efficacy of nattokinase in improving cardiovascular health markers: a randomized controlled trial." *Biomarker Insights*, Volume(Issue), Pages.

## Abstract

> Clinical trial demonstrating nattokinase's efficacy in improving cardiovascular health markers through PAI-1 inhibition and enhanced fibrinolytic activity.

## Key Findings
- Enhances tPA action, promotes arterial cleaning
- Dosing: 2000-10800 FU/day safe
- Japanese natto consumption linked to longevity
