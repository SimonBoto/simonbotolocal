## Key Findings

### Main Result
**11 million deaths annually (1 in 5 globally) are associated with poor diet** — making diet the #1 risk factor for death worldwide.

### Dietary Risk Ranking
| Rank | Dietary Risk Factor | Deaths Attributable |
|------|---------------------|---------------------|
| **1** | High sodium | 3 million |
| **2** | Low whole grains | 3 million |
| **3** | Low fruits | 2 million |
| **4** | Low nuts/seeds | 2 million |
| **5** | Low vegetables | 1 million |
| **6** | Low omega-3 | 1 million |
| **7** | High processed meat | 500,000 |
| **8** | Low fiber | 500,000 |

### What Kills vs What We Focus On
| Actual Problem | Public Focus |
|----------------|--------------|
| **Not enough healthy foods** | Too many "bad" foods |
| **Sodium excess** | Sugar (important but not #1) |
| **Whole grain deficiency** | Low-fat vs high-fat |
| **Fruit/vegetable deficiency** | Calories only |

### Global Pattern
| Region | Diet-Related Deaths |
|--------|---------------------|
| **Worldwide** | 11 million/year |
| **Proportion** | 22% of all deaths |
| **Leading cause** | Cardiovascular disease |

## Clinical Significance

### The Diet Paradox
- **We obsess over:** Calories, fat, sugar
- **Actually kills us:** Sodium, low whole grains, low fruits
- **Solution:** Add healthy foods, not just remove "bad" ones

### Actionable Priorities
| Priority | Action |
|----------|--------|
| **1** | Reduce sodium (processed foods) |
| **2** | Increase whole grains |
| **3** | Eat more fruits |
| **4** | Add nuts/seeds |
| **5** | Eat more vegetables |

### Message to Patients
"**What you don't eat** may be killing you more than what you do eat."
