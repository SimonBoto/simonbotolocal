# Hong Kong: Highest Meat Consumption, Longest Life Expectancy — Population Data

## Citation
Health & Protection. "Hong Kong has world's longest life expectancy." February 2025. AHDB. Asia: Meat consumption data. Our World in Data: Life Expectancy — Meat supply per person.

## Source
- **Primary sources:** Health & Protection 2025, AHDB, Our World in Data
- **Citation extracted from:** Facebook Posts Collection
- **Type:** Population health data / epidemiological observation
- **Status:** Citation from source

## Key Findings
- Hong Kong has highest life expectancy globally
- Hong Kong has highest meat consumption per capita globally (~145 kg annually)
- High meat intake does not appear to reduce lifespan
- Coexistence of high meat consumption and longest life expectancy
- Contributing factors: high fish/seafood consumption, low smoking rates, efficient healthcare, lifestyle factors

## Clinical Relevance
Population-level data challenges assumptions that meat consumption necessarily reduces lifespan. Context (meat quality, preparation, overall diet pattern) matters more than meat quantity alone.
