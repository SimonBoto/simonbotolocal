## Key Findings

### Main Result
**Highest vs. lowest HOMA-IR: 111% greater CVD mortality risk, 34% greater all-cause mortality.**

### Insulin vs. Glucose
- **HOMA-IR better biomarker** than insulin or glucose alone
- Incorporates both fasting insulin and fasting glucose
- More predictive of metabolic dysfunction

### Non-Diabetic Population
- Risk elevated even in people without diabetes
- Insulin resistance precedes hyperglycemia
- Early marker of metabolic disease

### Clinical Implication
- Fasting insulin + HOMA-IR should be routine screening
- HbA1c and fasting glucose miss early insulin resistance
- Insulin-centric approach catches disease earlier
