## Key Findings
- **Swedish study (n=2,157 adults)**
- APOE4 carriers (25% of population) have 2.5× higher Alzheimer's risk
- **High unprocessed meat consumption completely eliminated APOE4 dementia risk**
- Cognitive functions improved to levels similar to non-APOE4 carriers
- **Unprocessed meat protective; processed meat harmful** (independent of genetics)
- Demonstrates gene-diet interaction in neurodegeneration
