## Key Findings

### 16-Hour Fasting Protocol
- Brief 16-hour fasting enhances immunotherapy efficacy
- Tested in both mice and humans
- Alters tumor-cell nutrient preferences
- Creates metabolic window for immune attack

### Mechanism
- NRF2 activation in CD8+ T cells
- Rewires metabolic program
- Reduces glucose/glutamine consumption
- Increases intracellular nucleotides
- Promotes T-cell proliferation

### Clinical Significance
- Enhances anti-tumor immune response
- Synergizes with checkpoint inhibitors
- Clinically relevant and feasible
- Improves immunotherapy outcomes
