# Pittas 2023 — Vitamin D and Risk for Type 2 Diabetes (D2d Trial)

## Citation
Pittas AG, Dawson-Hughes B, Sheehan P, et al. Vitamin D and Risk for Type 2 Diabetes in People With Prediabetes: The D2d Randomized Clinical Trial. Ann Intern Med. 2023;176(3):355-363. doi:10.7326/M22-3018

## Key Findings
- **D2d Trial:** Randomized clinical trial of vitamin D for diabetes prevention
- **Participants:** People with prediabetes
- **Result:** Vitamin D supplementation did not significantly reduce diabetes risk vs placebo
- **Note:** Controversial findings; debate about dosing and baseline vitamin D levels
