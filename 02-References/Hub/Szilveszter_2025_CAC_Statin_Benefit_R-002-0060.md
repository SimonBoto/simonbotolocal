# Szilveszter 2025 — Calcium Score Stratifies Statin Benefit

## Citation
Szilveszter B, et al. Calcium score stratifies statin benefit. JACC Cardiovasc Imaging. 2025.

## Source
- **Primary source:** JACC Cardiovascular Imaging
- **Citation extracted from:** Facebook Posts Collection
- **Status:** Citation from source; full text pending

## Key Findings
- CAC score 0: No statin benefit — avoid overtreatment
- CAC score 1-100: No benefit from statins
- CAC score 101-399: Benefit not statistically significant
- CAC score >400: Clear benefit from statins
- Moderate/extensive plaque (>400): clear benefit
- Artery stenosis ≥50%: benefit regardless of CAC
- High-risk plaque (vulnerable/unstable): benefit regardless of CAC
- Multi-vessel disease (>4 vessels): benefit regardless of CAC

## Clinical Relevance
CAC scoring helps avoid overtreatment in people without atherosclerotic plaque and target treatment to those with significant established disease.
