# Insulin Resistance 14x CAD Risk — Metabolic Cardiology Literature

## Citation
Multiple studies demonstrating insulin resistance as major CAD risk factor:
- Various metabolic cardiology studies (2010-2025)
- Nature Reviews Cardiology 2025 series on endothelial metabolism

## Source
- **Primary sources:** Various cardiology and metabolism journals
- **Citation extracted from:** Facebook Posts Collection
- **Type:** Literature synthesis
- **Status:** Citation from source

## Key Findings
- Insulin resistance: up to 14× greater CAD risk vs cholesterol alone
- Metabolic factors dominate cardiovascular risk
- Endothelial cell metabolism critical for vascular health
- Silent progression: T2D, hypertension, obesity create CVD ground before symptoms
- Energy production, nutrient adequacy, insulin action, inflammation management central to heart health

## Clinical Relevance
Metabolic health is more important than cholesterol for cardiovascular disease prevention.
