## Key Findings
- **Identified statistical error in 2011 IOM vitamin D recommendations**
- IOM used average requirement data instead of 97.5th percentile data
- Correct calculation shows **8,895 IU/day needed** to maintain 20 ng/mL in 97.5% of population
- This is **~15× higher** than the IOM's recommended 600 IU/day
- Original IOM recommendation leaves large portion of population deficient
- Demonstrates how statistical methodology errors propagate into public health guidelines
