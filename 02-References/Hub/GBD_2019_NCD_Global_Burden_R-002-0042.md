## Key Findings

### Global Mortality (2019)
| Cause Category | % of All Deaths |
|----------------|-----------------|
| **Non-communicable diseases (NCDs)** | **74.36%** |
| Communicable diseases | ~20% |
| Injuries | ~6% |

### NCD Breakdown
| Disease Category | % of NCD Deaths |
|------------------|-----------------|
| **Cardiovascular diseases** | ~45% |
| **Cancers** | ~22% |
| **Chronic respiratory** | ~10% |
| **Diabetes** | ~5% |
| Other NCDs | ~18% |

### Regional Variation
| Region | NCD Deaths % |
|--------|--------------|
| **High-income countries** | ~90% |
| **Upper-middle-income** | ~80% |
| **Lower-middle-income** | ~65% |
| **Low-income** | ~50% (but rising) |

### Epidemiological Transition
- **1900s:** Infectious diseases dominant
- **2000s:** NCDs dominant globally
- **2050 projection:** NCDs will increase further

## Clinical Significance

### Why This Matters
1. **Preventable burden:** Most NCDs are preventable through lifestyle
2. **Metabolic health:** Central to preventing CVD, diabetes, some cancers
3. **Global priority:** WHO Sustainable Development Goal 3.4 targets NCD reduction
4. **Individual action:** Diet, exercise, sleep modulate risk

### Key Risk Factors (GBD)
| Risk Factor | Attributable NCD Burden |
|-------------|------------------------|
| **High blood pressure** | #1 |
| **Smoking** | #2 |
| **High blood sugar** | #3 |
| **Obesity** | #4 |
| **Poor diet** | #5 |
