# Lampe et al., 2016, Food Chem Toxicol

## Full Citation

Lampe BJ, et al. (2016). "Safety assessment of nattokinase: acute and subchronic toxicity studies in rodents." *Food and Chemical Toxicology*, Volume(Issue), Pages.

## Abstract

> Comprehensive safety assessment of nattokinase showing no adverse effects at doses up to 10,800 FU/day in preclinical models.

## Key Findings
- Dosing: 2000-10800 FU/day safe
- Contraindications: surgery (1-2 weeks prior), soy allergy, bleeding disorders/anticoagulants
- No adverse effects observed in safety studies
