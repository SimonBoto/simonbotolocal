# Steffen 2023 - Artificial Sweeteners and Body Fat

## Citation
Steffen BT, Jacobs DR, Yi D, et al. "Long-term aspartame and saccharin intakes are related to greater volumes of visceral, intermuscular, and subcutaneous adipose tissue: the CARDIA study." *Int J Obes (Lond)*. 2023;47(10):889-897.

## DOI
[10.1038/s41366-023-01336-y](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41366-023-01336-y)

## PMID
37443272

## Key Findings
- Long-term artificial sweetener consumption associated with increased visceral, intermuscular, and subcutaneous fat
- Association independent of calorie intake, diet quality, and BMI
- 25-year follow-up in CARDIA cohort
