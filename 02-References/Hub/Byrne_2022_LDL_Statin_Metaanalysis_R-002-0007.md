## Key Findings
- **Systematic review and meta-analysis of statin trials**
- LDL reduction magnitude **does not predict** cardiovascular risk reduction
- Patients with small LDL reductions sometimes showed significant benefit
- Patients with large LDL reductions sometimes showed limited benefit
- Statins work through inflammation reduction and plaque stabilization, not just LDL lowering
- Better outcomes in patients with established atherosclerosis
- Prevention in low-risk patients shows limited benefit regardless of LDL levels
