## Key Findings
- **Population-based cohort of 22 million UK individuals**
- Rising incidence of autoimmune disorders
- Cannot be explained by genetics alone
- Supports 30% genetic / 70% environmental contribution
- Socioeconomic and lifestyle factors play major roles
- Co-occurrence patterns suggest shared mechanisms
