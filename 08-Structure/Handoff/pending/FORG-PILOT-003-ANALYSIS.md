## 2. All Claims Numbered

1. PAI-1 increases → harder to clear clots/plaques
2. PAI-1 accelerates aging (not just marker, causal)
3. Produced by senescent cells ("zombie cells")
4. Causes chronic inflammation, arterial stiffness, fibrosis, ↓NO
5. Spreads aging systemically (Vaughan 2017)
6. Normal levels help wound healing (youth)
7. Elevated levels accelerate aging + CVD risk
8. "Evolutionary leftover" — helps reach reproductive age, harms later
9. Senescent cells → PAI-1 → more senescent cells (vicious cycle, Eren 2014)
10. Cellular aging, stem cell depletion, metabolic disruption
11. CVD aging, cognitive decline, cancer, sarcopenia (Jiang 2025)
12. Amish mutation (50% PAI-1 function) → longer telomeres, lower fasting insulin, diabetes protection, +10 years lifespan (Khan 2017)
13. Nattokinase (from fermented soy) cleaves/inactivates PAI-1 directly (Urano 2001)
14. Enhances tPA action, promotes arterial cleaning (Chen 2018)
15. Japanese natto consumption linked to longevity
16. Dosing: 2000-10800 FU/day safe (Lampe 2016, Chen 2018)
17. Contraindications: surgery (1-2 weeks prior), soy allergy, bleeding disorders/anticoagulants
