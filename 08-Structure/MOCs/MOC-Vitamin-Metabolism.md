# MOC — Vitamin Metabolism

> B-vitamins and cofactors in metabolic pathways

---

## B-Vitamins in Metabolism

### Energy Metabolism
- [[Z-001-0479] Vitamin Dependence in Organic Acid Metabolism
- B1 (Thiamine): PDH, α-KGDH, transketolase
- B2 (Riboflavin): FAD synthesis
- B3 (Niacin): NAD⁺ synthesis
- B5 (Pantothenate): CoA synthesis

### Carboxylation Reactions
- B7 (Biotin): Pyruvate carboxylase, propionyl-CoA carboxylase
- B12 (Cobalamin): Methylmalonyl-CoA mutase, methionine synthase
