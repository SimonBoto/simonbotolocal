## Central Molecules

## Pathways
1. **Amino acid catabolism** → Ammonia release
2. **Transamination** → Amino group transfer
3. **Glutamine synthesis** → Safe ammonia transport
4. **Urea cycle** → Nitrogen disposal

## Tissue-Specific Handling
| Tissue | Primary Role |
|--------|--------------|
| **Liver** | Urea synthesis (CPS I) |
| **Kidney** | Ammonia excretion, acid-base |
| **Muscle** | Glutamine synthesis (nitrogen export) |
| **Brain** | Glutamine synthesis (detoxification) |
| **Intestine** | Glutamine utilization (fuel) |
