## Emanuel's Seven Requirements

From [[Emanuel_Seven_Requirements_Ethical_Research_Z-101-0478]]:

1. **Social/Clinical Value** — Research must produce valuable knowledge
2. **Scientific Validity** — Sound methodology required
3. **Fair Subject Selection** — Equitable recruitment
4. **Favorable Risk-Benefit Ratio** — Minimize harm, maximize benefit
5. **Independent Review** — IRB/ethics committee oversight
6. **Informed Consent** — Respect for persons
7. **Respect for Enrolled Subjects** — Ongoing protection
