## Therapeutic Hierarchy

### First Line (Foundation)
- Vitamin D3 (with K2)
- Magnesium (glycinate or citrate)
- B-complex (activated forms)
- Zinc (if deficient)

### Second Line (Specific)
- Methylfolate + methylcobalamin (methylation)
- Additional K2 (vascular protection)
- Iron (if deficient)
- Selenium (thyroid, antioxidant)

### Third Line (Targeted)
- Specific amino acids (neurotransmitter support)
- Trace minerals (individualized)
- Probiotics (gut restoration)

---

## Key Clinical Principles

1. **Cofactor dependence** — Every enzyme needs specific vitamins/minerals
2. **D-K2 partnership** — D absorbs Ca, K2 directs it to bone
3. **Mg is central** — Required for D activation, PTH function, ATP
4. **Methylation matters** — B12 + folate for neurological and cardiovascular health
5. **Gut-brain axis** — Microbiome affects neurotransmitter synthesis
6. **OAT visibility** — Organic acids reveal micronutrient status in real-time
