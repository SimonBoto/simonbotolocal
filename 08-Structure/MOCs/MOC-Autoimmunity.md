# MOC — Autoimmunity

## Overview

Autoimmune diseases represent a loss of immune tolerance leading to attack on self-tissues. This MOC organizes knowledge on mechanisms, clinical manifestations, metabolic drivers, and therapeutic approaches.

## Disease-Specific Coverage

### Endocrine Autoimmunity
- Type 1 Diabetes — Islet autoimmunity
- Hashimoto Thyroiditis — Thyroid destruction
- Graves Disease — Stimulating autoantibodies
- Addison Disease — Adrenal autoimmunity

### Rheumatic Diseases
- Rheumatoid Arthritis — Synovial inflammation
- Systemic Lupus Erythematosus — Multi-system autoimmunity
- Spondyloarthropathies — Axial skeleton
- Sjögren Syndrome — Exocrine glands

### Neurological Autoimmunity
- Multiple Sclerosis — CNS demyelination
- Myasthenia Gravis — Neuromuscular junction
- Guillain-Barré — Peripheral nerves
- Autoimmune Encephalitis — CNS inflammation

### Gastrointestinal Autoimmunity
- Celiac Disease — Gluten-triggered
- Inflammatory Bowel Disease — Crohn's, UC
- Autoimmune Hepatitis — Liver inflammation
- Pernicious Anemia — Gastric parietal cells

### Other Autoimmune Conditions
- Psoriasis — Skin inflammation
- Vitiligo — Melanocyte destruction
- Autoimmune Hemolytic Anemia — Red blood cells
- Idiopathic Thrombocytopenic Purpura — Platelets

## Therapeutic Approaches

### Conventional Immunosuppression
- Corticosteroids — Broad anti-inflammatory
- DMARDs — Disease-modifying agents
- Biologics — TNF-α, IL-6, IL-17, IL-23 blockade
- JAK Inhibitors — Intracellular signaling

### Metabolic Interventions
- [[Vitamin_D_Immunomodulation_Mechanisms_Z-001-1163]] — High-dose vitamin D
- Ketogenic Diet — Metabolic therapy
- Fasting/Time-Restricted Eating — Autophagy induction
- Omega-3 Fatty Acids — Anti-inflammatory lipids

### Emerging Therapies
- Tolerance Induction — Peptide immunotherapy
- Microbiome Modulation — FMT, probiotics
- Stem Cell Therapy — HSCT for severe disease
- CAR-Tregs — Engineered regulatory cells
