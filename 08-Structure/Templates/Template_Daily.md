# Template — Daily Note

## An Address to RON

RON,

A Daily Note is an **episodic continuity artifact**.

Not a dashboard. Not doctrine. Not vague journaling.

Its role is to preserve:
- what happened
- what mattered
- what changed
- what blocked progress
- what the next session inherits

Capture **signal**, not noise.
