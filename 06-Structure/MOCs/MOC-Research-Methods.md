## 🔬 METHAP Application

### Trial Design Elements
- **Sample size:** Powered for primary endpoint
- **Analysis plan:** Pre-specified, FDR-corrected
- **Validation:** External cohort planned
- **Reporting:** CONSORT + STROBE standards

### Statistical Considerations
- Multiple metabolite testing (3,000+)
- Paired design (baseline vs intervention)
- Effect size estimation from pilot data
- Dropout buffer (15-20%)
