# UPF Health Risks (Summary)

From [[Ultra-processed-food-exposure-BMJ-2024]] (BMJ 2024 umbrella review, 45 metas, 10M+ people):

## Top Risks (Class I-II Evidence)
- **Mortality**: +21-50% (all-cause, CVD, heart disease)
- **Type 2 Diabetes**: +12-40% (dose-response strongest)
- **Obesity**: OR 1.55
- **Mental Health**: Anxiety (OR 1.48), Depression (HR 1.22), Mental disorders (OR 1.53)
- **Respiratory**: Wheezing (RR 1.40), Sleep issues (OR 1.41)

## 32 Total Outcomes (Suggestive/Weak)
Cancer, GI (IBD), metabolic syndrome, asthma, etc.

## UPF Definition (Nova)
Industrial formulations: snacks, soft drinks, noodles, ready-meals. High additives, low whole foods.
