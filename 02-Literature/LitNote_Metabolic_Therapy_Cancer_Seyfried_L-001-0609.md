## The Seyfried Framework

| Cancer Cell | Normal Cell |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Mitochondrial defect** | Healthy mitochondria |
| **Cannot use ketones** | Can use ketones |
| **Dependent on glucose** | Metabolically flexible |
| **Target for starvation** | Protected by metabolic flexibility |

## Clinical Applications

### GBM (Glioblastoma) Protocol
- **Ketogenic diet:** <20g carbs/day
- **Glucose-ketone index (GKI):** Target 1-4
- **Hyperbaric oxygen:** Combined with ketosis
- **Results:** Case reports of prolonged survival

### Implementation Strategy
| Phase | Intervention | Duration |
|-------|--------------|----------|
| **Induction** | Strict ketogenic diet | 2-4 weeks |
| **Maintenance** | Moderate ketosis | Ongoing |
| **Press** | Chronic metabolic stress | Continuous |
| **Pulse** | Drug/radiation bursts | Intermittent |

## Evidence Status
