## Week-by-Week Syllabus

| Week | Phase | Focus | Deliverable |
|------|-------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Foundation | Biochemical literacy reboot | Interactive diagrams, micro-quizzes, thought puzzles |
| 2 | Foundation | Pathway megastructure | Pathway Atlas, 5 failure patterns, oral exam |
| 3 | OAT Mastery | Pattern recognition | Category-based marker interpretation |
| 4 | OAT Mastery | Case-based immersion | 10 curated cases, biochemical inference |
| 5 | Lipidomics | Physiology + interpretation | Ratio gospel, membrane repair |
| 6 | Lipidomics | Clinical decision-making | Fatty acid patterns reveal diet truths |
| 7 | Integration | Three integration algorithms | Energy-first, detox/methylation, membrane |
| 8 | Integration | Clinical practice drills | Daily cases, treatment plans, communication |

## Competency Targets (Week 8)
By graduation, trainees must:
- [ ] Interpret OAT without drowning
- [ ] Read fatty acids like an x-ray
- [ ] Build personalized, mechanistic regimens
- [ ] Understand patient's metabolic story
- [ ] Speak patient-friendly language
- [ ] Fit into clinic workflow without chaos
