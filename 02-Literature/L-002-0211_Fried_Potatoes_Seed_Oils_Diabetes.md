# Fried Potatoes in Seed Oils Increase Diabetes Risk 20%

## Source
- **Original:** Τηγανητές Πατάτες σε Σπορέλαιο Αυξάνουν τον Κίνδυνο Διαβήτη κατά 20%

## Key Findings

### Risk Increase
| Exposure | Risk |
|----------|------|
| **Fried potatoes in seed oils** | +20% diabetes |
| **Mechanism** | Oxidized omega-6 |

### Better Alternatives
| Option | Rationale |
|--------|-----------|
| **Olive oil** | Stable |
| **Animal fats** | Heat-stable |
| **Air frying** | No added oil |
