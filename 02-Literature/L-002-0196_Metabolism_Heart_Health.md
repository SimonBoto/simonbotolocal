# Metabolism & Heart Health

## Source
- **Original:** Μεταβολισμός & Υγεία της Καρδιάς
- **English:** "Metabolism & Heart Health"
- **Primary:** Pasut A, et al. Nature Reviews Cardiology 2025

## Key Findings

### Beyond Cholesterol
- Cholesterol is only one part
- NOT primary determinant for most

### Metabolic Factors Dominance
- Insulin resistance: **14× greater CAD risk** vs isolated cholesterol
- Metabolic processes central to CVD

### Conditions Increasing Risk
- Type 2 diabetes, hypertension, obesity, kidney dysfunction
