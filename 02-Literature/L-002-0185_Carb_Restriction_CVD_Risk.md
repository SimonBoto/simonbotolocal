# Carbohydrate Restriction Reduces Cardiovascular Risk

## Source
- **Original:** Ο Περιορισμός των Υδατανθράκων Μειώνει τον Καρδιαγγειακό Κίνδυνο
- **English:** "Carbohydrate Restriction Reduces Cardiovascular Risk"

## Key Findings

### Metabolic Benefits
| Marker | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Triglycerides** | ↓ Significant reduction |
| **HDL cholesterol** | ↑ Increase |
| **LDL particle size** | ↑ Shift to larger, less atherogenic |
| **Insulin resistance** | ↓ Improvement |
| **Inflammation markers** | ↓ Reduction |

### Comparison to Low-Fat
| Outcome | Low-Carb vs Low-Fat |
|---------|---------------------|
| **Weight loss** | Greater with low-carb |
| **Glycemic control** | Superior |
| **Adherence** | Better long-term |
| **Metabolic syndrome** | More effective reversal |

## Mechanisms

### Lipid Effects
| Mechanism | Result |
|-----------|--------|
| **Reduced VLDL production** | Lower triglycerides |
| **Increased HDL synthesis** | Higher HDL |
| **Improved LDL quality** | Pattern A (large, buoyant) |

### Metabolic Effects
| Mechanism | Result |
|-----------|--------|
| **Reduced insulin** | Less lipogenesis |
| **Increased fat oxidation** | Weight loss |
| **Decreased inflammation** | Lower CRP |

## Clinical Context

### Who Benefits Most
| Population | Benefit |
|------------|---------|
| **Metabolic syndrome** | High |
| **Insulin resistant** | High |
| **Type 2 diabetes** | High |
| **Elevated triglycerides** | High |
