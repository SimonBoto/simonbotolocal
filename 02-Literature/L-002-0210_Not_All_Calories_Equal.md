# Not All Calories Are Equal

## Source
- **Original:** Γιατί Δεν Μετράνε Όλες οι Θερμίδες το Ίδιο
- **English:** "Why Not All Calories Count the Same"

## Key Findings

### Metabolic Effect
| Macronutrient | Energy Expenditure |
|---------------|-------------------|
| **Carbohydrates** | Lower TEF |
| **Fats** | Moderate TEF |
| **Protein** | Higher TEF |

### Low-Carb Effect
| Finding | Result |
|---------|--------|
| **Reduced carbs** | Higher energy expenditure |
| **Same calories** | Different metabolic effect |
