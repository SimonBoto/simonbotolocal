# Lowering Cholesterol Does Not Always Mean Better Health

## Source
- **Original:** Η Μείωση της Χοληστερίνης Δεν Σημαίνει Πάντα Καλύτερη υγεία
- **English:** "Lowering Cholesterol Does Not Always Mean Better Health"
- **Primary:** Szilveszter B, et al. JACC Cardiovasc Imaging. 2025.

## Calcium Score Stratification

### CAC Score Interpretation
| Score | Plaque Burden | Statin Benefit |
|-------|---------------|----------------|
| **0** | None | No benefit |
| **1-10** | Minimal | No benefit |
| **11-100** | Mild | Minimal |
| **101-400** | Moderate | Some benefit |
| **>400** | Extensive | Clear benefit |

## JACC Study Findings

### 11,000 Patients, 8 Years
| Finding | Implication |
|---------|-------------|
| **Statin benefit only with CVD or high CAC** | Overtreatment common |
| **No benefit when CAC = 0** | Don't treat zero plaque |
| **No benefit when CAC <100** | Low-risk overtreatment |

## Clinical Implication

### Precision Approach
| Strategy | Rationale |
|----------|-----------|
| **CAC screening** | Personalize statin therapy |
| **Avoid treatment with CAC = 0** | No plaque, no benefit |
| **Focus on other risk factors** | When CAC low |
