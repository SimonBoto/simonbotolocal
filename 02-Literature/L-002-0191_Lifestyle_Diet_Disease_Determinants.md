# Lifestyle and Diet as Primary Disease Determinants

## Source
- **Original:** Οι Πιο Καθοριστικοί Παράγοντες Νόσου Οφείλονται στον Τρόπο Ζωής και Στη Διατροφή
- **English:** "The Most Determinative Disease Factors Are Due to Lifestyle and Diet"
- **Primary:** Global Effect of Cardiovascular Risk Factors on Lifetime Estimates. *NEJM*. March 2025.

## NEJM Study (2 Million People, 39 Countries)

### Five Modifiable Risk Factors
1. High blood pressure
2. Cholesterol
3. Body weight (very low or high)
4. Diabetes
5. Smoking

### Impact on Lifespan
| Profile | Women | Men |
|---------|-------|-----|
| **None present** | +14.5 years | +11.8 years |
| **Healthy life years** | +13.3 years | +10.6 years |

### Hierarchy of Impact
| Rank | Factor | Benefit |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Smoking cessation | Greatest |
| 2 | Blood pressure control | Second greatest |
| 3 | Weight/diabetes | Significant |
| 5 | Cholesterol | Minimal independent |

## Reversibility
All factors modifiable through:
- Diet changes
- Lifestyle interventions
- Micronutrient repletion
