# Inflammation Increases CVD Risk (hsCRP)

## Source
- **Original:** Η φλεγμονή στο σώμα αυξάνει τον κίνδυνο για καρδιακά επεισόδια και θάνατο
- **English:** "Inflammation Increases Risk for Heart Attacks and Death"
- **Primary:** Kurt B, et al. European Heart Journal 2025

## Key Findings

### CRP as Predictor
- High CRP linked to greater heart attack probability
- Predicts risk even without known CVD

### Chronic Inflammation Causes
1. Ongoing damage: UPF, smoking, excess alcohol
2. Deficiencies: Vitamins, minerals, omega-3s

### Consequences
- CVD, insulin resistance, autoimmune disease, diabetes, cancer
