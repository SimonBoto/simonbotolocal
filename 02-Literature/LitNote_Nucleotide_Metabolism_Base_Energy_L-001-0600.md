## The Nucleotide Hierarchy

```
Adenine (base) + Ribose (sugar) → Adenosine (nucleoside)
                                     ↓
                              Phosphorylation
                                     ↓
            AMP → ADP → ATP (nucleotides - energy & genetic material)
```

## Evolutionary Significance
- Adenine presence in DNA/RNA is **fundamental and conserved** across all life
- Suggests presence early in evolution of life
- Central to **RNA world hypothesis**: life may have initially been RNA-based
