# Calcium Score Stratifies Statin Benefit — JACC 2025

## Source
- **Original:** Σκορ Ασβεστίου – Calcium Score
- **English:** "Calcium Score Stratifies Statin Benefit"
- **Study:** JACC Cardiovascular Imaging 2025

## Statin Benefit by CAC Score

### Clear Benefit When:
| Condition | CAC Score |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Moderate/extensive plaque** | >400 |
| **Artery stenosis ≥50%** | Any |
| **High-risk plaque** (vulnerable/unstable) | Any |
| **Multi-vessel disease** (>4 vessels) | Any |

### No Benefit When:
| Condition | CAC Score |
|-----------|-----------|
| **No plaque** | 0 |
| **Minimal plaque** | <100 |

### Non-Significant Benefit:
| Condition | CAC Score |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Mild plaque** | <399 |

## Clinical Application

| CAC Score | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| **0** | Avoid statins — no benefit |
| **1-100** | No benefit from statins |
| **101-399** | Benefit not statistically significant |
| **>400** | Clear benefit from statins |

## Key Value of CAC

| Purpose | Application |
|---------|-------------|
| **Avoid overtreatment** | In people without atherosclerotic plaque |
| **Target treatment** | To those with significant established disease |

## Evidence

> "The calcium score can help avoid overtreatment in individuals without atherosclerotic plaque and target treatment to those with significant established disease."
> — JACC Cardiovasc Imaging 2025
