# Nintedanib (Ofev) — Tyrosine Kinase Inhibitor Mechanism and Clinical Use

## Drug Overview

**Generic:** Nintedanib
**Brand:** Ofev
**Class:** Multi-targeted tyrosine kinase inhibitor (TKI)

## Indications

- **Idiopathic Pulmonary Fibrosis (IPF)**
- **Systemic sclerosis-associated interstitial lung disease (SSc-ILD)**
- **Other progressive fibrosing interstitial lung diseases**
- Oncology: Advanced NSCLC (EU, with docetaxel)

## Mechanism of Action

### Target Receptor Tyrosine Kinases

**1. FGFR 1-3 (Fibroblast Growth Factor Receptors)**
- Role: Fibroblast proliferation, migration, myofibroblast differentiation
- Effect: Reduced fibrogenesis

**2. VEGFR 1-3 (Vascular Endothelial Growth Factor Receptors)**
- Role: Angiogenesis, vascular permeability
- Effect: Reduced vascular remodeling

**3. PDGFR α/β (Platelet-Derived Growth Factor Receptors)**
- Role: Fibroblast recruitment, proliferation, collagen deposition
- Effect: Reduced ECM accumulation

### Downstream Effects

**Signaling Blockade:**
- Autophosphorylation inhibition
- MAPK pathway inhibition
- PI3K-Akt pathway inhibition
- STAT pathway inhibition

**Cellular Outcomes:**
- ↓ Fibroblast activity
- ↓ ECM deposition
- ↓ Pathological angiogenesis
- Slower fibrosis progression (not reversal)

## Metabolism

**Primary Pathway:**
- Esterases (hydrolytic cleavage)
- Forms active metabolite BIBF 1202

**Secondary Pathways:**
- Glucuronidation: UGT1A1, UGT1A7, UGT1A9, UGT2B7
- Minor CYP3A4 involvement

**Elimination:**
- Fecal: ~93%
- Renal: Minimal

## Clinical Outcomes

**IPF:**
- ~50% reduction in annual FVC decline
- Slows progression
- No mortality benefit demonstrated

**SSc-ILD:**
- Improves progression-free survival

## Safety

**Common:**
- Diarrhea (kinase inhibition in gut)
- Nausea, vomiting

**Serious:**
- Hepatotoxicity (monitor ALT/AST)
- Bleeding risk (VEGFR effect)
- Cardiovascular events (rare)

## Cancer History

**Not Contraindicated:**
- History of bladder cancer acceptable
- Not immunosuppressive
- Anti-angiogenic (theoretically beneficial)
- Monitor usual parameters
