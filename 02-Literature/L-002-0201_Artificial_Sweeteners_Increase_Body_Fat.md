# Artificial Sweeteners Increase Body Fat

## Source
- **Original:** Τεχνητά Γλυκαντικά και Αναψυκτικά Διαίτης Αυξάνουν το Σωματικό Λίπος
- **English:** "Artificial Sweeteners and Diet Soda Increase Body Fat"
- **Study:** CARDIA 25-year study, 3,088 adults

## Key Findings

### Fat Accumulation
| Type | Association |
|------|-------------|
| **Visceral fat** | Increased |
| **Intramuscular fat** | Increased |
| **Subcutaneous fat** | Increased |

### Independent of Calories
| Factor | Result |
|--------|--------|
| **Calorie intake** | Not explanatory |
| **Diet quality** | Not explanatory |
| **Sweetener use** | Direct association |
