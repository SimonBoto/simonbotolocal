# Fasting Enhances Immunotherapy Effectiveness in Cancer

## Source

- **Primary:** Chen S, et al. Cell Metabolism 2026

## 16-Hour Fasting Protocol
- **16-hour fasting** before immunotherapy enhances T-cell function
- Increases cancer cell destruction capability
- Synergistic with immunotherapy drugs

## Fasting-Mimicking Diet (FMD)
- Low-protein, low-carb plant-based
- 2-4 day cycles alongside treatment
- Mimics fasting without complete abstinence
