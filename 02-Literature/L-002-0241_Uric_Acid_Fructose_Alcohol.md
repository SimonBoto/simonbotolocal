# High Uric Acid: Reduce Fructose, Alcohol & Processed Foods

## Source
- **Original:** Αυξημένο Ουρικό Οξύ: Μειώστε την Πρόσληψη Φρουκτόζης, Αλκοόλ & Επεξεργασμένων Τροφών
- **English:** "High Uric Acid: Reduce Fructose, Alcohol & Processed Foods"

## The Common Misconception

| What People Avoid | Actual Impact |
|-------------------|---------------|
| Red meat | Minor contribution |
| Cheese | Minor contribution |
| Sardines | Minor contribution |
| Seafood | Minor contribution |
| Game meat | Minor contribution |

## What is Uric Acid?

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Production** | Breakdown and recycling of purines |
| **Purines** | Cell components |
| **High cell density foods** | More purines (sardines, liver, red meat, game, meat broths, brewer's yeast) |

## The Real Sources of Purines

| Source | Contribution |
|--------|--------------|
| **Endogenous (body cell recycling)** | **~70%** |
| **Dietary purines** | ~30% |

**Conclusion:** Diet contributes only a small portion to uric acid levels.

## The Real Culprits

| Factor | Mechanism |
|--------|-----------|
| **Alcohol** | Increases uric acid production, reduces excretion |
| **Fructose** | Rapidly raises uric acid via purine degradation |
| **Insulin resistance** | Reduces uric acid excretion |

## High-Fructose Sources to Avoid

| Food/Drink | Problem |
|------------|---------|
| **Sugary sodas** | High fructose content |
| **Fruit juices with added sugar** | Concentrated fructose |
| **High-fructose corn syrup** | Industrial fructose source |
| **High-carb diets with processed sugars** | Metabolic stress |

## Clinical Approach

| Old Approach | Evidence-Based Approach |
|--------------|------------------------|
| Avoid all purine-rich foods | Focus on metabolic factors |
| Restrict meat heavily | Address fructose and alcohol |
| Generic low-purine diet | Target insulin resistance |

## Key Insight

> "Consumption of these foods [meat, sardines, etc.] contributes only to a small degree to increased uric acid. Approximately 70% of purines come from recycling the body's own cells, not from diet."

## The Metabolic Connection

| Condition | Effect on Uric Acid |
|-----------|---------------------|
| **Insulin resistance** | ↓ Reduced excretion |
| **Metabolic syndrome** | ↑ Increased production |
| **Fructose metabolism** | ↑ Rapid uric acid generation |

## Practical Recommendations

| Priority | Action |
|----------|--------|
| 1 | Eliminate sugary drinks |
| 2 | Reduce alcohol intake |
| 3 | Address insulin resistance |
| 4 | Limit processed foods |
| 5 | Moderate (don't eliminate) purine-rich foods |
