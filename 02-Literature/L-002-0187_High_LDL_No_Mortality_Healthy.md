# High LDL Not Associated with Mortality in People Without CVD

## Source
- **Original:** Τα Υψηλά Επίπεδα LDL ("κακής") Χοληστερίνης Δεν Συνδέονται με Αυξημένο Κίνδυνο Θανάτου σε Άτομα Χωρίς Καρδιαγγειακή Νόσο
- **English:** "High LDL ('Bad') Cholesterol Not Associated with Increased Death Risk in People Without CVD"

## The LDL Paradox

### Key Finding
| Population | LDL-Mortality Association |
|------------|---------------------------|
| **Without CVD** | No association |
| **With CVD** | Association present |
| **Implication** | LDL less predictive in healthy |

### Context
| Factor | Importance |
|--------|------------|
| **LDL alone** | Insufficient risk predictor |
| **Metabolic health** | More important |
| **Inflammation** | More predictive |
| **Insulin resistance** | Key driver |

## Clinical Implications

### Statin Reconsideration
| Scenario | Rationale |
|----------|-----------|
| **Primary prevention** | Question universal statins |
| **LDL-centric risk** | Insufficient alone |
| **Metabolic assessment** | Prioritize |
