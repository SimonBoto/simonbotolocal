# Hashimoto's: Low-Carb Diet Reduces Autoantibodies and Thyroid Inflammation

## Source
- **Original:** Hashimoto: Διατροφή με Χαμηλούς Υδατάνθρακες Μειώνει τα Αυτοαντισώματα & τη Φλεγμονή στο Θυρεοειδή
- **English:** "Hashimoto's: Low-Carb Diet Reduces Autoantibodies and Thyroid Inflammation"
- **Primary:** Huang XS, et al. MRI quantitative assessment of thyroid inflammation in Hashimoto's thyroiditis.

## Key Findings

### Clinical Effects
| Marker | Change |
|--------|--------|
| **Thyroid autoantibodies** | ↓ Reduction |
| **Thyroid inflammation (MRI)** | ↓ Decrease |
| **TSH** | May improve |
| **Thyroid function** | Preservation |

### Mechanisms
| Pathway | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Reduced insulin resistance** | Linked to autoimmune activity |
| **Decreased systemic inflammation** | Lower cytokines |
| **Improved gut barrier** | Reduced antigen exposure |
| **Reduced oxidative stress** | Less tissue damage |

## Clinical Significance

### Therapeutic Potential
| Application | Evidence |
|-------------|----------|
| **First-line dietary intervention** | Supported |
| **Medication reduction** | Possible in early stages |
| **Thyroid preservation** | Function maintained |
