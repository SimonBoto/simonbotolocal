## OAT Marker Categories

| Category | Key Markers | Clinical Focus |
|----------|-------------|----------------|
| **Glycolysis** | Lactate, pyruvate | Energy metabolism |
| **TCA cycle** | Citrate, succinate, α-KG | Mitochondrial function |
| **Fatty acid oxidation** | Suberate, adipate | β-oxidation capacity |
| **B-vitamin dependent** | Methylmalonate, FIGLU | Cofactor status |
| **Mitochondrial** | Lactate/pyruvate ratio | Redox status |
| **Gut-derived** | Hippurate, indican | Microbiome health |
| **Oxidative stress** | 8-OHdG | Free radical burden |

## Clinical Decision Framework
1. **Scan by category** — Systematic review
2. **Identify patterns** — Markers cluster by dysfunction
3. **Prioritize severity** — Life-threatening vs. optimization
4. **Map to intervention** — Specific markers → specific treatments
5. **Integrate with clinical picture** — Labs + symptoms + history
