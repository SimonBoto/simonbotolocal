## TL;DR (Actionable)
- MetS epidemic (>1B) IR/obesity/hidden hunger/microbiome/sugar → CVD/T2D/NAFLD; reversible via time-restricted eating (Wilkinson 10h 3kg loss), low-carb, micronuts (VitD/adipic GC-MS).
- Criteria: Waist>94/80cm, TG>150, HDL<40/50, BP>130/85, glucose>100.
- Rx: Metabolomics 174 markers personalize (Tsoukalas); fructose toxin (Lustig); gut diet modulate (Singh/Yang).
- PhD Hooks: Adipic IR proxy, VitD gaps, microbiome dysbiosis T2D/NAFLD chain.

## Full Extract
**Core Thesis**: MetS (IR syndrome X Reaven) abdominal obesity + dyslipidemia + hypertension + hyperglycemia → CVD death. Causes: empty calories/fructose NAFLD, hidden hunger, dysbiosis. Test metabolomics (org/fatty acids); Rx lifestyle (10h eating), supps.

**Key Findings**:
- Prevalence: 20-30% adults, 40+ >60yo.
- Criteria: Waist, TG, HDL, BP, glucose.
- Patho: IR central; fructose toxin, microbiome gut-brain.
- Rx: Time-restricted (Wilkinson Cell Metab), micronuts COVID/MetS link.
