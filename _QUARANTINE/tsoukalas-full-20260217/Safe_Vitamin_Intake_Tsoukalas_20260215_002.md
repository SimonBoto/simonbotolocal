## TL;DR (Actionable)
- RDA flawed historical averages; use metabolomics for individual dosing (VitD/Mg synergy IR).
- Fat-solubles (A/D/E/K/Q10/CoQ10) safe high-dose; no toxicity deaths from supps (P450 handles).
- Mega-dose VitD (50k IU) safe short-term; test via GC-MS biomarkers (adipic proxy?).

## Full Extract
**Core Thesis**: Safe vitamin intake debunked myths—RDA evolution (Leitch 1942) averaged populations, ignores individual metab; fat-solubles safe (no reported deaths), metabolomics Rx key.

**RDA History**: 1835-1942 standards flawed; WWI/II shortages → bans; Sherman 1939 halved needs.

**Fat-Solubles Safe**: A/D/E/K high-dose absorbed fats; P450 detox VitD (500mcg safe); no OD reports.

**Toxicity Myth**: CRN/Harvard/JAMA: 0 supp deaths vs drug ADRs; Poison Control confirms.

**Metabolomics Rx**: Personal dosing prevents empty calories (Harvard); test 174+ markers.
