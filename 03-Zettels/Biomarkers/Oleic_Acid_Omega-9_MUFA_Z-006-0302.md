# Oleic Acid — Omega-9, Primary MUFA

## 1. ETYMOLOGY

**Oleic acid:** From Latin "oleum" (oil)

**Omega-9:** First double bond at 9th carbon from methyl end

**Also called:**
- C18:1 (18 carbons, 1 double bond)
- Oleate

source-litnote: Synthesized from clinical practice
---

## 2. PHYSIOLOGY

### Biological Function

**Most abundant monounsaturated fatty acid:**
- Found in all tissues
- Major component of olive oil
- Cardioprotective

**Benefits:**
| Effect | Mechanism |
|--------|-----------|
| **Cardiovascular** | Reduces LDL, maintains HDL |
| **Anti-inflammatory** | Reduces CRP |
| **Insulin sensitivity** | Improves glucose metabolism |
| **Cell membrane** | Maintains fluidity |
