# Stearic Acid — Saturated Fat, C18:0

## 1. ETYMOLOGY

**Stearic acid:** From Greek "stear" (tallow/fat)

**Also called:**
- C18:0 (18 carbons, saturated)
- Stearate

source-litnote: Synthesized from clinical practice
---

## 2. PHYSIOLOGY

### Biological Function

**Saturated fatty acid:**
- 18 carbons, no double bonds
- Found in animal fats, cocoa butter

**Unique among saturated fats:**
- **Neutral effect on LDL cholesterol**
- Does not raise cholesterol like other saturated fats
- Converts to oleic acid in body
