# 2-Hydroxybutyric Acid — Oxidative Stress & Glutathione Synthesis Marker

## 1. ETYMOLOGY

**2-Hydroxybutyric acid:** Hydroxy-substituted butyric acid at position 2

**Also called:**
- Alpha-hydroxybutyrate
- 2-HB

source-litnote: Synthesized from clinical practice
---

## 2. PHYSIOLOGY

### Biological Function

**Metabolic intermediate:**
- Formed from 2-ketobutyrate (methionine degradation)
- Related to glutathione synthesis pathway

**Formation pathways:**
1. **Methionine degradation** → 2-ketobutyrate → 2-HB
2. **Threonine degradation** → 2-ketobutyrate → 2-HB

**Clinical significance:**
- Marker of oxidative stress
- Indicator of glutathione synthesis demand
- Related to insulin resistance

## 3. METABOLIC INTERPRETATION

### 2-HB Levels

| Level | Status | Interpretation |
|-------|--------|----------------|
| **Normal** | Low | Normal oxidative status |
| **Elevated** | High | Oxidative stress or GSH demand |

### Causes of Elevation

**Oxidative stress:**
- Increased ROS production
- Glutathione consumption
- Methionine cycle activation

**Insulin resistance:**
- Associated with elevated 2-HB
- Predictive of diabetes development

**Glutathione depletion:**
- Increased demand for synthesis
- Upregulated methionine cycle

**Liver stress:**
- Hepatic oxidative stress
- Detoxification burden

### 2-HB Metabolism

| Nutrient | Role | Deficiency Effect |
|----------|------|-------------------|
| **Methionine** | Precursor | Altered pathway flux |
| **B12** | Methionine synthase | Homocysteine elevation |
| **Folate** | Methionine regeneration | Methionine depletion |
| **B6** | Cystathionine beta-synthase | Homocysteine elevation |
| **Glutathione** | End product | Increased demand |

## 5. INTERVENTIONS

### For Elevated 2-HB

**Antioxidant support:**
- N-acetylcysteine (NAC)
- Glutathione support
- Vitamin C, E
- Alpha-lipoic acid

**Methylation support:**
- B12, folate, B6
- Methionine (if deficient)
- TMG (trimethylglycine)

**Insulin sensitivity:**
- If elevated with insulin resistance
- Weight loss
- Exercise
- Low-carb diet

| Biomarker | Relationship | Pattern |
