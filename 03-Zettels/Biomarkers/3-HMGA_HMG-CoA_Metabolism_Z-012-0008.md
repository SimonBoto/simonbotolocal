# 3-HMGA — 3-Hydroxy-3-methylglutaric Acid, HMG-CoA Metabolism

## 1. ETYMOLOGY

**3-HMGA:** 3-Hydroxy-3-methylglutaric acid

**HMG-CoA:** 3-Hydroxy-3-methylglutaryl-coenzyme A

**Named for:** Structure (hydroxy + methyl + glutaric)

source-litnote: Synthesized from clinical practice
---

## 2. PHYSIOLOGY

### Biological Function

**Critical metabolic intermediate:**
- **Ketogenesis:** HMG-CoA → acetoacetate → ketone bodies
- **Cholesterol synthesis:** HMG-CoA → mevalonate
- **Leucine metabolism:** Degradation product

**HMG-CoA reductase:**
- Rate-limiting enzyme for cholesterol synthesis
- Target of statin drugs

## 3. METABOLIC INTERPRETATION

### 3-HMGA Levels

| Level | Status | Interpretation |
|-------|--------|----------------|
| **Normal** | Low | Properly metabolized |
| **Elevated** | High | Metabolic block |

### Causes of Elevation

**Genetic (HMG-CoA lyase deficiency):**
- Rare inborn error of metabolism
- Impaired ketogenesis
- Leucine degradation defect
- Hypoglycemia, metabolic acidosis

**Secondary causes:**
- Mitochondrial dysfunction
- Ketogenic stress without adaptation
- Leucine overload

## 5. INTERVENTIONS

### For Elevated 3-HMGA

**If genetic deficiency:**
- Avoid fasting
- Low-leucine diet
- Glucose management
- Medical supervision

**If secondary:**
- Mitochondrial support
- B vitamins
- CoQ10
