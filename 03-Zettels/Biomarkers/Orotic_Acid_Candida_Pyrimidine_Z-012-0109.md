# Orotic Acid — Candida Overgrowth & Pyrimidine Marker

## 1. ETYMOLOGY

**Orotic acid:** Discovered in whey (Latin "oros" = whey)

**Also called:**
- Orotate
- Vitamin B13 (obsolete term)

source-litnote: Synthesized from clinical practice
---

## 2. PHYSIOLOGY

### Biological Function

**Pyrimidine synthesis intermediate:**
- Precursor to UMP (uridine monophosphate)
- Essential for RNA/DNA synthesis

**Two sources of elevation:**
1. **Candida overgrowth** — yeast produces orotic acid
2. **Pyrimidine synthesis defect** — rare genetic disorder

## 3. METABOLIC INTERPRETATION

### Orotic Acid Levels

| Level | Status | Interpretation |
|-------|--------|----------------|
| **Normal** | Low | No overgrowth |
| **Elevated** | High | Candida or metabolic defect |

### Causes of Elevation

**Candida overgrowth:**
| Factor | Contribution |
|--------|--------------|
| **Antibiotic use** | Disrupts flora |
| **High sugar diet** | Feeds yeast |
| **Immune suppression** | Reduced control |
| **Hormonal changes** | Pregnancy, OCP |
| **Diabetes** | High sugar environment |

**Genetic (orotic aciduria):**
- UMP synthase deficiency
- Severe elevation
- Megaloblastic anemia
- Growth retardation

## 5. INTERVENTIONS

### For Elevated Orotic Acid (Candida)

**Antifungal approach:**
- Caprylic acid: 1-2 g/day
- Oregano oil: 150-300 mg/day
- Berberine: 500-1500 mg/day
- Nystatin or fluconazole (prescription)

**Probiotic support:**
- Saccharomyces boulardii
- Lactobacillus strains
- High CFU count

**Dietary:**
- Low sugar, low refined carbs
- Avoid alcohol
- Anti-candida diet if severe

### For Genetic Orotic Aciduria

**Uridine supplementation:**
- Bypasses blocked enzyme
- Medical management
