# SHBG — Sex Hormone Binding Globulin, Hormone Transport

## 1. ETYMOLOGY

**SHBG:** Sex Hormone Binding Globulin

**Also called:**
- Sex steroid-binding globulin (SSBG)
- Testosterone-estradiol binding globulin (TeBG)

---

## 2. PHYSIOLOGY

### Biological Function

**Transport protein for sex hormones:**
- Binds testosterone and estradiol
- Produced by liver
- Regulates hormone bioavailability

**Binding affinity:**
| Hormone | Affinity |
|---------|----------|
| **Dihydrotestosterone (DHT)** | Highest |
| **Testosterone** | High |
| **Estradiol** | Moderate |
| **Estrone** | Low |

**Effect on free hormones:**
- High SHBG → less free testosterone/estradiol
- Low SHBG → more free testosterone/estradiol

---

## 3. METABOLIC INTERPRETATION

### SHBG Levels

| Level (nmol/L) | Status | Interpretation |
|----------------|--------|----------------|
| **Men: 10-50** | Normal | Typical range |
| **Women: 20-80** | Normal | Higher than men |
| **<10 (men)** | Low | Increased free hormones |
| **>50 (men)** | High | Decreased free hormones |

### Factors Affecting SHBG

**Increase SHBG:**
| Factor | Mechanism |
|--------|-----------|
| **Aging** | Natural increase |
| **Liver disease** | Impaired clearance |
| **Hyperthyroidism** | Increased production |
| **Estrogen** | Stimulates production |
| **Pregnancy** | Very high |
| **Certain medications** | Phenytoin, anticonvulsants |

**Decrease SHBG:**
| Factor | Mechanism |
|--------|-----------|
| **Obesity** | Insulin resistance |
| **Insulin resistance** | Suppresses production |
| **Hypothyroidism** | Decreased production |
| **Androgens** | Suppress production |
| **PCOS** | Insulin resistance |
| **Cushing's** | Cortisol effect |

---

## 4. COFACTORS

### SHBG Regulation

| Hormone | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Estrogen** | Increases SHBG |
| **Testosterone** | Decreases SHBG |
| **Insulin** | Decreases SHBG |
| **Thyroid** | Increases SHBG (hyper), decreases (hypo) |
| **Liver function** | Affects production/clearance |

---

## 5. INTERVENTIONS

### Based on SHBG Level

**High SHBG:**
- Address cause (liver, thyroid)
- Consider free testosterone measurement
- May need higher TRT dose

**Low SHBG:**
- Weight loss
- Improve insulin sensitivity
- Treat PCOS if present

---

## 6. CONNECTED BIOMARKERS

| Biomarker | Relationship | Pattern |
|-----------|--------------|---------|
| **Total testosterone** | Bound fraction | High SHBG → high total T, low free T |
| **Free testosterone** | Unbound fraction | Inverse relationship with SHBG |
| **Insulin** | Regulator | High insulin → low SHBG |
| **TSH** | Thyroid | Abnormal thyroid → abnormal SHBG |
| **Liver enzymes** | Production | Elevated in liver disease |

### Clinical Patterns

**Aging Male:**
- SHBG: High
- Total T: Borderline
- Free T: Low
- **Symptomatic hypogonadism**

**Obesity/Metabolic Syndrome:**
- SHBG: Low
- Total T: Low
- Free T: Variable
- **Insulin resistance**

**PCOS:**
- SHBG: Low
- Free testosterone: High
- Insulin: High
- **Hyperandrogenism**

**Liver Disease:**
- SHBG: High
- Total T: High (artifact)
- Free T: Low
- **Impaired clearance**
