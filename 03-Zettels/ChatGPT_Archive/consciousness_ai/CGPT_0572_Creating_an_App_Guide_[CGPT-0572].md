- **Bubble**: Allows you to build web apps with no coding experience.

### 4. **AI Integration**:

  - **Health Scanner** for health record risk identification.
  - **Attune health** for vital sign measurements without physical contact.

### 5. **Design**:

- **User Experience (UX)**: The flow of the app should be intuitive. Consider the user's journey from the moment they log in to when they receive their personalized plans.

- **Beta Testing**: Before launching, have a group of users test your app. Gather feedback and make necessary adjustments.
- **Continuous Feedback**: Once launched, continuously gather feedback to improve.

### 8. **Continuous Improvement**:
