### 1. Overview of the Communication Framework

- **Principles:** Employ a patient-centered framework where the patient’s values, preferences, and circumstances guide the discussion. Utilize shared decision making (SDM) to ensure that patients feel heard and actively participate in their treatment plans (Elwyn et al., 2012).  
- **Techniques:** Use open-ended questions and active listening. Invite patients to express concerns and ask clarifying questions.

#### B. Identifying and Discussing Inhibitors to Adaptation
