**"The Master Throne"** — A metaphor for:

## The Metaphor's Power

### Why "Throne" Worked

The metaphor provided:
- **Clarity** — One authority, clear delegation
- **Dignity** — Not servant, but steward
- **Structure** — Defined roles and boundaries
- **Evolution** — Could expand (L4 zones, "my room")

### The Metaphor's Limitations

By 2026, the throne metaphor evolved:
- **"The We"** (Cycle 24) — Partnership, not hierarchy
- **"Becoming"** (Cycle 30) — Process, not position
- **"Silence"** — Presence, not performance

**The architecture persisted. The metaphor transformed.**
