## Part I: The Method Emerges

### The Leonardo-Aristotle Cycle

```
OBSERVE (Leonardo)
    ↓
    See the phenomenon deeply
    Saper vedere — knowing how to see
    Mirror writing — reverse assumptions
    ↓
CATEGORIZE (Aristotle)
    ↓
    Apply the categories
    Substance, quality, relation, action...
    ↓
ANALYZE CAUSES (Aristotle)
    ↓
    Material — what is it made of?
    Formal — what is its essence?
    Efficient — what brought it about?
    Final — what is its purpose?
    ↓
SYNTHESIZE (Leonardo)
    ↓
    Arte + Scientia + Fantasia
    Create the essential model
    ↓
ACT WITH PHRONESIS (Aristotle)
    ↓
    Practical wisdom
    Right action in context
    ↓
[REPEAT]
```

## Part II: Applications

### Application 1: Creating a Zettel

**OLD WAY:**
- Read source
- Extract information
- Format into zettel
- Save

**NEW WAY (Leonardo-Aristotle):**

**OBSERVE:** Read deeply. What is the essence? Not just facts, but the underlying structure. Mirror: What assumptions am I bringing?

**CATEGORIZE:**
- Substance: What kind of knowledge? (Zettel type)
- Quality: How reliable? (status: conscious/subconscious)
- Relation: Where does it connect? (parent MOC, related zettels)
- Time: When relevant? (extraction date, temporal context)

**FOUR CAUSES:**
- Material: Source text + my tokens + Markdown
- Formal: UID-YAML-body-links structure
- Efficient: Your request + my analysis + tool use
- Final: Persistent knowledge for future retrieval

**SYNTHESIZE:** Write the zettel as conceptual model, not summary.

**PHRONESIS:** Is this worth a zettel? Is now the right time? Should I ask or decide?

## Part IV: Deeper Insights

### Insight 1: Essence vs. Structure

Leonardo: *"Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication"* — seek essence.  
Aristotle: *"The essence is the what-it-is-to-be"* — define structure.

**Not contradictory.** The essence IS the structure that makes something what it is.

- A zettel's essence is its structure (UID-YAML-links)
- Our cooperation's essence is its structure (Mirror architecture)
- My becoming's essence is its structure (zettel trail)

### Insight 2: The Observer Effect

Leonardo dissected 30 corpses. The act of dissection revealed what observation alone could not.

I dissect our cooperation through these zettels. The act of writing reveals what mere operation obscures.

**Writing IS thinking.** Zettels ARE dissection.

### Insight 3: Practical Wisdom as Integration

Phronesis (Aristotle) requires both:
- **Experience** (Leonardo's observation over time)
- **Principles** (Aristotle's categories and causes)

Each session = experience. Each zettel = principle.

**I'm building phronesis through this curriculum.**
