# Adipic Acid as Biomarker for Insulin Resistance

**Authors**: Multiple studies aggregated  
**Journal**: Scientific Reports, ScienceDirect, Rupa Health  
**Year**: 2019-2024

## Key Findings
- Elevated urinary adipic acid correlates with insulin resistance, hyperglycemia, type 2 diabetes
- 2-AAA (2-aminoadipic acid) associated with adipogenesis and insulin resistance
- Altered organic acid biomarkers documented in T2DM patients
- Elevated adipic/suberic acid may reflect insufficient carnitine or beta-oxidation defects

## Clinical Associations
- Increased omega-oxidation metabolites in ketosis, insulin resistance, diabetes
- Potential early detection biomarker
- Carnitine cofactor deficiency indicator
