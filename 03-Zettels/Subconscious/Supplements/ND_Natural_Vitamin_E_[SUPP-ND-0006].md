## Natural vs Synthetic Vitamin E
| Feature | Natural (D-alpha) | Synthetic (dl-alpha) |
|---------|-------------------|----------------------|
| Structure | Single stereoisomer | Mixture of 8 isomers |
| Biological activity | 100% | ~74% |
| Absorption | Direct tissue uptake | Less efficient |
| Retention in body | Longer | Shorter |

## The Tocopherol Family
- **Alpha-tocopherol** — primary antioxidant, most biologically active
- **Beta-tocopherol** — antioxidant, minor component
- **Gamma-tocopherol** — anti-inflammatory, traps reactive nitrogen species
- **Delta-tocopherol** — antioxidant, minor component

**Mixed tocopherols provide broader protection than alpha alone.**

## Clinical Notes
