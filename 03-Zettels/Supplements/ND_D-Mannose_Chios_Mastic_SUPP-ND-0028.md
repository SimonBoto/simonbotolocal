## Description
Urinary tract health formula with pure D-mannose and Chios mastic. D-mannose is a simple sugar naturally found in fruits like cranberries. Chios mastic has protected designation of origin with antimicrobial properties.

Take 2 capsules with water, three times daily (6 total), for up to one week or as directed by your physician. Can open capsules and dissolve in water or milk. For recurrence, follow same protocol.

## D-Mannose Mechanism

### How It Works
- **Bacterial adhesion blocker** — E. coli binds to mannose instead of bladder wall
- **Competitive inhibition** — saturates bacterial lectins
- **Flushed out** — harmless sugar excreted in urine
- **Safe** — minimal impact on blood sugar

### Why Not Just Cranberry?
- Cranberry contains D-mannose but in small amounts
- **Cranberry juice** — high sugar, acidic, variable mannose content
- **Pure D-mannose** — concentrated, effective dose
- **No acidity** — gentler on stomach

## Chios Mastic Benefits
- **Antimicrobial** — against various pathogens
- **GI protection** — supports gut barrier
- **Anti-inflammatory** — reduces tissue irritation
- **PDO status** — authentic Chios origin

## Clinical Notes
- **Acute UTI support** — 3 g daily for 3-7 days
- **Prevention** — lower doses (1-2 g) may suffice
- **Take with water** — helps flush urinary tract
- **Not antibiotic** — works preventively, not for active infection
- **See physician** — for confirmed infections, fever, blood in urine
