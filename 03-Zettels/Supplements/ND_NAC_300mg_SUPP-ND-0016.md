## NAC Mechanisms

### Glutathione Production
**NAC → Cysteine → Glutathione (GSH)**
- Cysteine is the rate-limiting amino acid for glutathione synthesis
- NAC provides bioavailable cysteine
- Essential for phase II liver detoxification

### Direct Actions
- **Antioxidant** — scavenges ROS directly
- **Mucolytic** — breaks disulfide bonds in mucus
- **Heavy metal chelation** — helps remove mercury, lead
- **Immune modulation** — supports immune cell function
