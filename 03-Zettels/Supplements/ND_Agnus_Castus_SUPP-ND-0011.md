## Agnus Castus (Vitex) Mechanisms

### Hormonal Actions
- **Dopaminergic effect** — inhibits prolactin secretion
- **Progesterone support** — indirectly supports luteal phase progesterone
- **Estrogen modulation** — binds to estrogen receptors (weak effect)
- **LH stimulation** — may increase luteinizing hormone

### Clinical Applications
- **PMS** — reduces breast tenderness, mood swings, bloating
- **Irregular cycles** — helps regulate menstrual timing
- **Luteal phase defect** — supports progesterone production
- **Hyperprolactinemia** — lowers elevated prolactin
- **Menopause** — may help transition symptoms
- **Acne (hormonal)** — reduces androgen-related breakouts

## Clinical Notes
- **Takes time** — effects typically seen after 2-3 cycles
- **Cycle regulation** — best started early in cycle (day 1-5)
- **Not for pregnancy** — discontinue if pregnant
- **Hormonal medications** — consult physician if on HRT or contraceptives
- **Dopamine interaction** — may interact with dopaminergic drugs

## Research Evidence
