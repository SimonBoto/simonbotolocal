# Agnus Castus (Natural Doctor)

**Product Code:** —

## Description
Agnus castus (chaste tree, Vitex agnus-castus) fruit extract supplement. Agnus castus is a deciduous tree native to Europe with over 2000 years of traditional use. The fruits contain terpenoids, flavonoids, and iridoids as active secondary metabolites.

## Ingredients per 2 Capsules (Daily Dose)

| Component | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| Agnus castus fruit extract (Vitex agnus-castus L.) | 300 mg |

## Usage
Take 2 capsules daily or as directed by your physician.

## Key Features
- **Traditional herb** — 2000+ years of use for women's health
- **Fruit extract** — contains active terpenoids and flavonoids
- **Moderate dose** — 300 mg daily
- **Vegan suitable** — vegetable capsules

## Agnus Castus (Vitex) Mechanisms

### Hormonal Actions
- **Dopaminergic effect** — inhibits prolactin secretion
- **Progesterone support** — indirectly supports luteal phase progesterone
- **Estrogen modulation** — binds to estrogen receptors (weak effect)
- **LH stimulation** — may increase luteinizing hormone

### Clinical Applications
- **PMS** — reduces breast tenderness, mood swings, bloating
- **Irregular cycles** — helps regulate menstrual timing
- **Luteal phase defect** — supports progesterone production
- **Hyperprolactinemia** — lowers elevated prolactin
- **Menopause** — may help transition symptoms
- **Acne (hormonal)** — reduces androgen-related breakouts

## Clinical Notes
- **Takes time** — effects typically seen after 2-3 cycles
- **Cycle regulation** — best started early in cycle (day 1-5)
- **Not for pregnancy** — discontinue if pregnant
- **Hormonal medications** — consult physician if on HRT or contraceptives
- **Dopamine interaction** — may interact with dopaminergic drugs

## Research Evidence
- **PMS** — multiple studies show symptom reduction
- **Mastalgia** — effective for cyclic breast pain
- **Infertility (luteal phase)** — may improve pregnancy rates
- **PCOS** — limited evidence, may help some cases

## Ideal For
- PMS symptoms (physical and emotional)
- Irregular menstrual cycles
- Luteal phase deficiency
- Cyclic breast pain (mastalgia)
- Transition to menopause
- Hormonal acne
