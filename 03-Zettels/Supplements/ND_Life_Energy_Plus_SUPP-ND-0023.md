## The NAD+ Story

### What is NAD+?
- **Nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide** — essential coenzyme
- **Found in all living cells**
- **Declines with age** — 50% by age 50
- **Critical for:**
  - Cellular energy (ATP production)
  - DNA repair
  - Sirtuin activation (longevity genes)
  - Circadian rhythm

### NAD+ Precursors Compared
| Precursor | Conversion Steps | Research Status |
|-----------|------------------|-----------------|
| **Nicotinamide riboside (NR)** | NR → NMN → NAD+ | Extensive human trials |
| NMN | NMN → NAD+ | Growing research |
| Nicotinamide (niacinamide) | Direct, but inhibits sirtuins | Less preferred |
| Tryptophan | 8-step pathway | Inefficient |
