# My Gut Life (Meetab)

**Product Code:** MY29  
**Price:** €55.00 (60 tablets)  
**Daily Cost:** ~€1.83/day (2 tablets/day)

## Description
Supplement based on plant extracts with Garlic, Oregano and Fennel. Contains Cinnamon and Olive. Supports gut health through antimicrobial and digestive actions.

## Ingredients per 2 Tablets (Daily Dose)

| Herb | Amount | Standardization |
|------|--------|-----------------|
| Black Garlic bulb extract | 1200 mg | 0.1% S-allylcysteine (SAC) |
| - S-allylcysteine intake | 1.2 mg | - |
| Cinnamon bark | 937.5 mg | 4:1 extract |
| Olive leaves extract | 202.5 mg | 6% oleuropein |
| - Oleuropein intake | 12.5 mg | - |
| Origanum (Oregano) leaves extract | 100 mg | 4:1 extract |
| Fennel fruits extract | 100 mg | 4:1 extract |

## Usage
Take 2 tablets daily after meal.

## Key Features
- **Black garlic** — fermented, higher antioxidant, gentler than raw
- **Cinnamon** — antimicrobial, blood sugar support
- **Olive leaf** — oleuropein antimicrobial
- **Oregano** — potent antimicrobial herb
- **Fennel** — digestive, carminative

## Herb Actions

### Black Garlic
- **Prebiotic** — feeds beneficial bacteria
- **Antimicrobial** — against pathogens
- **Antioxidant** — SAC is potent antioxidant

### Cinnamon
- **Antimicrobial** — against Candida/bacteria
- **Blood sugar** — insulin sensitivity

### Olive Leaf
- **Oleuropein** — antimicrobial, antiviral
- **Immune support**

### Oregano
- **Carvacrol/thymol** — potent antimicrobials
- **Antifungal** — especially Candida

### Fennel
- **Carminative** — relieves gas/bloating
- **Digestive support**

## Clinical Notes
- **Broad antimicrobial** — bacteria, fungi, parasites
- **Selective** — targets pathogens, preserves beneficials
- **Good for:** SIBO, Candida, dysbiosis, bloating
- **Take with food** — gentler on stomach
