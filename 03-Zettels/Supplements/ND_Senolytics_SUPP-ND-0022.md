## Senolytics Explained

### What Are Senolytics?
Compounds that selectively clear **senescent cells** ("zombie cells"):
- Old, damaged cells that stopped dividing
- Secrete inflammatory factors (SASP)
- Contribute to aging and disease
- Accumulate with age

### The Senolytic Compounds

#### Fisetin (1500 mg)
- **Most potent natural senolytic**
- **Mayo Clinic research** — extends lifespan in mice
- **Brain-permeable** — crosses blood-brain barrier
- **Anti-inflammatory** — reduces SASP factors
- **Neuroprotective** — supports cognitive aging

#### Resveratrol (1000 mg)
- **SIRT1 activator** — longevity gene activation
- **Calorie restriction mimetic**
- **Cardiovascular protection**
- **Trans-form** — more bioactive than cis-

#### Quercetin (1000 mg)
- **Senolytic synergist** — enhances fisetin
- **Anti-inflammatory**
- **Immune modulation**
- **Cardiovascular support**

#### Grape Seed OPCs (150 mg)
- **Potent antioxidants**
- **Vascular protection**
- **Collagen support**
