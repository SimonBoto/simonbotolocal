# Pure D3 (Natural Doctor)

**Product Code:** —  
**Price:** €28.00 (30 ml liquid, ~900 drops)  
**Daily Cost:** ~€0.93/day (1 drop = 2000 IU)  
**Total servings:** ~900 drops (30-month supply at 1 drop/day)

## Description
Liquid vitamin D3 supplement providing cholecalciferol, the active form of vitamin D3. Designed for rapid absorption and easy dose titration. Vitamin D deficiency is the most common nutritional deficiency even in sunny Mediterranean countries.

## Ingredients per Drop (Daily Dose)

| Nutrient | Amount | % NRV |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Vitamin D3 (cholecalciferol) | 2000 IU (50 μg) | 1000% |

**Other ingredients:** None listed — pure D3 in carrier oil

## Usage
Take 1 drop daily with water, juice, or as directed by your physician. Shake well before each use.

**Storage:** Cool, dry place. After opening, refrigerate and consume within 6 months.

## Key Features
- **Pure D3** — no additives, excipients, or fillers
- **Liquid form** — flexible dosing, easy absorption
- **High potency** — 2000 IU per drop
- **Long-lasting** — ~900 drops per bottle
- **Vegetarian suitable** — plant-derived D3

## Clinical Notes

- **Rapid absorption** — liquid form bypasses dissolution issues

- **Refrigeration required** after opening for stability

## Prescribing Logic
**When to choose this:** 25(OH)D <30 ng/mL, winter months, limited sun exposure, autoimmune risk, bone concerns
**When to choose alternative:** Need K2/Mg combo → Complete D3/K2/Mg; Maintenance only → lower dose
**Combination notes:** Always pair with magnesium. Add K2 if dose >2000 IU or calcium supplementation.
