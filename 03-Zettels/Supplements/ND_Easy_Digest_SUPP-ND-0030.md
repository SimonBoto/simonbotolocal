# Easy Digest (Natural Doctor)

**Product Code:** —  
**Price:** €36.00 (60 vegetable capsules)  
**Daily Cost:** ~€0.60-1.80/day (1-3 capsules/day)

## Description
Plant-based digestive enzyme formula supporting the digestive process. Enzymes are essential for digestion and nutrient absorption — breaking down foods into basic building blocks the body uses to create new cells, tissues, glands. Also have anti-inflammatory properties.

## Ingredients per Capsule (Per Meal Dose)

| Enzyme | Amount | Activity | Function |
|--------|--------|----------|----------|
| Protease | 200 mg | 140 casein protease U/g | Protein digestion |
| Amyloglucosidase | 100 mg | 216-264 U/g | Carbohydrate digestion |
| Amylase | 100 mg | 27×10³ U/mg | Starch breakdown |
| Lipase | 200 mg | 10⁴ U/g | Fat digestion |
| Cellulase | 66.6 mg | 3×10³ U/mg | Fiber breakdown |
| Bromelain | 4.8 mg | 2000 GDU/g | Protein digestion, anti-inflammatory |

## Usage
Take 1 capsule before each meal or as directed by your physician.

- **Plant-based** — vegan suitable

- **High activity units** — potent enzyme activity
- **Pre-meal dosing** — optimal timing

## The Digestive Enzymes

### Protease (200 mg)
- **Breaks down proteins** — into amino acids
- **Multiple sources** — fungal/bacterial proteases
- **Bromelain synergy** — additional protein digestion

### Carbohydrate Enzymes
| Enzyme | Function |
|--------|----------|
| Amylase | Breaks starch into sugars |
| Amyloglucosidase | Further breaks complex carbs |
| Cellulase | Breaks plant fiber (cellulose) |

### Lipase (200 mg)
- **Fat digestion** — breaks triglycerides
- **Essential for** — fat-soluble vitamin absorption
- **Gallbladder support** — compensates for low bile

### Bromelain (4.8 mg)
- **From pineapple** — stem and fruit
- **Protein digestion** — works in broad pH range
- **Anti-inflammatory** — systemic benefits when taken away from food

## When Digestive Enzymes Help

### Deficiency Signs
- Bloating after meals
- Gas, indigestion
- Feeling full quickly
- Undigested food in stool
- Nutrient deficiencies

### Common Causes
- Age-related decline (enzyme production drops after 40)
- Low stomach acid
- Pancreatic insufficiency
- Gallbladder removal
- Stress, rushed eating

## Clinical Notes
- **Take before meals** — enzymes need contact with food
- **Start with 1** — increase if needed
- **Can open capsule** — sprinkle on food for children
- **Anti-inflammatory use** — take away from food for systemic bromelain effects
- **Well-tolerated** — few side effects
