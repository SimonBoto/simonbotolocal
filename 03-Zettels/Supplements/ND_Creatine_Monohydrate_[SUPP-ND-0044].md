## How Creatine Works

### ATP-PCr System
**Creatine + Phosphate ↔ Phosphocreatine (PCr)**
- **ATP depletion** — occurs during intense exercise
- **PCr donates phosphate** — rapidly regenerates ATP
- **Extends performance** — delays fatigue in short bursts
- **Allows more reps** — strength and power training

### Benefits
- **Strength gains** — 5-15% increase in 1RM
- **Power output** — improved sprint performance
- **Muscle volume** — intracellular water retention
- **Recovery** — between sets, between workouts
- **Cognitive** — potential brain energy benefits
