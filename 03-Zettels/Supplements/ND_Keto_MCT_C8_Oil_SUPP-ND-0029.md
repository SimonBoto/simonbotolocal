## MCT Oil Explained

### What are MCTs?
**Medium-chain triglycerides** — fatty acids with 6-12 carbon atoms:
- **C6 (Caproic acid)** — rapidly converted to ketones, unpleasant taste
- **C8 (Caprylic acid)** — fastest ketone production, preferred
- **C10 (Capric acid)** — slower ketone production
- **C12 (Lauric acid)** — behaves more like long-chain fat

### Why C8 is Superior
| Feature | C8 (Caprylic) | C10 (Capric) | C12 (Lauric) |
|---------|---------------|--------------|--------------|
| Ketone production | Fastest | Moderate | Slow |
| Taste | Neutral | Neutral | Coconutty |
| Absorption | Rapid | Moderate | Slower |
| Best for | Quick energy | Sustained energy | Antimicrobial |
