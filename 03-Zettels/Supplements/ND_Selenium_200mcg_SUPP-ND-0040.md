## Description
Organic selenium in the form of L-selenomethionine. Selenium contributes to normal thyroid function, maintenance of normal hair and nails, and normal immune system function. L-selenomethionine is more bioavailable than inorganic selenium forms.

## Clinical Notes
- **200 mcg is therapeutic** — RDA is ~55-70 μg
- **Don't exceed 400 μg** — upper limit for safety
- **Brazil nuts alternative** — 1-2 nuts = ~200 mcg
- **Soil dependent** — content varies by region
- **Toxicity rare** — but possible at very high doses
