### Nervous System

## Who Needs B1?
- **High carbohydrate diets** — increased requirement
- **Alcohol users** — reduced absorption, increased excretion
- **Diabetes** — higher thiamine needs
- **Bariatric surgery** — malabsorption risk
- **Elderly** — reduced intake and absorption
- **Pregnancy/lactation** — increased needs

## Clinical Notes
- **Very high dose** — 100 mg vs RDA 1.1-1.2 mg
- **Water-soluble** — excess excreted
- **No toxicity** — very safe even at high doses
- **Take with food** — reduces stomach upset
- **Alcohol depletes** — avoid concurrent use

## Deficiency Signs (Beriberi)
- **Wet beriberi** — heart failure, edema
- **Dry beriberi** — neuropathy, muscle wasting
- **Wernicke-Korsakoff** — brain damage (alcoholics)
- **Fatigue, irritability** — early symptoms
