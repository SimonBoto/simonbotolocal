# My Calmag Tablets (Meetab)

**Product Code:** MY30  
**Price:** €39.00 (120 tablets)  
**Daily Cost:** ~€0.33/day (1 tablet/day)

## Description
Dietary Supplement containing Magnesium, Calcium and Vitamin D3. Supports normal bones, neurotransmission, and immune function.

## Ingredients per Tablet (Daily Dose)

| Nutrient | Amount | % NRV |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Magnesium (as citrate) | 91.8 mg | 24.5% |
| Calcium (as citrate) | 78.1 mg | 9.7% |
| Vitamin D3 | 0.05 mg (2000 IU) | 1000% |

## Usage
Take 1 tablet in the morning with a glass of water.

## Key Features
- **Best value** D3/Mg/Ca combo in Meetab line
- **120 tablets** — 4-month supply
- **High-dose Vitamin D3** (2000 IU)
- **Citrate forms** — superior absorption
- **Balanced ratio** — magnesium aids calcium uptake

## Clinical Notes
- **Economical choice** — €0.33/day
- **Same formula as powder** — choose based on preference
- **High vitamin D** — monitor levels if combining with other D supplements
- **Not a high-dose calcium** — for patients needing more, add separate calcium

## Vitamin D Synergy
Vitamin D requires magnesium as a cofactor for activation. This formula provides both.

## Clinical Indications
**Primary:** Bone health, vitamin D and magnesium deficiency, sleep support
**Secondary:** Anxiety, muscle relaxation, calcium balance
**Adjunct:** With vitamin K2 (directs calcium to bone), additional magnesium if needed

**When to choose this:** Patient needs D3 + Mg + Ca in one convenient tablet, bone health focus
