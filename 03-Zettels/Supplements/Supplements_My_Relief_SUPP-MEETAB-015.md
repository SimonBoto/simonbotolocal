# My Relief (Meetab)

**Product Code:** MY18  
**Price:** €49.00 (90 vegetable capsules)  
**Daily Cost:** ~€0.54/day (1 capsule/day)

## Description
Supplement with extracts of hibiscus (Karkadè), Alisma and blackcurrant that have draining, water retention, anti-inflammatory, anti-edema properties.

## Ingredients per Capsule (Daily Dose)

| Herb | Amount | Extract Ratio |
|------|--------|---------------|
| Karkadè (Hibiscus) flowers dry extract | 250 mg | 1:4 |
| Blackcurrant leaves dry extract | 250 mg | 1:4 |
| Water plantain (Alisma) rhizome dry extract | 250 mg | 1:10 |

## Usage
Take 1 capsule daily.

## Key Features
- **Herbal diuretic** — natural water retention support
- **Anti-inflammatory** — hibiscus and blackcurrant
- **Triple herb formula** — synergistic drainage effect
- **Vegetarian capsules**

## Herb Actions

### Hibiscus (Karkadè)
- **Diuretic** — increases urine output
- **Antihypertensive** — mild blood pressure support
- **Anti-inflammatory**

### Blackcurrant Leaves
- **Diuretic** — promotes fluid elimination
- **Anti-inflammatory**
- **Joint support** — traditional use for arthritis

### Alisma (Water Plantain)
- **Strong diuretic** — traditional Chinese medicine
- **Anti-edema** — reduces swelling
- **Urinary tract support**

## Clinical Notes
- **Natural diuretic** — milder than pharmaceuticals
- **Potassium-sparing** — unlike synthetic diuretics
- **Good for:** PMS bloating, mild hypertension, edema
- **Caution:** Monitor electrolytes with long-term use
