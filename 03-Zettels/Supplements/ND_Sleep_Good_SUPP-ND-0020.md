# Sleep Good (Natural Doctor)

**Product Code:** —  
**Price:** €34.00 (60 vegetable capsules)  
**Daily Cost:** ~€0.57/day (1 capsule/day)

## Description
Comprehensive sleep formula with melatonin, L-tryptophan, L-theanine, GABA, 5-HTP, magnesium, magnolia bark, and chamomile. Melatonin reduces the time needed to fall asleep. Contains calming magnesium and chamomile extract.
