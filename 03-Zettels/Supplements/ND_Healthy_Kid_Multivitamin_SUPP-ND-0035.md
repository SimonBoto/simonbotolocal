## Description
Children's multivitamin to replenish nutritional deficiencies from daily diet. Contains all vitamins in active forms, beta-carotene from algae, citrus bioflavonoids, vitamin D, vitamin C, B-complex, folate, iron, selenium, zinc, chromium, calcium. Mini capsules for easy swallowing.
