Clinically studied joint formula with UC-II® undenatured type II collagen, MSM, glucosamine sulfate, and niacin. UC-II® works with the immune system to reduce cytokine levels. MSM provides sulfur for connective tissue formation. Glucosamine supports cartilage lubrication and inhibits breakdown.

## UC-II® Explained

### What is UC-II®?
- **Undenatured type II collagen** — from chicken sternum
- **Immunomodulatory mechanism** — works with immune system
- **Oral tolerance** — small doses trigger beneficial immune response
- **Reduces cytokines** — decreases inflammatory markers in joints

### Clinical Evidence
- **Human trials** — significant improvement in joint comfort/mobility
- **Dose:** 40 mg daily (included in Anoson)
- **Comparison:** More effective than glucosamine + chondroitin combination
- **Mechanism:** Different from hydrolyzed collagen (works via immune modulation)

## MSM (500 mg)
- **Organic sulfur source** — essential for connective tissue
- **Anti-inflammatory** — reduces joint inflammation
- **Cartilage support** — provides sulfur for glycosaminoglycan synthesis
- **Antioxidant** — supports glutathione production

## Glucosamine Sulfate (300 mg)
- **Cartilage component** — building block of proteoglycans
- **Lubrication support** — helps maintain synovial fluid
- **Modest dose** — often combined with chondroitin (not included here)

## Clinical Notes
- **UC-II® dose optimal** — 40 mg is clinically validated dose
- **Niacin high** — 200 mg may cause flushing in some (nicotinamide form less likely)
- **Evening dosing** — may support overnight repair
- **Shellfish warning** — glucosamine from crustaceans
- **Takes time** — effects typically seen after 8-12 weeks
