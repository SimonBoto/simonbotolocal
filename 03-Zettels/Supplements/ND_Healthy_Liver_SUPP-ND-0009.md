## Description
Natural liver protection supplement containing milk thistle fruit extract, alpha-lipoic acid, and choline. Milk thistle (Silybum marianum) is a Mediterranean thistle plant with traditional use for liver and gallbladder support. The formula provides antioxidant protection and supports normal liver function.

## Milk Thistle & Silymarin

### Traditional Use (2000+ years)
- Liver complaints
- Toxin exposure
- Mushroom poisoning (emergency use)
- Alcohol-related liver damage

### Silymarin Components
- **Silybin (silibinin)** — most active component (~50-70% of silymarin)
- **Silychristin** — antioxidant
- **Silydianin** — hepatoprotective
- **Taxifolin** — antioxidant

### Mechanisms
- **Hepatocyte membrane stabilization** — protects liver cells
- **Antioxidant** — scavenges free radicals
- **Regeneration support** — stimulates protein synthesis
- **Anti-inflammatory** — reduces liver inflammation
- **Toxin blockade** — inhibits toxin entry into hepatocytes

## Alpha-Lipoic Acid Benefits
- **Universal antioxidant** — water and fat soluble
- **Glutathione regeneration** — recycles GSH
- **Heavy metal chelation** — helps detoxify
- **Mitochondrial support** — energy production
- **Insulin sensitivity** — metabolic support

## Choline Functions
- **Phosphatidylcholine synthesis** — cell membrane component
- **Lipid transport** — prevents fatty liver
- **Methylation support** — methyl donor
- **Neurotransmitter synthesis** — acetylcholine precursor

## Clinical Notes
- **Milk thistle dose** — 143 mg extract (114 mg silymarin) is moderate
- **Therapeutic silymarin doses** — often 200-400 mg/day
- **ALA at 300 mg** — good therapeutic dose
- **Choline modest** — 82.5 mg (RDA is ~425-550 mg)
- **Well-tolerated** — minimal side effects reported

## Research Evidence
Milk thistle/silymarin has been studied for:
- Alcoholic liver disease (mixed results)
- Non-alcoholic fatty liver disease (NAFLD) — some positive outcomes
- Amanita mushroom poisoning (emergency IV use)
- Drug-induced liver injury (limited evidence)

## Ideal For
- Fatty liver support (NAFLD)
- Alcohol-related liver stress
- Detoxification support
- General liver health maintenance
- Post-medication liver recovery
- Metabolic syndrome with elevated liver enzymes
