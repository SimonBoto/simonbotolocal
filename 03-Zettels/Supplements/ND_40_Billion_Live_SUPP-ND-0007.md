## Strain Categories & Benefits

### Lactobacillus (9 strains)
- **Primary habitat:** Small intestine
- **Functions:** Lactose digestion, acid production, pathogen inhibition
- **Key strains:** L. rhamnosus (immune), L. acidophilus (digestion)

### Bifidobacterium (3 strains)
- **Primary habitat:** Large intestine
- **Functions:** Fiber fermentation, short-chain fatty acid production
- **Key strains:** B. longum (anti-inflammatory), B. lactis (immune)

### Streptococcus thermophilus
- **Primary function:** Lactose digestion, gut barrier support
- **Note:** Different from pathogenic streptococci
