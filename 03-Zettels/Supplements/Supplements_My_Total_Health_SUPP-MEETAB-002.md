# My Total Health (Meetab)

**Product Code:** MY31  
**Price:** €45.00 (60 capsules)  
**Daily Cost:** ~€1.50/day (2 capsules/day)

## Description
A multivitamin supplement composed of more than 145 natural elements. Improved formulation with better component balancing. Uses two alkaline forms of Vitamin C (magnesium ascorbate, calcium ascorbate) which are more tolerable for the stomach.

## Usage
Take 2 capsules daily after meals.

## Key Differences from My Health
- **Alkaline Vitamin C**: Uses magnesium ascorbate + calcium ascorbate (gentler on stomach)
- **Lower dosages**: ~50% of My Health concentrations
- **Same amino acid profile** but compressed into 2 capsules
- **Better for sensitive stomachs**
- **Lower cost per pack** but higher cost per day

## Clinical Notes
- Good option for patients with acid sensitivity
- Lower vitamin D (500 IU vs 1000 IU)
- Comparable amino acid diversity but at reduced concentrations
