# My Probiotics (Meetab)

**Product Code:** MY18  
**Price:** €57.00 (60 vegetable capsules)  
**Daily Cost:** ~€0.95/day (1 capsule/day)

## Description
Probiotics containing 60 billion CFU per capsule with more than 35 strains. Can be stored at room temperature (refrigeration preferred after opening).

## Ingredients per Capsule (Daily Dose)

### Probiotic Strains (60 billion CFU total)
| Strain | CFU Count |

| Lb. acidophilus LMG-S27762 | 1 billion |
| **Plus 31+ additional strains** | ~56 billion |

### Other Components
- L-Glutamine: 200 mg
- Lyophilised kefir granules

## Usage
Take 1 capsule daily.

## Key Features
- **60 billion CFU** — high potency
- **35+ strains** — broad spectrum
- **Room temperature stable** — convenient for travel
- **L-Glutamine added** — supports intestinal barrier
- **Kefir base** — natural probiotic culture

## Storage
- **Unopened:** Room temperature OK
- **After opening:** Refrigerate at 2-8°C for best viability

## Clinical Notes
- **High strain diversity** — good for dysbiosis
- **Glutamine addition** — supports gut barrier repair
- **Kefir cultures** — may contain beneficial yeasts too
- **Not enteric-coated** — take with food to protect from stomach acid
