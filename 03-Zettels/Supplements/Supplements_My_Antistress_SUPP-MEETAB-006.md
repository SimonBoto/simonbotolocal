# My Antistress (Meetab)

**Product Code:** MY24  
**Price:** €63.00 (60 tablets)  
**Daily Cost:** ~€1.05/day (1 tablet/day)

## Description
Supplement that helps the body respond to stress in a balanced way avoiding hormonal peaks of adrenaline and cortisol thanks to the three key adaptogenic substances: Ashwagandha, Rhodiola, Eleutherococcus (Siberian Ginseng) and the B vitamin complex.

## Adaptogen Profile

### Rhodiola rosea (400 mg, 3% rosavins)
- **Primary action:** Energy, fatigue resistance, mental performance
- **Best for:** Burnout, fatigue, cognitive decline

### Ashwagandha (350 mg, 2.5% withanolides)
- **Primary action:** Anxiety reduction, cortisol lowering
- **Best for:** Anxiety, insomnia, stress-induced inflammation

### Eleutherococcus/Siberian Ginseng (200 mg, 0.8% eleutherosides)
- **Primary action:** Physical endurance, immune support
- **Best for:** Physical fatigue, convalescence, immune weakness
