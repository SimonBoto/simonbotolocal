- **L-tyrosine** — direct precursor for thyroid hormone synthesis

- **Methylated B12** — optimal absorption

## Thyroid Support Mechanisms

### T3/T4 Synthesis
- **L-tyrosine** + **iodine** (from diet) → T3/T4 hormones
- **Selenium** — required for deiodinase enzymes (activate T4→T3)
- **Zinc** — required for T3 receptor function

### Stress Adaptation
- **Ashwagandha** — lowers cortisol, supports HPA axis
- **Cortisol-thyroid connection** — high cortisol inhibits T4→T3 conversion

### Antioxidant Protection
- **Selenium** — protects thyroid from oxidative damage
- **Vitamin C, ALA** — antioxidant support
- **Zinc, Copper** — SOD cofactors

## Clinical Notes
- **Comprehensive formula** — covers multiple thyroid support pathways
- **Selenium at 182% NRV** — good for thyroid conversion support
- **Vitamin D3 included** — many thyroid patients are D-deficient
- **Ashwagandha dose** — 300 mg is moderate (clinical doses often 500-600 mg)
- **Tyrosine modest** — 50 mg is low (therapeutic often 500+ mg)
- **No iodine added** — assumes dietary intake or separate supplementation

## Ideal For
- Subclinical hypothyroidism support
- T4→T3 conversion optimization
- Hashimoto's adjunct (with medical supervision)
- Stress-related thyroid dysfunction
- General thyroid health maintenance
