# Acetyl L-Carnitine 500mg (Natural Doctor)

## Description
Acetyl-L-carnitine (ALC) amino acid supplement with high bioavailability and absorption. Carnitine's role is to convert fat into energy by transporting fatty acids to cell mitochondria where they're used as fuel for energy production.

## Ingredients per Capsule (Daily Dose)

|-----------|--------|

## Usage
Take 1 capsule daily with water or as directed by your physician.

## Key Features
- **Acetyl-L-carnitine form** — crosses blood-brain barrier
- **High bioavailability** — superior to L-carnitine
- **500 mg dose** — therapeutic level
- **Cognitive support** — brain energy and neuroprotection
- **Antioxidant** — neutralizes free radicals
- **Physical endurance** — sustained energy levels
