# My Q10 (Meetab)

**Product Code:** MY13

## Description
Supplement with pure Ubiquinol, the active form of Coenzyme Q10 (CoQ10). One of the most powerful antioxidants, helps produce energy at cellular level.

## Ingredients per Softgel (Daily Dose)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Ubiquinol (Kaneka Ubiquinol™) | 50 mg |

### Other Components
- Vegetable oil
- Gelatin (capsule)
- Soy lecithin (non-GMO)
- Beeswax

## Usage
Take 1 softgel daily.

## Key Features
- **Ubiquinol form** — reduced, active CoQ10 (not ubiquinone)
- **Kaneka quality** — gold standard for CoQ10
- **50 mg dose** — maintenance level
- **Softgel** — fat-soluble, better absorption

## Ubiquinol vs Ubiquinone
| Form | Status | Absorption |
|------|--------|------------|
| Ubiquinone | Oxidized | Lower, requires conversion |
| **Ubiquinol** | **Reduced (active)** | **Higher, ready to use** |

## Clinical Notes
- **Active form** — no conversion needed in body
- **Mitochondrial energy** — essential for ATP production
- **Cardiac support** — heart muscle has highest CoQ10 needs
- **Statin users** — especially important (statins deplete CoQ10)
- **50 mg modest** — therapeutic doses often 100-200 mg

## Ideal For
- Cardiovascular support
- Statin therapy patients
- Energy production
- Antioxidant protection
- Mitochondrial dysfunction
- Aging (CoQ10 declines with age)
