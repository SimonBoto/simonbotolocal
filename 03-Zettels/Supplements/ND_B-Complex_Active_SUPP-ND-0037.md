## Why Active Forms Matter

### Methylated B12 (Methylcobalamin)
- **Ready to use** — no conversion needed
- **Methylation support** — donates methyl groups
- **Nerve health** — supports myelin
- **Superior to cyanocobalamin**

### P-5-P (Pyridoxal-5-Phosphate)
- **Active B6** — 10x more bioavailable
- **Neurotransmitter synthesis** — serotonin, dopamine, GABA
- **Homocysteine metabolism** — cardiovascular protection

### L-Methylfolate (5-MTHF)
- **Active folate** — bypasses MTHFR enzyme
- **Methylation support** — critical for DNA repair
- **Pregnancy-safe** — neural tube development
