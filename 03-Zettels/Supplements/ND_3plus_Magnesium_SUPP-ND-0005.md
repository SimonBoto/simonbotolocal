# 3plus Magnesium (Natural Doctor)

## Description
Triple-form magnesium supplement providing magnesium from three highly absorbable and bioavailable forms: malate, bisglycinate, and citrate. Each form offers distinct benefits for muscle function, nervous system support, and energy metabolism.

**Form distribution:** Not specified — blended forms

## Usage
Take 3 capsules daily after food or as directed by your physician.

## The Three Magnesium Forms

### 1. Magnesium Malate
- **Best for:** Energy production, muscle pain, fibromyalgia
- **Mechanism:** Malic acid participates in Krebs cycle
- **Benefits:** Reduces muscle pain, supports ATP production
- **Absorption:** Well-tolerated, gentle

### 2. Magnesium Bisglycinate (Glycinate)
- **Best for:** Sleep, anxiety, nervous system
- **Mechanism:** Glycine is calming neurotransmitter
- **Benefits:** Relaxation, stress reduction, no laxative effect
- **Absorption:** Very high, gentle on stomach

### 3. Magnesium Citrate
- **Best for:** Constipation, general supplementation
- **Mechanism:** Citrate has mild osmotic effect
- **Benefits:** Bowel regularity, kidney stone prevention
- **Absorption:** Good, slightly laxative at higher doses

## Key Features
- **Three forms in one** — comprehensive magnesium coverage
- **High absorption** — chelated and organic forms only
- **No magnesium oxide** — avoids poor absorption and diarrhea
- **Muscle & nerve support** — addresses multiple physiological needs
- **Energy metabolism** — malate supports ATP production

## Clinical Notes
- **Modest total dose** — 127 mg is 34% NRV (many adults need 300-400 mg)
- **Quality forms** — all three are premium, well-absorbed forms
- **Gentle on GI** — bisglycinate and malate are non-laxative
- **May need additional** — for severe deficiency, consider higher dose
