# My Postbiotics (Meetab)

**Product Code:** MY33  
**Price:** €49.00 (60 vegetable capsules)  
**Daily Cost:** ~€1.63/day (2 capsules/day)

## Description
Contains butyric acid (butyrate), metabiotics and ellagic acid. Designed to improve gut health, reduce inflammation, regulate immune system, correct insulin resistance, protect heart and brain, and reduce oxidative stress.

## Ingredients per 2 Capsules (Daily Dose)

| Component | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| Sodium butyrate | 600 mg |
| Pomegranate dry extract | 200 mg |
| Postbiotic ferment mix* | 200 mg |

*Mixture of non-viable cultures, their components, metabolites and catabolites:
- Lb. acidophilus LMG S-27762
- Lb. casei LMG S-27763
- Lb. reuteri LMG S-27759
- Lb. plantarum LMG S-29299
- Bb. bifidum LMG S-27761

## Usage
Take 2 capsules per day after meals.

## Key Features
- **Butyric acid** — primary fuel for colon cells
- **Postbiotics** — metabolites (faster than probiotics)
- **Ellagic acid** — from pomegranate, antioxidant
- **Immunomodulatory** — T-reg support for autoimmunity

## Butyric Acid Benefits
- **Intestinal barrier** — tight junctions, reduces leaky gut
- **Anti-inflammatory** — inhibits IL-6, TNF-a
- **Colon cancer** — induces apoptosis in cancer cells
- **Insulin sensitivity** — improves glucose control
- **Satiety** — improves PYY and leptin
- **Triglycerides** — reduces levels
- **T-regs** — immunomodulatory (autoimmunity)

## Metabiotics Benefits
- **Faster than probiotics** — no need to colonize
- **Strengthen gut barrier**
- **Reduce inflammatory cytokines**
- **Support beneficial bacteria growth**
- **Improve glucose regulation**
- **Body weight control**
- **Reduce anxiety/stress**
- **Skin health** (wrinkles, acne)

## Ellagic Acid Benefits
- **Antioxidant** — neutralizes free radicals
- **DNA protection**
- **Mitochondrial function**
- **Anti-aging**
- **Cognitive function**

## Clinical Notes
- **Postbiotics = next-gen** — more reliable than live probiotics
- **Butyrate critical** — many patients have low butyrate producers
- **Autoimmune** — T-reg support particularly valuable
- **Insulin resistance** — multiple mechanisms
- **Store refrigerated** after opening (2-8°C)
