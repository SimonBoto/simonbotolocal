Nicotinamide (vitamin B3) supplement contributing to normal nervous system function and psychological function. Nicotinamide is a water-soluble vitamin participating in over 200 bodily functions related to carbohydrate, fat, and protein metabolism for cellular energy production.

## Functions of Vitamin B3

### Energy Metabolism
- **NAD+ precursor** — nicotinamide → NAD+
- **Cellular respiration** — ATP production
- **Mitochondrial function** — energy factory support

### Nervous System
- **Neurotransmitter synthesis** — serotonin, dopamine
- **Nerve signaling** — maintains membrane potential
- **Cognitive function** — supports brain energy

### Other Benefits
- **Skin health** — maintains barrier function
- **DNA repair** — NAD+ required for PARP enzymes
- **Circadian rhythm** — NAD+ affects clock genes
