# Neurobalance (Natural Doctor)

**Product Code:** —

**Daily Cost:** ~€0.65-1.30/day (1-2 capsules/day)

## Description
Neurotransmitter support formula with L-glutamine, niacin, L-tyrosine, 5-HTP, vitamin C, B6, zinc, and iodine. Contains ingredients that contribute to normal nervous system function and normal psychological function. Specifically includes vitamin C, niacin, B6, and iodine for nervous system; vitamin C, niacin, and B6 for psychological function; zinc for cognitive function.

Take 1-2 capsules once daily after food or as directed by your physician.

## Key Features
- **Dual neurotransmitter support** — dopamine (tyrosine) + serotonin (5-HTP)
- **High-dose niacin** — 125 mg (781% NRV) for mood/energy
- **P-5-P B6** — active form for neurotransmitter synthesis
- **Glutamine** — 250 mg for calming, GABA support
- **Balanced approach** — addresses multiple neurotransmitter pathways

## The Neurotransmitter Balance

### Serotonin Pathway
**5-HTP → Serotonin → Melatonin**
- Mood regulation
- Sleep quality
- Appetite control

### Dopamine/Norepinephrine Pathway
**Tyrosine → L-DOPA → Dopamine → Norepinephrine**
- Motivation, focus
- Energy, alertness
- Reward processing

### Calming Support
| Ingredient | Mechanism |
|------------|-----------|
| Glutamine | GABA precursor, calming |
| Niacin | Flushing form can be calming |
| B6 | Cofactor for GABA synthesis |

## Clinical Notes
- **5-HTP modest** — 10 mg is low (therapeutic often 50-300 mg)
- **Tyrosine modest** — 50 mg is low (therapeutic often 500-2000 mg)
- **Niacin high** — 125 mg may cause flushing in some
- **Glutamine good** — 250 mg is reasonable
- **Take after food** — reduces nausea, improves absorption

## Cautions
- **Serotonin syndrome risk** — do not combine with SSRIs, MAOIs
- **Bipolar disorder** — 5-HTP may trigger mania
- **Pregnancy/breastfeeding** — avoid 5-HTP

## Ideal For
- Low mood support
- Stress-related mood issues
- Cognitive function
- Nervous system support
- General neurotransmitter balance
