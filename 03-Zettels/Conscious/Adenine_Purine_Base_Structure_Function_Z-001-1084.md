# Adenine Purine Base Structure Function

## Overview
Adenine is one of four nucleobases in DNA and RNA, essential for genetic information storage and energy .

## Chemical Structure
- **Class:** Purine (two-ring structure)
- **Structure:** Fused pyrimidine + imidazole rings
- **Formula:** C₅H₅N₅
- **Atoms:** 5 carbon, 5 hydrogen, 5 nitrogen

## Role in Nucleic Acids

### Base Pairing
| Nucleic Acid | Pairs With | Bond Type |
|--------------|------------|-----------|
| **DNA** | Thymine (T) | 2 hydrogen bonds |
| **RNA** | Uracil (U) | 2 hydrogen bonds |

### Structural Significance
- Purine-pyrimidine pairing maintains **constant helix width**
- 2 hydrogen bonds (vs. 3 for G-C) → allows separation for replication/transcription

## Metabolic Pathways

### De Novo Synthesis
- Built from scratch using: Glycine, Aspartate, Glutamine, CO₂, Formate
- Energy intensive (6 ATP per adenine)
- Occurs primarily in liver

### Salvage Pathway
- Recycles free adenine from nucleotide degradation
- **Enzyme:** Adenine phosphoribosyltransferase (APRT)
- **Product:** AMP (adenosine monophosphate)
- More energy efficient than de novo

## Clinical Relevance

### Lesch-Nyhan Syndrome
- **Defect:** HGPRT (hypoxanthine-guanine phosphoribosyltransferase)
- **Symptoms:** Hyperuricemia, neurological dysfunction, self-injury
- **Inheritance:** X-linked recessive
- **:** Purine overproduction, uric acid accumulation

### Adenine Phosphoribosyltransferase Deficiency
- **Defect:** APRT enzyme
- **Symptoms:** 2,8-dihydroxyadenine kidney stones, renal failure
- **Treatment:** Allopurinol, low-purine diet

## Evolutionary Conservation
- Present in **all known life forms**
- Biochemical pathways for synthesis highly conserved
- Indicates critical role in earliest life
- Part of genetic code universality

## Beyond Genetics: Energy Role
- Forms **ATP** (adenosine triphosphate)
- Forms **NAD** (nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide)
- Forms **FAD** (flavin adenine dinucleotide)
- Core component of cellular energy systems
