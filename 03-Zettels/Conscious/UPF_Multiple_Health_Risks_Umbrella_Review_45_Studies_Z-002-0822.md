## Core Claim
**Ultra-processed food consumption is associated with increased risk** of all-cause mortality, CVD mortality, mental health disorders, obesity, and type 2 diabetes — based on umbrella review of 45 clinical studies.

## Documented Risks

| Health Outcome | Association |
|----------------|-------------|
| **All-cause mortality** | ↑ Increased |
| **CVD mortality** | ↑ Increased |
| **Mental health disorders** | ↑ Increased |
| **Body weight gain** | ↑ Increased |
| **Obesity** | ↑ Increased |
| **Type 2 diabetes** | ↑ Increased |
