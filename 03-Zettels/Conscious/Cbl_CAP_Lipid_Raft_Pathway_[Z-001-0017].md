# Z_MECH: Cbl-CAP Lipid Raft Pathway

An alternative insulin signaling pathway involves Cbl-CAP complex and lipid raft localization, independent of IRS-PI3K.

**Mechanism:**
1. Insulin activates INSR → phosphorylates Cbl-associated protein (CAP)
2. Phosphorylated CAP binds activated Cbl
3. Cbl-CAP complex localizes to caveolin-enriched lipid rafts
4. Recruits CrkII and C3G to rafts
5. C3G activates TC10 (small GTPase)
6. TC10 promotes GLUT4 vesicle trafficking

**Distinction from canonical pathway:**
- Does not require IRS proteins
- Does not use PI3K or Akt
- Depends on lipid raft integrity
- May operate in parallel with PI3K pathway

**Tissue specificity:**
- Most studied in adipocytes
- Muscle also has lipid raft GLUT4 pools
- May explain residual insulin sensitivity when PI3K blocked
