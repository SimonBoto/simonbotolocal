# The Kellogg Brothers — John Harvey vs. Will Keith

> The contrasting philosophies of physician John Harvey Kellogg (health mission) and businessman Will Keith Kellogg (commercial vision) led to both the invention of cornflakes and the creation of the breakfast cereal industry—along with a permanent family rift.

## John Harvey Kellogg (1852-1943)

**Background:**
- Seventh-Day Adventist upbringing
- Medical training
- Brilliant, driven, eccentric
- Prolific author and lecturer
- Surgical innovator

**Values:**
- Religious health mission
- Anti-commercialism
- "Sanitarium food" for patients only
- Idealistic
- Controlling personality

**Role in Cornflakes:**
- Accidental invention (1894)
- Developed process
- Served at Sanitarium
- Opposed mass marketing

**Later Life:**
- Continued Sanitarium work
- Lost control of cereal business
- Estranged from brother
- Maintained health advocacy

## Will Keith Kellogg (1860-1951)

**Background:**
- Younger brother
- Initially worked as janitor at Sanitarium
- Business acumen
- Practical, ambitious
- Patient with brother's temperament

**Values:**
- Commercial opportunity
- Mass production vision
- "Health food for masses"
- Independent
- Persistent

**Business Innovation:**
- Founded Kellogg Company (1906)
- Modern marketing techniques
- Mass production
- National distribution
- Created breakfast cereal category

**Legacy:**
- Billion-dollar company
- Breakfast r
- Philanthropy (Kellogg Foundation)
- "Breakfast is most important meal" (invented)

## The Conflict

**Dispute Origin:**
- W.K. wanted to sell cornflakes commercially
- J.H. opposed (violated Sanitarium mission)
- Disagreement over business control
- Personal and professional tensions

**Escalation:**
- Lawsuits
- Bitter public battle
- Family estrangement
- Lifelong feud

**Resolution:**
- Never fully reconciled
- Both successful in own spheres
- Different historical legacies
- W.K.'s company far outlasted Sanitarium

## Historical Assessment

**John Harvey's Legacy:**
- Health reform pioneer
- Vegetarianism promotion
- Medical quackery elements
- Limited lasting impact

**W.K.'s Legacy:**
- Created global food industry
- Transformed breakfast habits
- Marketing innovations
- Lasting business empire

**Irony:**
- Health fanatic's invention
- Commercialized by pragmatic brother
- Original health mission diluted
- "Junk food" from health food origins
