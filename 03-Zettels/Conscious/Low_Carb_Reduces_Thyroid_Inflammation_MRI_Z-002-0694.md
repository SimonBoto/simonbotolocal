# Low Carb Reduces Thyroid Inflammation on MRI

## Core Claim
MRI quantitative assessment demonstrates that **low-carbohydrate diets reduce thyroid inflammation** in Hashimoto's thyroiditis, with objective imaging evidence of decreased autoimmune activity.

## MRI Findings

| Parameter | Change |
|-----------|--------|
| **Thyroid volume** | Reduced inflammation |
| **Signal characteristics** | Improved |
| **Heterogeneity** | Decreased |

## Evidence

> "MRI quantitative assessment demonstrates reduced thyroid inflammation following low-carbohydrate dietary intervention in Hashimoto's patients."
> — Huang XS et al.
