## Core Claim
**Traditional fats are safe for cooking and have been part of human diets for millennia** — unlike industrial seed oils, they do not produce harmful oxidation products when heated.

## Primary Recommendations
| Fat | Use |
|-----|-----|
| **Extra virgin olive oil** | Cooking, salads |
| **Goat butter** | Cooking, baking |
| **Sheep butter** | Cooking, baking |
| **Cold-pressed sesame oil** | Flavoring |

## Other Safe Traditional Fats
| Fat | Origin |
|-----|--------|
| **Avocado oil** | Traditional use |
| **Ghee** | Indian tradition |
| **Coconut oil** | Tropical traditions |
| **Macadamia oil** | Traditional use |

## Why Safe?
| Feature | Benefit |
|---------|---------|
| **Minimal processing** | Preserves nutrients |
| **Stable fatty acids** | Resist oxidation |
| **Traditional extraction** | Avoids industrial damage |
| **Centuries of use** | Safe human consumption |

## Contrast
| Seed Oils | Traditional Fats |
|-----------|------------------|
| ❌ Industrial, damaged, oxidized | ✅ Minimally processed, stable, safe |

## Clinical Implication
**Replace all seed oils with traditional fats** for cooking and consumption.
