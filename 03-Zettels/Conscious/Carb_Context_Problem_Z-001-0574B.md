# Carb Context Problem — Research Methodology Flaw

> Most protein-CKD research treats protein in isolation, ignoring the metabolic context created by concurrent carbohydrate intake.

## Core Concept

**Key Process:**
```
High carb + High protein ≠ Low carb + High protein
```

## Mechanism

1. **Traditional studies** measure protein intake only
2. **Ignore carbohydrate** quantity and quality
3. **Lump together** high-carb and low-carb protein consumers
4. **Results in flawed conclusions** about protein effects

## Clinical Significance

- Current protein guidelines may be overly simplistic
- Research design needs metabolic context
- Clinical recommendations should consider carbohydrate intake
