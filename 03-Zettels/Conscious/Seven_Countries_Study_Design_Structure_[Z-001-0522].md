# Seven Countries Study — Design and Structure

> The Seven Countries Study examined 12,763 men across 16 cohorts in 7 countries using population-level ecological data to investigate diet-heart relationships.

## Population

**Size:** 12,763 men
**Age:** 40-59 years
**Duration:** 5 years (extended to 50)

## Countries

| Country | Cohorts |
|---------|---------|
| USA | 1 (Railroad workers) |
| Italy | 3 (Rural villages) |
| Greece | 2 (Corfu, Crete) |
| Yugoslavia | 3 |
| Netherlands | 1 (Zutphen) |
| Finland | 2 |
| Japan | 2 |

## Data Collection

**Clinical:** BP, ECG, cholesterol, anthropometrics
**Lifestyle:** Smoking, occupation-based activity
**Diet:** 7-day household records, chemical analysis

## Key Ratio

**MUFA/SFA** claimed to predict 85-96% mortality variance
