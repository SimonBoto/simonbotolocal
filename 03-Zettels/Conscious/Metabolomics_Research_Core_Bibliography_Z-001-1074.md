### Key Clinical Insights
1. **OAT predictive:** Organic acids can predict autoimmune disease before clinical manifestation
2. **IR biomarkers:** α-hydroxybutyrate and pyroglutamic acid are early insulin resistance markers
3. **D safety:** 3200-4000 IU/day vitamin D3 is safe long-term (3+ years)
4. **Diet power:** Mediterranean diet provides significant CVD risk reduction
