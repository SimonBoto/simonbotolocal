# Organic Acid METHAP Integration

> Organic acid testing provides objective biomarkers for mitochondrial dysfunction in metabolic syndrome, guiding targeted nutrient therapy.

## METHAP Applications

**Baseline Assessment:**
- Screen for mitochondrial dysfunction at enrollment
- Identify specific nutrient deficiencies
- Personalize supplementation

**Targeted Interventions:**
- **Mg:** Enhances vitamin D activation
- **CoQ10:** Cardiovascular protection
- **B-vitamins:** Support methylation
- **Carnitine:** Enhances insulin sensitivity

## Monitoring

**Track alongside:**
- HbA1c
- Lipid profiles
- CRP (inflammation)
- Energy/exercise tolerance

## Research Potential

- Outcome measure for metabolic improvement
- Objective biomarker for mitochondrial health
- Personalization tool for precision nutrition
