# Fracture Risk Reassessment

## Clinical Monitoring Components

- **Fracture assessment:** New fragility fractures, location, mechanism
- **Fall assessment:** Frequency, circumstances, risk factors
- **Medication adherence:** Pill count, injection technique, side effects
- **Lifestyle assessment:** Exercise, diet, smoking, alcohol

## Physical Examination Findings

- **Height measurement:** Annual (loss >2 cm suggests vertebral fractures)
- **Spinal examination:** Kyphosis, tenderness, deformity
- **Neurologic assessment:** Balance, gait, fall risk
- **Dental examination:** Prior to antiresorptives (ONJ risk)

## Non-Response Algorithm

```
Non-response on DXA or BTMs
                ↓
      Assess adherence (pill count, injection log)
                ↓
      If adherent → Assess secondary causes
                ↓
      If secondary causes → Treat underlying condition
                ↓
      If no secondary causes → Switch therapy
```

## Secondary Causes to Evaluate

- **Malabsorption:** Celiac disease, IBD, gastric bypass
- **Endocrine:** Hyperparathyroidism, hyperthyroidism, diabetes
- **Medications:** Glucocorticoids, anticonvulsants, proton pump inhibitors
- **Lifestyle:** Smoking, alcohol, sedentary lifestyle
