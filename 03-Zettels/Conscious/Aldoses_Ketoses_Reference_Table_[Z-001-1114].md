# Aldoses Ketoses Reference Table

## Overview
Comprehensive reference of aldose and ketose sugars with their biological significance.

## Aldoses (Aldehyde Sugars)

### Trioses (3C)
| Sugar | Structure | Biological Role |
|-------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Glyceraldehyde** | Aldotriose |  intermediate, photosynthesis |
| **Dihydroxyacetone** | Ketotriose | Isomer of glyceraldehyde |

### Tetroses (4C)
| Sugar | Structure | Biological Role |
|-------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Erythrose** | Aldotetrose | PPP intermediate |
| **Threose** | Aldotetrose | Rare |

### Pentoses (5C)
| Sugar | Structure | Biological Role |
|-------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Ribose** | Aldopentose | RNA, ATP, nucleotide backbone |
| **Arabinose** | Aldopentose | Plant polysaccharides |
| **Xylose** | Aldopentose | Xylans (wood), xylose fermentation |
| **Lyxose** | Aldopentose | Rare, lyxoflavin in heart |

### Hexoses (6C)
| Sugar | Structure | Biological Role |
|-------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Glucose** | Aldohexose | Primary blood sugar, energy source |
| **Galactose** | Aldohexose | Lactose component, glycolipids |
| **Mannose** | Aldohexose | Glycoproteins, immune recognition |
| **Allose** | Aldohexose | Rare in nature |
| **Altrose** | Aldohexose | Rare |
| **Gulose** | Aldohexose | Rare |
| **Idose** | Aldohexose | Rare |
| **Talose** | Aldohexose | Rare |

## Ketoses (Ketone Sugars)

### Trioses
| Sugar | Structure | Biological Role |
|-------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Dihydroxyacetone** | Ketotriose | Glycolysis intermediate, tanning agent |

### Tetroses
| Sugar | Structure | Biological Role |
|-------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Erythrulose** | Ketotetrose | Rare |

### Pentoses
| Sugar | Structure | Biological Role |
|-------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Ribulose** | Ketopentose | Calvin cycle, PPP |

| Sugar | Structure | Biological Role |
|-------|-----------|-----------------|

| **Psicose** | Ketohexose | Rare sugar, sweetener research |

| **Erythritol** | Erythrose | Low-calorie sweetener |
