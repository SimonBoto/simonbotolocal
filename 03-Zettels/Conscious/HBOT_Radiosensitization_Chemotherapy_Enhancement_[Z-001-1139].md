# HBOT Radiosensitization Chemotherapy Enhancement

## Overview
Hyperbaric oxygen therapy enhances the efficacy of radiation and chemotherapy by overcoming tumor hypoxia—a major cause of treatment resistance—and improving drug delivery and ROS-mediated cytotoxicity.

## Radiosensitization by HBOT

### The Oxygen Effect
| Oxygen Status | Radiation Damage |
|---------------|------------------|
| **Well-oxygenated** | 2.5-3x more sensitive |
| **Hypoxic** | Radiation resistant |
| **[[KEYWORD-Mechanism|mechanism]]** | Oxygen fixes DNA damage (ROS) |

### Clinical Application
| Timing | Protocol |
|--------|----------|
| **Before radiation** | HBOT session 30-60 min prior |
| **During radiation course** | Daily or every other day |
| **Goal** | Maximize tumor oxygenation at treatment |

### Tumor Types with Evidence
| [[KEYWORD-Cancer|cancer]] | HBOT + Radiation Evidence |
|--------|---------------------------|
| **Head and neck** | Improved local control |
| **Glioblastoma** | Case reports of benefit |
| **Cervical** | Historical positive studies |
| **Soft tissue sarcoma** | Enhanced radiation effect |

### Mechanism of Radiosensitization
```
Radiation → DNA radicals → Without O₂: Repaired
                              With O₂: Fixed as peroxides → Cell death

HBOT increases tissue O₂ → More DNA damage fixed → Enhanced cell kill
```

## Chemotherapy Enhancement

### Drug Delivery Improvement
| Effect | Mechanism |
|--------|-----------|
| **Improved tumor perfusion** | Better drug penetration |
| **Vascular normalization** | Reduced interstitial pressure |
| **Cell membrane changes** | Altered drug uptake |

### Specific Drug Interactions
| Drug Class | HBOT Effect |
|------------|-------------|
| **Alkylating agents** | Enhanced DNA cross-linking |
| **Platinum compounds** | Increased ROS synergy |
| **Anthracyclines** | Enhanced oxidative damage |
| **Antimetabolites** | Variable effects |

### Timing Considerations
| Approach | Rationale |
|----------|-----------|
| **Concurrent** | Maximum synergy |
| **Sequential** | Reduce toxicity concerns |
| **Metronomic** | Chronic low-dose with HBOT |
