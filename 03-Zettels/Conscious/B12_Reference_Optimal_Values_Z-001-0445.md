|-------|----------|

## Neurological Risk

**Symptoms can occur at:**
- 200-400 pg/mL (sub deficiency)
- Even without anemia
- Peripheral neuropathy
- Cognitive issues
- Fatigue

**Recommendation:**
- Target >400-500 pg/mL
- Monitor if 200-400
- Consider MMA testing
