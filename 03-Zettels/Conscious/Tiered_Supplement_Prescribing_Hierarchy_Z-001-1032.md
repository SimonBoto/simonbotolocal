# Tiered Supplement Prescribing Hierarchy

Systematic approach to supplement prescribing: Foundational → Targeted mitochondrial → Detox/methylation → Gut rebalancing → Lipid repair → Lifestyle. This hierarchy ensures logical sequencing and avoids overwhelming patients.

## Evidence
> "Throwing every supplement at a patient at once creates confusion, poor compliance, and inability to identify what works. Tiered prescribing builds systematically." (Clinical nutrition)

## The Six Tiers

### Tier 1: Foundational (Weeks 1-4)
**Purpose:** Address common deficiencies, establish baseline

| Supplement | Dose | Rationale |
|------------|------|-----------|
| **Vitamin D3** | 2000-5000 IU | Universal deficiency, immune support |
| **Magnesium** | 400 mg | Cofactor for 300+ enzymes |
| **Omega-3 (EPA/DHA)** | 2-3 g | Anti-inflammatory, membrane support |
| **B-complex** | 1-2 capsules | Energy metabolism, methylation |
| **Probiotic** | 50 billion CFU | Gut foundation |

**Entry criteria:** All patients start here unless contraindicated

### Tier 2: Targeted Mitochondrial (Weeks 5-8)
**Purpose:** Support energy production if OAT shows dysfunction

| Supplement | Dose | Indication |
|------------|------|------------|
| **CoQ10** | 100-200 mg | Elevated lactate/pyruvate |
| **L-Carnitine** | 1-3 g | Elevated adipate/suberate |
| **Alpha-lipoic acid** | 300-600 mg | Mitochondrial support |
| **NAC** | 600-1200 mg | Glutathione precursor |

**Entry criteria:** Elevated mitochondrial markers on OAT

### Tier 3: Detox + Methylation (Weeks 9-12)
**Purpose:** Support detoxification and methylation pathways

| Supplement | Dose | Indication |
|------------|------|------------|
| **Methylfolate (5-MTHF)** | 400-1000 mcg | Elevated FIGLU |
| **Methylcobalamin** | 1000-5000 mcg | Elevated MMA |
| **TMG or DMG** | 500-1000 mg | Methylation support |
| **Glutathione** | 250-500 mg | Oxidative stress |

**Entry criteria:** Methylation markers elevated, oxidative stress present

### Tier 4: Gut Rebalancing (Weeks 13-16)
**Purpose:** Target specific gut dysbiosis patterns

| Supplement | Dose | Indication |
|------------|------|------------|
| **Berberine** | 500 mg 2-3×/day | Bacterial overgrowth |
| **Oregano oil** | As directed | Dysbiosis |
| **Caprylic acid** | As directed | Yeast overgrowth |
| **S. boulardii** | 250 mg 2×/day | Antimicrobial support |

**Entry criteria:** Gut markers elevated on OAT

### Tier 5: Lipid Repair (Weeks 17-20)
**Purpose:** Address fatty acid metabolism and membrane repair

| Supplement | Dose | Indication |
|------------|------|------------|
| **Phosphatidylcholine** | 1-2 g | Membrane repair |
| **Evening primrose oil** | 1-2 g | GLA for inflammation |
| **Vitamin E (mixed tocopherols)** | 400 IU | Antioxidant |

**Entry criteria:** Fatty acid markers abnormal, chronic inflammation

### Tier 6: Lifestyle Directives (Ongoing)
**Purpose:** Behavioral changes that support biochemistry

| Directive | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| **Sleep** | 7-9 hours, consistent schedule |
| **Movement** | 30 min daily moderate exercise |
| **Stress management** | Daily practice |
| **Diet** | Mediterranean pattern |
| **Hydration** | 2-3 L water daily |

**Entry criteria:** All patients, all phases

## Prescribing Principles

### Start Low, Go Slow
- Begin at lower end of dose range
- Increase based on tolerance and response
- Monitor for side effects

### One Change at a Time
- Add one new supplement per week
- If reaction occurs, source is clear
- Allows attribution of benefit

### Prioritize by Severity
- Address life-threatening first
- Then functional impairment
- Finally optimization

### Consider Interactions
- Drug-nutrient interactions
- Nutrient-nutrient competition
- Timing (with/without food)

## Patient Communication

### Explaining the Hierarchy
> "We're building your metabolic support in layers. First we establish the foundation—things almost everyone needs. Then we add specific support based on your test results. Each layer builds on the one before."

### Managing Expectations
- **Tier 1:** "These are basics—most people feel some improvement in energy and sleep"
- **Tier 2+:** "These target your specific patterns—response varies by individual"
- **Timeline:** "Give each tier 4 weeks before adding the next"

## Compliance Strategies

| Barrier | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| **Too many pills** | Pill organizer, combination products |
| **Cost** | Prioritize, generic options, phased approach |
| **Confusion** | Written schedule, pictures on bottles |
| **Side effects** | Dose reduction, timing change, alternative form |

## Monitoring and Adjustment

### At Each Tier Addition
- **Week 1:** Tolerance check
- **Week 4:** Response assessment
- **Before next tier:** Review necessity

### Criteria for Advancement
- Tolerating current tier well
- Some positive response (even if subtle)
- No new concerning symptoms
- Patient ready for more
