# Systematic Review Principles

> **Definition:** A systematic review is a comprehensive, structured, and reproducible method for identifying, selecting, appraising, and synthesizing all relevant research on a specific question.

## Core Principle

> **Definition:** A systematic review is a comprehensive, structured, and reproducible method for identifying, selecting, appraising, and synthesizing all relevant research on a specific question.

## Foundational Principles

- **Pre-specified protocol:** Methodology is planned and documented before conducting the review
- **Comprehensive search:** Systematic identification of all relevant studies regardless of publication status or language
- **Explicit selection criteria:** Transparent and objective inclusion/exclusion criteria applied consistently
- **Critical appraisal:** Assessment of methodological quality and risk of bias in included studies
- **Transparent reporting:** Comprehensive documentation of methods and results following established guidelines

## Key Steps in the Process

1. **Formulating the question:** Using PICO(S) framework (Population, Intervention, Comparison, Outcome, Study design)
2. **Developing the protocol:** Registration in PROSPERO and detailed methodology documentation
3. **Conducting the search:** Comprehensive strategy across multiple databases with appropriate filters
4. **Screening and selection:** Title/abstract screening followed by full-text review using predefined criteria
5. **Data extraction:** Standardized collection of study characteristics, methods, and outcomes
6. **Quality assessment:** Application of appropriate tools (e.g., Cochrane RoB 2, ROBINS-I)
7. **Data synthesis:** Narrative synthesis and/or statistical meta-analysis
8. **Reporting and dissemination:** Publication following PRISMA guidelines

## Distinction from Other Reviews

- **Narrative reviews:** Qualitative summaries without systematic search or appraisal
- **Scoping studies:** Map existing literature on a topic without quality appraisal
- **Rapid reviews:** Accelerated systematic reviews with methodological shortcuts
- **Umbrella reviews:** Reviews of systematic reviews
