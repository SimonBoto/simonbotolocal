# Clinical Assessment of Metabolic Health

> **Source:** ChatGPT conversation [[CGPT_0124_Profound_Truths_Revolutionizing_Health_CGPT-0124]] (July 15, 2025)

## Core Concept

Systematic clinical assessment framework for evaluating all five pillars of metabolic health to guide personalized interventions.

## Key Principles

- **Assessment Flow:** Mitochondrial function → Metabolomic profiling → Glycation status → Mineral assessment → Fatty acid balance
- **Functional Markers:** Focus on functional biomarkers rather than just diagnostic thresholds
- **Integration:** Assessment results must be integrated to develop comprehensive intervention strategies
- **Baseline Establishment:** Establish individual baselines before initiating interventions

## Clinical Significance

A comprehensive assessment framework ensures all five pillars are evaluated systematically, allowing for targeted interventions that address the root causes of metabolic dysfunction rather than just symptoms.
