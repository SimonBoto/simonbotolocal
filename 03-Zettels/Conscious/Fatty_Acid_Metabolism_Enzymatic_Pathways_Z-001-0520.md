# Fatty Acid  — Enzymatic Pathways

> Delta-6 and delta-5 desaturases are shared between  and omega-3 pathways, creating metabolic competition that determines inflammatory eicosanoid output.

## Key Enzymes

**Delta-6 Desaturase (FADS2):**
- Rate-limiting for both pathways
- Linoleic acid preferred (lower Km)

**Delta-5 Desaturase (FADS1):**
- Critical for eicosanoid production
- Requires zinc, , B6

## Eicosanoid Production

**Omega-6 (AA):**
- PGE2, LTB4 = pro-inflammatory

**Omega-3 (EPA):**
- PGE3, LTB5 = less inflammatory
- Resolvins, protectins = resolution

## Conversion Efficiency

**Omega-6:** 0.5-5% (efficient)
**Omega-3 ALA:** <5% EPA, <2% DHA
**Competition:** High omega-6 blocks omega-3

**Genetics:**
- FADS polymorphisms affect conversion
- Population differences
