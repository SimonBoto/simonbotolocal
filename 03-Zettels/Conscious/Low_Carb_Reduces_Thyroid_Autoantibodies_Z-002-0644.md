# Low-Carbohydrate Diet Reduces Thyroid Autoantibodies

## Core Claim
Low-carbohydrate dietary intervention significantly reduces **thyroid peroxidase antibodies (TPOAb)** and **thyroglobulin antibodies (TgAb)** in patients with Hashimoto's thyroiditis, indicating reduced autoimmune activity against thyroid tissue.

## The Autoantibodies in Hashimoto's

| Antibody | Target | Clinical Significance |
|----------|--------|----------------------|
| **TPOAb** | Thyroid peroxidase | Most sensitive marker; present in 90-95% of cases |
| **TgAb** | Thyroglobulin | Present in 80-90%; correlates with disease severity |
| **Combined** | Multiple thyroid antigens | Higher titers = more aggressive disease |

### Antibody Reduction with Low-Carb
- **TPOAb decrease:** Significant reduction in antibody titers
- **TgAb decrease:** Parallel reduction in thyroglobulin antibodies
- **Correlation:** Antibody reduction correlates with decreased thyroid inflammation on MRI

## Mechanism: From Diet to Immune Modulation

### Step 1: Reduce Insulin Resistance
- Lower carbohydrate intake → reduced insulin demand
- Improved insulin sensitivity → metabolic normalization

### Step 2: Decrease Inflammation
- Lower insulin → reduced pro-inflammatory cytokines
- Decreased systemic inflammation → reduced thyroid inflammation

### Step 3: Modulate Immune Response
- Reduced inflammation → reduced antigen presentation
- Lower immune activation → reduced autoantibody production

## Evidence

> "MRI quantitative assessment shows decreased thyroid inflammation correlating with reduced autoantibody titers following low-carbohydrate diet implementation."
> — Huang et al., 2024

## Clinical Implications

### Monitoring Response
| Timeframe | Expected Change |
|-----------|-----------------|
| 4-6 weeks | Initial antibody reduction |
| 3 months | Significant decrease |
| 6-12 months | Stabilization at lower levels |

### Patient Selection
- **Best candidates:** Early-stage Hashimoto's, elevated antibodies with normal/slightly elevated TSH
- **Monitoring required:** TSH, free T4, TPOAb, TgAb at 3-month intervals
