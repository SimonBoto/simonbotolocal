# Fructose Metabolism Produces Uric Acid

## Core Claim
**Fructose metabolism uniquely produces uric acid** as a byproduct — unlike glucose metabolism — making high fructose intake a major driver of hyperuricemia and gout.

## Mechanism

| Step | Result |
|------|--------|
| **Fructose → fructose-1-phosphate** | Rapid phosphorylation |
| **ATP depletion** | AMP accumulation |
| **AMP → uric acid** | Purine degradation |

## Clinical Implication

| Source | Action |
|--------|--------|
| **High-fructose corn syrup** | Eliminate |
| **Fruit juice** | Limit |
| **Sweetened beverages** | Avoid |
