# l Energy Terminology

## Overview
13 terms defining mitochondrial function and energy production mechanisms.

## The Terms (Category C)

| Term | Definition | Clinical Marker |
|------|------------|-----------------|
| **Μιτοχόνδριο** | Cellular energy factory | Function measured via OAT |
| **ATP** | Universal energy currency | ATP production = health metric |
| **Κύκλος Krebs** | TCA cycle, central  | Citrate, succinate, α-ketoglutarate |
| **Γλυκόλυση** | Glucose → pyruvate | Lactate/pyruvate ratio |
| **Οξειδωτική Φωσφορυλίωση** | Electron transport + ATP synthase | OXPHOS efficiency markers |
| **NAD+/NADH** | Redox cofactor pair | NAD+ availability = metabolic flexibility |
| **FAD/FADH₂** | Redox cofactor (Complex II) | Succinate → Fumarate |
| **Συνένζυμο Q10** | Electron shuttle | Mitochondrial support supplement |
| **Καρδιολιπίνη** | Inner membrane phospholipid | Membrane integrity |
| **Αναερόβιος Μεταβολισμός** | Lactate-producing glycolysis | Elevated lactate |
| **Λακτικό/Πυροσταφυλικό** | Lactate/pyruvate ratio | Redox status indicator |
| **Beta-Oxidation** | Fatty acid → acetyl-CoA | Adipate, suberate markers |
| **Ketosis** | Fat-derived ketone production | BHB as alternative fuel |

## Clinical Integration
These terms form the vocabulary for:
- OAT interpretation
- Fatigue workups
- Metabolic flexibility assessment
- Mitochondrial support protocols

## Core Principle
> "Mitochondria as decision-makers, not furnaces."
