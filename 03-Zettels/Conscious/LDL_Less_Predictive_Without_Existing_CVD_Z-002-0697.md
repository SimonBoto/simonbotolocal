## Core Claim
**LDL cholesterol is a weaker predictor of cardiovascular events** in individuals without established CVD compared to those with existing disease, suggesting different risk models needed for primary vs secondary prevention.

## Predictive Value

| Context | LDL Predictive Power |
|---------|---------------------|
| **Secondary prevention** | Strong |
| **Primary prevention** | Weak |
