# ICH Good  Practice (GCP) Guidelines

International Council for Harmonisation unified standard for designing, conducting, recording, and reporting trials involving human participants. Ensures data credibility, accuracy, and protection of participant rights.

## Scope

| Aspect | Application |
|--------|-------------|
| **Clinical trials** | Interventional studies with investigational products |
| **Pharmaceutical** | Drugs, biologics, vaccines |
| **Medical devices** | Where regulated as trials |
| **Global standard** | Adopted by FDA, EMA, PMDA, Health Canada |

## Core Principles (E6 R2)

1. **Clinical trials conducted ethically** — Declaration of Helsinki compliance
2. **Risk minimization** — Foreseeable risks justified by anticipated benefits
3. **Rights and well-being protected** — Overriding considerations
4. **Scientifically sound** — Based on adequate preclinical/laboratory data
5. **Quality assurance** — Systems ensuring quality at every stage
6. **Qualified investigators** — Trained and experienced
7. **Informed consent** — Freely given, documented
8. **Data integrity** — Accurate recording, verification, reporting
9. **Confidentiality** — Privacy protection
10. **Quality management** — Risk-based approach to trial management

## Key Documents

| Document | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| **Protocol** | Detailed study plan, scientifically justified |
| **Investigator's Brochure** | Product information, safety data |
| **Informed Consent Form** | Participant information, voluntary agreement |
| **Case Report Forms** | Standardized data collection |
| **Standard Operating Procedures** | Consistent trial conduct |

## Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| **Sponsor** | Trial design, data management, safety reporting |
| **Investigator** | Conduct, participant care, data integrity |
| **Ethics Committee** | Protocol approval, ongoing oversight |
| **Regulatory Authority** | Trial authorization, inspection |

## Application to METHAP

While METHAP is not a pharmaceutical trial, GCP principles apply:
- **Protocol:** Detailed study plan with scientific rationale
- **Ethics approval:** Independent committee review
- **Informed consent:** Documented voluntary participation
- **Data integrity:** Accurate  data collection
- **Quality assurance:** Standardized procedures, monitoring
