# Oxalic Acid Structure and Properties

## Chemical Identity

**IUPAC name:** Ethanedioic acid  
**Molecular formula:** C₂H₂O₄  
**Structure:** Two carboxylic acid groups (-COOH) on 2-carbon chain  
**Class:** Simplest dicarboxylic acid

## Two Forms

| Form | Formula | Properties |
|------|---------|------------|
| **Anhydrous** | C₂H₂O₄ | White crystalline solid |
| **Dihydrate** | C₂H₂O₄·2H₂O | White crystalline solid with water |

## Physical Properties

- **Appearance:** White crystalline solid
- **Solubility:** Highly soluble in water (forms colorless solution)
- **Melting point:** 189-191°C (anhydrous)
- **pKa:** 1.25, 4.14 (strong first acid dissociation)

## Chemical Properties

- **Strong organic acid:** Both carboxyl groups donate protons
- **Reducing agent:** Can donate electrons in redox reactions
- **Chelating agent:** Binds metal ions (, iron)
