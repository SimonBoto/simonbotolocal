# Low Carb Reduces Hashimoto Thyroid Autoantibodies

## Core Claim
Low-carbohydrate diets reduce **thyroid autoantibodies** in Hashimoto's thyroiditis, indicating decreased autoimmune activity against thyroid tissue.

## Mechanisms

| Pathway | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Reduced insulin resistance** | Lower autoimmune drive |
| **Decreased inflammation** | Less immune activation |
| **Improved gut barrier** | Reduced antigen exposure |

## Evidence

> "Low-carbohydrate dietary intervention reduces thyroid peroxidase and thyroglobulin antibodies in Hashimoto's patients."
> — Huang et al.

## Significance
