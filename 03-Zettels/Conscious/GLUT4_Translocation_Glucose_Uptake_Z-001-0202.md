# GLUT4 Translocation and Glucose Uptake

## Core Principle
Insulin stimulates glucose uptake by triggering translocation of GLUT4 glucose transporters from intracellular vesicles to the plasma membrane.

## Mechanism
1. Akt phosphorylates AS160 (TBC1D4)
2. AS160 inactivation releases GLUT4 vesicles
3. Vesicles traffic to plasma membrane
4. GLUT4 inserts → glucose uptake increases 10-20x
5. GLUT4 internalized when insulin falls

## Significance
- **Critical for:** Postprandial glucose clearance
- **Tissue:** Adipose, muscle (primary)
- **Clinical:** Impaired in insulin resistance, type 2 diabetes
