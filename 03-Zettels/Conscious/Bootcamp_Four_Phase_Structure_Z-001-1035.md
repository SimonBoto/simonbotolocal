## Core Claim
**An 8-week metabolomics bootcamp follows four progressive phases — Foundation → OAT Mastery → Lipidomics → Integration — designed to scaffold cognitive transformation from biochemical literacy to clinical reasoning, producing clinicians who can think in metabolomics rather than merely memorize pathways.**

## Evidence

### The Four Phases
| Phase | Weeks | Goal | Focus | Output |
|-------|-------|------|-------|--------|
| **1. Foundation** | 1-2 | Reformat brain's OS | Biochemical literacy, pathway megastructure | Mental models, failure patterns |
| **2. OAT Mastery** | 3-4 | Pattern recognition | Organic acids by category, case-based | Language fluency |
| **3. Lipidomics** | 5-6 | Ratio interpretation | Fatty acid physiology, lipid signaling | Clinical decision-making |
| **4. Integration** | 7-8 | Form metabolomics brain | Energy-first reasoning, practice drills | Competent clinician |

### Design Principles
| Principle | Implementation |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Time-bound** | 8 weeks creates urgency and focus |
| **Progressive** | Each phase builds on previous |
| **Modular** | Clear boundaries, measurable progress |
| **Clinically relevant** | Every lesson connects to practice |

### Competency Targets (Week 8)
- [ ] Interpret OAT without drowning
- [ ] Read fatty acids like an x-ray
- [ ] Build personalized, mechanistic regimens
- [ ] Understand patient's metabolic story
- [ ] Speak patient-friendly language
- [ ] Fit into clinic workflow

## Clinical Implication
- **Training efficiency:** Structured curriculum accelerates competence
- **Cognitive restructuring:** Phases designed to transform thinking, not just transfer knowledge
- **Scalability:** Reproducible framework for training multiple clinicians
- **Assessment:** Clear phase gates enable competency-based progression
- **METHAP relevance:** Four-phase structure applies to research training — foundation → technical mastery → specialized analysis → independent research
