## Core Claim
**Persistent fatigue despite adequate rest indicates mitochondrial dysfunction** — the body's energy factories are not producing sufficient ATP.

## Mitochondrial Function

### When Functioning Properly
- Sufficient energy available
- Good endurance
- Recovery with rest

### When Disrupted
- Fatigue
- Reduced endurance
- Exhaustion not relieved by rest

## ATP Powers Everything
| System | ATP Use |
|--------|---------|
| **Muscles** | Movement |
| **Brain** | Cognition |
| **Immune system** | Defense |
| **Organs** | Function |

## Fatigue Is Protective
- Body requesting rest
- Preserve limited energy resources
- **Not normal** — should not be ignored
