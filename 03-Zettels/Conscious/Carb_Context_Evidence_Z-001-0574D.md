# Carb Context Evidence — Evidence Gap in Research

> Traditional protein-CKD research creates a fundamental evidence gap by ignoring carbohydrate context.

## Core Concept

**Key Process:**
```
Traditional studies measure protein only → Ignore carb context → Evidence gap
```

## Mechanism

1. **Studies measure protein intake only**
2. **Ignore carbohydrate** quantity and quality
3. **Lump together** high-carb and low-carb protein consumers
4. **Create flawed conclusions** about protein effects

## Clinical Significance

Highlights fundamental flaw in current research design:
- Future studies must measure carbohydrate context
- Meta-analyses should stratify by carb intake
- Clinical trials needed for validation
