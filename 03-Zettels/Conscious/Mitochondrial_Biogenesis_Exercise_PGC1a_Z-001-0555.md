# l Biogenesis — Exercise and PGC-1α

> Exercise stimulates  biogenesis through PGC-1α activation, enhancing both quantity and quality of mitochondria.

## PGC-1α — Master Regulator

**Activators:**
- Exercise (muscle contraction)
- Fasting/energy stress
- Cold exposure

**Triggers:**
-  signaling (CaMK)
- AMPK (low energy)
- Moderate ROS (hormesis)

## Exercise Prescription

**Zone 2 (Optimal):**
- 60-70% max heart rate
- 150-180 min/week
- Sustainable, fat-oxidizing

**HIIT:**
- Brief intense efforts
- Strong PGC-1α activation
- 1-2 sessions/week

**Resistance:**
- Muscle fiber recruitment
- 2-3 sessions/week

## Downstream Effects

- TFAM activation
- mtDNA replication
- Complex I-V upregulation
- Enhanced oxidative capacity
