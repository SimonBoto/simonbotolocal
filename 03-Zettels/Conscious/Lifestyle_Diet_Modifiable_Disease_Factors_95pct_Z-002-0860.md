# Lifestyle and Diet Are Modifiable Disease Factors — 95.7% Improvement Rate

## Core Claim
**The most determinant factors in disease are modifiable through lifestyle and diet** — BMJ research shows 95.7% health improvement with metabolomic-based correction.

| Intervention | Outcome |
|--------------|---------|
| **Metabolomic-based deficiency correction** | 95.7% health improvement |
| **Metabolic disorder correction** | Quality of life improvement |

Lifestyle and diet interventions are **highly effective** when targeted to individual metabolic needs. 95.7% success rate far exceeds conventional approaches.
