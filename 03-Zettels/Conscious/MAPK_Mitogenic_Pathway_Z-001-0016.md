# MAPK Pathway for Mitogenic Signaling

 activates the Ras/MAPK pathway for mitogenic (growth) responses, distinct from metabolic signaling via PI3K.

**Activation cascade:**
1. Phosphorylated INSR recruits GRB2 and SHC proteins
2. GRB2-SOS complex activates Ras (GDP → GTP)
3. Active Ras activates Raf (MAPKKK)
4. Raf → MEK1/2 (MAPKK) → ERK1/2 (MAPK)
5. ERK phosphorylates transcription factors (Elk-1, c-Fos)

**Metabolic vs mitogenic divergence:**

| Pathway | Adapter | Downstream | Function |
|---------|---------|------------|----------|
| **Metabolic** | IRS → PI3K | Akt | Glucose transport, synthesis |
| **Mitogenic** | GRB2-SHC | ERK | Growth, proliferation, survival |

**Clinical relevance:**
- Metabolic and mitogenic signaling can be uncoupled
- Some insulin analogs have differential pathway activation
-  cells exploit mitogenic signaling
