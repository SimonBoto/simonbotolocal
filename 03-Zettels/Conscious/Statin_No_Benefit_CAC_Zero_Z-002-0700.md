## Core Claim
In patients with **CAC = 0** (no coronary plaque), statin therapy shows **no cardiovascular benefit** over 8+ years of follow-up, indicating overtreatment when imaging shows no atherosclerosis.

## JACC Evidence

| Finding | Result |
|---------|--------|
| **11,000 patients** | 8-year follow-up |
| **CAC = 0** | No statin benefit |
| **CAC <100** | No benefit |

## Implications

| Practice | Recommendation |
|----------|----------------|
| **CAC screening** | Before statin initiation |
| **CAC = 0** | Avoid statins |
