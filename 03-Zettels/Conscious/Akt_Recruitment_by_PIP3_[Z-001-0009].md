# Z_MECH: Akt Recruitment to Membrane

PIP3 generated by PI3K recruits Akt kinase to the plasma membrane, enabling its activation by PDK1.

**Mechanism:**
- Akt contains PH (pleckstrin homology) domain
- PH domain binds PIP3 with high affinity
- PIP3 recruits Akt from cytosol to plasma membrane
- Co-recruitment with PDK1 enables phosphorylation

**Spatial organization:**
- PIP3 localized to membrane microdomains
- Akt activation occurs at specific membrane sites
- Activated Akt then phosphorylates cytosolic targets

**Key proteins:**
- **PDK1:** Phosphorylates Akt at Thr308 (partial activation)
- **mTORC2:** Phosphorylates Akt at Ser473 (full activation)
