# RNA World Hypothesis

## Core Thesis
Early life relied on **RNA as both genetic material AND catalyst** (ribozymes), before DNA and proteins became dominant.

## Evidence Supporting RNA World
1. **RNA can store information** (like DNA)
2. **RNA can catalyze reactions** (like proteins)
3. **Ribosomes are ribozymes** — catalytic RNA still central to biology
4. **tRNA/rRNA ancient** — predates protein enzymes
5. **Prebiotic plausibility:** Nucleobases + ribose + phosphate can form spontaneously

## Adenine's Central Role
- ATP is energy currency (ribozyme reactions require energy)
- A-U base pairing in RNA
- Adenine-rich regions in catalytic RNAs

## Transition to DNA-Protein World
| Stage | Genetic Material | Catalyst |
|-------|------------------|----------|
| RNA World | RNA | Ribozymes |
| RNP World | RNA | Protein enzymes |
| DNA World | DNA | Protein enzymes |

**Advantages of DNA:**
- More stable (deoxyribose lacks 2'-OH)
- Better for long-term storage

**Advantages of proteins:**
- 20  vs 4 nucleotides → more catalytic diversity
- Better catalytic efficiency

## Timeline
- 4.2-4.0 BYA: RNA World emergence
- 3.8-3.5 BYA: Last Universal Common Ancestor (LUCA)
- DNA/protein takeover: Gradual transition
