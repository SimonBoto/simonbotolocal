# Nattokinase_Safety_Dosing

## Source
[[L-001-0654_Vaughan2017_PAI1_Aging_L-001-0654]]

## Core Claim
Nattokinase is safe for human consumption at doses ranging from 2000-10800 FU/day, with specific contraindications including recent surgery (1-2 weeks prior), soy allergy, and bleeding disorders or anticoagulant use.

## Key Details
- Dosing: 2000-10800 FU/day safe
- Contraindications: surgery (1-2 weeks prior), soy allergy, bleeding disorders/anticoagulants
- Normal levels help wound healing (youth)
