# METHAP IRB Feedback Action Items

## Source
Ethics Committee (Ε.Η.Δ.Ε.) review of PhD protocol application — February 2025

## Critical Issues (Must Resolve)

### 1. Participant Tracking Clarification (B4.6)
**Issue:** "Ιχνηλασία προσώπων" mentioned but not explained

**Action:**
- Define purpose: follow-up reminders vs. adherence monitoring
- Specify method: email/phone only (no GPS/physical tracking)
- Add to Methods section:

> *"Participants will receive periodic follow-up reminders via email or phone to ensure adherence. No GPS or physical tracking will be used. Personal data stored per GDPR."*

---

### 2. Ethical Considerations Section (G1)
**Issue:** Missing dedicated ethical framework discussion

**Action:**
Add section covering:
- Voluntary participation and withdrawal rights
- Minimal risk justification
- Data privacy protections (GDPR)
- Adverse event handling
- Incidental findings protocol

---

### 3. Age Inclusion Inconsistency
**Issue:** Conflicting age ranges (15-65 vs. 18+)

**Action:**
| Decision | Required Action |
|----------|-----------------|
| **Adults only (18+)** | Correct all documents to 18-65 |
| **Include minors (15+)** | Submit parental consent forms + additional safeguards |

**Recommendation:** Standardize to 18+ to streamline approval

---

### 4. Questionnaire Submission (D1, D9.1)
**Issue:** Questionnaires referenced but not submitted

**Action:**
- Submit all assessment instruments
- Provide copyright permissions for standardized tools
- Include brief rationale for each questionnaire

---

## Moderate Issues (Should Address)

### 8. Participant Costs
**Action:** Add explicit statement:
> *"All study-related tests and procedures are provided at no cost to participants."*

### 9. Sponsor Acknowledgment
**Action:** Add to informed consent:
> *"This study is funded by Toxplus S.A. and Metabolomic Medicine S.A. No financial conflicts of interest exist between researchers and funding sources."*

### 10. Financial Relationships
**Action:** Confirm and document:
- No ties to supplement manufacturers
- Academic independence maintained
- Disclosure in all publications
