# Zn and Se — Nutritional Sources and Supplementation

## Zinc Sources

**Animal (High Bioavailability):**
| Food | Zinc (mg/100g) |
|------|----------------|
| Oysters | 16-182 |
| Beef | 4-12 |
| Lamb | 4-8 |

**Plant:**
- Pumpkin seeds: 7-10 mg
- Hemp seeds: 7-12 mg
- Phytates reduce absorption

## Selenium Sources

**Brazil Nuts:** 1917 μg/100g (VARIES BY SOIL!)
**Animal:**
- Tuna: 90-110 μg
- Sardines: 50-70 μg
- Eggs: 15-20 μg

## Supplementation

**Zinc:**
- 15-30 mg elemental
- Forms: Picolinate, glycinate
- Away from iron, copper

**Selenium:**
- 100-200 μg
- Forms: Selenomethionine
- Caution: Brazil nuts vary wildly

## Testing

- Serum zinc (fasting)
- Plasma selenium
- RBC zinc (long-term)
- GPx activity (functional)
