## Core Principles

- Root cause identification rather than symptom suppression
- Comprehensive assessment including lifestyle factors
- Personalized treatment based on clinical patterns
- Prevention and optimization beyond disease management

## Assessment Components

- Comprehensive hormone panel (testosterone, estradiol, SHBG, DHEA-S, cortisol, thyroid)
- Metabolic markers (HbA1c, lipid panel, inflammatory markers)
- Nutritional status (vitamin D, zinc, magnesium, B12)
- Lifestyle assessment (sleep, stress, exercise, diet)

## Therapeutic Strategies

- Lifestyle interventions (sleep optimization, stress reduction, exercise programming)
- Nutritional support (vitamin D, zinc, magnesium supplementation)
- Botanical medicine (ashwagandha, fenugreek, tribulus)
- Hormone optimization (TRT when indicated)
- Addressing underlying conditions (sleep apnea, metabolic syndrome)

## Clinical Outcomes

- Improved symptoms and quality of life
- Enhanced metabolic health
- Optimized fertility potential
- Reduced risk of age-related chronic diseases
- Patient empowerment and engagement in health management
