# Metabolic Health Biomarkers

> **Source:** ChatGPT conversation [[CGPT_0124_Profound_Truths_Revolutionizing_Health_CGPT-0124]] (July 15, 2025)

## Core Concept

Comprehensive biomarker panel for assessing all five pillars of metabolic health, providing objective measures of functional status.

## Key Principles

- **Mitochondrial Biomarkers:** RER, fasting insulin/glucose, ketones, lactate/pyruvate ratio
- **Metabolomic Biomarkers:** Urine organic acids, plasma/serum metabolomics
- **Glycation Biomarkers:** HbA1c, fructosamine, skin autofluorescence
- **Mineral Biomarkers:** RBC minerals (magnesium, zinc, selenium, copper, manganese)
- **Fatty Acid Biomarkers:** Omega-6:omega-3 ratio, EPA/DHA levels

## Clinical Significance

A comprehensive biomarker panel provides objective data to assess the functional status of all five pillars, guide targeted interventions, and track progress over time.
