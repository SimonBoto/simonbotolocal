# Microbiome Gut Brain Terminology

## Overview
8 terms defining gut ecosystem, dysbiosis, and gut-brain axis concepts.

## The Terms (Category E)

| Term | Definition | OAT Marker |
|------|------------|------------|
| **Μικροβίωμα** | Gut microbial ecosystem | Hippurate, phenylacetate |
| **Δυσβίωση** | Microbial imbalance | Elevated bacterial markers |
| **Candida/Yeast** | Fungal overgrowth | Arabinose, tartaric acid |
| **Clostridia Toxins** | HPHPA, 4-cresol | Neurotransmitter disruption |
| **Leaky Gut** | Intestinal permeability | Indican, inflammatory markers |
| **SIBO** | Small intestinal bacterial overgrowth | Hydrogen, methane breath tests |
| **Indican** | Tryptophan metabolite | Gut permeability marker |
| **Νευροδιαβιβαστές** | Dopamine, serotonin, GABA | Gut production influences brain |

## Gut-Brain Axis
The microbiome:
- Produces vitamins (B, K)
- Regulates immunity (70% of immune system)
- Synthesizes neurotransmitters
- Influences mood and cognition

##  Approach
> "Energy before gut" — l support precedes aggressive gut protocols.
