## The Evolutionary Context

**Ancestral environment:**
- Continuous fruit availability (tropical/subtropical)
- Seasonal abundance
- No food processing
- Immediate consumption

**Estimated ancestral intake:**
- **2,000-10,000mg/day** from wild fruits
- Year-round availability in equatorial regions
- Higher vitamin C content in wild vs. cultivated varieties

## The Modern Reality

**87% reduction in vitamin C content:**
- Agricultural selection for shelf life, not nutrition
- Long transport and storage times
- Processing and cooking losses
- Reduced dietary diversity

**Modern intake patterns:**
- RDA-focused thinking (90mg = "enough")
- Processed food dominance
- Seasonal availability ignored
- Supplementation stigmatized
