# Twelve Evidence-Based Health Actions — Comprehensive Lifestyle Framework

## Core Claim
**Twelve evidence-based lifestyle actions substantially improve health outcomes, longevity, and quality of life** — addressing diet, movement, sleep, stress, social connection, environment, and mindset in an integrated framework supported by Blue Zones research and lifestyle medicine literature.

## The 12 Actions

### Diet (Actions 1-3)
| # | Action | Implementation |
|---|--------|----------------|
| **1** | Eliminate ultra-processed foods | Read labels, cook at home |
| **2** | Eat whole foods | Vegetables, meat, fish, eggs |
| **3** | Limit added sugar | <25g/day |

### Movement (Actions 4-5)
| # | Action | Implementation |
|---|--------|----------------|
| **4** | Daily walking/activity | 7,000-10,000 steps |
| **5** | Resistance training | 2-3x/week |

### Sleep (Action 6)
| # | Action | Implementation |
|---|--------|----------------|
| **6** | Sleep 7-9 hours | Consistent schedule, dark room |

### Stress (Actions 7-8)
| # | Action | Implementation |
|---|--------|----------------|
| **7** | Practice stress management | Meditation, breathing |
| **8** | Spend time in nature | Daily outdoor time |

### Social (Action 9)
| # | Action | Implementation |
|---|--------|----------------|
| **9** | Maintain social connections | Regular contact, community |

### Environment (Actions 10-11)
| # | Action | Implementation |
|---|--------|----------------|
| **10** | Reduce toxin exposure | Filter water, organic when possible |
| **11** | Get regular sunlight | Morning exposure, vitamin D |

### Mindset (Action 12)
| # | Action | Implementation |
|---|--------|----------------|
| **12** | Cultivate purpose and gratitude | Journaling, meaning |

## Evidence Base

### Strong Evidence
| Action | Outcome |
|--------|---------|
| **Eliminate UPF** | Reduced all-cause mortality |
| **Daily walking** | Reduced CVD, dementia |
| **7-9 hours sleep** | Reduced obesity, depression |
| **Social connection** | Reduced mortality (comparable to smoking) |

### Blue Zones Validation
| Region | Practices |
|--------|-----------|
| **Okinawa** | Plant-rich, social, purpose |
| **Sardinia** | Walking, wine, family |
| **Ikaria** | Naps, Mediterranean, community |
