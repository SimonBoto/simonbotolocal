## Core Concept

Evidence-based stress management strategies to regulate cortisol and support metabolic health across all five pillars.

## Key Principles

- **Cortisol Rhythm:** Maintaining healthy cortisol circadian rhythm is essential for metabolic balance
- **Stress Resilience:** Building physiological resilience to prevent chronic cortisol elevation
- **Mind-Body Techniques:** Evidence-based approaches including breathwork, meditation, and mindfulness
- **Lifestyle Integration:** Stress management as an integral component of daily metabolic health routine

## Clinical Significance

Effective stress management and cortisol regulation support all five pillars of metabolic health by optimizing mitochondrial function, normalizing metabolomic profiles, reducing glycation stress, maintaining mineral balance, and supporting healthy fatty acid metabolism.
