# INSR Mutations Cause Severe IR

Mutations in the  receptor gene cause extreme  syndromes, revealing the critical role of INSR in .

**Clinical syndromes:**

| Syndrome | Mutation Type | Clinical Features |
|----------|---------------|-------------------|
| Donohue (Leprechaunism) | Homozygous null | IUGR, failure to thrive, death in infancy |
| Rabson-Mendenhall | Homozygous hypomorphic | Acanthosis nigricans, pineal hyperplasia |
| Type A IR | Heterozygous | Severe IR, hyperandrogenism, PCOS |

**Molecular impact:**
- Reduced insulin binding
- Impaired kinase activity
- Defective autophosphorylation
- Accelerated receptor degradation

**Implication:** Complete loss of INSR function is incompatible with life, demonstrating insulin's essential role.
