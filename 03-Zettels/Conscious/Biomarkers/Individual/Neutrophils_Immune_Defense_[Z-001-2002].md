# Neutrophils — First Line Immune Defense

## Overview
Most abundant white blood cells. Primary bacterial defense.

**See complete guide:** [[WBC_[B-001-0001].md|WBC Comprehensive Clinical Guide]]

## Quick Reference

| Level | % | Interpretation |
|-------|---|----------------|
| **Optimal** | 40-60% | Normal |
| **Accepted** | 40-70% | Standard range |
| **High** | >70% | Bacterial infection, acute inflammation |
| **Low** | <40% | Viral, chronic, immunosuppression |

## Clinical Context
- **Left shift** = immature neutrophils released = acute infection
- **Toxic granulation** = severe bacterial infection
- Always check absolute count, not just percentage
