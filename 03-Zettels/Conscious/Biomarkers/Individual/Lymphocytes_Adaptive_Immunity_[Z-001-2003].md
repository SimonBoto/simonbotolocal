# Lymphocytes — Adaptive Immune Response

## Overview
White blood cells responsible for adaptive immunity: T-cells, B-cells, NK cells.

**See complete guide:** [[WBC_[B-001-0001].md|WBC Comprehensive Clinical Guide]]

## Quick Reference

| Level | % | Interpretation |
|-------|---|----------------|
| **Optimal** | 25-35% | Normal |
| **Accepted** | 20-40% | Standard range |
| **High** | >40% | Viral, chronic infection, lymphocytic leukemia |
| **Low** | <20% | Chronic stress, immunosuppression, cortisol |

## Clinical Context
- **High with low neutrophils** = viral pattern
- **Low in chronic disease** = immune exhaustion
- **Low with high neutrophils** = stress response
