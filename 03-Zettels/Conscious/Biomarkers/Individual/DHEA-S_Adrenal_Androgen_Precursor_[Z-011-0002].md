# DHEA-S — Adrenal Androgen Precursor

## 1. ETYMOLOGY

**DHEA:** Dehydroepiandrosterone

**DHEA-S:** DHEA sulfate (sulfated form, more stable)

**Also called:**
- Prasterone
- Androstenolone

**Discovery:** 1934 by Adolf Butenandt (Nobel Prize 1939)

## 2. PHYSIOLOGY

### Biological Function

**Most abundant circulating steroid:**
- Produced in adrenal cortex (zona reticularis)
- Weak androgen
- Precursor to sex hormones

**Conversion pathway:**
```
DHEA-S → DHEA → Androstenedione → Testosterone → Estradiol
```

**Functions:**
| Function | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Androgen precursor** | Source of testosterone, estradiol |
| **Immune modulation** | Enhances immune function |
| **Neuroprotection** | Brain health |
| **Anti-aging** | Declines with age |
| **Bone health** | Supports bone density |

### Age-Related Decline

**Peak:** Early 20s (20-30 mg/day)
**Decline:** ~10% per decade after 30
**Elderly:** 10-20% of peak levels

| **30-39** | 120-520 | 45-270 |
| **40-49** | 95-530 | 32-240 |
| **50-59** | 70-310 | 26-200 |
| **60-69** | 42-290 | 13-130 |

### Clinical Significance

**Low DHEA-S associated with:**
- Adrenal insufficiency
- Aging
- Chronic illness
- Depression
- Cognitive decline
- Cardiovascular disease
- Osteoporosis

**High DHEA-S associated with:**
- PCOS (women)
- Adrenal hyperplasia
- Adrenal tumors (rare)
- Supplement use

| Factor | Effect |
|--------|--------|

| **Aging** | Declines naturally |
| **Stress** | May alter production |
| **Insulin** | Associated with levels |

## 5. INTERVENTIONS

### For Low DHEA-S

**DHEA supplementation:**
- Dose: 10-50 mg/day (women: lower dose)
- Monitor: Androgen levels, side effects
- Contraindications: Hormone-sensitive cancers

**Benefits (evidence mixed):**
- Mood improvement
- Libido enhancement
- Bone density
- Skin quality

**Risks:**
- Androgenic side effects (acne, hair loss)
- Hormone-sensitive cancer risk
- Cardiovascular effects unclear

### For High DHEA-S

**Investigate cause:**
- PCOS workup (women)
- Adrenal imaging if very high
- Review supplements
