## The Barrier

"Daily exercise" requirement stops many from starting:
- Too time-intensive
- Inflexible schedule
- All-or-nothing mindset
- Perfectionism paralysis

## The Evidence

**JAMA 2023 study shows:**
- 1-2 days/week: **50%+ mortality reduction**
- 3-7 days/week: Additional incremental benefit
- No "daily" requirement for major effect

## Clinical Application

**Prescribe the minimum effective dose first:**

1. Start with 1-2 days/week
2. 8,000 steps per session
3. Build habit before increasing frequency
4. Add days only after consistency achieved

## Behavioral Principle

> Something is infinitely more than nothing.

**Compliance beats perfection.**

A patient who walks 1x/week for life gains more than one who plans daily walks but never starts.
