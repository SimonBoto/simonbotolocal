# Trials.gov Registration Structure

## Overview
ClinicalTrials.gov is the NIH-maintained registry for clinical studies. Registration is mandatory for FDA-regulated trials and strongly encouraged for all clinical .

## Registration Timing
| Milestone | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| **Before enrollment** | Registration must be submitted |
| **Within 21 days** | Of first participant enrollment |
| **After IRB approval** | Optimal timing for METHAP |

## Required Sections (14 Total)

### 1. Study Identification
- Official Title (full scientific)
- Brief Title (<300 characters)
- Acronym
- Unique Protocol ID

### 2. Study Status
- Recruitment status
- Actual study start date
- Primary completion date
- Study completion date

### 3. Sponsor and Collaborators
- Primary sponsor
- Collaborators
- Funding sources

### 4. Oversight
- Human subjects review (IRB/Ethics Committee)
- Data Monitoring Committee (if applicable)
- FDA-regulated product (if applicable)

### 5. Study Description
- Brief summary (lay-friendly)
- Detailed description (scientific)

### 6. Conditions and Keywords
- Conditions studied
- Keywords for searchability

### 7. Study Design
- Study type (interventional/observational)
- Phase (if applicable)
- Intervention model
- Allocation (randomized/non-randomized)
- Masking (blinding)
- Primary purpose

### 8. Arms and Interventions
- Arm descriptions
- Intervention details
- Dosage/duration (if applicable)

### 9. Outcome Measures
- Primary outcomes (timeframe + measure)
- Secondary outcomes
- Other outcomes (optional)

### 10. Eligibility
- Sex/gender
- Age limits
- Inclusion criteria
- Exclusion criteria
- Healthy volunteers (yes/no)

### 11. Contacts and Locations
- Central contact
- Facility information
- Location (city, country)

### 12. IPD Sharing Statement
- Will individual participant data be shared?
- When and with whom?
- Under what conditions?

### 13. References
- Background publications
- Related trials

### 14. Administrative Information
- Responsible party
- Study official (PI)
- Sponsor information

## Writing Principles

### Scientific Neutrality
| ❌ Avoid | ✅ Use Instead |
|----------|----------------|
| "This rary treatment..." | "This intervention aims to..." |
| "Will prove that..." | "Will evaluate whether..." |
| "Significant improvement" | "Change in [specific measure]" |

### Clarity
- Define all abbreviations at first use
- Use specific measurements (not vague terms)
- Include units for all quantities

### Completeness
- No section left blank without justification
- All dates realistic and consistent
- Contact information current and accurate

## METHAP-Specific Considerations

### Study Type Classification
| Aspect | Classification |
|--------|----------------|
| **Type** | Interventional |
| **Phase** | Not Applicable (dietary/supplement) |
| **Purpose** | Diagnostic + Treatment |
| **Masking** | None (open label) |

### Intervention Description
- Be specific about supplement dosages
- Clarify dietary intervention components
- Note EOΦ-approved products

### Outcome Measures
- Primary: Metabolomic discrimination accuracy
- Secondary: Clinical parameters (HOMA-IR, HbA1c, etc.)
- Timeframes clearly specified
