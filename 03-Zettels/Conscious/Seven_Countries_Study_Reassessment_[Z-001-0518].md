# Seven Countries Study — Reassessment

> Ancel Keys' Seven Countries Study shaped global dietary guidelines, but modern reassessment reveals complexities in the saturated fat-heart disease hypothesis.

## Original Study (1958-1964)

**Design:** 12,763 men, 16 cohorts, 7 countries
**Key Finding:** Saturated fat correlated with CHD
**Influence:** Basis for low-fat guidelines

## Critiques

**Selection Bias:**
- Countries selected post-hoc
- France excluded (low CHD, high saturated fat)

**Confounding:**
- Sugar not controlled
- Processed vs. traditional fats
- Smoking differences

## Reassessment Findings

**Protective Factor:**
- **Mediterranean pattern** truly protective
- **Olive oil/oleic acid** strongest predictor
- MUFA > saturated fat avoidance

**Public Health Impact:**
- Saturated fat demonized
- Seed oils promoted
- Refined carbs increased
- Metabolic epidemic followed
