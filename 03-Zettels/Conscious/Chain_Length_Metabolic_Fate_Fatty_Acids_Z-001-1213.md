## Core Claim
**Fatty acid chain length determines absorption, transport, and metabolic fate — with short-chain fatty acids (SCFAs, <C6) being directly absorbed and used for gut health, medium-chain fatty acids (MCFAs, C6-C12) bypassing carnitine transport for rapid ketogenesis, long-chain fatty acids (LCFAs, C13-C21) requiring standard β-oxidation, and very long-chain fatty acids (VLCFAs, >C22) requiring peroxisomal processing — with each class having distinct physiological roles and clinical applications.**

## Evidence

### Classification by Chain Length
| Category | Carbons | Examples | Primary Source |
|----------|---------|----------|----------------|
| **Short-chain (SCFA)** | <6 | Butyrate (C4), Propionate (C3), Acetate (C2) | Gut microbiome, fiber fermentation |
| **Medium-chain (MCFA)** | 6-12 | Caprylic (C8), Capric (C10), Lauric (C12) | Coconut oil, MCT oil, palm kernel |
| **Long-chain (LCFA)** | 13-21 | Palmitic (C16), Oleic (C18), AA (C20) | Most dietary fats, animal/vegetable |
| **Very long-chain (VLCFA)** | >22 | DHA (C22), Nervonic acid (C24) | Fish oil, brain lipids |

### Metabolic Characteristics
| Feature | SCFA | MCFA | LCFA | VLCFA |
|---------|------|------|------|-------|
| **Absorption** | Direct (colon) | Direct (portal) | Micelle-dependent | Micelle-dependent |
| **Transport** | Portal blood | Portal blood | Chylomicrons | Chylomicrons/VLDL |
| **Carnitine required** | No | No | Yes | Yes |
| **β-oxidation site** | Mitochondria | Mitochondria | Mitochondria | Peroxisomes → mitochondria |
| **Ketogenic potential** | Moderate | High | Moderate | Low |

### Physiological Roles
| Chain Length | Primary Roles | Clinical Applications |
|--------------|---------------|----------------------|
| **SCFA** | Gut barrier, immune modulation, energy for colonocytes | IBD treatment, gut health |
| **MCFA** | Rapid energy, ketogenesis, weight management | MCT oil for epilepsy, ketogenic diets |
| **LCFA** | Energy storage, membrane structure, hormone precursors | Standard dietary fats |
| **VLCFA** | Brain structure, neural function, specialized membranes | DHA for development, cognitive health |

### Special Considerations
| Aspect | Implication |
|--------|-------------|
| **MCFA in liver disease** | Bypasses impaired carnitine shuttle |
| **SCFA in gut dysbiosis** | Reduced production linked to inflammation |
| **VLCFA accumulation** | Adrenoleukodystrophy (X-ALD) |
| **LCFA overload** | Insulin resistance, steatosis |

## Clinical Implication
- **Therapeutic targeting:** MCFAs for metabolic disorders
- **Gut health:** SCFAs critical for colonocyte energy
- **Brain health:** VLCFAs (DHA) essential for development
- **Metabolic flexibility:** Chain length affects oxidation efficiency
- **METHAP relevance:** Chain length distribution affects metabolic health and insulin sensitivity
