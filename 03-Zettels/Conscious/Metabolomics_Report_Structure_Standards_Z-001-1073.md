# Metabolomics Report Structure Standards

Live and email report formats—concise, visual, actionable. These standards ensure patients understand their results and know exactly what to do next.

## Evidence
> "A report that confuses the patient is worse than no report at all. Clarity drives compliance; complexity drives dropout." (Medical communication design)

## Core Principles

### 1. Patient-First Language
| ❌ Don't | ✅ Do |
|----------|-------|
| "Elevated methylmalonate" | "B12 status needs support" |
| "Mitochondrial dysfunction pattern" | "Energy system needs fuel" |
| "Dysbiosis markers elevated" | "Gut balance needs attention" |

### 2. Visual Hierarchy
- **Red/Yellow/Green** — Immediate visual assessment
- **Traffic light system** — Patients understand instantly
- **Icons** — Supplement, diet, lifestyle actions

### 3. Actionable Next Steps
Every section ends with: "What to do"

## Live Report Format (In-Person)

### Section 1: The Story (5 minutes)
> "Your OAT tells a story about your metabolism. Here's what it's saying..."

- **One-sentence summary** — "Your energy production and gut balance need support"
- **Big picture** — Visual overview (traffic light)
- **Connect to symptoms** — "This explains your fatigue and bloating"

### Section 2: The Categories (10 minutes)
Walk through each category:

| Category | Status | Key Finding | Action |
|----------|--------|-------------|--------|
| **Energy** | 🔴 | Power plants need fuel | CoQ10, B-vitamins |
| **Gut** | 🟡 | Some imbalance | Probiotics, diet |
| **Oxidative** | 🟢 | Good | Maintain |

### Section 3: The Plan (10 minutes)
**Phase 1 (Weeks 1-4):**
- Supplement list (3-5 items max)
- Diet focus (one change)
- Lifestyle (sleep, movement)

**Phase 2 (Weeks 5-8):**
- Add based on response
- Retest timeline

### Section 4: Questions (5 minutes)
- "What questions do you have?"
- Address objections
- Confirm understanding

## Email Report Format

### Subject Line
> "Your Metabolomics Results + Personalized Plan — [Patient Name]"

### Email Structure

**Opening:**
> Hi [Name],
> 
> Thank you for completing your organic acids test. Your results tell a clear story about your metabolism. Here's what we found and what to do next.

**The Overview:**
> ## Your Metabolic Status
> 
> 🔴 **Needs Support:** Energy production, Gut balance
> 🟡 **Watch:** Detoxification capacity  
> 🟢 **Good:** Oxidative stress, Methylation

**The Details:**
> ## Category Breakdown
> 
> ### Energy Metabolism 🔴
> **Finding:** Your lactate/pyruvate ratio suggests your cells are working harder than they should to make energy.
> 
> **What this means:** You may feel tired, especially after exertion.
> 
> **Action:** CoQ10 (100mg twice daily), B-complex, magnesium
> 
> ---
> 
> ### Gut Microbiome 🟡
> **Finding:** Some bacterial metabolites are elevated.
> 
> **What this means:** Your gut bacteria may be out of balance.
> 
> **Action:** Probiotic (50 billion CFU), reduce sugar, add fermented foods

**The Plan:**
> ## Your 8-Week Protocol
> 
> ### Phase 1 (Weeks 1-4)
> **Supplements:**
> - CoQ10 100mg — morning
> - B-complex — with breakfast
> - Magnesium glycinate 400mg — evening
> - Probiotic — morning, empty stomach
> 
> **Diet:**
> - Add: 2 cups leafy greens daily
> - Reduce: Sugar to < 25g/day
> 
> **Lifestyle:**
> - Sleep: 10pm bedtime target
> - Movement: 20 min walk daily
> 
> ### Phase 2 (Weeks 5-8)
> We'll add based on your response. Retest at week 8.

**Closing:**
> ## Next Steps
> 
> 1. Order supplements (links below)
> 2. Start Phase 1 Monday
> 3. Track symptoms daily (app link)
> 4. Schedule follow-up (calendar link)
> 
> Questions? Reply to this email or call the clinic.
> 
> To your health,  
> [Your name]

## Report Design Standards

### Visual Elements
- **Traffic lights** — Universal understanding
- **Progress bars** — For severity
- **Icons** — Quick recognition
- **White space** — Prevents overwhelm

### Typography
- **Headers:** Clear, bold
- **Body:** Readable (12pt minimum)
- **Actions:** Highlighted, bold

### Length
- **Live report:** 30 minutes max
- **Email report:** 2-3 scrolls max
- **PDF attachment:** Optional detailed version

## Quality Checklist

Before sending any report:
- [ ] Patient can explain findings back to you
- [ ] Actions are specific (not "eat better")
- [ ] Timeline is clear
- [ ] Next steps are obvious
- [ ] Contact info is prominent
