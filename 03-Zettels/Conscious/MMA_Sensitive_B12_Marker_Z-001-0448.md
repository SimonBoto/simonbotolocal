## Advantages

**Most Sensitive:**
- Elevates before serum B12 drops
- Early indicator of deficiency

**Specific for B12:**
- Not affected by  status
- Differentiates from folate deficiency

**Functional Marker:**
- Reflects tissue status
- Cellular/l B12

- Borderline serum B12 (200-400)
- Symptoms with normal B12
- Confirming deficiency
- Monitoring treatment
