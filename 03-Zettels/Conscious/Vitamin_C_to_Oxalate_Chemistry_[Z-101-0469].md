## Conversion Efficiency
**Not all ascorbate becomes oxalate:**
- Fraction excreted as oxalate varies by individual
- Remainder: metabolized through other pathways, direct excretion, antioxidant consumption
- Influencing factors: oxidative stress, metabolic state, enzyme activity, genetics

## PhD Relevance
- **Dietary confounder:** High vitamin C intake increases endogenous oxalate production
- **Measurement challenge:** Urine oxalate reflects dietary + metabolic sources
- **Adipic acid advantage:** No known conversion from vitamin C; cleaner metabolic signal
