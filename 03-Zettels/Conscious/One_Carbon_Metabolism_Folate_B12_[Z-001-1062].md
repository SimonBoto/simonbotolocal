# One Carbon [[KEYWORD-Metabolism|metabolism]] Folate B12

## Overview
Integrated pathway of folate and B12 for methylation reactions, DNA synthesis, and homocysteine metabolism.

## The Folate Cycle

### Dietary Folate → Active Forms
- **Dietary folate** → Dihydrofolate (DHF) → Tetrahydrofolate (THF)
- **5-MTHF (methylfolate):** Primary circulating form
- **10-formyl-THF:** DNA synthesis
- **5,10-methylene-THF:** Thymidine synthesis

### Key Enzymes
| Enzyme | Function | Polymorphism |
|--------|----------|--------------|
| **MTHFR** | Converts 5,10-CH₂-THF → 5-MTHF | C677T, A1298C |
| **MTR** | Uses B12 to remethylate homocysteine | Common variants |
| **MTRR** | Regenerates B12 (methionine synthase reductase) | Multiple SNPs |

## The Methionine Cycle

### Methionine → SAM → SAH → Homocysteine
```
Methionine + ATP → SAM (methyl donor)
SAM → methyl transfer → SAH
SAH → Homocysteine
```

### Homocysteine Disposal
| Pathway | Requires | Product |
|---------|----------|---------|
| **Remethylation** | B12, folate, betaine | Methionine |
| **Transsulfuration** | B6 | Cysteine → Glutathione |

## B12-Folate Interdependence
- **B12 required for:** MTR activity (homocysteine → methionine)
- **Folate trap:** Without B12, folate accumulates as 5-MTHF (unusable)
- **Methylfolate trap:** Without folate, B12 cannot function

## [[KEYWORD-Clinical|clinical]] Markers

### Homocysteine
| Level | Risk |
|-------|------|
| **< 7 μmol/L** | Optimal |
| **7-10 μmol/L** | Mild elevation |
| **10-15 μmol/L** | Cardiovascular risk |
| **> 15 μmol/L** | High risk |

### OAT Markers
| Marker | Indicates |
|--------|-----------|
| **Methylmalonate ↑** | Functional B12 deficiency |
| **FIGLU ↑** | Functional folate deficiency |
| **Formate ↑** | Impaired one-carbon metabolism |

## Supplementation Strategy
| Deficiency | Form | Dose |
|------------|------|------|
| Folate | 5-MTHF (methylfolate) | 400-1000 mcg |
| B12 | Methylcobalamin + Adenosylcobalamin | 1000 mcg |
| B6 | P5P (active form) | 25-50 mg |
| Betaine | TMG (trimethylglycine) | 500-3000 mg |
