# HMG-CoA Reductase — Regulation and Inhibition

> HMG-CoA reductase is the committed, rate-limiting enzyme of the mevalonate pathway, making it the therapeutic target for statins.

## Structure

- Integral ER membrane protein
- Tetramer (4 subunits)
- Active site: cytosolic domain

## Regulation

**Transcriptional (SREBP-2):**
- Low  → HMGCR expression ↑
- High cholesterol → HMGCR expression ↓

**Post-translational:**
- AMPK phosphorylation → INACTIVATION
- Dephosphorylation → Activation
- Sterol abundance → Degradation

## Statin Inhibition

- Competitive inhibition with HMG-CoA
- 10,000x higher affinity than substrate
- Blocks ALL mevalonate products

## CoQ10 Depletion

**:**
- Statins block CoQ10 synthesis
- 40-50% reduction in CoQ10
- l dysfunction

**Mitigation:**
- CoQ10 supplementation: 100-200 mg/day
- Ubiquinol preferred
- Take 2-4 hours away from statin
