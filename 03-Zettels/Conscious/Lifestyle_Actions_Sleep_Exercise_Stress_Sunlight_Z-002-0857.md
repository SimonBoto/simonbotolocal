# Lifestyle Actions — Sleep, Exercise, Stress Management, Sunlight

## Core Claim
**The lifestyle component includes physical activity, sleep, stress management, outdoor time, and avoiding smoking/alcohol** — optimizing metabolic, immune, and mental health.

## Lifestyle Actions (5 of 12)
| Action | Benefit |
|--------|---------|
| **Regular physical activity** | Improves insulin sensitivity, cardiovascular fitness, mood |
| **Prioritize sleep quality** | Hormonal regulation, recovery, cognitive function |
| **Manage stress effectively** | Reduces cortisol, inflammation, metabolic dysfunction |
| **Outdoor time and sunlight** | Vitamin D synthesis, circadian regulation, mental health |
| **Stop smoking, limit alcohol** | Reduces oxidative stress, inflammation, cancer risk |

## Supplementation Bridge (2 of 12)
| Action | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| **Correct micronutrient deficiencies** | Addresses gaps not met by diet alone |
| **Optimize vitamin D levels** | Immune function, bone health, mood regulation |

## Synergistic Effects
| Combination | Benefit |
|-------------|---------|
| Exercise + Sleep | Enhanced recovery and adaptation |
| Sunlight + Vitamin D | Maximal immune benefit |
| Stress management + Sleep | Hormonal balance |
| Outdoor time + Exercise | Nature exposure benefits |

## Clinical Implementation
- Prioritize **sleep as foundation** (7-9 hours)
- Build **regular movement** into daily routine
- Practice **stress management** (meditation, breathing)
- Get **15-30 min sunlight** daily
- **Eliminate smoking**, keep alcohol minimal
