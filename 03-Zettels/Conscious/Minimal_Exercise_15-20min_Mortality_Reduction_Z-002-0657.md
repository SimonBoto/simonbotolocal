# Minimal Exercise Dose 15-20 Min Reduces Mortality 16-40%

## Core Claim
As little as **15-20 minutes of vigorous physical activity per week** provides **16-40% reduction in all-cause mortality**, demonstrating that exercise benefits follow a curve of diminishing returns where the smallest doses provide the greatest relative protection.

## The Evidence

### Polo-López 2024 Findings
| Exercise Dose | Mortality Reduction |
|---------------|---------------------|
| **15-20 min/week vigorous** | **16-40% lower risk** |
| ~25 min/week | 46% lower all-cause mortality |
| ~25 min/week | 58% lower CVD mortality |
| ~45 min/week | 37% lower cancer mortality |

### Key Insight
**The biggest bang for the buck is at the lowest doses.**

### Clinical Interpretation
| Message | Evidence Support |
|---------|------------------|
| "Some is better than none" | Strong — minimal dose has large effect |
| "More is always better" | Weak — diminishing returns |
| "All-or-nothing" | False — small amounts matter |

## Practical Implications

### For Sedentary Patients
| Barrier | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| "No time for exercise" | 15 min/week is achievable |
| "Gym membership required" | Home bodyweight exercises |
| "Need special equipment" | Sprinting, stairs, burpees |
| "Too out of shape" | Start with walking, progress |

### Minimal Effective Program
| Day | Activity | Duration |
|-----|----------|----------|
| **Monday** | Bodyweight circuit | 8-10 min |
| **Thursday** | Sprint intervals | 8-10 min |
| **Total** | — | ~20 min/week |

**Result:** 16-40% mortality reduction

## Evidence

> "Prospective cohort studies show dose-response relationship between vigorous exercise and mortality, with steep benefit curve at low doses (15-20 min/week) that gradually plateaus with additional activity."
> — Polo-López et al., 2024

## Comparison to Guidelines

### Traditional Guidelines
| Organization | Recommendation |
|--------------|----------------|
| **WHO** | 150-300 min moderate OR 75-150 min vigorous/week |
| **ACSM** | 150 min moderate or 75 min vigorous/week |

### The Gap
- **Guidelines:** 75+ min/week vigorous
- **Evidence:** Benefits start at 15-20 min/week
- **Implication:** Guidelines may discourage those who can't meet thresholds

### New Messaging
| Old | New |
|-----|-----|
| "You need 150 minutes" | "Start with 15 minutes" |
| "All-or-nothing" | "Every minute counts" |
| "Minimum effective dose" | "Any activity helps" |
