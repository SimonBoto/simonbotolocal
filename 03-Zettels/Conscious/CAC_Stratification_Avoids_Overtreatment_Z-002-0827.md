# CAC Stratification Avoids Overtreatment and Targets Therapy Appropriately

## Core Claim
**CAC score stratification helps avoid overtreatment in individuals without atherosclerotic plaque and appropriately target treatment to those with significant established disease** — personalizing statin therapy based on actual plaque burden rather than population risk estimates.

## Problem with Current Approach
- Statins prescribed based on **10-year risk calculators**
- FRAMINGHAM, ASCVD estimate **population risk**, not individual plaque burden
- Result: many low-risk individuals **overtreated**, many high-risk individuals **undertreated**

## CAC Solution: Personalized Medicine
| CAC Score | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| **0** | Avoid statins (no target for drug action) |
| **1-100** | Lifestyle intervention preferred |
| **101-399** | Individualized decision (age, other factors) |
| **>400** | Clear statin indication |

## Benefits of CAC Stratification
| Benefit | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Treat those who benefit** | CAC >400 or high-risk features |
| **Avoid treating those who don't** | CAC = 0 or minimal plaque |
| **Cost-effective** | CAC scan cost <1 year statin cost |
| **Minimal radiation** | ~1 mSv |
| **High predictive value** | Direct visualization of disease |
