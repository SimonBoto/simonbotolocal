## Core Claim
**Small dense LDL (sdLDL) is more atherogenic** than total LDL quantity — created by glucose exposure and oxidation, sdLDL penetrates endothelium more easily and is more oxidizable.

## sdLDL Characteristics

| Feature | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Small size** | Easier endothelial penetration |
| **Dense** | More atherogenic |
| **Oxidizable** | More inflammatory |

## Creation
| Driver | Result |
|--------|--------|
| **Glucose exposure** | Pattern B shift |
| **Oxidation** | sdLDL formation |
