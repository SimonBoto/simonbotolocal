# Olive Oil — The Mediterranean Advantage

> Olive oil, rich in monounsaturated oleic acid and polyphenols, provides metabolic stability and anti-inflammatory benefits that define the protective Mediterranean pattern.

## Composition

| Component | Content |
|-----------|---------|
| **Oleic acid** | 70-75% |
| **Linoleic acid** | 10% |
| **Saturated fat** | 15% |
| **Polyphenols** | Hydroxytyrosol, oleuropein |

## Advantages

**Metabolic Stability:**
- One double bond = oxidation resistant
- Heat stable for cooking
- Preserves membrane integrity

**Cellular Effects:**
- Improved  sensitivity
- Anti-inflammatory gene expression
- Enhanced lipid 

##  Evidence

**PREDIMED:**
- Mediterranean + olive oil
- 30% CVD reduction

**EPIC:**
- Dose-response mortality benefit

**Clinical Recommendation:**
> "Build fat base around monounsaturated-rich fats — especially olive oil — rather than seed oils."
