# Common Aldoses and Their Roles

## Aldohexoses (6-Carbon)

### Glucose
- **Role:** Primary energy source, blood sugar
- **Source:** Dietary carbohydrates, gluconeogenesis
- **:**  when dysregulated

### Galactose
- **Role:** Component of lactose (milk sugar)
- **Source:** Dairy products
- **Clinical:** Galactosemia (enzyme deficiency)

### Mannose
- **Role:** Glycoprotein synthesis, immune modulation
- **Source:** Fruits, some plants
- **Function:** Cell-cell recognition

## Aldopentoses (5-Carbon)

### Ribose
- **Role:** RNA backbone, ATP, coenzymes
- **Source:** PPP, diet
- **Clinical:** Ribose supplements for cardiac energetics

### Arabinose
- **Role:** Plant polysaccharides
- **Source:** Plant fiber
- **Use:** Biotechnology (protein expression)

### Xylose
- **Role:** Xylitol precursor
- **Source:** Wood, straw
- **Use:** Sugar-free products

## Aldotrioses (3-Carbon)

### Glyceraldehyde
- **Role:** Glycolysis intermediate (G3P)
- **Source:** Glucose 
- **Function:** Central metabolic intermediate
