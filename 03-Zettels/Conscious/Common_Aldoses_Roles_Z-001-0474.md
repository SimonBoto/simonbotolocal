# Common Aldoses and Their Roles

## Core Claim
**Aldoses are aldehyde-containing sugars classified by carbon number — aldohexoses (glucose, galactose, mannose) serve as primary energy sources and structural components, while aldopentoses (ribose, arabinose, xylose) form nucleic acids and polysaccharides** — each has distinct metabolic fates and clinical significance.

## Evidence

### Aldohexoses (6-Carbon)
| Aldose | Role | Source | Clinical Note |
|--------|------|--------|---------------|
| **Glucose** | Primary energy, blood sugar | Diet, gluconeogenesis | Diabetes when dysregulated |
| **Galactose** | Lactose component, glycoproteins | Dairy | Galactosemia (enzyme deficiency) |
| **Mannose** | Glycoprotein synthesis, immune modulation | Fruits, plants | Cell-cell recognition |

### Aldopentoses (5-Carbon)
| Aldose | Role | Source | Clinical Note |
|--------|------|--------|---------------|
| **Ribose** | RNA backbone, ATP, coenzymes | PPP, diet | Cardiac energetics supplements |
| **Arabinose** | Plant polysaccharides | Plant fiber | Biotechnology applications |
| **Xylose** | Xylitol precursor, hemicellulose | Wood, straw | Sugar-free products |

### Structural Features
- **All have aldehyde at C1** (reducing end)
- **Form pyranose rings** (6-membered, most stable)
- **Strong reducing agents** (positive Benedict's test)

## Clinical Implication
- **Galactosemia:** Autosomal recessive deficiency → infantile cataracts, liver disease
- **Ribose supplements:** Mixed evidence for cardiac energetics, fibromyalgia
- **Xylitol:** Dental caries prevention (non-cariogenic)
- **METHAP relevance:** Understanding sugar diversity informs dietary choices; glucose remains the primary regulated fuel
