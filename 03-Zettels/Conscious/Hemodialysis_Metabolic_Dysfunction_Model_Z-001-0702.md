# Hemodialysis as Extreme Metabolic Dysfunction Model

## Core Principle
**Hemodialysis patients represent an extreme metabolic dysfunction phenotype** — chronic inflammation, insulin resistance, dyslipidemia, and the lowest omega-3 blood levels recorded — making them a rigorous test population for metabolic interventions.

## Characteristics

### Metabolic Profile
- **Chronic inflammation** — persistent systemic inflammation
- **Insulin resistance** — severe metabolic dysregulation
- **Dyslipidemia** — abnormal lipid metabolism
- **Lowest omega-3 levels** — most deficient population studied

### Cardiovascular Risk
- **40-50% die from CVD** — dominant mortality cause
- **Annual mortality ~18%** — higher than heart failure or many cancers
- **Extreme risk phenotype** — if intervention works here, broader applicability likely

## Scientific Value
If omega-3 supplementation demonstrates CVD benefit in this population, it validates efficacy under maximum metabolic stress conditions.
