# sdLDL Quality Matters More Than LDL Quantity

## Core Claim
**Small dense LDL (sdLDL) is more atherogenic than total LDL quantity** — created by glucose exposure and oxidation, sdLDL represents "damaged" cholesterol that is more dangerous than the total LDL number.

## sdLDL Characteristics

| Feature | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Small size** | Easier endothelial penetration |
| **Dense** | More atherogenic |
| **Oxidized** | Triggers inflammation |
| **Pattern B** | Associated with insulin resistance |

## Creation Mechanisms

| Driver | Process |
|--------|---------|
| **Glucose exposure** | Glycation of LDL particles |
| **Oxidation** | Smoking, alcohol, processed foods |
| **Insulin resistance** | Shift to Pattern B phenotype |

## Quality vs. Quantity

| Measurement | Predictive Value |
|-------------|------------------|
| **Total LDL** | Modest |
| **LDL particle number** | Better |
| **sdLDL concentration** | **Best** |
| **Pattern A vs. B** | Critical |

## Clinical Implication

| Outdated | Updated |
|----------|---------|
| "Lower LDL at all costs" | "Improve LDL quality" |
| Focus on total number | Focus on particle characteristics |
| Statins alone | Lifestyle + metabolic health |

## Reduction Strategies

| Approach | Effect on sdLDL |
|----------|-----------------|
| **Low-carb diet** | Reduces Pattern B shift |
| **Reduce sugar** | Less glycation |
| **Stop smoking** | Less oxidation |
| **Exercise** | Improves insulin sensitivity |

## Evidence

> "Small dense LDL (sdLDL) — cholesterol that has shrunk from exposure to glucose and oxidation from smoking, alcohol, and processed foods — ranks #7 in risk factor importance, above total LDL."
> — CVD risk hierarchy
