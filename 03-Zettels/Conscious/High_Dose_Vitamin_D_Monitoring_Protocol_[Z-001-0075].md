# High-Dose  Monitoring Protocol

 studies (Gallo, Saadi, Gordon, Stoss therapy) demonstrate safety of doses 2-50x RDA with systematic monitoring. Essential parameters: serum 25(OH)D, , phosphate, creatinine/eGFR, urinary calcium/creatinine ratio.

**Monitoring schedule (based on research protocols):**
- **Baseline:** 25(OH)D, calcium, phosphate, creatinine, Mg
- **Week 4-8:** 25(OH)D (assess response)
- **Week 12:** Full panel including urinary calcium
- **Ongoing:** Every 3-6 months for maintenance

**Safety thresholds:**
- **Target 25(OH)D:** 40-60 ng/mL (100-150 nmol/L)
- **Upper safety limit:** <100 ng/mL (hypercalcemia risk increases)
- **Serum calcium:** <10.5 mg/dL (watch for hypercalcemia)
- **Urinary calcium/creatinine:** <0.20 (hypercalciuria screening)

**Stop/reduction criteria:**
- 25(OH)D >100 ng/mL
- Serum calcium >10.5 mg/dL
- Urinary Ca/Cr >0.20
- Symptoms: nausea, confusion, polyuria
