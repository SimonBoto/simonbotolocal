# Glucocorticoid Osteoporosis Prevention

## Management Guidelines

### Non-Pharmacologic
- **Vitamin D:** 1000–2000 IU/day (target 25(OH)D >30 ng/mL)
- **Calcium:** 1000–1200 mg/day
- **Exercise:** Weight-bearing and resistance training
- **Fall prevention:** Home assessment, vision evaluation

### Pharmacologic Therapy
| Risk Level | First-Line Therapy | Alternatives |
|------------|-------------------|--------------|
| **High** | Bisphosphonate (alendronate, risedronate) | Teriparatide (if severe) |
| **Moderate** | Bisphosphonate or Calcium/Vitamin D | Calcitonin (limited evidence) |
| **Low** | Calcium/Vitamin D only | Monitor |

### Special Considerations
- **Teriparatide:** Preferred for severe GIOP (T-score < -3.5 or fragility fracture)
- **Denosumab:** Alternative for patients intolerant to oral bisphosphonates
- **Avoid:** Estrogen therapy (not approved for GIOP)
