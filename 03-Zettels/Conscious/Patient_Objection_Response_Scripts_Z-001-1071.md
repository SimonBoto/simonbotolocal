# Patient Objection Response Scripts

## Overview
Pre-scripted responses to common patient objections—validated, reframed, actionable.

## Cost/Value Objections

### "This is too expensive"
**Response:**
> "I understand. Let's look at what you're already spending on symptoms that aren't resolving—medications, supplements that aren't targeted, lost work days. This is an investment in understanding the root cause rather than managing symptoms indefinitely."

### "Why not just take a multivitamin?"
**Response:**
> "Multivitamins are like using a sprinkler when you need a targeted hose. Your test shows specific deficiencies and blocks—we address those precisely, not generically."

## Skepticism Objections

### "My doctor says this is nonsense"
**Response:**
> "Your doctor cares about you—that's why they speak up.  is newer, but it's based on  textbooks. The markers we measure are real molecules with known functions. Would you like to see the research?"

### "This isn't FDA approved"
**Response:**
> "The FDA approves drugs and devices. What we do is clinical —using vitamins, minerals, and diet changes. These are substances your body already uses; we're just optimizing levels based on your specific test results."

## Complexity Objections

### "This is too complicated"
**Response:**
> "It seems that way at first, but we break it into steps. Week 1: start these 3 supplements. Week 2: add these dietary changes. You'll have a simple protocol, and we check in regularly to adjust."

### "I don't understand the science"
**Response:**
> "You don't need to understand the biochemistry—you need to understand what to do. Think of me as your translator: I read the complex test, I tell you the simple actions."

## Compliance Objections

### "Too many supplements"
**Response:**
> "We start with the highest-impact 3-4. As you improve, we often reduce. This is temporary, targeted support—not forever."

### "I can't change my diet"
**Response:**
> "Let's find the minimum effective change. What's one meal you could adjust? Small wins build momentum."

## Previous Failure Objections

### "Tried everything, nothing works"
**Response:**
> "Until now, everything was guesswork. This test shows us exactly what's happening metabolically. We're not trying random solutions—we're addressing your specific biochemistry."

## Science Challenge Objections

### "Where's the evidence?"
**Response:**
> "Great question. Organic acids testing has decades of clinical use. The metabolites we measure—lactate, pyruvate, methylmalonate—are textbook biochemistry. Would you like to see the references?"
