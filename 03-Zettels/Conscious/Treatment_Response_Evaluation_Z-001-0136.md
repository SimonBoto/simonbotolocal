## Response Criteria

| Parameter | Responding | Non-responding | Action |
|-----------|------------|----------------|--------|
| **DXA (spine)** | ≥3% increase or stabilization | >3% decrease | Reassess adherence, consider switch |
| **DXA (hip)** | ≥4% increase or stabilization | >4% decrease | Reassess adherence, consider switch |
| **Fractures** | No new fragility fractures | New fragility fracture | Comprehensive assessment |
| **BTMs** | >30% change (P1NP↑, CTX↓) | <30% change | Assess adherence, consider switch |

## Comprehensive Assessment Protocol

When non-response is identified:

1. **Assess adherence:** Pill count, injection technique verification, side effect management
2. **Evaluate secondary causes:** Malabsorption, endocrine disorders, medications, lifestyle
3. **Review treatment duration:** Ensure appropriate timeframe for response assessment
4. **Consider therapy switch:** From antiresorptive to anabolic or vice versa
5. **Reassess fracture risk:** Update FRAX score with new clinical information

## Clinical Decision Framework

- **DXA + BTMs + Clinical:** Triangulate assessment for most accurate response evaluation
- **Timeframe:** DXA response in 12-24 months, BTMs in 3-6 months, clinical in 6-12 months
- **Documentation:** Record all assessment components and rationale for treatment decisions
