## Core Claim
**Minimally processed foods provide nutrients and improve health** — any degree of processing removes nutrients and increases harmful content.

## Minimally Processed Options

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Animal proteins** | Meat, fish, seafood, eggs |
| **Dairy** | Cheese, yogurt (full-fat, unsweetened) |
| **Healthy fats** | Olive oil, olives, avocado |
| **Nuts/seeds** | Natural, raw or dry-roasted |
| **Vegetables** | Fresh or frozen, unprocessed |
| **Fruits** | Whole, fresh or frozen |
| **Legumes** | Dried or canned (no additives) |

## Processing Principle

| Degree | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Any processing** | Removes nutrients |
| **Any processing** | Increases harmful content |
| **Industrial processing** | Most damaging |

## Comparison

| UPF | Minimally Processed Alternative |
|-----|--------------------------------|
| Cornflakes | Eggs, oatmeal |
| Margarine | Butter, olive oil |
| Instant pasta | Whole grains, legumes |
| Packaged snacks | Nuts, cheese, fruit |
| Ready meals | Home-cooked meals |
| Soda | Water, tea |

## Health Benefits

| Benefit | Mechanism |
|---------|-----------|
| **Nutrient density** | Vitamins, minerals intact |
| **Satiety** | Fiber, protein, water content |
| **Metabolic health** | Stable glucose, insulin |
| **Gut health** | Fiber feeds microbiome |

## Practical Transition

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| **1** | Eliminate obvious UPF (soda, chips, candy) |
| **2** | Replace packaged snacks with whole foods |
| **3** | Cook meals from scratch |
| **4** | Choose restaurants carefully |
