# One Carbon Metabolism Folate B12

The folate-B12-methionine-homocysteine pathway for methylation, DNA synthesis, and cellular repair. Critical for neurological health, pregnancy, and cardiovascular protection.

## Evidence
> "One-carbon metabolism is the molecular foundation of epigenetics — B12 and folate are its gatekeepers." (Nutritional epigenetics)

## The Pathway

### Folate Cycle
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Entry** | Dietary folate → dihydrofolate (DHF) |
| **Activation** | DHF → tetrahydrofolate (THF) |
| **Methyl donor** | 5-MTHF (methylfolate) |
| **Function** | Donates methyl group to B12 |

### B12 (Cobalamin) Role
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Forms** | Methylcobalamin, adenosylcobalamin |
| **Function** | Accepts methyl from 5-MTHF; donates to homocysteine |
| **Result** | Methionine synthesis |

### Homocysteine → Methionine
- **Enzyme:** Methionine synthase
- **Cofactor:** B12 (methylcobalamin)
- **Methyl donor:** 5-MTHF
- **Critical:** Requires both B12 AND folate

## Clinical Markers

### Homocysteine
| Level | Interpretation |
|-------|----------------|
| < 8 μmol/L | Optimal |
| 8-12 μmol/L | Mild elevation |
| > 12 μmol/L | Deficiency/increased CV risk |
| > 15 μmol/L | Significant dysfunction |

### OAT Markers
- **Methylmalonate** → B12 deficiency (specific)
- **FIGLU** → Folate deficiency (specific)
- **Formiminoglutamate** → Folate deficiency

## Clinical Consequences of Deficiency

| System | B12 Deficiency | Folate Deficiency |
|--------|----------------|-------------------|
| **Neurological** | Neuropathy, cognitive decline, dementia risk | Neural tube defects (pregnancy) |
| **Hematological** | Macrocytic anemia | Macrocytic anemia |
| **Cardiovascular** | Elevated homocysteine → CV risk | Elevated homocysteine → CV risk |
| **Psychiatric** | Depression, fatigue | Depression, fatigue |

## Supplementation Strategy

### B12
- **Forms:** Methylcobalamin + adenosylcobalamin (both active)
- **Dose:** 1000-5000 mcg/day
- **Route:** Sublingual or oral (high doses)

### Folate
- **Form:** 5-MTHF (methylfolate) — active, bypasses MTHFR
- **Dose:** 400-1000 mcg/day
- **Caution:** Never folic acid (synthetic, can mask B12)

### Together
- Always supplement B12 + folate together
- Imbalance can mask deficiency or worsen neurological issues
