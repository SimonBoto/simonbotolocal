# Olive Oil — Special Populations

> Specific populations including metabolic syndrome, , and cardiovascular disease patients show particular benefit from olive oil-based interventions.

## Metabolic Syndrome

**Benefits:**
- Improves all components
- Reduces TG, increases HDL
- Improves  sensitivity
- Reduces BP and central obesity

## Type 2 Diabetes

**Glycemic Control:**
- Reduces HbA1c
- Improves postprandial glucose
- May reduce medication needs

**CVD Protection:**
- High CVD risk in T2DM
- Mediterranean particularly beneficial

## Cardiovascular Disease

**Secondary Prevention:**
- LYON: 73% event reduction
- Improves endothelial function
- Reduces 

**Primary Prevention:**
- PREDIMED: 30% reduction

## Weight Management

**Paradox:**
- High-fat Mediterranean not obesogenic
- Improved satiety
- Better adherence

## Cognitive Health

- Reduces cognitive decline
- Polyphenols neuroprotective
- Potential AD prevention

## Safety

**Generally Safe:**
- Well-tolerated
- GRAS status

**Considerations:**
- Calorie density (moderate)
- Gallbladder disease
- Rare allergy
