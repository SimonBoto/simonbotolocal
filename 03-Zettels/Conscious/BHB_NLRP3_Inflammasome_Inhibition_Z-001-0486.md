## Core Claim
**BHB directly inhibits the NLRP3 inflammasome — a multi-protein complex that activates caspase-1, cleaving pro-IL-1β and pro-IL-18 into active inflammatory cytokines — by blocking NLRP3 ATPase activity and preventing ASC oligomerization, BHB reduces IL-1β/IL-18 production and pyroptosis** — this anti-inflammatory mechanism is independent of BHB's energy function.

## Evidence

### NLRP3 Inflammasome Components
| Component | Function |
|-----------|----------|
| **NLRP3** | NOD-like receptor (sensor) |
| **ASC** | Adaptor protein (oligomerization) |
| **Caspase-1** | Protease (activation) |

### Activation Triggers
| Trigger | Example |
|---------|---------|
| **PAMPs** | Bacterial components |
| **DAMPs** | ATP, uric acid crystals |
| **Crystals** | Cholesterol, monosodium urate |
| **Mitochondrial ROS** | Oxidative stress |

### BHB Inhibition Mechanism
| Step | BHB Effect |
|------|------------|
| **ATPase activity** | Blocks NLRP3 ATP hydrolysis |
| **ASC oligomerization** | Prevents ASC speck formation |
| **Caspase-1 activation** | Reduces active caspase-1 |

### Result
```
↓ IL-1β production
↓ IL-18 production  
↓ Pyroptosis (inflammatory cell death)
```

### Clinical Relevance
| Condition | NLRP3 Role | BHB Potential |
|-----------|------------|---------------|
| **Gout** | Urate crystals activate NLRP3 | Anti-inflammatory |
| **Atherosclerosis** | Cholesterol crystals | Plaque stabilization |
| **Alzheimer's** | Amyloid-β activation | Neuroprotection |
| **Metabolic syndrome** | Chronic low-grade inflammation | Systemic benefit |

## Clinical Implication
- **Chronic inflammation:** BHB may reduce NLRP3-driven inflammation
- **Autoimmune diseases:** Potential therapeutic application
- **Metabolic syndrome:** IL-1β drives insulin resistance
- **METHAP relevance:** Anti-inflammatory effect of ketosis complements metabolic benefits; dual mechanism for metabolic syndrome reversal
