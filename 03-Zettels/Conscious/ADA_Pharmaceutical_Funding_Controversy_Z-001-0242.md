## Conflict of Interest Concerns

**Issues Raised:**
1. **Insulin pricing:** Donors charge high prices for insulin
2. **Guideline influence:** Potential to favor donor products
3. **Research priorities:** May align with commercial interests
4. **Independence:** Perceived and actual conflicts

**Examples:**
- Insulin prices increased dramatically while ADA received insulin-maker funding
- Guidelines may favor pharmaceutical over lifestyle interventions
- Conservative approach to alternative therapies

**Defense by ADA:**
- Firewalls between funding and guidelines
