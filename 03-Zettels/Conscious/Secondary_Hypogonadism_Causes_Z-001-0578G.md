## Genetic Causes

- Kallmann syndrome (anosmia + hypogonadism)
- Congenital hypogonadotropic hypogonadism
- Prader-Willi syndrome
- Bardet-Biedl syndrome

## Acquired Causes

- Pituitary adenomas (prolactinomas, non-functioning tumors)
- Craniopharyngiomas
- Sellar/suprasellar tumors
- Traumatic brain injury
- Radiation to hypothalamic-pituitary axis

## Functional Causes

- Obesity
- Type 2 diabetes
- Chronic opioid use
- Hyperprolactinemia
- Chronic systemic illness

## Clinical Features

- Low or normal LH and FSH
- Low testosterone
- Variable testicular size
- May have other pituitary hormone deficiencies
