## Core Finding

**8,000 steps taken 1-2 times per week:**
- Reduces all-cause mortality by **>50%** over 10 years
- **70% reduction in men**
- **50% reduction in women**
- Additional benefit at 3-7 days/week

## Study
- **Source:** JAMA Network Open. 2023
- **Design:** Prospective cohort
- **Population:** US adults
- **Follow-up:** 10 years

## Clinical Significance

Effect size of this magnitude is rare in medical literature. Comparable to:
- Smoking cessation
- Statin therapy (for high-risk)
- Blood pressure control

## Minimum Effective Dose

The critical insight: **1-2x/week is sufficient** for major benefit.

This removes the barrier of "daily exercise" that prevents many from starting.
