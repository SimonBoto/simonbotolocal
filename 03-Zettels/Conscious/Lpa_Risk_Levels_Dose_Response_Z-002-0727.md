# Lp(a) Risk Levels Show Dose-Response

## Core Claim
**Lp(a) risk increases in dose-response fashion** — ~10% risk increase for every 20 mg/dL elevation.

## Risk Levels

| Level | Interpretation |
|-------|----------------|
| <30 mg/dL | Not increased |
| 30-50 mg/dL | Moderate increase |
| >50 mg/dL | Significant (50%+) |

## Dose-Response
| Increase | Risk Change |
|----------|-------------|
| +20 mg/dL | ~10% higher |
| +40 mg/dL | ~20% higher |
