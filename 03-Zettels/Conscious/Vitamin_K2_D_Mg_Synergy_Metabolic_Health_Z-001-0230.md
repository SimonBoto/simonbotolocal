# Vitamin K2 D Mg Synergy Metabolic Health

> **Core Claim:** Vitamin D, K2, and magnesium work synergistically for optimal vascular and metabolic health — the triad prevents calcification while supporting bone metabolism.

## The Triad

| Nutrient | Primary Role | Mechanism |
|----------|--------------|-----------|
| **Vitamin D** | Increases calcium absorption | Enhances intestinal Ca uptake |
| **Magnesium** | Prevents soft tissue calcification | Ca antagonist, supports MGP |
| **Vitamin K2** | Directs calcium to bone | Carboxylates MGP, osteocalcin |

## Synergistic Relationship

```
D increases calcium → K2 directs it to bone → Mg prevents arterial deposition
```

## Clinical Implications

- **High-dose D without K2** may promote vascular calcification (controversial)
- **Combined supplementation** optimal for bone and vascular health
- **Mg ensures** D activation and provides independent anti-calcification

## METHAP Protocol

| Nutrient | Dose |
|----------|------|
| K2 (MK-7) | 100-200 mcg daily |
| D3 | 8000 IU daily |
| Magnesium | Per individual needs |

## Key Insight

> **No nutrient works alone** — optimal metabolic health requires the complete triad.
