## Core Claim
**Ultra-processed foods disrupt the body's ability to regulate food intake**, leading to overconsumption, while causing insulin resistance, gut dysbiosis, and chronic inflammation.

## Mechanisms of Harm

| Mechanism | Effect |
|-----------|--------|
| **High calories, low nutrients** | Energy excess + deficiency states |
| **Satiety disruption** | Cannot regulate intake |
| **Overconsumption** | Weight gain, obesity |
| **Micronutrient deficiencies** | Vitamin/mineral gaps |
| **Insulin resistance** | Metabolic dysfunction |
| **Gut microbiome disruption** | Dysbiosis |
| **Chronic inflammation** | Systemic inflammation |

## The Vicious Cycle

```
UPF consumption
    ↓
Satiety disruption → Overeating
    ↓
Nutrient deficiencies + Excess calories
    ↓
Insulin resistance + Inflammation + Dysbiosis
    ↓
Metabolic disease + Weight gain
    ↓
More UPF cravings (addictive properties)
```

## Industrial Design

| Feature | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **Hyper-palatability** | Override natural satiety |
| **Rapid digestion** | Quick glucose spikes |
| **Low fiber/protein** | Poor satiety signaling |
| **Additives** | Enhance taste, hide poor quality |

## Reversal Strategy

| Action | Benefit |
|--------|---------|
| **Eliminate UPF** | Restore satiety signals |
| **Eat whole foods** | Proper nutrient intake |
| **Increase fiber/protein** | Improved satiety |
| **Time to eat** | Allow satiety to register |
