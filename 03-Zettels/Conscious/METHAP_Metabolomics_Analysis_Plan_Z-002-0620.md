## Key Metabolites of Interest

### Organic Acids (Urine)
- **Citric acid** — Krebs cycle intermediate
- **Aconitic acid** — Krebs cycle, iron-sulfur cluster 
- **Oxalic acid** — Glyoxylate metabolism, vitamin C degradation
- **Glyceric acid** — Glycerol metabolism
- **2-Ketoisovaleric acid** — Branched-chain amino acid metabolism
- **Acetoacetic acid** — Ketone body

### Free Fatty Acids (Plasma)
- **Alpha-linolenic acid** — Omega-3 essential fatty acid
- **Nervonic acid** — Myelin synthesis marker
- **Eicosatrienoic acid** — Eicosanoid precursor
- **Lauric acid** — Medium-chain fatty acid
