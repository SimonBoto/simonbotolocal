# l Supplementation Protocol

> Targeted supplementation based on organic acid patterns, combined with dietary modifications, restores  function.

## Tier 1 — Critical

| Nutrient | Dose | Form |
|----------|------|------|
| **Magnesium** | 200-400 mg elemental | Glycinate or citrate |
| **CoQ10** | 100-200 mg | Ubiquinol |
| **B-Complex** | High-quality | Activated forms |
| **L-Carnitine** | 500-2000 mg | L-carnitine or ALCAR |

## Tier 2 — Supporting

- **Iron:** If deficiency confirmed (Fe-S clusters)
- **Zinc:** 15-30 mg
- **Selenium:** 100-200 mcg

## Dietary

**Low-carb, high-fat:**
- Reduces glycolytic burden
- Shifts to ketone 
- Bypasses some TCA blocks

**Animal-based:**
- Bioavailable B-vitamins, iron, carnitine
- Organ meats, shellfish

## Monitoring

- **Retest:** 6-8 weeks
- **Expected:** Reduction in elevated markers
- **Clinical:** Energy, exercise tolerance
