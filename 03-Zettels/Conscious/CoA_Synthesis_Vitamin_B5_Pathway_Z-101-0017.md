# CoA Synthesis from Vitamin B5 Pathway

## Core Claim
**Coenzyme A (CoA) is synthesized from pantothenic acid (vitamin B5) through a five-step enzymatic pathway — involving phosphorylation, cysteine addition, decarboxylation, adenylation, and final phosphorylation — with pantothenate kinase (PanK) being the rate-limiting and regulated step, and defects in this pathway causing rare but severe neurological and metabolic disorders due to impaired fatty acid oxidation and energy metabolism.**

## Evidence

### Synthesis Pathway
| Step | Reaction | Enzyme | Regulation |
|------|----------|--------|------------|
| **1** | Pantothenate + ATP → 4'-phosphopantothenate | Pantothenate kinase (PanK) | Rate-limiting; inhibited by CoA/acyl-CoA |
| **2** | + Cysteine + ATP → 4'-phosphopantothenoylcysteine | PPCS | — |
| **3** | → 4'-phosphopantetheine + CO₂ | PPCDC | Decarboxylation |
| **4** | + ATP → dephospho-CoA + PPi | PPAT | Adenylation |
| **5** | + ATP → CoA + ADP | DPCK | Phosphorylation |

### Enzyme Details
| Enzyme | Gene | Function | Clinical Defect |
|--------|------|----------|-----------------|
| **PanK** | PANK | Rate-limiting kinase | PKAN (neurodegeneration) |
| **PPCS** | PPCS | Cysteine ligation | Rare, metabolic disorder |
| **PPCDC** | PPCDC | Decarboxylation | Rare, metabolic disorder |
| **PPAT** | PPAT | Adenylation | Not reported |
| **DPCK** | DPCK | Final phosphorylation | Not reported |

### CoA Compartmentalization
| Location | Function | Synthesis |
|----------|----------|-----------|
| **Mitochondria** | β-oxidation, Krebs cycle | Imported from cytosol |
| **Cytosol** | Fatty acid synthesis | Local synthesis |
| **Nucleus** | Histone acetylation | Imported |

## Clinical Implication
- **B5 deficiency:** Rare but causes fatigue, paresthesias
- **PKAN:** Pantothenate kinase-associated neurodegeneration (formerly Hallervorden-Spatz)
- **Iron accumulation:** PKAN associated with brain iron accumulation
- **No toxicity:** B5 water-soluble, excess excreted
- **METHAP relevance:** CoA essential for fatty acid metabolism and metabolic flexibility
