## Risk Assessment and Screening

### Risk Stratification
| Risk Level | Criteria | Intervention Timing |
|------------|----------|---------------------|
| **High** | ≥7.5 mg/day prednisone equivalents, age >65, prior fracture, T-score < -2.5 | Immediate (same day as starting GC) |
| **Moderate** | 2.5–7.5 mg/day, age 30–65, no prior fracture, T-score > -1.5 | Within 1 month |
| **Low** | <2.5 mg/day, age <30, no risk factors | Consider monitoring only |

### Baseline Assessment
- **DXA:** Lumbar spine and hip (spine most sensitive for GIOP)
- **FRAX:** Calculated (but underestimates GIOP risk)
- **Bone turnover markers:** P1NP and CTX (baseline for monitoring)

### Monitoring Frequency
- **High risk:** DXA every 12 months
- **Moderate risk:** DXA every 18–24 months
- **Low risk:** Clinical assessment only
