#  Steps and Regulation

## Overview
```
Glucose + 2 NAD⁺ + 2 ADP + 2 Pi → 2 Pyruvate + 2 NADH + 2 ATP + 2 H⁺ + 2 H₂O
```

## Investment Phase (Steps 1-5)

| Step | Reaction | Enzyme | Cost/Key |
|------|----------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Glucose → G6P | **Hexokinase** | -1 ATP, traps glucose |
| 2 | G6P → F6P | PGI | Isomerization |
| 3 | F6P → F1,6BP | **PFK-1** | -1 ATP, **committed step** |
| 4 | F1,6BP → DHAP + G3P | Aldolase | Cleavage |
| 5 | DHAP ↔ G3P | TPI | Equilibrium |

## Payoff Phase (Steps 6-10)

| Step | Reaction | Yield |
|------|----------|-------|
| 6 | G3P → 1,3-BPG | + NADH |
| 7 | 1,3-BPG → 3-PG | + ATP |
| 8 | 3-PG → 2-PG | Isomerization |
| 9 | 2-PG → PEP | Dehydration |
| 10 | PEP → Pyruvate | + ATP (PK) |

**Net yield:** 2 ATP, 2 NADH per glucose

## Key Regulatory Enzymes

### Hexokinase
- Inhibited by: G6P
- Isozymes: Glucokinase (liver, high Km)

### PFK-1 (Phosphofructokinase-1)
- **The committed step**
- Allosteric regulation:
  - **Inhibited by:** ATP, citrate
  - **Activated by:** AMP, F2,6BP, 

### Pyruvate Kinase
- Inhibited by: ATP, acetyl-CoA
- Activated by: F1,6BP (feed-forward)
