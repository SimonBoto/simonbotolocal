# Clinician Training Competency Checklist

Competency verification at each training stage before independent practice. Ensures new doctors can deliver consistent, high-quality metabolomics care.

## Evidence
> "Competency-based training ensures patients receive the same quality of care regardless of which clinician they see." (Medical education)

## Training Stages

### Stage 1: Knowledge Foundation
**Duration:** Weeks 1-4

**Competency Requirements:**
| Domain | Competency | Verification |
|--------|------------|--------------|
| **Biochemistry** | Explain Krebs cycle, OXPHOS, methylation | Oral exam |
| **OAT markers** | Identify 20+ markers and significance | Written test |
| **Patterns** | Recognize 5 major OAT patterns | Case review |
| **Supplements** | Know mechanisms, doses, interactions | Written test |

**Pass Criteria:**
- Written test: > 80%
- Oral exam: Satisfactory
- Case review: Correct pattern identification

### Stage 2: Interpretation Skills
**Duration:** Weeks 5-8

**Competency Requirements:**
| Domain | Competency | Verification |
|--------|------------|--------------|
| **OAT interpretation** | Systematic review by category | Supervised interpretation (10 cases) |
| **Pattern recognition** | Identify mitochondrial, gut, cofactor patterns | Case review |
| **Clinical correlation** | Match markers to symptoms | 10 case write-ups |
| **Treatment mapping** | Select appropriate interventions | Supervised prescribing (5 cases) |

**Pass Criteria:**
- Supervised interpretation: > 90% accuracy
- Case write-ups: Satisfactory
- Supervisor sign-off

### Stage 3: Patient Communication
**Duration:** Weeks 9-12

**Competency Requirements:**
| Domain | Competency | Verification |
|--------|------------|--------------|
| **Metabolic storytelling** | Explain results in patient language | Observed patient encounter (3) |
| **Objection handling** | Respond to common objections | Role-play (5 scenarios) |
| **Report delivery** | Present findings clearly | Observed report visit (3) |
| **Treatment explanation** | Explain rationale for recommendations | Patient feedback survey |

**Pass Criteria:**
- Observed encounters: Satisfactory ratings
- Role-play: Pass all scenarios
- Patient satisfaction: > 90%

### Stage 4: Independent Practice
**Duration:** Weeks 13-16

**Competency Requirements:**
| Domain | Competency | Verification |
|--------|------------|--------------|
| **Full patient flow** | Manage from assessment to follow-up | 10 supervised full cases |
| **Quality standards** | Maintain pre-analytical, analytical, post-analytical quality | Chart audit |
| **Documentation** | Complete, accurate EMR entries | Chart review |
| **Follow-up management** | Appropriate scheduling and monitoring | Chart audit |

**Pass Criteria:**
- 10 supervised cases: All satisfactory
- Chart audit: > 95% compliance
- No critical errors

## Competency Assessment Tools

### Written Tests
| Test | Content | Pass Score |
|------|---------|------------|
| **Biochemistry** | Metabolic pathways, mechanisms | 80% |
| **OAT markers** | Marker identification, significance | 85% |
| **Supplements** | Dosing, interactions, contraindications | 85% |
| **Case studies** | Interpretation, treatment planning | 80% |

### Practical Assessments
| Assessment | Method | Pass Criteria |
|------------|--------|---------------|
| **OAT interpretation** | 10 supervised cases | > 90% accuracy |
| **Patient encounters** | 3 observed visits | Satisfactory rating |
| **Report delivery** | 3 observed reports | Patient comprehension |
| **Role-play** | 5 objection scenarios | Appropriate response |

### Chart Audits
| Element | Standard | Measurement |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| **Documentation completeness** | All required fields | 100% |
| **Interpretation accuracy** | Correct pattern ID | > 95% |
| **Treatment appropriateness** | Evidence-based | > 95% |
| **Follow-up planning** | Clear timeline | 100% |

## Remediation Process

### When Competency Not Achieved
1. **Identify gaps** — Specific deficiencies
2. **Additional training** — Targeted education
3. **Re-assessment** — Repeat competency check
4. **Documentation** — Record remediation
