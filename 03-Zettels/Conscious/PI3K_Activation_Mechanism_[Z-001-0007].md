# Z_MECH: PI3K Activation

Class IA PI3K is a lipid kinase activated by IRS recruitment, generating the second messenger PIP3.

**PI3K structure:**
- **p85 regulatory subunit:** Contains SH2 domains (binds IRS phosphotyrosines)
- **p110 catalytic subunit:** Phosphorylates PI(4,5)P2 → PI(3,4,5)P3 (PIP3)

**Activation sequence:**
1. IRS tyrosine phosphorylation creates docking sites
2. p85 SH2 domains bind IRS YXXM motifs
3. p85 relieves p110 inhibition → catalytic activation
4. p110 generates PIP3 at plasma membrane

**Downstream:** PIP3 recruits Akt and PDK1 to membrane for Akt activation.

**Clinical:** PI3K inhibitors (cancer therapy) cause severe insulin resistance.
