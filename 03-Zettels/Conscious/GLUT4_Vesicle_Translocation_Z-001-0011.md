# GLUT4 Vesicle Translocation

 stimulates glucose uptake by triggering GLUT4 vesicle translocation to the plasma membrane in muscle and adipose tissue.

**:**
1. Akt phosphorylates AS160 (Akt substrate of 160 kDa) at multiple sites
2. Phosphorylated AS160 loses GAP activity for Rab proteins
3. Active Rab proteins promote GLUT4 vesicle trafficking
4. GLUT4 vesicles fuse with plasma membrane
5. GLUT4 now available for glucose transport

**Glucose transport:**
- GLUT4 Km ~5 mM (physiological glucose range)
- Without insulin: GLUT4 sequestered in intracellular vesicles
- With insulin: 10-20 fold increase in surface GLUT4

**Tissue specificity:**
- **Muscle:** Accounts for ~80% of insulin-stimulated glucose disposal
- **Adipose:** Less quantitative but metabolically significant
