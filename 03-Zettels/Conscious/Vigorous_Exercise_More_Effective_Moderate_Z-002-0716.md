# Vigorous Exercise More Effective Than Moderate

## Core Claim
**Vigorous exercise provides greater mortality reduction per minute** than moderate exercise — intensity matters more than duration for health outcomes.

## Comparison

| Type | Relative Benefit |
|------|------------------|
| **Vigorous (15 min)** | High per-minute |
| **Moderate (30 min)** | Lower per-minute |
| **Total benefit** | Vigorous wins at low doses |

## Practical Application
| Time-Limited | Recommendation |
|--------------|----------------|
| **15 min available** | Make it vigorous |
| **30 min available** | Mix vigorous + moderate |
