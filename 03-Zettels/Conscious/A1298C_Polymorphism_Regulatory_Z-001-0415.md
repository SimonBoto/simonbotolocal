# A1298C Polymorphism — Regulatory Variant

> The A1298C MTHFR polymorphism (rs1801131) substitutes glutamate with alanine in the regulatory domain, affecting enzyme regulation rather than catalytic activity, with homozygotes (CC) having ~60% enzyme activity.

## Genetics

**Substitution:**
- Glutamate → Alanine at position 429
- In regulatory domain
- Affects SAM inhibition sensitivity

**Genotypes:**
| Genotype | Population | Enzyme Activity |
|----------|------------|-----------------|
| AA (wild) | ~60-70% | 100% |
| AC (heterozygote) | ~25-30% | ~80% |
| CC (homozygote) | ~7-12% | ~60% |

## Effects

**Milder than C677T:**
- Less severe activity reduction
- Affects regulation, not catalysis
- Compounds with C677T

**Compound Heterozygote (C677T/A1298C):**
- One copy of each variant
- ~50-60% enzyme activity
- Significant  requirement increase

## 

**Associations:**
- Mood disorders (depression, anxiety)
- ADHD (controversial)
- Similar to C677T but milder

**Management:**
- Similar to C677T
- Methylfolate preferred
- Moderate-high folate intake
