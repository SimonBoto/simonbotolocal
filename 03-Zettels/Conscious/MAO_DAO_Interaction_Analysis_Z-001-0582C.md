# MAO-DAO Interaction Analysis

> **Core Claim:** MAO inhibitors do NOT directly affect DAO activity — they are highly selective for MAO isoforms only.

## Key Finding

**No cross-reactivity** between MAO inhibitors and DAO at therapeutic doses.

## Why No Interaction?

| Factor | MAO | DAO |
|--------|-----|-----|
| **Substrate specificity** | Monoamines | Diamines (histamine) |
| **Active site** | FAD-dependent | Copper-dependent |
| **Structure** | Different protein families |
| **Inhibitor binding** | Highly selective | No overlap |

## Clinical Implication

> **Patients on MAO inhibitors do NOT need histamine restriction** for DAO-related reasons.

## Common Misconception

Many clinicians incorrectly assume MAOIs affect histamine metabolism through DAO inhibition.

## Separate Pathways

```
MAO pathway:    Neurotransmitters → MAO → Metabolites
DAO pathway:    Histamine → DAO → Imidazole acetaldehyde
```
