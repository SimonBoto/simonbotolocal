## Core Concepts

### Clinical Biomarker

**Category:** Clinical Biomarker

**Key Process:** 
```
Albumin <2.5 g/dL → Significant edema risk; Trend > single value
```

**Mechanism/Steps:**
1. Maintains oncotic pressure and prevents edema
2. Acts as transport protein for drugs and hormones
3. Has antioxidant properties through thiol groups
4. Is an inverse acute phase reactant

**Purpose/Significance:** Critical prognostic indicator in CKD progression
