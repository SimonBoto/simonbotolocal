## Physiological Functions

The microbiome maintains homeostasis through:

1. **Metabolic pathway regulation** — Bacterial  complements human metabolism
2. **Immune system modulation** — Training and balancing immune responses
3. **Nutrient production** — SCFA (short-chain fatty acids) like **butyrate**
4. **Barrier protection** — Preventing pathogen colonization

## Gut-Metabolome Axis

**Critical connection:**
- Gut bacteria produce metabolites (SCFAs: butyrate, propionate, acetate)
- These enter host circulation
- Modulate host metabolism, inflammation, insulin sensitivity
