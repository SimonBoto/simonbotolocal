# Metabotypes — Individual Metabolic Phenotypes

A **metabotype** is an individual's specific metabolic profile that reflects what is actually happening in their body at a given moment under current conditions, manifesting a particular phenotype. It differs fundamentally from genotype, which represents the probability that a genetic variation will be expressed phenotypically.

## Metabotype vs. Genotype

| Feature | Genotype | Metabotype |
|---------|----------|------------|
| **Nature** | Static genetic potential | Dynamic functional state |
| **Time scale** | Lifetime | Minutes to hours |
| **Environmental sensitivity** | Fixed | Highly responsive |
| **Reflects** | What *could* happen | What *is* happening |
| **Clinical utility** | Risk prediction | Real-time status monitoring |

## Influencing Factors

### Unmodifiable (Baseline)
- Genes
- Age
- Sex
- Ethnicity

### Modifiable (Environmental)
| Factor | Metabolic Impact |
|--------|-----------------|
| **Diet** | Macronutrient flux, micronutrient availability |
| **Alcohol** | Hepatic [[KEYWORD-Metabolism|metabolism]], detoxification burden |
| **Exercise** | Energy substrate utilization |
| **Smoking** | Oxidative stress pathways |
| **Supplements** | Coenzyme availability (e.g., B12, VD) |
| **Medications** | Drug metabolism, nutrient depletion |
| **Circadian** | Time-of-day metabolic rhythms |
| **Stress** | Cortisol, catecholamine effects |
| **Disease** | Pathophysiological alterations |
| **Microbiome** | SCFA production, nutrient synthesis |
| **Environment** | Toxin exposure, temperature |

## Clinical Significance

### Why Metabotypes Matter for Precision Medicine

> "Η σημαντική επίδραση της γενετικής στη συγκέντρωση των μεταβολιτών φαίνεται και από το γεγονός ότι, αλλαγή σε μία μόνο βάση του DNA μπορεί να μεταβάλλει έως και 10.000 φορές τη συγκέντρωση συγκεκριμένων μεταβολιτών"

**Translation:** A single DNA base change can alter specific metabolite concentrations up to 10,000-fold.

**Implication:** Both genetics AND environment (via metabotype) must be considered for individualized therapy.

## METHAP Application

### Individual VD Response Prediction

**Population approach (flawed):**
- "[[KEYWORD-Vitamin_D|vitamin d]] 2000 IU daily for everyone"
- Ignores individual metabotype differences

**Metabotype-guided approach (METHAP):**
1. **Baseline metabotyping:** Urine OA + plasma TFA profile
2. **Stratification:** Identify metabolic subgroups
3. **Targeted intervention:** VD dose matched to metabolic phenotype
4. **Response monitoring:** Metabolite trajectory tracking

**Expected outcome:** Greater efficacy, fewer non-responders
