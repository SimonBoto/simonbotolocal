## Sample Size Calculation

**Method:** MetSizeR package (R 4.3.2)  
**Parameters:**
- FDR (False Discovery Rate): 5%
- Total metabolites analyzed: 65
- Expected significant metabolites: 20% (13 metabolites)
- **Calculated N:** 200 total

**Allocation:**
- Control group (no IR): n=100
-  resistance group: n=100
  - IR control (no intervention): n=50
  - IR intervention: n=50

- Private medical clinics

## Selection Process

1. **Initial screening:** Medical and dietary history
2. **Physical measurements:** Height, weight, waist circumference, BP
3. **Eligibility assessment:** Inclusion/exclusion criteria
4. **Informed consent:** Written and oral explanation
5. **Baseline sampling:** Blood and urine collection

## Core Principle

Rigorous sample size calculation and clear selection criteria ensure scientific validity and ethical participant protection.

## Inclusion Criteria — Control Group

All must be met:
- Fasting glucose < 100 mg/dL
- HbA1c < 5.6%
- HOMA-IR < 2
- Triglycerides < 150 mg/dL

## Inclusion Criteria — IR Group

At least one must be met:
- Fasting glucose 100-125 mg/dL
- HbA1c 5.6-6.4%
- HOMA-IR ≥ 2
- Triglycerides ≥ 150 mg/dL

## Core Principle

Rigorous sample size calculation and clear selection criteria ensure scientific validity and ethical participant protection.

## Exclusion Criteria

- Infectious diseases or 
- Renal, hepatic, or cardiac failure
- Pregnancy or lactation
- Acute or chronic disease with medication
- Antidepressants or other regular medications
- Abnormal TSH, anemia, low ferritin
- Dietary restrictions (celiac, allergies, eating disorders)
- Refusal to take supplements
