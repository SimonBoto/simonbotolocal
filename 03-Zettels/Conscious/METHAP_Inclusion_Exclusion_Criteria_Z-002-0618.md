# METHAP Inclusion and Exclusion Criteria

Clear eligibility criteria ensure scientific validity, participant safety, and ethical research conduct.

## Control Group Criteria (All Required)

| Parameter | Threshold |
|-----------|-----------|
| Fasting glucose | < 100 mg/dL |
| HbA1c | < 5.6% |
| HOMA-IR | < 2 |
| Triglycerides | < 150 mg/dL |

**Purpose:** Establish metabolically healthy reference population

##  Resistance Group Criteria (≥1 Required)

| Parameter | Threshold |
|-----------|-----------|
| Fasting glucose | 100-125 mg/dL (prediabetes range) |
| HbA1c | 5.6-6.4% |
| HOMA-IR | ≥ 2 |
| Triglycerides | ≥ 150 mg/dL |

**Purpose:** Identify individuals with early metabolic dysfunction

## Universal Exclusion Criteria

### Medical Conditions
- Infectious diseases (active)
-  (any type)
- Renal failure
- Hepatic failure
- Cardiac failure
- Pregnancy or lactation
- Acute or chronic disease requiring medication

### Medications
- Antidepressants
- Regular prescription medications
- Preventive or therapeutic drugs affecting 

### Laboratory Abnormalities
- Abnormal TSH (thyroid dysfunction)
- Anemia
- Low ferritin

### Dietary/Lifestyle
- Celiac disease
- Food allergies/intolerances affecting diet
- Eating disorders
- Vegetarian/vegan (if affects intervention)
- Refusal to take study supplements

## Rationale

| Exclusion | Reason |
|-----------|--------|
| **Renal/hepatic failure** | Metabolite clearance altered |
| **Pregnancy** | Safety, altered metabolism |
| **Medications** | Confounding metabolic effects |
| **Thyroid dysfunction** | Major metabolic confounder |
| **Dietary restrictions** | Cannot follow intervention protocol |
