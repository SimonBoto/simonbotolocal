> Carbohydrate context creates different metabolic environments that fundamentally alter protein's renal effects.

## Core Concept

**Key Process:**
```
Carb context → Metabolic environment → Protein renal effects
```

## Mechanism

### High-Carb + High-Protein Pathway
1. **High glycemic load** elevates insulin levels
2. **Insulin resistance** perpetuated
3. **Synergistic stress** on kidneys accelerates progression

### Low-Carb + High-Protein Pathway
1. **Reduced glycemic load** lowers insulin levels
2. **Improved insulin sensitivity** offsets protein-induced GFR increase
3. **Beta-hydroxybutyrate** may be renoprotective

## Clinical Significance

Explains the physiological basis for context-dependent protein effects:
- Hyperinsulinemia + Hyperfiltration = Combined metabolic stress
- Ketone production = Potential renal protection
