# B Vitamins Enzyme Cofactors 

## Overview
B-vitamins serve as essential cofactors for hundreds of metabolic enzymes—each with specific roles in energy production, amino acid metabolism, and nucleotide synthesis.

## B-Vitamin Functions

### B1 (Thiamine) → Thiamine Pyrophosphate (TPP)
| Enzyme Complex | Pathway |
|----------------|---------|
| **Pyruvate dehydrogenase** | Pyruvate → Acetyl-CoA |
| **Alpha-ketoglutarate dehydrogenase** | Krebs cycle |
| **Transketolase** | Pentose phosphate pathway |
| **Branched-chain ketoacid dehydrogenase** | BCAA catabolism |

**Deficiency:** Beriberi, Wernicke-Korsakoff syndrome

### B2 (Riboflavin) → FAD/FMN
| Function | Enzyme Examples |
|----------|-----------------|
| **Oxidation-reduction** | Succinate dehydrogenase, acyl-CoA dehydrogenases |
| **Electron transport** | Complex I, Complex II |
| **Antioxidant** | Glutathione reductase |

**Deficiency:** Mucocutaneous lesions, normocytic anemia

### B3 (Niacin) → NAD⁺/NADP⁺
| Function | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Redox reactions** | Glycolysis, Krebs, fatty acid oxidation |
| **DNA repair** | PARP enzymes |
| **Cell signaling** | Sirtuins, ADP-ribosylation |

**Deficiency:** Pellagra (3 Ds: dermatitis, diarrhea, dementia)

### B5 (Pantothenate) → Coenzyme A
| Function | Pathway |
|----------|---------|
| **Acyl group transfer** | Fatty acid oxidation, Krebs cycle |
| **Biosynthesis** | Fatty acid synthesis,  |

**Deficiency:** Rare (widespread in food); fatigue, paresthesia

### B6 (Pyridoxine) → Pyridoxal Phosphate (PLP)
| Function | Enzymes |
|----------|---------|
| **Transamination** | All amino acid transaminases |
| **Decarboxylation** | Neurotransmitter synthesis |
| **Deamination** | Serine, threonine dehydratases |

**Deficiency:** Microcytic anemia, neurological symptoms, elevated homocysteine

### B7 (Biotin)
| Enzyme | Function |
|--------|----------|
| **Pyruvate carboxylase** | Gluconeogenesis (pyruvate → oxaloacetate) |
| **Acetyl-CoA carboxylase** | Fatty acid synthesis |
| **Propionyl-CoA carboxylase** | Odd-chain fatty acid metabolism |

**Deficiency:** Rare; dermatitis, alopecia, neurological symptoms

### B9 (Folate) → Tetrahydrofolate (THF)
| Function | Pathway |
|----------|---------|
| **One-carbon transfer** | DNA synthesis, methylation |
| **Purine synthesis** | Nucleotide metabolism |

**Deficiency:** Megaloblastic anemia, neural tube defects

### B12 (Cobalamin)
| Enzyme | Function |
|--------|----------|
| **Methionine synthase** | Homocysteine → methionine |
| **Methylmalonyl-CoA mutase** | Methylmalonyl-CoA → Succinyl-CoA |

**Deficiency:** Pernicious anemia, methylmalonic aciduria, neuropathy

## B-Vitamin Interdependence
- **B6, B9, B12:** Linked in methionine metabolism
- **B1, B2, B3, B5:** Linked in energy metabolism
- **Multiple deficiencies:** More common than single deficiencies
