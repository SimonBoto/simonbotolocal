# OAT Clinical Action Mapping

## Overview
Direct translation of OAT markers to clinical interventions.

## Energy  Interventions

| Marker | Intervention |
|--------|--------------|
| **Pyruvate ↑** | B1 (thiamine) 100-300 mg |
| **α-Ketoglutarate ↑** | B-complex, magnesium |
| **Succinate ↑** | B2 (riboflavin) 50-100 mg, CoQ10 100-300 mg |
| **Lactate ↑** | B1, B3, magnesium; address hypoxia |

## Cofactor Replenishment

| Marker | Nutrient | Form | Dose |
|--------|----------|------|------|
| **Methylmalonate ↑** | B12 | Methyl + adenosylcobalamin | 1000 mcg |
| **FIGLU ↑** | Folate | 5-MTHF | 400-1000 mcg |
| **Kynurenate ↑** | B6 | P5P | 25-50 mg |
| **Suberate/adipate ↑** | Carnitine | L-carnitine | 1000-2000 mg |

## l Support

| Pattern | Intervention |
|---------|--------------|
| **General dysfunction** | B-complex, CoQ10, magnesium, alpha-lipoic acid |
| **Oxidative stress (citrate ↑)** | Glutathione, NAC, vitamin C, vitamin E |
| **Fatty acid oxidation impaired** | L-carnitine, B2, CoQ10 |

## Gut Interventions

| Marker | Intervention |
|--------|--------------|
| **Arabinose ↑ (Candida)** | Berberine, oregano, caprylic acid; low sugar |
| **HPHPA ↑ (Clostridia)** | Berberine, Saccharomyces boulardii; probiotics |
| **Low hippurate** | Lactobacillus/Bifidobacterium probiotics |
| **High indican** | Digestive enzymes, HCl if low |

## Oxidative Stress

| Marker | Intervention |
|--------|--------------|
| **8-OHdG ↑** | Antioxidant protocol: glutathione, vitamin C, vitamin E, polyphenols |

## Priority Matrix

| Priority | Condition | Action |
|----------|-----------|--------|
| **Critical** | Severe lactic acidosis | Urgent B1, address cause |
| **High** | Multiple B-eficiencies | Comprehensive B-complex |
| **Moderate** | Mild mitochondrial dysfunction | Mitochondrial support |
| **Optimization** | Minor elevations | Dietary modification |

## Follow-Up Testing
| Timeline | Reassess |
|----------|----------|
| 4 weeks | Clinical response |
| 8-12 weeks | Repeat OAT (expect 50%+ improvement) |
| 6 months | Full resolution target |
