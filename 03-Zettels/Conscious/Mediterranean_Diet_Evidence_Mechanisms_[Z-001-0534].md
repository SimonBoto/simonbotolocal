# Mediterranean Diet — Evidence and s

> The Mediterranean dietary pattern, characterized by high olive oil consumption, consistently demonstrates cardiovascular and metabolic benefits in  trials.

## PREDIMED (2013)

**Design:**
- 7,447 high-risk individuals
- Mediterranean + EVOO vs. + nuts vs. low-fat
- No calorie restriction

**Results:**
- **30% CVD reduction**
- **39% stroke reduction**
- **31% heart attack reduction**

**Significance:**
- First RCT of Mediterranean diet
- High-fat beneficial vs. low-fat

## LYON (1994)

**Design:**
- 605 post-MI patients

**Results:**
- **73% combined endpoint reduction**
- **70% cardiac death reduction**

## EPIC Study

**Observational:**
- 521,000 participants
- Olive oil inverse with mortality
- **2 tbsp/day threshold**

## Mechanisms

**Beyond Oleic Acid:**
- Polyphenols (hydroxytyrosol)
- Fiber from vegetables/legumes
-  from fish
- Social pattern, physical activity

**Synergy:**
- Whole pattern > single nutrients
- Olive oil enables vegetable consumption
- Enhances fat-soluble vitamin absorption
