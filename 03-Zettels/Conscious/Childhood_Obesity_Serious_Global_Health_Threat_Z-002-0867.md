## Core Claim
**Childhood obesity is one of the most serious threats to children's health globally** — with future chronic disease epidemic, healthcare strain, and trillions in costs.

## Global Health Implications
| Aspect | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **Disease burden** | Future chronic disease epidemic |
| **Healthcare systems** | Unprecedented strain |
| **Economic cost** | Trillions in long-term care |
| **Quality of life** | Reduced for entire generation |

## Urgency Projections
| Timeline | Projection |
|----------|------------|
| **Current** | Obese > underweight |
| **Next decade** | Gap widens |
| **Adult population** | Today's obese children |

## Prevention Priorities
| Level | Intervention |
|-------|--------------|
| **Individual** | Family nutrition education |
| **School** | Healthy food programs |
| **Policy** | Sugar taxes, marketing restrictions |
| **Healthcare** | Early metabolic screening |

## Intergenerational Impact
- Obese children → obese adults → metabolic syndrome → early mortality
- Today's children: **first generation with shorter life expectancy than parents**

## Clinical Implication
Urgent need for **multi-level intervention** — cannot be addressed by healthcare alone.
