## Core Concept

Sleep as a fundamental metabolic regulator that supports mitochondrial repair, metabolomic homeostasis, glycation control, mineral balance, and fatty acid metabolism.

## Key Principles

- **Circadian Rhythm:** Alignment with natural light/dark cycles optimizes metabolic processes
- **Mitochondrial Repair:** Sleep provides critical time for mitochondrial biogenesis and repair
- **Metabolomic Reset:** Sleep helps normalize metabolic profiles and reduce oxidative stress
- **Glycation Control:** Quality sleep supports healthy blood sugar regulation and reduces glycation

## Clinical Significance

Sleep directly influences all five pillars of metabolic health by supporting mitochondrial recovery, optimizing metabolomic profiles, enhancing glycation control, maintaining mineral balance, and regulating fatty acid metabolism.
