## Core Claim
**Insulin resistance is associated with up to 14 times greater increase in coronary artery disease risk** compared to isolated cholesterol elevation — making it a far more significant cardiovascular risk factor than LDL alone.

## The Comparison

| Factor | Relative CAD Risk |
|--------|-------------------|
| **Insulin resistance** | **14× baseline** |
| **Elevated cholesterol alone** | 1× (baseline) |

## Why the Disconnect?

| Clinical Focus | Actual Risk Driver |
|----------------|-------------------|
| LDL measurement | Insulin resistance ignored |
| Statin prescription | Lifestyle factors underaddressed |
| Cholesterol targets | Metabolic health overlooked |

## Mechanisms

| Pathway | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Endothelial dysfunction** | Impaired vascular tone |
| **Inflammation** | Chronic low-grade activation |
| **Dyslipidemia** | High TG, low HDL, sdLDL |
| **Hypercoagulability** | Thrombotic tendency |

## Clinical Priority

| Assessment | Action |
|------------|--------|
| **Fasting insulin** | Measure in all patients |
| **HOMA-IR** | Calculate insulin resistance |
| **Metabolic markers** | TG/HDL ratio, fasting glucose |
