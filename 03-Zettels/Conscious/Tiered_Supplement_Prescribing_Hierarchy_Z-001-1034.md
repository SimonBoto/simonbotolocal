# Tiered Supplement Prescribing Hierarchy

## Overview
A structured, sequential approach to supplementation that prioritizes foundational support before targeted interventions. Prevents random supplement shopping.

## The Six Tiers

### Tier 1 — Foundational
**Components:**
-  (status-dependent dosing)
- Magnesium (form matters)
- Omega-3 (target: index >8%)
- B-complex (activated forms)
- Gut basics (probiotics, digestive support)

**Timing:** Always first, always ongoing

### Tier 2 — Targeted l Support
**Indications:**
- Elevated lactate/pyruvate
- Fatigue-dominant presentations
- Poor oxidative  markers

**Components:**
- CoQ10, B2, B3
- Alpha-lipoic acid
- Acetyl-L-carnitine
- NAD+ precursors (if indicated)

### Tier 3 — Detox + Methylation Optimization
**Indications:**
- Elevated toxic metabolites on OAT
- COMT/MTHFR variants
- High homocysteine

**Components:**
- Methylated B12, methylfolate
- Glutathione or precursors
- Liver support (NAC, milk thistle)

### Tier 4 — Gut Rebalancing
**Indications:**
- Dysbiosis markers on OAT
- Digestive symptoms
- SIBO/SIFO patterns

**Components:**
- Targeted antimicrobials (if needed)
- Specific probiotics
- Mucosal healing support

**Note:** Energy must be repaired *before* aggressive gut protocols.

### Tier 5 — Lipid Repair
**Indications:**
- Elevated omega-6:3 ratio
- Low DHA/EPA
- Membrane dysfunction patterns

**Components:**
- High-dose EPA/DHA
- Phosphatidylcholine
- Omega-3 index optimization

### Tier 6 — Lifestyle Directives
**Always included:**
- Sleep optimization
- Stress reduction
- Movement/exercise
- Light exposure

## Sequencing Rules

1. **Never skip tiers** — Foundation must be solid
2. **Energy before gut** — Mitochondrial support precedes aggressive gut protocols
3. **One tier at a time** — Assess response before adding complexity
4. **Monitor compliance** — Adjust form/dosing for adherence

## Clinical Wisdom
> "Mechanism-based prescribing, not random supplement shopping."
