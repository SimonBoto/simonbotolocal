# Multiple Myeloma Staging ISS R-ISS

## Overview
Staging in multiple myeloma stratifies patients by prognosis using biomarkers—primarily beta-2 microglobulin (tumor burden) and albumin (patient status), with refinement for cytogenetics and LDH.

## International Staging System (ISS)

### Stage I
| Criteria | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Beta-2 microglobulin** | <3.5 mg/L |
| **AND Serum albumin** | ≥3.5 g/dL |
| **Median survival** | ~62 months |
| **Proportion** | ~28% of patients |

### Stage II
| Criteria | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Neither Stage I nor III** | Intermediate values |
| **Median survival** | ~44 months |
| **Proportion** | ~39% of patients |

### Stage III
| Criteria | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Beta-2 microglobulin** | ≥5.5 mg/L |
| **Median survival** | ~29 months |
| **Proportion** | ~33% of patients |

## Revised-ISS (R-ISS)

### Incorporates Additional Risk Factors
| Factor | Why Included |
|--------|--------------|
| **Cytogenetic abnormalities** | High-risk genetics worsen prognosis |
| **LDH** | Reflects tumor burden/aggressiveness |

### R-ISS Stage I
| Criteria |
|----------|
| **ISS Stage I** |
| **AND No high-risk cytogenetics** |
| **AND Normal LDH** |
| **Median survival** | Not reached (>100 months) |

### R-ISS Stage II
| Criteria |
|----------|
| **Neither R-ISS I nor III** |
| **Median survival** | ~83 months |

### R-ISS Stage III
| Criteria |
|----------|
| **ISS Stage III** |
| **PLUS High-risk cytogenetics OR Elevated LDH** |
| **Median survival** | ~43 months |

## High-Risk Cytogenetics

### FISH Abnormalities
| Abnormality | Frequency | Prognosis |
|-------------|-----------|-----------|
| **del(17p)** | 5-10% | Poor |
| **t(4;14)** | 15% | Poor |
| **t(14;16)** | 5% | Poor |
| **t(4;20)** | Rare | Poor |

### Other Adverse Features
| Feature | Significance |
|---------|--------------|
| **Amp(1q21)** | High risk |
| **del(1p)** | High risk |
| **Complex karyotype** | High risk |
| **Hypodiploidy** | Adverse |

## Prognostic Factors Beyond Staging

### Patient Factors
| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **Age** | Older = worse (treatment tolerance) |
| **Performance status** | Poor = worse |
| **Frailty** | Limits treatment options |

### Disease Factors
| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **Plasma cell proliferation rate** | High = worse |
| **Circulating plasma cells** | Present = worse |
| **Extramedullary disease** | Present = worse |

### Response to Treatment
| Response | Definition | Prognostic Value |
|----------|------------|------------------|
| **Complete response (CR)** | Negative immunofixation | Best |
| **Very good partial response (VGPR)** | M-protein reduction >90% | Good |
| **Partial response (PR)** | >50% reduction | Standard |
| **Minimal response** | 25-49% reduction | Suboptimal |
| **Progressive disease** | >25% increase | Poor |

## Minimal Residual Disease (MRD)

### Assessment Methods
| Method | Sensitivity |
|--------|-------------|
| **Multiparameter flow cytometry** | 10⁻⁴ to 10⁻⁵ |
| **Next-generation sequencing (NGS)** | 10⁻⁶ |
| **PET/CT** | Functional imaging |

### Prognostic Significance
| Status | Outcome |
|--------|---------|
| **MRD negative** | Superior progression-free survival |
| **Sustained MRD negativity** | Best long-term outcomes |

## Risk-Adapted Treatment

### Standard Risk (R-ISS I-II)
| Approach | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Triplet induction** | VRd, DRd, KRd |
| **ASCT eligible** | Transplant consolidation |
| **Maintenance** | Lenalidomide |

### High Risk (R-ISS III)
| Approach | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Quad induction** | Dara-VRd, KRd + Dara |
| **Tandem ASCT** | Consider for eligible |
| **Intensified maintenance** | Proteasome inhibitor + IMiD |
