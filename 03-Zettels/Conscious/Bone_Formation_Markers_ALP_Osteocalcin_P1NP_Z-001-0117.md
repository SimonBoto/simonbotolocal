## Serum Alkaline Phosphatase (BALP)
- **Source:** Bone-specific isoenzyme
- **Timing:** Peaks 3–6 months on anabolic therapy
- **Utility:** Monitor anabolic response
- **Limitations:** Liver disease confounds interpretation

## Osteocalcin (OC)
- **Source:** Non-collagenous protein secreted by osteoblasts
- **Timing:** Rises earlier than BALP (1–3 months)
- **Utility:** Sensitive marker of osteoblast activity
- **Limitations:** Circadian variation, renal clearance

## Significance

- **Limitations:** Liver disease confounds interpretation

## Procollagen Type I N-terminal Propeptide (P1NP)
- **Source:** Cleavage product of type I collagen synthesis
- **Timing:** Rapid rise (1–2 months) on anabolic therapy
- **Utility:** Most specific and sensitive formation marker
- **Limitations:** Minimal circadian variation, stable in storage
