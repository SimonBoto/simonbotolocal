# Emanuel Seven Requirements Ethical Research

> **Core Claim:** Seven requirements must all be satisfied: social value, scientific validity, fair selection, favorable risk-benefit, independent review, informed consent, and respect for subjects.

## Key Point

Poor-quality research is inherently unethical
