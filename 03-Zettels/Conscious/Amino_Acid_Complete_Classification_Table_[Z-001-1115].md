# Amino Acid Complete Classification Table

## Overview
Comprehensive classification of the 20 proteinogenic  by multiple criteria.

## By Side Chain Polarity

### Non-Polar (Hydrophobic)
| AA | 3-Letter | 1-Letter | Structure | Function |
|----|----------|----------|-----------|----------|
| **Glycine** | Gly | G | H | Flexible, small |
| **Alanine** | Ala | A | CH₃ | Helix former |
| **Valine** | Val | V | Isopropyl | Beta-branched |
| **Leucine** | Leu | L | Isobutyl | mTOR signaling |
| **Isoleucine** | Ile | I | Sec-butyl | Beta-branched |
| **Methionine** | Met | M | Thioether | Start codon |
| **Phenylalanine** | Phe | F | Benzyl | Aromatic |
| **Tryptophan** | Trp | W | Indole | Aromatic, rare |
| **Proline** | Pro | P | Pyrrolidine | Helix breaker |

### Polar (Uncharged)
| AA | 3-Letter | 1-Letter | Structure | Function |
|----|----------|----------|-----------|----------|
| **Serine** | Ser | S | -CH₂OH | Phosphorylation |
| **Threonine** | Thr | T | -CH(OH)CH₃ | Phosphorylation |
| **Cysteine** | Cys | C | -CH₂SH | Disulfide bonds |
| **Tyrosine** | Tyr | Y | p-OH-benzyl | Phosphorylation |
| **Asparagine** | Asn | N | -CH₂CONH₂ | N-glycosylation |
| **Glutamine** | Gln | Q | -CH₂CH₂CONH₂ | N-source |

### Polar (Charged)

#### Acidic (Negative at pH 7)
| AA | 3-Letter | 1-Letter | pKa | Function |
|----|----------|----------|-----|----------|
| **Aspartate** | Asp | D | ~3.9 | Active sites, Ca²⁺ binding |
| **Glutamate** | Glu | E | ~4.3 | Active sites, neurotransmitter |

#### Basic (Positive at pH 7)
| AA | 3-Letter | 1-Letter | pKa | Function |
|----|----------|----------|-----|----------|
| **Lysine** | Lys | K | ~10.5 | Acetylation, ubiquitination |
| **Arginine** | Arg | R | ~12.5 | Salt bridges, NO synthesis |
| **Histidine** | His | H | ~6.0 | Catalytic, pH sensing |

## By Metabolic Fate

### Essential (Cannot Synthesize)
| AA | Source | Deficiency Risk |
|----|--------|-----------------|
| **Histidine** | Diet | Growth impairment |
| **Isoleucine** | Diet | Muscle wasting |
| **Leucine** | Diet | Protein synthesis ↓ |
| **Lysine** | Diet | Poor growth |
| **Methionine** | Diet | Fatty liver |
| **Phenylalanine** | Diet | PKU if can't convert |
| **Threonine** | Diet | Immune dysfunction |
| **Tryptophan** | Diet | Niacin deficiency |
| **Valine** | Diet | Maple syrup urine disease |

| **Arginine** | Glutamate | Conditionally essential |
| **Asparagine** | Aspartate | Usually sufficient |
| **Aspartate** | Oxaloacetate | Usually sufficient |
| **Cysteine** | Methionine + serine | Conditionally essential |
| **Glutamate** | Alpha-ketoglutarate | Usually sufficient |
| **Glutamine** | Glutamate | Conditionally essential |
| **Glycine** | Serine, CO₂ | Usually sufficient |
| **Proline** | Glutamate | Usually sufficient |
| **Serine** | 3-phosphoglycerate | Usually sufficient |
| **Tyrosine** | Phenylalanine | PKU if can't convert |
