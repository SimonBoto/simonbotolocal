# IRS1 Phosphorylation and Signal Transduction

## Core Principle
IRS1 (Insulin Receptor Substrate 1) serves as the primary docking protein linking the insulin receptor to downstream signaling cascades.

## Mechanism
1. Activated insulin receptor phosphorylates IRS1 on YXXM motifs
2. Phosphorylated IRS1 recruits PI3K (p85 regulatory subunit)
3. PI3K activates → PIP2 → PIP3 conversion
4. PIP3 recruits PDK1 and Akt to membrane
5. Akt phosphorylated → downstream effects

## Significance
- **Critical for:** Metabolic actions of insulin
- **Downstream:** GLUT4 translocation, glycogen synthesis
- **Clinical:** IRS1 mutations linked to insulin resistance
