## The Controversy

**Banting's Reaction:**
- **Furious** that Best was not recognized
- Felt Best deserved equal credit
- Saw MacLeod's contribution as administrative
- Threatened to refuse prize

**Resolution:**
- Accepted prize after pressure
- Immediately announced 50/50 split with Best
- Public statement of Best's essential role

**MacLeod's Response:**
- Acknowledged Collip's critical contribution
- Announced 50/50 split with Collip
- Recognized purification was essential
