# CAC Score Above 400 Indicates Clear Statin Benefit

## Core Claim
**CAC scores above 400 indicate clear benefit from statin therapy** — individuals with moderate to extensive plaque burden show significant cardiovascular risk reduction with statin treatment.

## Clear Benefit Indicators

### By CAC Score
| CAC Score | Benefit |
|-----------|---------|
| **>400** | ✅ Clear benefit |
| **101-399** | Benefit not statistically significant |

### By Plaque Features (Any CAC)
| Feature | Benefit |
|---------|---------|
| **Artery stenosis ≥50%** | ✅ Clear benefit |
| **High-risk (vulnerable/unstable) plaque** | ✅ Clear benefit |
| **Multi-vessel disease (>4 vessels)** | ✅ Clear benefit |

## Mechanism of Benefit
- Statins **stabilize existing plaque**
- Reduce **inflammation**
- Prevent **plaque rupture**
- In established disease, benefits outweigh risks

## Risk Reduction
| Metric | Finding |
|--------|---------|
| **Absolute risk reduction** | Greater in high-risk individuals |
| **Number needed to treat (NNT)** | Lowest in CAC >400 group |

## Clinical Implication
CAC >400 or high-risk features = **clear indication for statin therapy**.
