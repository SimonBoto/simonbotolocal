# Training Teaching Style Framework

## Overview
Three pedagogical approaches for  training, each suited to different trainee personalities and clinic contexts.

## Style A: Military Instructor

### Characteristics
| Aspect | Approach |
|--------|----------|
| **Clarity** | Explicit, structured |
| **Accountability** | High, with consequences |
| **Pace** | Fast, intensive |
| **Correction** | Immediate, direct |

### Pros
- Guaranteed competence
- No ambiguity
- Rapid skill acquisition

### Cons
- Can demoralize sensitive trainees
- May suppress creative thinking
- High stress environment

### Best For
- Trainees with thick skin
- High-stakes, time-limited contexts
- Personality-driven clinics

## Style B: Socratic Mentor

### Characteristics
| Aspect | Approach |
|--------|----------|
| **Method** | Always questioning |
| **Discovery** | Guided self-discovery |
| **Pace** | Variable, trainee-dependent |
| **Correction** | Through insight, not directive |

### Pros
- Deep understanding
- Builds reasoning independence
- Less confrontational

### Cons
- Slower for some learners
- Requires high trainee engagement
- Can frustrate those wanting answers

### Best For
- Philosophically-minded trainees
- Long-term development
- -oriented contexts

## Style C: Hybrid Precision (Recommended)

### Characteristics
| Situation | Approach |
|-----------|----------|
| **Critical errors** | Military (immediate correction) |
| **Pattern building** | Socratic (guided discovery) |
| **Standard protocols** | Military (follow exactly) |
| **Complex cases** | Socratic (explore reasoning) |

### The Blend
> "Clear standards + guided discovery. High accountability without authoritarianism."

### Implementation
- Define non-negotiables (military)
- Explore reasoning pathways (Socratic)
- Correct errors immediately (military)
- Build  judgment (Socratic)

### Best For
- Most clinical training contexts
- Mixed-ability trainee groups
- Sustainable long-term development

## Selection Criteria

Choose based on:
1. **Trainee personality** — Thick skin vs. sensitive
2. **Time pressure** — Urgent vs. developmental
3. **Clinic culture** — Hierarchical vs. collaborative
4. **Content type** — Protocol vs. reasoning

## Recommendation
**Default to Hybrid Precision.** Adjust ratio based on trainee response.
