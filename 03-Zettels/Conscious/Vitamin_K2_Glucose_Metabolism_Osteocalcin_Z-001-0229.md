# Vitamin K2 Glucose Metabolism Osteocalcin

> **Core Claim:** Vitamin K2 influences glucose metabolism through the osteocalcin-endocrine axis, with emerging evidence for insulin-sensitizing effects.

## Osteocalcin Connection

| Form | Function |
|------|----------|
| **Carboxylated** | Bone matrix binding |
| **Undercarboxylated (ucOC)** | **Hormonal — improves insulin sensitivity** |

## Mechanism

1. Bone secretes ucOC
2. ucOC improves insulin sensitivity and glucose uptake
3. Vitamin K2 increases carboxylation (complex effect on ucOC levels)

## Direct Effects

- **Adiponectin:** K2 may increase (insulin-sensitizing)
- **Inflammation:** May reduce inflammatory markers
- **Lipid metabolism:** Possible beneficial effects

## Current Evidence

| Status | Finding |
|--------|---------|
| **Human data** | Limited — mostly animal models |
| **Mechanism** | Plausible via osteocalcin axis |
| **Clinical trials** | Ongoing — emerging research area |
