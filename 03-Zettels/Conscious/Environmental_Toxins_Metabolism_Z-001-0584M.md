## Core Concept

The impact of environmental toxins on metabolic health and strategies for detoxification and toxin avoidance to support all five pillars.

## Key Principles

- **Toxin Sources:** Industrial chemicals, heavy metals, pesticides, air/water pollutants, and endocrine disruptors
- **Metabolic Disruption:** Toxins impair mitochondrial function, disrupt metabolomic pathways, enhance glycation, deplete minerals, and alter fatty acid metabolism
- **Detoxification Support:** Nutritional and lifestyle strategies to support endogenous detoxification pathways
- **Exposure Reduction:** Practical strategies for minimizing environmental toxin exposure

## Clinical Significance

Environmental toxin management is critical for metabolic health, as toxins directly impair all five pillars by disrupting mitochondrial function, altering metabolomic profiles, accelerating glycation, depleting essential minerals, and interfering with fatty acid metabolism.
