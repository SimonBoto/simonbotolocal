# ER Stress and  Resistance

Endoplasmic reticulum (ER) stress activates the unfolded protein response (UPR), which induces  through JNK activation.

**ER stress triggers:**
- Nutrient excess (glucose, lipids)
- Hypoxia
- 
- Genetic mutations

**UPR signaling:**
1. **PERK:** Phosphorylates eIF2α → attenuates translation
2. **IRE1α:** Splices XBP1 → expands ER capacity
3. **ATF6:** Transcription factor → chaperone induction

**IR mechanism:**
- IRE1α activates JNK via TRAF2/ASK1
- JNK phosphorylates IRS-1 at Ser307
- Serine-phosphorylated IRS cannot activate PI3K

**Also:** ER stress induces inflammation via NF-κB → cytokine production
