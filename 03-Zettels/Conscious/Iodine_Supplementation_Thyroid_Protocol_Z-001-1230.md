# Iodine Supplementation — Thyroid and Detox Protocol

## Overview

Iodine is essential for thyroid hormone synthesis, but also plays critical roles in immune function, breast health, and detoxification of halides (bromine, fluoride, chlorine). High-dose iodine requires careful co-factor support.

---

## Biological Functions

### 1. Thyroid Hormone Synthesis
```
Iodide (I⁻) → Thyroid follicular cells
    ↓
Oxidation to Iodine (I⁰)
    ↓
Iodination of tyrosine → MIT, DIT
    ↓
Coupling → T3 (triiodothyronine), T4 (thyroxine)
```

### 2. Halide Detoxification
**Competitive displacement:**
- Iodine displaces bromine, fluoride, chlorine
- Stored in tissues (breast, prostate, thyroid)
- Released during iodine supplementation

### 3. Immune Function
- Antimicrobial (kills bacteria, viruses, fungi)
- Supports neutrophil function
- Autoimmune modulation (complex effects)

### 4. Extrathyroidal Tissues
- **Breast tissue:** Protection against fibrocystic changes
- **Prostate:** Protective against dysfunction
- **Pancreas:** Beta cell function
- **Brain:** Myelination, cognitive function

## Supplementation Protocol

### Dosing Tiers
| Level | Dose | Duration | Indication |
|-------|------|----------|------------|
| **RDA** | 150 mcg | Maintenance | General population |
| **Moderate** | 1-3 mg | 3-6 months | Thyroid support |
| **High** | 12.5 mg | Supervised | Detox, chronic issues |
| **Very high** | 50 mg | Specialist only |  protocols |

### Titration Schedule
| Week | Dose | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| 1 | 500 mcg/day | Establish tolerance |
| 2 | 750 mcg/day | Monitor symptoms |
| 3-4 | 1 mg/day | Target maintenance |
| 5+ | 2-3 mg/day | If needed |

## Mandatory Co-Factors

### Critical: Selenium
| Dose | Form | Timing |
|------|------|--------|
| 200-400 mcg | Selenomethionine or Se-methylselenocysteine | With iodine |

**Why essential:**
- Protects thyroid from oxidative damage
- Required for deiodinase enzymes (T4→T3 conversion)
- Prevents Hashimoto's flare (theoretical)

## Detoxification Effects

### Expected (Temporary)
- **Bromine detox:** Headache, rash, metallic taste
- **Fluoride release:** Joint aches
- **Duration:** 1-4 weeks typically

### Management
- **Salt loading:** ½ tsp sea salt in water (binds bromide)
- **Vitamin C:** 3-5 g/day
- **Selenium:** Ensure adequate (400 mcg)
- **Hydration:** 3L+ water daily

---

## Contraindications and Cautions

### Absolute Contraindications
- **Active hyperthyroidism** (Graves' disease flare)
- **Thyroid storm**
- **Iodine allergy** (rare, usually to contrast dye)

### Relative Cautions
- **Hashimoto's thyroiditis:** Controversial; some worsen, some improve
- **Autonomous thyroid nodules:** May cause hyperthyroidism
- **Pregnancy:** Use RDA only unless supervised

### Monitoring
| Test | Baseline | Follow-up |
|------|----------|-----------|
| **TSH** | ✓ | Every 6-8 weeks |
| **Free T3/T4** | ✓ | Every 6-8 weeks |
| **TPO antibodies** | ✓ | Monitor for autoimmune flare |
| **TG antibodies** | ✓ | Monitor |
