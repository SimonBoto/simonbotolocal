## Core Claim
**Metabolomics quality standards encompassing pre-analytical variables, sample integrity, interpretation consistency, and reporting accuracy — with defined checkpoints at each stage — ensure reliable, reproducible results that support clinical decision-making and maintain laboratory accreditation standards.**

## Evidence

### Quality Framework Domains
| Domain | Focus | Key Standards |
|--------|-------|---------------|
| **Pre-analytical** | Sample collection, handling | Patient prep, collection timing |
| **Analytical** | Lab processing, instrumentation | Calibration, QC materials |
| **Post-analytical** | Interpretation, reporting | Clinician review, peer check |
| **System** | Documentation, training | SOPs, competency |

### Quality Metrics Dashboard
| Metric | Target | Action if Exceeded |
|--------|--------|-------------------|
| **Pre-analytical error rate** | < 2% | Root cause analysis |
| **QC failure rate** | < 5% | Instrument review |
| **Turnaround time** | < 5 days | Process improvement |
| **Peer review discrepancy** | < 10% | Training intervention |

## Clinical Implication
- **Reliability:** Quality standards ensure trustworthy results
- **Safety:** Pre-analytical controls prevent misleading data
- **Credibility:** Accreditation maintains professional standing
- **Improvement:** Metrics drive continuous enhancement
- **METHAP relevance:** Rigorous quality standards essential for research validity
