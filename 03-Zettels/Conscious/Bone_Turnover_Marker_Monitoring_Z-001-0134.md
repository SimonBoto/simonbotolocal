# Bone Turnover Marker Monitoring

## Laboratory Assessment

- **Baseline assessment:** 25(OH)D, calcium, PTH, creatinine, LFTs
- **Annual monitoring:** 25(OH)D, calcium, renal function
- **As needed:** Bone turnover markers (P1NP, CTX), PTH

## Treatment Response Assessment

#### Response Criteria
| Parameter | Responding | Non-responding | Action |
|-----------|------------|----------------|--------|
| **BTMs** | >30% change (P1NP↑, CTX↓) | <30% change | Assess adherence, consider switch |

## Interpretation Guidelines

- **P1NP (Procollagen type 1 N-terminal propeptide):** Marker of bone formation
- **CTX (C-terminal telopeptide of type I collagen):** Marker of bone resorption
- **Timing:** Morning sample, fasting, avoid recent physical activity
- **Variability:** Diurnal variation (CTx highest in early morning), menstrual cycle effects
- **Reference ranges:** Lab-specific, use same lab for serial monitoring

## Clinical Utility

- **Early response indicator:** Changes in BTMs occur within 3-6 months of therapy initiation
- **Treatment adherence:** BTMs can help identify non-adherent patients
- **Therapy selection:** Different patterns for anabolic vs. antiresorptive therapies
