# Albumin-Inflammation Confounder

> **Source:** ChatGPT conversation [[CGPT_0370_Protein_and_Kidney_Disease_CGPT-0370]] (May 26, 2024)
> **Core Claim:** Low serum albumin in CKD patients is often confounded by inflammation, making it a marker of both nutritional status and inflammatory burden rather than protein deficiency alone.

## Core Concepts

### Clinical Assessment

**Category:** Clinical Assessment

**Key Process:** 
```
Albumin is an inverse acute phase reactant → decreases during inflammation
```

**Mechanism/Steps:**
1. Hepatic synthesis is suppressed by inflammatory cytokines (IL-6, TNF-α)
2. Increased capillary permeability leads to albumin extravasation
3. Altered metabolism and degradation during inflammatory states
4. Results in hypoalbuminemia that does not reflect protein intake

**Purpose/Significance:** Prevents inappropriate protein supplementation in inflammatory states
