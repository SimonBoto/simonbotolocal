## Core Claim
**The IRE-IRP system operates through precise molecular recognition between the Iron Regulatory Protein (IRP) and the Iron-Responsive Element (IRE) вЂ” a conserved RNA stem-loop structure with a CAGUGN bulge вЂ” with IRE location (5' vs 3' UTR) determining whether IRP binding blocks translation or stabilizes mRNA, enabling coordinated post-transcriptional control of iron metabolism genes.**

## Clinical Implication
- **Molecular precision:** Specific RNA-protein recognition
- **Location matters:** Same protein, different effects based on IRE position
- **Evolutionary conservation:** IRE sequence conserved across species
- **Disease mutations:** IRE mutations cause iron disorders
