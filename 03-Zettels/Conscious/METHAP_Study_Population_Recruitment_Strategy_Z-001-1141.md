# METHAP Study Population Recruitment Strategy

## Overview
The METHAP ( and Insulin Resistance) trial recruits 220 participants stratified by metabolic status to compare  profiles and assess intervention efficacy.

## Study Population

### Total Sample Size
| Group | N | Purpose |
|-------|---|---------|
| **Metabolically healthy** | 60 | Reference/baseline |
| **Insulin-resistant** | 160 | Intervention study |
| **TOTAL** | **220** | Full cohort |

### Insulin-Resistant Group Division
| Subgroup | N | Allocation |
|----------|---|------------|
| **Intervention** | 80 | Diet + supplements |
| **Control** | 80 | No intervention |
| **Total IR** | 160 | Randomized |

## Inclusion Criteria

### Metabolically Healthy Group
| Criterion | Threshold |
|-----------|-----------|
| **HbA1c** | <5.7% |
| **Fasting glucose** | <100 mg/dL |
| **HOMA-IR** | <2.5 |
| **BMI** | 18.5-24.9 kg/m² |
| **No metabolic disease** | No diabetes, CVD, metabolic syndrome |

### Insulin-Resistant Group
| Criterion | Threshold |
|-----------|-----------|
| **HOMA-IR** | ≥2.5 |
| **Fasting glucose** | 100-125 mg/dL (prediabetes) |
| **HbA1c** | 5.7-6.4% |
| **No diabetes diagnosis** | Excludes T2DM |
| **Willing to participate** | Intervention commitment |

## Exclusion Criteria

| Condition | Reason |
|-----------|--------|
| **Diabetes mellitus** | Different metabolic state |
| **Severe kidney disease** | Metabolomics interference |
| **Liver disease** | Metabolic derangement |
| **Pregnancy/lactation** | Physiological changes |
| **Medications affecting ** | Confounding |
| **Recent antibiotics** | Gut microbiome disruption |

## Recruitment Strategy

### Sources
| Channel | Approach |
|---------|----------|
| **Clinic referrals** | Primary care, endocrinology |
| **Community screening** | Health fairs, advertisements |
| **University population** | Students, staff |
| **Social media** | Targeted local advertising |

### Screening Process
1. **Initial questionnaire** — Self-reported health
2. **Fasting blood draw** — Glucose, insulin, HbA1c
3. **Eligibility confirmation** — HOMA-IR calculation
4. **Informed consent** — Full study explanation

## Stratification and Randomization

### Insulin-Resistant Group
| Factor | Stratification |
|--------|----------------|
| **Age** | <40, 40-60, >60 |
| **Sex** | Male, Female |
| **HOMA-IR severity** | 2.5-4.0, 4.0-6.0, >6.0 |

### Randomization Method
- **Block randomization** — Ensures balance
- **Computer-generated** — Sealed allocation
- **Concealed allocation** — Until enrollment

## Ethical Considerations

### IRB Approval
- **Protocol review** — Ethics committee
- **Informed consent** — Detailed, understandable
- **Right to withdraw** — At any time
- **Data protection** — GDPR compliance

### Control Group Ethics
| Issue | Resolution |
|-------|------------|
| **Withholding intervention** | Standard care available |
| **Post-study access** | Intervention offered after |
| **Monitoring** — Safety checks throughout |
