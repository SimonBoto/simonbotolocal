## Core Claim
**Ten hormonal terms — Insulin, Insulin Resistance, Cortisol, PTH-VitD Axis, Lipid Regulation, and AA/EPA Ratio — describe the systemic control systems that coordinate metabolism across tissues, with insulin resistance representing a central pathological node in modern metabolic disease.**

## Evidence

### Core Definitions (10 Terms)
| Term | Definition | Clinical Relevance |
|------|------------|-------------------|
| **Insulin** | Pancreatic hormone regulating glucose | Anabolic signal, nutrient storage |
| **Insulin Resistance** | Reduced cellular insulin response | Metabolic syndrome, T2DM precursor |
| **Cortisol** | Adrenal stress hormone | Glucose mobilization, anti-inflammatory |
| **PTH-VitD Axis** | Calcium-phosphate homeostasis | Bone health, immune function |
| **Lipid Regulation** | Cholesterol/triglyceride control | Cardiovascular risk |
| **AA/EPA Ratio** | Arachidonic to eicosapentaenoic acid | Inflammation index |

### Insulin Resistance Progression
| Stage | Fasting Glucose | HbA1c | Clinical |
|-------|-----------------|-------|----------|
| **Compensated** | Normal | Normal | Hyperinsulinemia |
| **Impaired fasting** | 100-125 mg/dL | 5.7-6.4% | Prediabetes |
| **Diabetes** | >126 mg/dL | >6.5% | Symptomatic |

### Cortisol Patterns
| Pattern | Cause | Consequence |
|---------|-------|-------------|
| **Elevated AM** | Chronic stress | Metabolic disruption |
| **Flat rhythm** | Burnout, HPA dysfunction | Fatigue, inflammation |
| **Elevated PM** | Poor sleep, stress | Insomnia, weight gain |

### PTH-VitD Axis
| Component | Function | Assessment |
|-----------|----------|------------|
| **Vitamin D** | Calcium absorption, immune | 25-OH-D level |
| **PTH** | Calcium homeostasis | Counter-regulatory hormone |
| **Calcitonin** | Bone protection | Less clinically measured |

### AA/EPA Ratio Interpretation
| Ratio | Status | Intervention |
|-------|--------|--------------|
| <3:1 | Optimal | Maintain |
| 3-10:1 | Mild elevation | Increase omega-3 |
| >10:1 | High risk | Aggressive omega-3, reduce omega-6 |

## Clinical Implication
- **Metabolic syndrome:** Insulin resistance is central pathophysiology
- **Stress management:** Cortisol dysregulation drives metabolic dysfunction
- **Bone health:** PTH-VitD axis critical for skeletal integrity
- **Inflammation:** AA/EPA ratio predicts inflammatory state
- **METHAP relevance:** Hormonal regulation is core to metabolic syndrome research; insulin resistance mechanisms are primary research focus
