# METHAP  Panel Integration

The METHAP (Metabolic Health Assessment Protocol) biomarker panel integrates multiple biomarker categories for comprehensive insulin resistance assessment across the disease spectrum — from early detection through monitoring.

**Panel Structure:**

| Category |  | Sample | Purpose |
|----------|-----------|--------|---------|
| **Early IR** | Adipic acid, BCAAs, FFA profile | Urine + Plasma | Detect Stage 1 |
| **Inflammation** | CRP, IL-6 (research) | Plasma | Assess immune activation |
| **Nutritional** | , Mg, Zn, Se | Plasma | Identify cofactor needs |
| **Thyroid** | fT3, fT4, anti-TPO | Plasma | Endocrine comorbidity |
| **Microbiome** | SCFAs, diversity | Stool | Gut metabolic health |

**Tiered approach:**
- **Tier 1 (Essential):** Adipic acid, fasting insulin, vitamin D, hs-CRP
- **Tier 2 (Expanded):** BCAAs, FFA profile, magnesium, advanced lipids
- **Tier 3 (Research):** Microbiome, telomeres, cytokine panel

**Integration value:** Single biomarkers miss the multifactorial nature of IR; panel approach captures metabolic, inflammatory, nutritional, and microbial dimensions simultaneously.

## Key Details

**Sample requirements:** Mixed — urine (organic acids), plasma (metabolic/inflammatory), stool (microbiome).
