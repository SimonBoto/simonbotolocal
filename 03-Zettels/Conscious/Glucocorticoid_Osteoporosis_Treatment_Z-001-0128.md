## Treatment Monitoring and Outcomes

### Monitoring Parameters
| Parameter | Frequency | Target |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| **DXA** | Every 12–24 months | Stabilization or improvement |
| **P1NP/CTX** | At 3 and 6 months | >30% change indicates response |
| **Serum calcium** | Annually | Normal range |
| **Renal function** | Annually | Normal |

### Expected Outcomes
| Therapy | 12-Month DXA Change | Fracture Risk Reduction |
|---------|---------------------|------------------------|
| **Bisphosphonate** | +2–4% spine, +1–2% hip | 40–50% vertebral |
| **Teriparatide** | +7–10% spine, +2–4% hip | 60–70% vertebral |
| **Calcium/Vit D** | +0.5–1.0% spine | 20–30% vertebral |

### Treatment Failure Criteria
- **DXA:** Continued bone loss despite therapy
- **BTMs:** No significant change at 3 months
- **Fracture:** New fragility fracture on therapy
