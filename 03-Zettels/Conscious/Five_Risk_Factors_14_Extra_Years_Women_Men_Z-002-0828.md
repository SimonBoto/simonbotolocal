# Five Modifiable Risk Factors Add ~14 Extra Years for Women, ~12 for Men

## Core Claim
**Individuals without any of the five major cardiovascular risk factors live approximately 14 extra years for women and 12 extra years for men** compared to those with all five factors.

## The Five Factors
| Factor | Modifiable? |
|--------|-------------|
| **High blood pressure** | ✅ Yes |
| **Cholesterol** | ✅ Yes |
| **Abnormal body weight** | ✅ Yes |
| **Diabetes** | ✅ Yes |
| **Smoking** | ✅ Yes |

## Longevity Impact
| Sex | Extra Years | Healthy Years |
|-----|-------------|---------------|
| **Women** | ~14 years | 13.3 years |
| **Men** | ~12 years | 10.6 years |

## CVD Risk with All 5 Factors
| Sex | Lifetime CVD Risk |
|-----|-------------------|
| **Women** | 25% (1 in 4) |
| **Men** | 33% (1 in 3) |

## Study Scale
- **2 million people**
- **39 countries**
- All factors are modifiable
