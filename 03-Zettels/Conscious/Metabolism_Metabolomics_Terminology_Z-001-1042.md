## Core Claim
**Nine core terms — Metabolism, Metabolites, Metabolomics, Biochemical Pathways, Redox Balance, and Energy Flow — form the methodological foundation of metabolomics practice, defining how we measure, interpret, and intervene in human biochemistry.**

## Evidence

### Core Definitions (9 Terms)
| Term | Definition | Clinical Relevance |
|------|------------|-------------------|
| **Metabolism** | Sum of all chemical reactions in organism | Anabolic vs catabolic balance |
| **Metabolites** | Small molecule intermediates and products | Markers of pathway function |
| **Metabolomics** | Comprehensive study of metabolites | Systems-level biochemical assessment |
| **Biochemical Pathways** | Linked enzymatic reactions | Interconnected networks, not isolated |
| **Redox Balance** | Oxidation-reduction equilibrium | NAD+/NADH, cellular energy status |
| **Energy Flow** | ATP production and utilization | Mitochondrial function central |

### Metabolomics Approaches
| Approach | Description | Application |
|----------|-------------|-------------|
| **Targeted** | Predefined metabolite panels | Clinical testing (OAT, amino acids) |
| **Untargeted** | Global metabolite profiling | Research, biomarker discovery |
| **Functional** | Dynamic metabolic assessment | Challenge tests, flux analysis |

### Key Pathways
| Pathway | Function | Key Metabolites |
|---------|----------|-----------------|
| **Glycolysis** | Glucose → Pyruvate | Glucose, pyruvate, lactate |
| **Krebs Cycle** | Acetyl-CoA oxidation | Citrate, α-KG, succinate |
| **Beta-oxidation** | Fatty acid breakdown | Acetyl-CoA, ketones |
| **Pentose Phosphate** | NADPH, ribose | 6-phosphogluconate |

### Redox Fundamentals
| Redox Pair | Role | Clinical Marker |
|------------|------|-----------------|
| **NAD+/NADH** | Electron transfer | Lactate/pyruvate ratio |
| **NADP+/NADPH** | Biosynthesis, antioxidant | Glutathione status |
| **GSH/GSSG** | Antioxidant defense | Oxidative stress indicator |

## Clinical Implication
- **Assessment framework:** Understanding terminology enables proper test selection
- **Interpretation:** Metabolite patterns reveal pathway function
- **Intervention:** Target pathways, not just symptoms
- **Communication:** Precise terminology ensures clear clinical notes
- **METHAP relevance:** Research methodology requires precise terminology; metabolomics as field depends on standardized definitions
