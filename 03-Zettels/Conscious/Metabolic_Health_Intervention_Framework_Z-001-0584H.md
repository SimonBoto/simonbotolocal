## Core Concept

Hierarchical intervention framework for addressing metabolic health, progressing from foundational to advanced strategies based on assessment results.

## Key Principles

- **Foundation Level:** Diet, sleep, movement, stress management, environmental optimization
- **Targeted Level:** Nutritional supplementation based on biomarker testing
- **Advanced Level:** Pharmaceutical interventions when clinically indicated
- **Integration:** Interventions must address all five pillars simultaneously for optimal results

## Clinical Significance

A structured intervention framework ensures systematic implementation of strategies that address the root causes of metabolic dysfunction across all five pillars, rather than isolated symptoms.
