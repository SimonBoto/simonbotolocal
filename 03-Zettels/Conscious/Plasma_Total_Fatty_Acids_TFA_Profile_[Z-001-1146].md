# Plasma Total Fatty Acids TFA Profile

## Overview
Plasma total fatty acid (TFA) analysis provides a comprehensive profile of membrane composition and dietary fat intake—reflecting inflammatory status, metabolic health, and cardiovascular risk in insulin resistance.

## Key Fatty Acid Markers

### Omega-3 Index
| Component | Optimal Range | IR Association |
|-----------|---------------|----------------|
| **EPA + DHA** | >8% of total fatty acids | Cardioprotection, anti-inflammatory |
| **Low levels** | <4% | Associated with IR, inflammation |

### Omega-6:3 Ratio
| Ratio | Interpretation |
|-------|----------------|
| **1-4:1** | Optimal, anti-inflammatory |
| **4-10:1** | Moderate risk |
| **>15:1** | High pro-inflammatory, common in IR |

### Saturated Fatty Acids (SFA)
| Fatty Acid | Source | IR Relevance |
|------------|--------|--------------|
| **Palmitic (16:0)** | Endogenous synthesis | Elevated in IR, lipotoxicity |
| **Stearic (18:0)** | Dietary | Less pathogenic |

### Monounsaturated Fatty Acids (MUFA)
| Fatty Acid | Association |
|------------|-------------|
| **Oleic acid (18:1)** | Cardioprotective |
| **Palmitoleic (16:1)** | Marker of DNL (de novo lipogenesis) |

### Polyunsaturated Fatty Acids (PUFA)
| Class | Members | Function |
|-------|---------|----------|
| **n-3** | EPA, DHA, ALA | Anti-inflammatory |
| **n-6** | LA, AA | Pro-inflammatory (in excess) |

## IR-Specific TFA Patterns

### Typical Insulin-Resistant Profile
| Feature | Observation | Mechanism |
|---------|-------------|-----------|
| **Low omega-3 index** | <4% | Poor intake, increased utilization |
| **High omega-6:3 ratio** | >15:1 | Seed oil consumption, inflammation |
| **Elevated palmitic acid** | High % | DNL upregulation |
| **Increased palmitoleic** | Elevated | DNL marker |
| **Low DHA** | Reduced | Membrane fluidity compromised |

### Metabolically Healthy Profile
| Feature | Observation |
|---------|-------------|
| **Omega-3 index** | >8% |
| **Omega-6:3 ratio** | 2-4:1 |
| **Balanced SFA/MUFA/PUFA** | Appropriate ratios |
| **High DHA** | Good membrane composition |

## METHAP TFA Protocol

### Sample Collection
| Parameter | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Fasting** | 12 hours |
| **Tube** | EDTA (lavender top) |
| **Volume** | 5-10 mL whole blood |
| **Processing** | Centrifuge within 2 hours |
| **Storage** | Plasma at -80°C |
| **Analysis** | Gas chromatography with FID |

### Analytical Panel
| Fatty Acid | Clinical Relevance |
|------------|-------------------|
| **C14:0 (Myristic)** | Dairy intake marker |
| **C16:0 (Palmitic)** | DNL, cardiovascular risk |
| **C16:1 (Palmitoleic)** | DNL marker |
| **C18:0 (Stearic)** | Dietary saturated fat |
| **C18:1 (Oleic)** | Olive oil, cardiovascular |
| **C18:2 (Linoleic)** | Omega-6, seed oils |
| **C18:3 (ALA)** | Plant omega-3 |
| **C20:4 (Arachidonic)** | Pro-inflammatory precursor |
| **C20:5 (EPA)** | Anti-inflammatory |
| **C22:6 (DHA)** | Brain, membrane function |

## Calculated Indices

### Cardiovascular Risk Markers
| Index | Calculation | Interpretation |
|-------|-------------|----------------|
| **Omega-3 index** | (EPA+DHA)/total × 100 | >8% optimal |
| **Omega-6:3 ratio** | Omega-6/Omega-3 | 2-4:1 optimal |
| **AA:EPA ratio** | Arachidonic/EPA | <3 optimal |
| **SFA:PUFA ratio** | SFA/PUFA | Lower better |

### Metabolic Indices
| Index | Metabolic Significance |
|-------|----------------------|
| **Palmitoleic:Palmitic** | DNL activity |
| **DNL index** | (16:0 + 16:1)/total | De novo lipogenesis |

## Intervention Targets

### Dietary Recommendations Based on TFA
| Finding | Recommendation |
|---------|----------------|
| **Low omega-3 index** | Increase fatty fish, EPA/DHA supplements |
| **High omega-6:3** | Reduce seed oils, increase omega-3 |
| **High palmitic** | Reduce carbs, increase exercise |
| **Low DHA** | Direct DHA supplementation |
