# Reviewer's Role Framework

> **Core Claim:** The reviewer's primary duty is to evaluate scientific validity, methodology, and ethical standards—not to rewrite manuscripts or impose personal preferences.

## Key Responsibilities

| Duty | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Evaluate validity** | Assess scientific soundness of claims |
| **Assess methodology** | Review study design and methods |
| **Ensure ethics** | Verify ethical compliance |
| **Maintain confidentiality** | Protect unpublished findings |
| **Meet deadlines** | Complete review within 2-4 weeks |

## Review Types

| Type | Description | Bias Reduction |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| **Single-blind** | Reviewer knows author | Low |
| **Double-blind** | Neither knows identity | Moderate |
| **Open review** | Both identities known | High transparency |

## What NOT to Do

- ❌ Rewrite the manuscript
- ❌ Impose personal preferences
- ❌ Delay without cause
- ❌ Breach confidentiality
