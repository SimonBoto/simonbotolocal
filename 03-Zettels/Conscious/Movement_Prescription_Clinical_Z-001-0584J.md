## Core Concept

Evidence-based movement prescription framework for clinical practice, designed to optimize metabolic health across all five pillars.

## Key Principles

- **Individualized Approach:** Movement prescriptions must be tailored to individual capabilities and metabolic status
- **Progressive Overload:** Gradually increase intensity and duration to avoid metabolic stress
- **Diverse Modalities:** Incorporate resistance training, aerobic conditioning, flexibility, and neuromuscular coordination
- **Metabolic Goals:** Prescribe movement to specifically improve mitochondrial function, insulin sensitivity, and metabolomic balance

## Clinical Significance

A clinical movement prescription framework ensures that physical activity interventions are evidence-based, individualized, and targeted to improve specific metabolic outcomes across all five pillars.
