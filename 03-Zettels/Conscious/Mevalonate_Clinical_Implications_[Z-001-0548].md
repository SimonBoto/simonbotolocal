# Mevalonate Pathway — Clinical Implications

> Understanding the pathway illuminates statin mechanism, side effects, and guides CoQ10 supplementation.

## Statin Effects

**Mechanism:**
- Competitive HMGCR inhibition
- Reduces cholesterol synthesis
- **Also reduces ALL pathway products**

## Side Effects

| Effect | Frequency | Mechanism |
|--------|-----------|-----------|
| **Myalgia** | 5-10% | CoQ10 depletion |
| **Fatigue** | Common | Mitochondrial dysfunction |
| **Rhabdomyolysis** | <0.1% | Severe muscle breakdown |

## Risk Factors

- High statin dose
- Lipophilic statins
- Drug interactions
- Renal impairment

## Management

**CoQ10 Supplementation:**
- 100-200 mg daily
- Ubiquinol preferred
- Separate from statin by 2-4 hours
