## Core Claim
**Just 15-20 minutes of vigorous exercise per week** is associated with **16-40% lower mortality risk** — a remarkably low threshold for significant health benefits.

## Dose-Response

| Amount | Benefit |
|--------|---------|
| **15-20 min/week** | 16-40% reduction |
| **~25 min/week** | 46% all-cause |
| **~45 min/week** | 37% cancer |

## Clinical Message

| Barrier | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| "No time" | 15 min/week sufficient |
| "Too hard" | Any vigorous activity counts |
