# Combined l Intervention Strategy

> Optimal  restoration requires multi-modal approach: diet, exercise, and targeted supplementation guided by .

## The Triad

**1. Diet — Metabolic Reset:**
- Low-carb (<100g/day)
- Adequate protein (1.2-1.6 g/kg)
- Healthy fats
- Intermittent fasting (16:8)

**2. Exercise — Biogenesis:**
- Zone 2: 150-180 min/week
- HIIT: 1-2 sessions/week
- Resistance: 2-3 sessions/week

**3. Supplementation:**
- B-complex (activated)
- CoQ10 (100-200 mg)
- Magnesium (200-400 mg)
- Carnitine (500-2000 mg)

## Synergy

**Diet + Exercise:**
- Fat adaptation enables exercise
- Exercise accelerates adaptation
- Combined > additive

## Monitoring

- **Baseline:** Organic acids, lipids, HbA1c
- **6-8 weeks:** Repeat organic acids
- **3-6 months:** Maintenance assessment
