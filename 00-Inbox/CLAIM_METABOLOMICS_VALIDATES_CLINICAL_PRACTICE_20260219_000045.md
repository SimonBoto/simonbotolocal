# Metabolomics Validation in Clinical Practice is Primary Goal

**Core Claim:** This research aims to validate the role of metabolomics (urine organic acids, plasma FFA) as clinically useful tools for insulin resistance detection.

**Current Gap:**
Metabolomics = research tool, not standard clinical practice

**Validation Pathway:**
1. Establish metabolite signatures that differentiate IR
2. Correlate with gold-standard measures (clamp studies)
3. Demonstrate earlier detection than conventional markers
4. Show reproducibility across populations
5. Develop clinical decision algorithms

**Clinical Translation Targets:**
- Point-of-care metabolite panels
- Risk stratification tools
- Monitoring therapeutic response
- Population screening programs

**Implication:**
Moving metabolomics from "research curiosity" to "clinical standard"

**Quote:**
> "validate the role of metabolomics in clinical practice"
