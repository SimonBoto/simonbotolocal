> **Capture zone for all new inputs**

## Purpose

The Inbox is the entry point for the Zettelkasten workflow:
1. **Capture** — Dump everything here first
2. **Process** — Review and decide destination
3. **Move** — To appropriate folder (Literature, References, Zettels)
