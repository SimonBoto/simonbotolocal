## 2. **Direct Website Subscriptions**  
Some apps ignore Google/Apple and bill you directly.

### Netflix
If Play Store doesn’t show Netflix:
- Open the **Netflix app**
- Top right → **Account**
- It will redirect to the **website** with your billing details  
or  
- Go directly: https://www.netflix.com/account  
Look under **Membership & Billing** → **Cancel Membership**

### Huberman Lab Premium (FoundMyFitness? Momentous? LMNT? Patreon?)

- hubermanlab.com → Log in → Account → Subscriptions

- go to **Momentous** or **FoundMyFitness Premium** depending on what you bought  
or  
If you subscribed to **Huberman via Patreon** (very common):
- patreon.com → Profile → **Memberships** → Cancel

If you’re unsure, search your email (below).

These apps in your screenshot are all **expense-management** platforms. They’re mainly used by companies to track receipts, travel expenses, and reimbursements.  

Most of them **are not fully free** for personal use — they offer free trials or very limited free tiers, but the real features are behind paid business plans.

Here’s the quick, honest breakdown:

### ✅ **Totally free (or very close to free)**
**Zoho Expense**

• One user  
• Basic receipt capture  
• Basic reports  
Still limited, but it actually works without needing a company account.
