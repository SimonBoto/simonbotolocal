Mitigations: multi-metric dashboards, random audits, outcome sampling, adversarial metrics.

Mitigations: new dignity institutions (service corps, apprenticeship, civic roles), safety nets tied to participation not coercion.

**R9) Model Monoculture Failure**  
Mechanism: a few models dominate; correlated errors become systemic.  
Early signals: same vendor everywhere; simultaneous failures; identical bias patterns.

Mitigations: value disclosure, stakeholder review, constraint-based design, impact assessments.

# 9) PROVENANCE STACK: minimum viable (and where it fails)

### Goal
Make verification **fast, default, and boring**—like HTTPS.

### Components
1) **Signing:** originator signs content (org key, journalist key, agency key).  
2) **Chain-of-custody:** edits/transforms logged (who/when/what).  
3) **Verification UI:** the viewer sees a simple “Verified / Unverified / Broken chain” indicator.  
4) **Auditability:** third parties can validate without trusting a platform.  
5) **Fallback:** watermarking is optional; signatures are primary.

### Failure modes
- Key theft → “verified lies”  
- Social engineering → users ignore warnings  
- Platform capture → verification hidden

### Countermeasures
- Hardware-backed keys; rotation; incident revocation lists  
- UX design: warnings must be hard to ignore for high-reach content  
- Open standards + independent verification tools
