## **I. Defining Anthropocentric Bias**

**Anthropocentric** (Greek *ánthrōpos*, human being + *kéntron*, center):

- **Anthropocentrism** is the philosophical or cognitive bias that positions human beings at the center of reality or existence, inherently assuming human superiority or unique significance above all other species and forms of existence.
- **Anthropocentric Bias**, specifically, refers to the often unconscious tendency to evaluate other entities (animals, nature, intelligence) using human standards and values, thus distorting objective understanding.

---

### **1. Religious Foundations**
