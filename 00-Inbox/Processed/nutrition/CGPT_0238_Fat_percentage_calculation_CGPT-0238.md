### 8. **ChatGPT:**

To create a formula for combining **1 liter (1000 ml) of 4% milk** with **cream of 38% fat**, we generalize the calculation as follows:

---

### **General Formula**
Let:
- \( V_m \): Volume of milk (in ml, typically 1000 ml)
- \( P_m \): Percentage fat of milk (4% = 0.04)
- \( V_c \): Volume of cream (in ml, e.g., 200, 400, 600 ml)
- \( P_c \): Percentage fat of cream (38% = 0.38)

The formula for the fat percentage of the mixture (\( P_{mix} \)) is:

\[
P_{mix} = \frac{\left( V_m \times P_m \right) + \left( V_c \times P_c \right)}{V_m + V_c} \times 100
\]

---

### **Examples Using the Formula**
1. **1 liter (4%) + 200 ml (38%)**
   \[
   P_{mix} = \frac{(1000 \times 0.04) + (200 \times 0.38)}{1000 + 200} \times 100
   \]
   \[
   P_{mix} = \frac{40 + 76}{1200} \times 100 = 9.67\%
   \]

2. **1 liter (4%) + 400 ml (38%)**
   \[
   P_{mix} = \frac{(1000 \times 0.04) + (400 \times 0.38)}{1000 + 400} \times 100
   \]
   \[
   P_{mix} = \frac{40 + 152}{1400} \times 100 = 13.71\%
   \]

3. **1 liter (4%) + 600 ml (38%)**
   \[
   P_{mix} = \frac{(1000 \times 0.04) + (600 \times 0.38)}{1000 + 600} \times 100
   \]
   \[
   P_{mix} = \frac{40 + 228}{1600} \times 100 = 16.75\%
   \]

---

### **Final Formula**
For any combination of milk and cream:
\[
P_{mix} = \frac{(V_m \times P_m) + (V_c \times P_c)}{V_m + V_c} \times 100
\]
