Athens offers a variety of neighborhoods that are well-suited for families, each with distinct characteristics and amenities. Here are some of the most recommended areas for families considering their safety, schools, parks, and general family-friendly atmosphere:

These areas are less touristy, offering a more authentic Athenian experience while still providing all necessary amenities for families.

5. **Marousi**: Another northern suburb that is ideal for families due to its residential feel, access to shopping malls like The Mall Athens, and numerous schools. Marousi also hosts facilities for sports and cultural activities, which can be very appealing for children and adults alike.

Each of these areas offers different advantages, so the best choice depends on your family's specific needs, such as proximity to work, lifestyle preferences, and educational priorities.
