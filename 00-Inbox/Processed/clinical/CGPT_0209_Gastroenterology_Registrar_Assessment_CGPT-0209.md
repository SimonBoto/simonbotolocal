## 1. Mechanisms and Diagnosis of Inflammatory Bowel Disease

**Question**:

**Points to Cover**:  
- Immunological dysregulation, microbiome influence, and genetic predisposition.  
- Clinical differences (e.g., location, extent, and nature of lesions).

- Role of fecal calprotectin, C-reactive protein (CRP), and advanced imaging (MRI enterography vs. capsule endoscopy).
