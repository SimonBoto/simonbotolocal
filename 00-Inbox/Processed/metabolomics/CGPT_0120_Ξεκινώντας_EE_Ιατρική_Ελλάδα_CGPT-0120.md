### **2. Legal & Regulatory Registration**
#### **a) Establishing the Company**
- **Prepare the Articles of Association (Καταστατικό)**  
  This document defines the company’s purpose, structure, and shareholder agreements.
- **Submit to GEMI (Γενικό Εμπορικό Μητρώο)**
  - Registration is done via the **One-Stop-Shop Service** (Ενιαίο Κέντρο Εξυπηρέτησης – EKEM).
  - Submit identity documents, the Articles of Association, and proof of address.

  - Essential for all financial transactions.

#### **b) Licensing as a Medical Company**
- **Medical Business License**  
  Since you are a doctor, you may need:
  - Approval from the **National Organization for Healthcare Services (EOPYY)** if dealing with patient insurance.
  - **Ministry of Health approval** for diagnostic/metabolomics-related activities.
- **Medical Association Registration**  
  If you plan to practice, your business must be registered with the Greek Medical Association.
