# Omega-3 & Hemodialysis — 43% CVD Risk Reduction

**Source:** Lok et al., NEJM 2026
**Population:** 1,228 hemodialysis patients, 26 centers, Canada/Australia, 3.5 years
**Intervention:** 4g fish oil daily (1600mg EPA + 800mg DHA)
**Result:** 43% reduction in major cardiovascular events vs placebo (corn oil)

## Key Points
- High-dose omega-3 works where low-dose failed (dose-response)
- Hemodialysis patients = extreme metabolic dysfunction model
- Mechanisms: anti-inflammatory, endothelial function, ↓TG, antithrombotic
- Supported by 2 meta-analyses (Dong 2024, Dinu 2024)
