# Three-Member Committee Unanimous Approval Process

**Committee Structure:**
Three-member Advisory Committee (Τριμελής Συμβουλευτική Επιτροπή)

**Decision:**
**Unanimous approval** (ομόφωνα)

**Approval Criteria Met:**
1. ✓ Can constitute PhD dissertation subject
2. ✓ Expects to produce new knowledge
3. ✓ Results publishable in international journals
4. ✓ Applicant considered suitable with sufficient background

**Post-Approval Requirements:**
- Minimum 2 courses (8 ECTS) related to thesis topic
- Course declaration during PhD execution
- Annual progress reports (implied)

**Publication Strategy:**
- Target: International journals
- Implied: Peer-reviewed, PubMed-indexed
- Language: English (thesis language specified)

**Website Posting:**
After approval, thesis title and summary posted on School website for public record.

**Next Steps After Approval:**
1. Course registration
2. IRB/Ethics Committee (Επιτροπή Ηθικής και Δεοντολογίας)
3. Patient recruitment
4. Data collection initiation
