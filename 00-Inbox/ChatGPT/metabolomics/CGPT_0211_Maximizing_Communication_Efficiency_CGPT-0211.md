### Step 5: Implement a Structured Communication Framework
- **Action**: Choose a communication cadence and format that keeps you on track—this could be weekly check-ins, daily reminders, or real-time chats.  
- **Rationale**: Regular feedback loops strengthen the efficiency of dialogue and help refine your strategies (Argyris & Schön, 1974).  
- **Tip**: Use concise bullet points for clarity, but schedule deeper, more reflective sessions for strategic planning or conceptual exploration.
