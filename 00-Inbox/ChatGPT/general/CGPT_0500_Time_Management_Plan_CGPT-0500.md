It sounds like you have a lot on your plate! Here's a general approach to organizing your time:

1. **Prioritize tasks:** Identify the most important tasks each day and week. These could include work commitments, spending time with your girlfriend, household chores, and studying for candidates.

2. **Time blocking:** Allocate specific time slots for different activities. Dedicate blocks of time for work, study, relaxation, and spending time with your girlfriend.

3. **Set boundaries:** Make sure to set boundaries to avoid overcommitting yourself. This could involve saying no to additional responsibilities or delegating tasks when possible.

By following these steps, you can effectively manage your time and balance your various commitments.
