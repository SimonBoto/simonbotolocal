8. **Malate** - Produced by the hydration of fumarate.
9. **Oxaloacetate** - Regenerated from the oxidation of malate, which then combines with acetyl-CoA to continue the cycle.

These metabolites and the reactions that connect them facilitate the conversion of energy from organic molecules into usable forms, making the Krebs cycle a pivotal pathway in aerobic metabolism.
