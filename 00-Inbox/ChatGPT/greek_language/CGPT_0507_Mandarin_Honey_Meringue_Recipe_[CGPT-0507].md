You can make a Mandarin Honey Meringue with your ingredients. Here's a simple recipe:

### Mandarin Honey Meringue

#### Ingredients
- 4 egg whites
- 1/2 cup honey
- 2-3 mandarins (zest and juice)
- A pinch of salt

#### Instructions
1. **Preheat Oven:** Preheat your oven to 250°F (120°C).
   
2. **Whip Egg Whites:** In a clean, dry bowl, whip the egg whites with a pinch of salt until soft peaks form.

3. **Add Honey:** Gradually add the honey while continuing to whip. The mixture should become glossy and form stiff peaks.

4. **Mandarin Zest:** Gently fold in half of the mandarin zest.

5. **Bake:** Spoon the meringue onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Form into small nests or a large pavlova.

6. **Slow Bake:** Bake for 1 to 1.5 hours, until the meringue is dry and crisp. Turn off the oven and let the meringue cool inside to avoid cracks.

7. **Serve:** Top with mandarin segments, the remaining zest, and a drizzle of honey. Optionally, add whipped cream or Greek yogurt.

Enjoy your Mandarin Honey Meringue! It’s a light, citrusy dessert that's perfect for a healthy treat.

### Baked Mandarin and Honey Yogurt Custard

#### Ingredients
- 4 egg whites
- 1/2 cup honey, plus extra for drizzling
- 2-3 mandarins (zest and juice)
- 1 cup Greek yogurt
- A pinch of salt

#### Instructions
1. **Preheat Oven:** Preheat your oven to 325°F (165°C).

5. **Fill Ramekins:** Pour the mixture into the ramekins or baking dish.

7. **Bake:** Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the custard is set but still slightly wobbly in the center.

8. **Cool and Chill:** Remove from the oven and let cool. Chill in the refrigerator for at least an hour.

9. **Serve:** Drizzle with a little extra honey and garnish with mandarin segments or zest before serving.

### Mandarin Yogurt Cake with Honey Meringue Frosting

#### Ingredients
- Cake:
  - 1 cup Greek yogurt
  - 2 cups all-purpose flour
  - 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  - 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  - 1/4 teaspoon salt
  - 3/4 cup honey
  - 1/2 cup ghee, melted
  - Zest of 2 mandarins
  - 2 tablespoons mandarin juice
- Meringue Frosting:
  - 4 egg whites
  - 1 cup honey
  - A pinch of salt

#### Instructions
1. **Preheat Oven:** Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a cake pan with a little ghee.

2. **Combine Dry Ingredients:** In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

3. **Mix Wet Ingredients:** In another bowl, whisk together the yogurt, honey, melted ghee, mandarin zest, and juice.

4. **Combine:** Gradually mix the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until just combined.

5. **Bake the Cake:** Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean. Let the cake cool.

6. **Make Meringue Frosting:** In a heatproof bowl, whisk egg whites, honey, and salt. Set over a pot of simmering water (double boiler). Whisk continuously until the mixture is warm and honey is dissolved.

7. **Whip Meringue:** Remove from heat and whip the mixture with an electric mixer until stiff peaks form and the meringue has cooled.
