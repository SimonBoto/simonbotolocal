### **Pelias: The Ruthless Usurper**

Pelias became infamous for his ambition and cruelty. His actions set the stage for some of the most significant events in Greek mythology, including the quest for the Golden Fleece.

1. **Claiming the Throne**  
   - Pelias overthrew his stepmother Sidero, who had mistreated Tyro. He killed her in a temple of Hera, offending the goddess.
   - He seized the throne of Iolcus (a city in Thessaly), exiling Neleus in the process.

2. **Jason and the Golden Fleece**  
   - A prophecy warned Pelias to beware a man wearing one sandal. This man turned out to be Jason, his nephew.
   - Pelias sent Jason on the perilous quest to retrieve the Golden Fleece, hoping the journey would lead to his death. This quest forms the basis of the famous Argonauts' expedition.

3. **Death**

3. **Conflict with Heracles**
