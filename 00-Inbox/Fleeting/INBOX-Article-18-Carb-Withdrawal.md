# Article 18: Carbohydrate Withdrawal and Keto-Adaptation

## Key Findings

### Carb Withdrawal Syndrome (Week 1-2)
| Symptom | Peak | Duration |
|---------|------|----------|
| **Fatigue** | Days 3-7 | Resolves by week 2 |
| **Irritability** | Days 3-7 | Resolves by week 2 |
| **Carb cravings** | Days 3-7 | Resolves by week 2 |
| **Impaired concentration** | Days 3-7 | Resolves by week 2 |
| **Decreased energy** | Days 3-7 | Resolves by week 2 |

**Not true hypoglycemia** — distinct metabolic adaptation process.

### Keto-Adaptation Timeline
| Phase | Timeline | Status |
|-------|----------|--------|
| **Nutritional ketosis** | Days 2-3 | Ketones detectable |
| **Withdrawal resolution** | Week 2 | Symptoms resolve |
| **Full adaptation** | 3-4 weeks | Energy, mood, cognition exceed baseline |

### Clinical Counseling Points
1. **Pre-emptive education** — warn patients about week 1-2 difficulty
2. **Set expectations** — "3-4 weeks to feel better than before"
3. **Supportive counseling** — critical during days 3-14
4. **Persistence pays off** — energy and cognition improve beyond baseline
