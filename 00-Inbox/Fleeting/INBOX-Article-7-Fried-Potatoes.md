# Article 7: Fried Potatoes in Seed Oils Increase T2D Risk

## Key Findings

### Primary Evidence
- Fried potatoes in seed oils: **+20% diabetes risk**
- Independent of calorie content
- Mechanism: Oxidized omega-6 fatty acids

### Seed Oil Problems
- High omega-6:omega-3 ratio promotes inflammation
- Oxidation during frying creates toxic byproducts
- Disrupts insulin signaling
- Damages mitochondrial function

### Foods to Avoid
- French fries in vegetable/seed oils
- Fried foods in restaurants
- Processed snacks with seed oils

### Better Alternatives
- Olive oil (monounsaturated, more stable)
- Animal fats (tallow, lard)
- Coconut oil (saturated, heat-stable)
- Air frying or baking instead of deep frying
