# Article 33: Pediatric Obesity Historic Reversal

## Key Findings

### The Historic Shift
| Metric | 2000 | Today (2025) |
|--------|------|--------------|
| **Underweight children** | 13% | 9.2% |
| **Obese children** | 3% | 9.4% |
| **Historic reversal** | Underweight > Obese | Obese > Underweight |

### Scale of Problem
| Statistic | Number |
|-----------|--------|
| **Children with obesity** | 188 million (1 in 10) |
| **Countries affected** | 190+ |
| **Global regions affected** | All except sub-Saharan Africa, South Asia |

### Root Cause: Ultra-Processed Foods
| Factor | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **UPF availability** | Cheap, everywhere |
| **Aggressive marketing** | Targets children |
| **High calories, low nutrition** | Causes malnutrition + obesity |
| **Industry tactics** | Undermine health policies |

### UNICEF Warning
> "Unhealthy foods and beverages, including ultra-processed, are widely available, cheap, and aggressively promoted where children live, learn, and play."

### Key Insight
**Obesity is now a bigger threat than undernutrition** — the fight has changed character from food scarcity to food quality.
