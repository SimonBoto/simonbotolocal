# Article 5: High LDL Not Linked to Mortality in Healthy People

## Key Findings

### The LDL Paradox
- High LDL **not** linked to increased mortality in people without existing CVD
- Challenges universal statin prescription
- Questions LDL-centric risk assessment

### Context
- LDL is one factor among many
- Metabolic health matters more than isolated lipid values
- Inflammation, insulin resistance more predictive

### Clinical Implications
- Reconsider statin therapy for primary prevention
- Focus on overall metabolic profile
- Personalized risk assessment needed

## Key References
