# Article 32: Lifestyle Disease Factors Are Modifiable

## Key Findings

### The 80/20 Rule of Health
| Factor Type | Contribution | Modifiable? |
|-------------|--------------|-------------|
| **Lifestyle** | 80% | ✅ Yes |
| **Genetics** | 20% | ❌ No (but expression is) |

### Modifiable Determinants
| Factor | Impact | Intervention |
|--------|--------|--------------|
| **Diet** | Highest | Whole foods, eliminate UPF |
| **Exercise** | High | Daily movement |
| **Sleep** | High | 7-9 hours, quality |
| **Stress** | Moderate-high | Management techniques |
| **Smoking** | Very high | Cessation |
| **Alcohol** | Moderate | Limit/eliminate |

### Non-Modifiable (But Expression Is)
| Factor | What We Can Do |
|--------|----------------|
| **Genetics** | Epigenetic modification via lifestyle |
| **Age** | Slow biological aging |
| **Sex** | Optimize for biology |

### Key Insight
**Genetics loads the gun, lifestyle pulls the trigger.**
