# Article 4: Hashimoto's Low-Carb Diet Reduces Autoantibodies

## Key Findings

### Clinical Evidence
Low-carbohydrate diet reduces:
- Thyroid autoantibodies (TPOAb, TgAb)
- Thyroid inflammation (measured by MRI Dixon-T2WI)
- Autoimmune activity in Hashimoto's thyroiditis

### Mechanism
- Reduces insulin resistance (linked to autoimmune activity)
- Decreases systemic inflammation
- May improve gut barrier function
- Reduces oxidative stress

### Clinical Significance
- First-line dietary intervention for Hashimoto's
- May reduce need for medication in early stages
- Supports thyroid function preservation

## Key References
1. **Huang XS, et al.** MRI quantitative assessment of the effects of low-carbohydrate therapy on Hashimoto's thyroiditis. *Endocr Connect*. 2024;13(5):e230477. doi:10.1530/EC-23-0477
