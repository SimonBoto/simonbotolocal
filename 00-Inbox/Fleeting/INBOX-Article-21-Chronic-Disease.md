# Article 21: Chronic Diseases Cause 74% of Global Deaths

## Key Findings

### Global Burden (GBD 2019)
| Statistic | Figure |
|-----------|--------|
| **Global deaths from NCDs** | 74.36% of all deaths |
| **Developed countries** | ~90% from chronic disease |
| **Developing countries** | Increasing burden rapidly |

### Non-Communicable Diseases (NCDs)
| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Cardiovascular diseases** | Heart attack, stroke |
| **Cancers** | All types |
| **Chronic respiratory** | COPD, asthma |
| **Diabetes** | T2D, T1D |

### Epidemiological Transition
| Era | Primary Causes of Death |
|-----|------------------------|
| **Past (1900s)** | Infectious diseases, acute conditions |
| **Present (2020s)** | Chronic, lifestyle-related diseases |

### Implications
- Chronic diseases are **preventable** through lifestyle
- **Metabolic health** is central to prevention
- **Diet, exercise, sleep** modulate risk
