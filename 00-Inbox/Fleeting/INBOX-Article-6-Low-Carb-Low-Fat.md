# Article 6: Low Carb vs. Low Fat for Metabolic Health

## Key Findings

### Diet Comparison
- **Low-carb:** Better for metabolic syndrome, insulin resistance
- **Low-fat:** Traditional approach, less effective for metabolic health
- **RCT evidence:** Low-carb superior for weight loss, glycemic control

### Metabolic Outcomes
| Outcome | Low-Carb | Low-Fat |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Weight loss | Greater | Less |
| Triglycerides | Lower | Higher |
| HDL | Higher | Lower |
| Insulin sensitivity | Improved | Minimal change |
| Satiety | Better | Worse |

### Clinical Context
- Individual response varies
- Insulin resistance determines optimal approach
- Metabolic health > calorie counting

## Key References
