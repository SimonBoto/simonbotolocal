# Article 24: Alcohol Causes 1.8 Million Deaths Annually

## Key Findings

### Mortality Burden (GBD 2019)
| Statistic | Figure |
|-----------|--------|
| **Annual deaths** | 1.8 million |
| **Global ranking** | #9 risk factor |
| **Gender split** | 2.07M male, 374K female |

### Disease Mechanisms
| Condition | Association |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Liver disease** | Direct (cirrhosis, hepatitis) |
| **Cancer** | Multiple types (oral, throat, liver, breast) |
| **Cardiovascular** | Hypertension, stroke |
| **Accidents** | Injuries, violence |
| **Mental health** | Depression, suicide |

### Key Insight
**No safe level of alcohol consumption** — risk increases with any amount.

### Zero vs Low
| Consumption | Risk |
|-------------|------|
| **Zero alcohol** | Lowest risk |
| **Low alcohol** | Higher than zero |
| **Moderate** | Progressive increase |
| **High** | Highest risk |
