# Article 3: Carbohydrate Restriction Reduces CVD Risk

## Key Findings

### Primary Evidence
Low-carbohydrate diets reduce cardiovascular risk factors through multiple mechanisms.

### Metabolic Benefits
- Improves lipid profile (reduces triglycerides, increases HDL)
- Reduces insulin resistance
- Promotes weight loss
- Decreases inflammation markers

### Comparison to Low-Fat
- More effective for metabolic syndrome
- Better adherence in long-term studies
- Superior for glycemic control

### Clinical Context
- Challenges traditional diet-heart hypothesis
- Supports personalized nutrition approaches
- Particularly effective for insulin-resistant individuals

## Key References
1. **Zheng Q, et al.** Are low carbohydrate diet interventions beneficial for metabolic syndrome and its components? A systematic review and meta-analysis. *Int J Obes*. 2025. doi:10.1038/s41366-025-01822-5
