# Article 29: Not All Calories Are Equal

## Key Findings

### The "Calories In, Calories Out" Myth
| Assumption | Reality |
|------------|---------|
| "All calories equal" | ❌ Metabolically different |
| "Just eat less" | ❌ Ignores metabolic adaptation |
| "A calorie is a calorie" | ❌ Macronutrients differ |

### Thermic Effect of Food (TEF)
| Macronutrient | TEF | Net Energy |
|---------------|-----|------------|
| **Protein** | 20-30% | 70-80% absorbed |
| **Carbohydrates** | 5-10% | 90-95% absorbed |
| **Fats** | 0-3% | 97-100% absorbed |

### Low-Carb Metabolic Advantage
| Finding | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Reduced carbs** | Higher energy expenditure |
| **Same calories** | More weight loss on low-carb |
| **Metabolic adaptation** | Less on low-carb |

### Ebbeling Study (BMJ 2018)
| Diet | Energy Expenditure |
|------|-------------------|
| **High-carb** | Baseline |
| **Moderate-carb** | +91 kcal/day |
| **Low-carb** | +278 kcal/day |
