# Article 22: Unhealthy Diet Causes 11 Million Deaths Annually

## Key Findings

### Mortality Impact (GBD 2017)
| Statistic | Figure |
|-----------|--------|
| **Deaths from unhealthy diet** | 11 million/year |
| **Global proportion** | 1 in 5 deaths |
| **Leading risk factor** | #1 cause of death globally |

### Dietary Problems
| Issue | Result |
|-------|--------|
| **Reduced healthy foods** | Nutrient deficiencies |
| **High processed foods** | Metabolic disease |
| **Excess sodium** | Hypertension, CVD |
| **Low whole grains** | Missed fiber, nutrients |
| **Low fruits/vegetables** | Missed antioxidants |

### Top Dietary Risk Factors
| Risk | Impact |
|------|--------|
| **High sodium** | Cardiovascular disease |
| **Low whole grains** | CVD, diabetes |
| **Low fruits** | CVD, cancer |
| **Low nuts/seeds** | CVD |
| **Low vegetables** | Multiple diseases |

### Key Insight
**Diet quality matters more than diet quantity** — what you eat (or don't eat) kills more people than how much you eat.
