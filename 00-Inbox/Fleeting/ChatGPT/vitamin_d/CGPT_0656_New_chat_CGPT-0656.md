I. Vitamin D Production

There are two main forms of vitamin D: cholecalciferol (vitamin D3) and ergocalciferol (vitamin D2). Vitamin D3 is synthesized in the skin from 7-dehydrocholesterol, while vitamin D2 is obtained through the diet from sources like mushrooms, yeast, and fortified foods.
