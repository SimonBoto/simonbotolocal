# Article 15: Inflammation and CVD Risk (hsCRP)

## Key Findings

### CRP as Cardiovascular Predictor
- **High CRP** linked to greater heart attack probability
- Predicts risk even in people without known CVD
- **hsCRP (high-sensitivity CRP)** measurement valuable for risk stratification

### Chronic Inflammation: Root Cause
| Category | Causes |
|----------|--------|
| **Ongoing damage** | UPF, smoking, excess alcohol |
| **Deficiencies** | Vitamins, minerals, omega-3s preventing healing completion |

### Consequences of Chronic Inflammation
- Increased CVD risk
- Insulin resistance
- Autoimmune diseases
- Diabetes
- Cancer

### Clinical Approach
- **Resolving chronic inflammation is central to prevention**
- Address root causes, not just symptoms
- hsCRP as biomarker for monitoring
