# Metabolomics Method: Urine Organic Acids + Plasma Free Fatty Acids via GC-MS

**Purpose:** Detect insulin resistance at metabolic pathway level before clinical manifestation

**Biomaterials:**
| Sample Type | Analytes | Method |
|-------------|----------|--------|
| Urine | Organic acids | GC-MS |
| Plasma | Free fatty acids | GC-MS |

**Procedure Overview:**
1. Sample collection (fasting state)
2. Metabolite extraction
3. Derivatization (for GC compatibility)
4. GC-MS analysis
5. Data processing (peak identification, quantification)
6. Statistical analysis (PCA, multivariate)

**Parameters:**
- **Platform:** Gas Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry
- **Detection:** Mass spectrometry (electron impact ionization)
- **Data Volume:** Large dataset (hundreds of metabolites per sample)

**Advantages Over Conventional:**
- Captures real-time metabolic flux
- Multiple pathways simultaneously
- Earlier changes than glucose/insulin

**Controls:**
- Healthy control group (age/sex matched)
- Quality control samples (pooled)
- Internal standards
