## Decision Trees / Checklists

### Initial Assessment
- [ ] OAT: citrate, isocitrate, aconitate, succinate
- [ ] Plasma lactate/pyruvate ratio
- [ ] BHB fasting levels
- [ ] Organic acids: adipate, suberic, ethylmalonic

### Severity Stratification
```
Mild (1-2 markers elevated) → Dietary + basic supplements
Moderate (3-4 markers) → Targeted mitochondrial support
Severe (5+ markers) → Comprehensive metabolic therapy
```

### Intervention Hierarchy
1. **Foundation:** B-vitamins, magnesium, CoQ10
2. **Ketones:** MCT oil, intermittent fasting
3. **Advanced:** Alpha-lipoic acid, PQQ, NAD+ precursors
