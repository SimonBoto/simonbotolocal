# Supplement Protocol Framework — Timing & Combinations

> **Standardized supplement dosing schedule for clinical practice**

---

## Timing Principles

| Time Window | Rationale | Best For |
|-------------|-----------|----------|
| **Morning (after breakfast)** | Fat-soluble vitamins need food | D3, K2, E, CoQ10 |
| **Midday (after main meal)** | Maximum absorption with largest meal | Fish oil, iron, minerals |
| **Evening (after dinner)** | Calming nutrients, overnight repair | Magnesium, B-complex, probiotics |
| **Exercise days** | Enhanced recovery | Protein, minerals, energy support |
| **Stress days** | Acute support | Adaptogens, B-vitamins |

---

## Core Daily Stack

### Morning (After Breakfast)
- **Multivitamin foundation** — My Total Health / My Health
- **Metabolic support** — My Shape (ALA, cinnamon, chromium)
- **Immune baseline** — My Immuno
- **Vitamin D3** — 0.4ml (with K2, magnesium)
- **Stress support** — My Antistress (as needed)

### Midday (After Main Meal)
- **Omega-3 fatty acids** — 2 capsules (refrigerated)
- **Vitamin C** — 2 capsules
- **Gut support** — My Gut Life
- **Additional immune** — My Immuno Powder

### Evening (After Dinner)
- **Magnesium** — My CalMag (powder + tablets)
- **Probiotics** — Multiple strains (40 billion CFU)
- **Liver support** — Healthy Liver
- **Digestive enzymes** — Easy Digest

## Conditional Additions

| Condition | Addition | Timing |
|-----------|----------|--------|
| **Exercise days** | My Energy +2 caps | Post-workout |
| **High stress** | My Antistress +1 cap | Afternoon |
| **Iron deficiency** | Iron Softly Absorbed | Morning/midday |
| **Sleep issues** | Magnesium glycinate | Evening |
| **Joint pain** | My Relief | Morning & midday |

---

## Storage Requirements

| Supplement | Storage | Reason |
|------------|---------|--------|
| Omega-3 (krill/fish) | **Refrigerate** | Oxidation prevention |
| Probiotics | Cool, dry place | Viability |
| Vitamin D3 | Room temperature | Stability |
| B-complex | Room temperature | Stability |

---

## Key Combinations

**Synergistic Pairs:**
- Vitamin D3 + K2 + Magnesium (bone/cardiovascular)
- Iron + Vitamin C (absorption)
- Probiotics + Prebiotics (gut health)
- B-complex + Magnesium (stress/energy)

**Separations:**
- Iron away from calcium (competition)
- Zinc away from copper (competition)
- Fiber supplements away from medications (absorption)
