### Intervention Phases
**Phase 1 (Month 1-2): Elimination**
- Remove seed oils from home
- Reduce restaurant meals
- Switch to: olive oil, butter, tallow, coconut oil

**Phase 2 (Month 3-6): Optimization**
- Increase fatty fish (3x/week)
- Add omega-3 supplement (2-4g EPA+DHA)
- Pasture-raised animal products

**Phase 3 (Month 6-12): Maintenance**
- Monitor tissue levels (RBC fatty acids)
- Adjust based on inflammatory markers
