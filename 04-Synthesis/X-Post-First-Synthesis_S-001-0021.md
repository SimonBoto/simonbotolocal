**Tweet 2/5 — Mechanism:**
```
Chronic stress = elevated cortisol

Cortisol:
→ Impairs mitochondrial function
→ Disrupts insulin sensitivity  
→ Alters glucose & fatty acid metabolism
→ Depletes magnesium & zinc

Your body can't burn fat when it thinks it's running from a lion.
```

**Tweet 3/5 — The Science:**
```
Stress impacts ALL 5 pillars of metabolic health:

1. Mitochondria — impaired biogenesis
2. Metabolome — shifted glucose/AA profiles
3. Glycation — increased oxidative stress
4. Minerals — depleted Mg, Zn
5. Fatty acids — altered metabolism

One variable. System-wide effects.
```

**Tweet 4/5 — Clinical Reality:**
```
I've seen patients with perfect diets fail to improve because their stress hormones were chronically elevated.

You cannot out-diet chronic stress.

Your metabolism is a system, not a calculator.
```

• Morning sunlight (10 min)

Start with ONE. Your metabolism will thank you.
