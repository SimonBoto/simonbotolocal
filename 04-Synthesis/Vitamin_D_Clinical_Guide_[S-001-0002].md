## Decision Trees / Checklists

### Initial Assessment
- [ ] Baseline 25(OH)D level
- [ ] PTH level
- [ ] Calcium, magnesium
- [ ] Vitamin K2 status (MK-7)
- [ ] Medications (thiazides, statins)

### Dosing Decision
```
25(OH)D < 30 nmol/L → 50,000 IU x 4 weeks, then 10,000 IU daily
25(OH)D 30-80 nmol/L → 10,000 IU daily
25(OH)D > 150 nmol/L → Reduce dose, monitor
```

### Monitoring Schedule
- [ ] 25(OH)D at 8 weeks
- [ ] Calcium, PTH at 12 weeks
- [ ] Adjust to maintain 80-150 nmol/L
