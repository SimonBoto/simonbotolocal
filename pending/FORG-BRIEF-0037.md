## Summary
Beta-carotene sources: nat extraction (Dunaliella salina cult/salt ponds/solvent/CO2 purif E160a(i)), synth (acetone/isophorone → isoprenoids → C40 chain/cycliz/purif E160a(ii), no direct petrol). Lung ca trials: ATBC smokers +18% (20mg/d), CARET smokers/asbestos +28% (30mg+retinol 25kIU, stopped early), Physicians non-smokers no effect. Pro-ox hyp in smokers (ROS metab harm), high-dose/combo issues, food matrix safer.

## Key Points
- Nat prod: algae stress → high yield, sust.
- Synth: chem id to nat, cost/high vol.
- Risks: smokers/asbestos avoid supp; diet OK.
- CARET probs: high-risk/high-dose/no low-risk ctrl/early stop/persist risk.
