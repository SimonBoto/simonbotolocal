## Summary
Guide to start Zettelkasten in Obsidian: vault setup, templates (literature/permanent), atomic notes, contextual links (direct/contradict/extend), avoid overlinking. Prototypes: lit notes (summary/quotes/insights), perm notes (core idea/connections/implications). Ex literature zettels from Greek article on vitamin safety/history (RDA origins 1835-1942, modern deficiencies, supplements safe/not hepatotoxic).

## Key Points
- Atomic: 1 idea/note; link intentionally.
- Links: context/contradict/extend; use Obsidian graph/unlinked mentions.
- Templates: Source/summary/quotes for lit; core/evidence/connections for perm.
- Overlink risks: overload/dilute; prune/review.
- Vitamins: RDA mins for deficiency; supps essential vs processed food; fat-sol (A/D/E) monitor high doses.
