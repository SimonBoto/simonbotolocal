## Summary
Quick reminder on glutathione (GSH) biosynthesis: tripeptide from glutamine, cysteine, glycine. Step 1: γ-glutamylcysteine synthetase (glutamate + cysteine -> γ-glutamylcysteine). Step 2: glutathione synthetase (adds glycine -> GSH). Requires γ-glutamyl cycle. Functions: redox homeostasis, detoxification, protein thiol maintenance.

## Key Points
- Building blocks: glutamate, cysteine, glycine.
- Enzymes: glutamate-cysteine ligase (GCL), glutathione synthetase (GSS).
- Pathway: γ-glutamyl cycle essential.
- Short 4-msg convo.
