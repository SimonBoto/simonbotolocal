## Summary
Introduction to Zettelkasten method: atomic notes, literature/permanent notes, linking in Obsidian. Example lit zettels from article on vitamin safety/supplementation history, RDAs origins, modern deficiencies, safety of vitamins vs drugs.

## Key Points
- Obsidian vault setup: folders for lit/permanent/daily notes, templates plugin.
- Atomic notes: one idea per note, summaries, bullets.
- Contextual links: direct/background, contradictions, extensions; use graph view, unlinked mentions.
- Avoid overlinking: intentional, review, depth over breadth.
- Lit zettel templates: source, summary, quotes, insights, questions.
- Article: RDAs from 1942 min survival levels; modern foods nutrient-poor; vitamins safe, not xenobiotics.
