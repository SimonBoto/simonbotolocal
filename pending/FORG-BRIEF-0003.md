## Summary
ChatGPT guide on tracking competitors' FB/IG/YT metrics over time (e.g., 18 months), emphasizing public data only: followers/subs, post/video counts, likes/comments/views (no private reach/impressions/profile views). Tools: Social Blade (YT history), Popsters/Fanpage Karma (IG/FB posts). Manual snapshot workflow + spreadsheet schema. Example for @metabolomicmedicine YT (~70K subs). Limits: no retro follower growth unless pre-tracked; medians for trends.

## Key Points
- Public metrics: growth, cadence, engagement/post (normalize by audience).
- Free methods: manual post lists, Social Blade for YT curves.
- Paid: Rival IQ, etc. for benchmarks.
- Sheet: monthly rows w/ posts, med likes/comments/views, top items.
- Prompt for agentic tracking provided.
