## Summary
Framingham (1948) linked cholesterol to CVD but later analysis: >50yo higher chol not assoc w/ CHD, even lower mortality. Statins intro 1987+; key trials (4S 1994 Merck simva 30% mort red high-risk; WOSCOPS 1995 BMS prav 31% events primary; HPS 2002 MRC/Merck simva; CARE 1996 BMS; JUPITER 2008 AZ rosuv 44% events infl). Primary NNT 25-56/5yr. Critiques: Abramson13 BMJ NNT104 low-risk NNH100-250 diabetes; Kristensen15 BMJ lifespan +days; rel risk benefits vs abs harms; all pharma-funded (disclosed). Journals pharma ads/sponsors but indep policies.

## Key Points
- Framingham reversal >50yo.
- NNT primary: WOSCOPS42, JUPITER25; low-risk104.
- Harms: diabetes NNH100-250, muscle/liver var.
- Funding: Merck/BMS/AZ etc in trials/pubs.
