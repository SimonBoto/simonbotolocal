## Summary
Detailed mevalonate pathway: acetyl-CoA -> acetoacetyl-CoA (thiolase), -> HMG-CoA (synthase), -> mevalonate (reductase, 2 NADPH), ->5-P-mevalonate (kinase, ATP), ->5-PP-mevalonate (kinase, ATP), -> IPP (decarboxylase). Energy: 2NADPH+2ATP. IPP to isoprenoids/terpenes (mono:limonene; sesqui:farnesene; tri:squalene), cholesterol/steroids/vitD. MEP alt in plants/bacteria. Ketogenesis shares acetyl->acetoacetyl->HMG-CoA but to acetoacetate/3-hydroxybutyrate (lyase/dehydrogenase). Statins target reductase.

## Key Points
- Enzymes/cofactors: thiolase(none), HMG-syn(none), red(2NADPH), kin(ATP), PP-kin(ATP), decarb(none).
- Branches: terpenes (plant aromas/defense/drugs:taxol), human: cholesterol/hormones/bile/vitD.
- Terpenes fat-soluble like cholesterol.
- DMT unrelated (tryptophan pathway).
