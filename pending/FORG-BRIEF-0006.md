## Summary
Guide to find/cancel subscriptions (Google Play, OpenAI/ChatGPT site, Apple, email search "receipt/invoice"). Specific: Netflix (app/account), Huberman (Patreon/Momentous/site). Bank apps for recurring. Screenshot expense apps: Zoho free tier; others trials (Certify/Expensify/etc. not free long-term).

## Key Points
- Google Play: Profile > Payments & subs > Subscriptions.
- Direct sites: Netflix account, Huberman login/Patreon.
- Email keywords: receipt, invoice, renewed, Stripe/PayPal.
- Not for expense tracking; use for subs only.
