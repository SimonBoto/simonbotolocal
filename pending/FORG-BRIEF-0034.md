## Summary
Para-by-para proofreading/review of friend's master's thesis theoretical chapter: adolescence dev (physical/psych/social/identity crisis Erikson), exam anxiety/stressors (performance worry, family exp), interventions (CBT/muscle relax/school progs >individual, Bandura self-efficacy/human agency). Whole-doc changes: struct/transitions/subheads, theory-practice links (Erikson/Bandura to CBT), evidence/stats/effectsizes/cites accuracy, intervention details/limits/challenges, lang simplify/consistency, visuals/tables, conclusion actionable. Rejection risks: theory-practice disconnect, weak evidence/no stats, cite incompleteness/sloppiness.

- Fixes: specifics ex/data, barriers/resources, cultural tailors.
