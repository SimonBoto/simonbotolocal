## Summary
ChatGPT guide to starting Zettelkasten in Obsidian: atomic notes, literature/permanent notes, linking (contextual/contradictory/extensive), avoid overlinking. Templates for lit/permanent notes. Example lit zettels from Greek article by Dr. Tsoukalas on vitamin safety: RDA history (1835-lemon juice to WWII), modern food nutrient depletion (10x poorer), vitamins safe (not xenobiotics like drugs, aid detox via P450), fat-sol (A/D/E/K) precautions but low toxicity risk.

## Key Points
- Obsidian setup: Vault folders, templates plugin.
- Atomic notes: 1 idea/note, summaries, bullets.
- Links: Direct/background/contradictory/extensive; use graph/unlinked mentions.
- Overlinking risks: Overload/dilution; link intentionally, review.
- Templates: Lit (source/summary/quotes/insights); Perm (core idea/connections/implications).
- Vitamins: RDAs minima for deficiency prevention; supplements essential for modern diets; no liver/kidney burden, aid xenobiotic detox.
