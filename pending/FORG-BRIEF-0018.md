## Summary
ChatGPT lists popular online reference organizers: Zotero (free/open-source), Mendeley (social network), EndNote (commercial biblio), RefWorks (web DB), ReadCube (organize/annotate), Paperpile (Google Docs/PubMed), Citavi (Windows/knowledge/tasks).

## Key Points
- Zotero: Collect/organize/cite/share.
- Mendeley: Research collab/discovery.
- EndNote: Essays/articles.
- RefWorks: Personal DB/research.
- ReadCube: Papers/annotate.
- Paperpile: Google Scholar integration.
- Citavi: Citations/biblio/tasks.
