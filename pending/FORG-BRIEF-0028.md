## Summary
Corn/grain-fed chickens: meat/adipose omega-6 ~35-40% total FA, ratio 15-20:1 (linoleic high). Eggs yolk: 25-30% omega-6, ratio 10-12:1. Pasture/omega-3 enriched: ratios 3-8:1 (meat 5-8:1, eggs 3-5:1). Pro-inflammatory via arachidonic/eicosanoids. Measurements: Cherian08, Glahn07, Surai02. Diet drives deposition.

## Key Points
- Conv broiler meat: 35-40% omega-6.
- Conv eggs: 25-30% (linoleic 18-20%).
- Enriched eggs: linoleic drops 15-18%, ratio 3-4:1.
- Pasture: better balance via diverse feed.
