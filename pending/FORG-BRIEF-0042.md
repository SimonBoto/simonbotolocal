## Summary
List of popular reference managers: Zotero (free open-source), Mendeley (social network), EndNote (commercial), RefWorks (web-based), ReadCube (organize/annotate), Paperpile (Google Docs), Citavi (Windows knowledge mgmt).

## Key Points
- Zotero: collect/organize/cite/share.
- Mendeley: organize/collaborate/discover.
- EndNote: bibliographies for essays/articles.
- RefWorks: personal databases/research.
- ReadCube: organize/discover/annotate papers.
- Paperpile: Google Scholar/PubMed integration.
- Citavi: knowledge/tasks/citations.
